name:
Caponata cups with tuna and tzatziki
ingredients:
Eggplant: 3.5oz(100 g)
Spring onions: 3.5oz(100 g)- from Tropea
Celery: 1.4oz(40 g)
Datterino tomatoes: 5.3oz(150 g)
Green olives: 2.1oz(60 g)- pitted
Salted capers: 1tsp(5 g)
Garlic: 2cloves
Extra virgin olive oil: 2tbsp(30 g)
Apple cider vinegar: 2spoonfuls
Brown sugar: 1tsp
Fine salt: 1tsp
Black pepper: 1tsp
Tuna filet: 8.5oz(240 g)
Lemon peel: 1
Salt: 1tsp
Greek yogurt: 0.67cup(150 g)
Cucumbers: 0.3cup(50 g)
Basil: 0.7oz(20 g)
Pistachios: 1tbsp(10 g)
Peeled almonds: 0.8tbsp(10 g)

info:
Difficulty: Easy
Prep time: 30 min
Cook time: 15 min
Serving: 4
Cost: High

instructions:
To prepare the caponata cups with tuna and tzatziki, start with the caponata. Cut off the top of the eggplant, slice it and cut into 1/2-inch cubes . Heat a pan with some oil and fry the cubes for about 5 minutes, stirring occasionally .
As soon as they are toasted, drain them on absorbent paper . In the same pan, add another drizzle of oil and heat together with the garlic cloves, cleaned and finely sliced spring onions , cherry tomatoes halved .
Season with salt and pepper and brown sugar . Stir and let the sugar dissolve, then deglaze with vinegar. Let it cook gently for 5 minutes .
Meanwhile, finely chop the desalted capers and pitted green olives, set aside for a moment . Do the same with almonds and pine nuts and toast them in another pan, being very careful not to burn them .
Set these aside too and finally prepare the celery. Peel it superficially with a vegetable peeler to remove the strings . Finally, cut into slices . At this point, the cherry tomatoes should be cooked, turn off the heat, discard the garlic ,
and add the eggplant cubes . Also add the celery , chopped olives and capers ,
stir and let cool . In the meantime, prepare the tuna. Cut it into strips and then into very small cubes .
Gather the pieces in a bowl and grate lemon zest , add flaked salt and mix . Move on to the tzatziki. In a bowl, pour in the Greek yogurt, hand-torn basil, lemon juice .
Add 2/3 of the toasted almond and pistachio mix . Then thinly slice the cucumber, cut the slices into quarters, thus obtaining 4 triangles and add to the mixture. Mix gently .
Now you have everything ready, proceed with the composition. At the bottom of a glass, first pour the caponata, then the tuna , the Greek yogurt and some of the toasted nut mix . Continue this way with all the others and your caponata cups with tuna and tzatziki are ready to be served with a nice sprig of basil !
Any type of storage is discouraged once the caponata cups with tuna and tzatziki are ready. If you prefer, you can prepare the caponata in advance and store it for a couple of days.
The caponata cups with tuna and tzatziki only lack a note of spiciness: add a few drops of Tabasco to the marinated tuna. Vary the nuts according to your taste: hazelnuts, walnuts, and even pecans. If you miss sesame in your tuna, you just need to toast some seeds, light or dark, and always add them during the marination phase.
