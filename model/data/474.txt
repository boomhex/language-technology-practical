name:
Brie and Pomegranate Risotto
ingredients:
Roma rice: 1 ¾cup(320 g)
Pomegranate: 2 ¾cups(400 g)
Yellow onions: 1
Thyme: to taste
Marjoram: to taste
Rosemary: to taste
Oregano: to taste
White wine: 2.8oz(80 g)
Water: 2tbsp(30 g)
Vegetable broth: 3.4cups(800 g)
Extra virgin olive oil: to taste
Fine salt: to taste
Brie cheese: 7.1oz(200 g)
Butter: 2tbsp(30 g)

info:
Difficulty: Easy
Prep time: 20 min
Cook time: 25 min
Serving: 4
Note + the preparation time for the vegetable broth

instructions:
To make the brie and pomegranate risotto, first prepare the Vegetable broth and keep it warm. Deseed the pomegranate in a bowl , then transfer the seeds to a jug, saving a few for decoration. Add the water and blend with an immersion mixer . Set aside.
Peel and chop the onion , then place it in a saucepan with a drizzle of oil, add salt and let it soften for a few minutes . Set aside.
Pour the rice into a wide-bottomed pot and toast it, stirring continuously, then add the stewed onion and deglaze with the white wine .
When the alcohol has evaporated, pour in a couple of ladles of hot broth , salt, and continue to cook the rice, adding broth as needed. Meanwhile, chop the aromatic herbs and cut the brie into cubes .
When the rice is almost cooked, stop adding the broth and add the pomegranate juice , then let it dry slightly. At this point, turn off the heat and cream with the brie and the cubed butter .
Fragrance with the chopped herbs and mix well to combine everything . Serve your brie and pomegranate risotto garnished with the reserved pomegranate seeds and some sprigs of aromatic herbs !
The brie and pomegranate risotto can be stored in the refrigerator for 2 days in an airtight container.
The color of the pomegranate juice will be more or less intense depending on the ripeness of the fruit.
Butter makes the risotto glossy and even more flavorful, but you can omit it if you prefer.
