name:
Grana Padano and Celery Soup
ingredients:
Grana Padano PDO cheese: 3.5oz(100 g)- (Reserve over 20 months) to grate
Potato starch: 1 ¼tbsp(10 g)
Whole milk: 1 ¼cup(280 g)- at room temperature
Extra virgin olive oil: 1 ½tbsp(20 g)
Vegetable broth: ⅛cup(30 g)
Celery: to taste- only the leaves
Extra virgin olive oil: to taste
Croutons: to taste

info:
Difficulty: Easy
Prep time: 15 min
Cook time: 15 min
Serving: 4

instructions:

To prepare the Grana Padano and celery soup, first make the Vegetable broth . Then place the celery leaves on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper, brush them with olive oil .
Cover with a sheet of parchment paper and place another baking sheet of the same size on top . Bake the celery leaves in a preheated static oven at 482°F for 4 minutes. After this time, remove the baking sheet from the oven .
Remove the parchment paper and let cool. Meanwhile, prepare the soup: in a saucepan, pour the olive oil and the cornstarch . Stir and cook over low heat for 2/3 minutes to form the roux.
Pour in the milk and the vegetable broth and mix with a whisk. Cook for about 5 minutes then add the grated Grana Padano PDO cheese .
Continue to stir with the whisk until completely melted. Continue cooking over low heat until the sauce has slightly thickened . Once ready, pour the soup onto the plate , garnish with the crispy celery leaves, and drizzle with raw olive oil .
Season with pepper to taste and add a handful of croutons . The Grana Padano and celery soup is ready to be served .
Consume the Grana Padano and celery soup immediately. It is not recommended to store it in the refrigerator or freezer.
When preparing the broth, don't forget to use the leftover celery stalks. To make this recipe, only a small amount of broth is needed, and any leftover can be frozen and used for flavorful risottos!
