name:
Eggs in cocotte with salmon
ingredients:
Smoked Salmon: 8.8oz(250 g)-
Cow's milk ricotta cheese: 0.4cup(100 g)
Eggs: 4
Bread: 4slices-
Dill: to taste-
Extra virgin olive oil: to taste-
Black pepper: to taste

info:
Difficulty: Easy
Prep time: 10 min
Cook time: 15 min
Serving: 4
Cost: Low

To prepare eggs en cocotte with smoked salmon, start by removing the crust from the bread slices with a knife, lightly brush them with extra virgin olive oil and place them in the oven to grill, using the grill setting for a few minutes, turning them during cooking. Meanwhile, cut the smoked salmon into thin strips and put it in a bowl, add the sprigs of dill
the ricotta cheese and mix the ingredients well . Butter 4 ovenproof cocottes, place the salmon and ricotta mixture into them and make a small groove in the center of each cocotte with the back of a teaspoon .
Place an egg (being careful not to break it) on top of each cocotte . At this point, fill a baking dish with boiling water halfway, place the cocottes inside and bake at 356°F (180°C) for 15 minutes. Garnish with ground pepper, dill leaves, and serve the eggs en cocotte with salmon accompanied by the grilled bread slices cut into strips .
We recommend consuming the eggs en cocotte with salmon as soon as they are ready, but if you wish, you can store them in the refrigerator, sealed tightly, for up to one day.
Among the variants of this recipe that I find delicious are the one with cooked ham (or Prague ham) instead of salmon; the vegetarian one, with fontina or Gruyère cheese and grilled (or sautéed, diced or julienne) vegetables; and the one for a unique evening: eggs and truffle, a delicacy for true connoisseurs!
