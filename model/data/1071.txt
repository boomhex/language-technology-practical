name:
Rice salad in jar cooking
ingredients:
Basmati rice: ⅓cup(70 g)
Yellow peppers: ½
Zucchini: 1- (small)
Cherry tomatoes: 5
Black Greek olives: 4
Basil: to taste
Extra virgin olive oil: 1tsp
Fine salt: to taste

info:
Difficulty: Easy
Prep time: 30 min
Cook time: 9 min
Makes: 1 pieces
Cost: Low
Note + resting times

instructions:
To prepare the rice salad in jar cooking, start with the vegetables. Cut the zucchini, peppers, and cherry tomatoes into cubes , then pour them into a jar suitable for jar cooking . Add salt and oil
and cook, uncovered, in the microwave for 2 minutes at 800 Watts . Remove the jar from the microwave using an oven mitt and add the rice ,
pour in 140 g (5 oz) of water , stir , and use a damp napkin to clean the rim well .
Close with the lid and put in the microwave at 880 Watts for 6 minutes . After the time has elapsed, remove the jar, still wearing the oven mitt, and let it rest for 10 minutes with the lid on. During this time the rice will continue cooking .
In the meantime, cut the black olives into strips and before serving, open the jar and add them along with the basil .
A final cook in the microwave at 800 Watts, for another 3 minutes . Don't worry if when you reopen the microwave you find the jar open, the steam will make it pop. If it doesn't open, cook for another minute . Your jar-cooked rice salad is ready, enjoy your meal !
You can store the rice salad in jar cooking for a maximum of one day in the refrigerator.
Freezing is not recommended.
Vary the vegetables in your jar-cooked rice salad to your liking. You can also add pre-cooked legumes if you like, making it a truly unique dish! At the end of cooking, add some cheese cubes, like feta or Quartirolo Lombardo!
