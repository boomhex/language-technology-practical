name:
Frittata with Zucchini Flowers
ingredients:
Eggs: 8
Zucchini flowers: 20cups(600 g)
Shallot: ½
Garlic: 1clove
Chives: to taste
Extra virgin olive oil: 4tsp
Fine salt: to taste
Black pepper: to taste

instructions:

To prepare the frittata with zucchini flowers, first, clean the flowers: remove the base and the internal stalk , then tear them gently into strips with your hands . In a 10-inch pan, pour oil, garlic, and chopped shallots .
Let them sauté, then remove the garlic clove . Add the flowers and salt . Cook them for about 5 minutes on medium-low heat, adding a little water if necessary or covering with a lid to maintain moisture.
Meanwhile, break the eggs into a bowl, add salt and pepper. Also add the chopped chives and whisk the eggs with a whisk .
As soon as the flowers are tender, remove the lid and pour in the egg mixture , then level the frittata well .
Cover again and cook with lid on medium-low heat for 20-25 minutes , until the egg is fully set . If you prefer, you can also flip it for a golden color on both sides. Serve your zucchini flower frittata hot or warm !
The frittata with zucchini flowers can be stored in the refrigerator for up to one day.
You can enrich the frittata with zucchini flowers with fresh mint or even with ricotta!
