name:
Greek Rice Salad
ingredients:
Rice: 1 Â½cup(320 g)- for salad
Cherry tomatoes: 10.6oz(300 g)
Cucumbers: 8.8oz(250 g)
Feta cheese: 6.3oz(180 g)
Black Greek olives: 3.5oz(100 g)
Basil: to taste
Extra virgin olive oil: to taste
Fine salt: to taste
Black pepper: to taste

info:
Difficulty: Very easy
Prep time: 20 min
Cook time: 10 min
Serving: 4

instructions:
The heat is on, and for lunch, you're torn between a classic rice salad and a refreshing Greek salad ? To please everyone, we recommend preparing a delicious Greek rice salad! Just cook the rice and dress it with raw ingredients inspired by the famous Hellenic dish: tomatoes, cucumbers, olives, and the indispensable feta cheese. The scent of basil will add another Mediterranean touch, making your Greek rice salad ready to be served... or taken to the beach!
To make the Greek rice salad, first bring a pot of water to a boil, then salt it and cook the rice for 10 minutes . Meanwhile, prepare the dressing: wash the cucumbers and cut them first into slices and then into cubes .
Wash the cherry tomatoes and quarter them , then halve the olives . Finally, cut the feta into cubes .
In a bowl, combine the cherry tomatoes with the cucumbers , olives , and feta .
Tear the basil leaves with your hands and season with salt, pepper , and a drizzle of oil. Once the rice is cooked, drain it and transfer it to another bowl. Drizzle with a bit of oil and mix, then let it cool.
When the rice has cooled, add the dressing and mix everything well . Your Greek rice salad is ready to be enjoyed !
The Greek rice salad can be stored in the refrigerator for 2 days.
If you like, you can substitute basil with mint leaves!
Add salt sparingly because feta is already very salty.
