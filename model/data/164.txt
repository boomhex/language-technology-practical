name:
Pasta with zucchini and stracchino
ingredients:
Fusilloni pasta: 11.3oz(320 g)
Zucchini: 2 ½cups(300 g)
Stracchino cheese: ¾cup(200 g)
Parmigiano Reggiano PDO cheese: 2tbsp(30 g)- (to grate)
Garlic: 2cloves
Thyme: to taste
Extra virgin olive oil: to taste
Fine salt: to taste
Black pepper: to taste

info:
Difficulty: Easy
Prep time: 5 min
Cook time: 20 min
Serving: 4
Cost: Low

instructions:
To prepare the pasta with zucchini and stracchino, first trim the zucchini and cut them into slices 1/4 inch thick . In a large pan, heat a splash of oil with the crushed garlic cloves, then add the zucchini .
Fragrance with thyme leaves and sauté over medium-high heat for about 5-7 minutes, then salt . Set aside a few zucchini for plating and keep the others in the pan. Meanwhile, bring a pot of salted water to a boil and cook the fusilloni until al dente .
Towards the end of the pasta cooking time, pour a ladle of its water into the pan with the zucchini , then add the stracchino . Turn on the low heat and mix well until you achieve a creamy consistency , then adjust the salt.
Drain the pasta into the pan and add the grated Parmigiano Reggiano . Mix everything with the heat off, adding more cooking water if necessary, then plate .
Complete the dishes with the zucchini set aside , thyme, and black pepper to taste . Your pasta with zucchini and stracchino is ready to be served !
It is recommended to consume the pasta with zucchini and stracchino immediately.
If you prefer, you can replace thyme with mint or basil.
