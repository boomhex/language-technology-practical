name:
Pasta with arugula pesto and tuna
ingredients:
Fusilli bucati corti pasta: 11oz(320 g)
Tuna filet: 4.6oz(130 g)- drained
Lemon peel: 1
Fine salt: to taste
Arugula: 4.2oz(120 g)
Almonds: 0.3cup(50 g)
Extra virgin olive oil: 10tbsp(140 g)
Piccadilly tomatoes: 0.6cup(100 g)
Basil: to taste

info:
Difficulty: Very easy
Prep time: 15 min
Cook time: 10 min
Serving: 4
Cost: Low

instructions:
To prepare the pasta with arugula pesto and tuna, start by putting the water on the stove to cook the fusilli. When it comes to a boil, salt it and cook the pasta . In the meantime, prepare the pesto. In the bowl of a blender, add the arugula, a few basil leaves, the almonds , and the tomatoes
then the oil and finally the salt. Now blend everything until you get a smooth and homogeneous cream .
Pour the pesto into a pan without turning on the stove and as soon as the pasta is al dente, drain it directly into the pan . Add a ladle of pasta cooking water to thin the mixture and combine .
Now add the drained tuna fillets and grated lemon zest . Give it a final stir before plating and enjoy your pasta with arugula pesto and tuna right away .
Once the pasta with arugula pesto and tuna is ready, it is recommended to consume it immediately.
The sauce can be prepared in advance and stored as indicated in the arugula pesto storage box.
When fresh tomatoes are out of season, or if you want a more flavorful dish, replace the piccadilly tomatoes with sun-dried tomatoes in oil: your pasta with pesto and tuna will be truly delicious!
