name:
Gorgonzola escalopes with walnuts and cocoa
ingredients:
Sliced veal: 1.5lbs(700 g)
Gorgonzola cheese: 7oz(200 g)
Walnuts: 10
Unsweetened cocoa powder: 1spoonful
Whole milk: 0.42cup(100 g)
Type 00 flour: â…“cup(50 g)
Extra virgin olive oil: to taste
Fine salt: to taste
Black pepper: to taste

info:
Difficulty: Easy
Prep time: 10 min
Cook time: 10 min
Serving: 6
Cost: Average

instructions:
To prepare the gorgonzola escalopes, first, salt the slices of meat by massaging them with your fingers, then pass them through the flour and place them on a cutting board .
Put the gorgonzola in a bowl, add the milk , and work with a fork to quickly blend everything . Then salt and set aside.
Shell the walnuts , place the kernels in the mixer , and coarsely chop .
Heat a drizzle of oil in a pan and brown the meat over high heat on both sides .
Add the gorgonzola with milk , cover with the lid , lower the heat, and cook for 5 minutes, turning the slices of meat halfway through. Remove the lid, let the sauce thicken, and turn off the heat .
Arrange the meat on plates and finish with a sprinkle of unsweetened cocoa and a handful of walnut crumbles . Serve the gorgonzola escalopes hot .
We recommend consuming the gorgonzola escalopes hot.
Accompany the gorgonzola escalopes with creamy mashed potatoes.
