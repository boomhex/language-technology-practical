name:
Pistachio Caprese Cake
ingredients:
Pistachio flour: 1cup(200 g)
Pistachio paste: 0.625cup(150 g)
Eggs: 2
Vegetable oil: 0.7cup(150 g)
Sugar: 0.67cup(130 g)
Pistachio flour: 0.7oz(20 g)
Powdered sugar: to taste

info:
Difficulty: Easy
Prep time: 20 min
Cook time: 30 min
Serving: 6
Cost: Average

instructions:
The pistachio caprese cake is an irresistible, delicious, and fragrant dessert, decorated with a delightful Ganache cream . It is a reinterpretation of the classic caprese cake , which leaves behind dark chocolate and almonds to make room for pistachios. With its distinctive fudgy texture, it's perfect to serve as a dessert after a meal, but also at breakfast or as a snack, perhaps with a good coffee or a cup of green tea.
An easy recipe to make, it can be served to celebrate a special occasion or simply to treat yourself a little. If you love desserts that combine simplicity and taste, you must try the pistachio caprese cake: it will win you over slice after slice!

To prepare the pistachio caprese cake, first separate the yolks from the egg whites. Beat the egg whites until stiff peaks form and set aside. Put the yolks in another bowl .
Add the sugar and beat with an electric mixer until you get a smooth mixture .
Add the pistachio paste and mix again to combine. At this point, add the vegetable oil and the pistachio flour .
Incorporate everything with the mixer until you get a smooth mixture . Finally, add the stiff egg whites .
Gently fold from bottom to top to avoid deflating the egg whites . Pour everything into a 8-inch springform pan lined with parchment paper and bake in a preheated static oven at 340Â°F for 30 minutes .
Let it cool completely, then place a small bowl in the center of the cake and dust with powdered sugar . Remove the bowl and decorate the empty space with pistachio flour . Your pistachio caprese cake is ready to be enjoyed !
The pistachio caprese cake can be stored at room temperature for 3-4 days, covered with a glass dome, in a cool, dry place.
If you wish, you can add a handful of white chocolate chips to the pistachio caprese batter for an even more indulgent touch. Alternatively, you can cover the surface with pistachio glaze or with a drizzle of melted white chocolate and chopped pistachios for a super scenic effect!
