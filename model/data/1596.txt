name:
Pasta with onions and potatoes
ingredients:
Potatoes: 1.3lbs(600 g)- yellow-fleshed
Red onions: 2.8oz(80 g)
Ditaloni rigati pasta: 1 ¼cup(250 g)
Peeled tomatoes: 1cup(240 g)
Fine salt: to taste
Black pepper: to taste
Rosemary: to taste
Parsley: to taste
Extra virgin olive oil: to taste

info:
Difficulty: Easy
Prep time: 20 min
Cook time: 30 min
Serving: 4
Cost: Low

instructions:
To make the pasta with onions and potatoes, first peel the potatoes , then wash and dry them before cutting them into cubes .
Peel the onion and slice it , chop the rosemary first and then the parsley .
In a large pot, pour the oil and the sliced onions , sauté for 2 minutes then flavor with the rosemary and the parsley ,
let it season for another minute, then add the peeled tomatoes (10-11) and cook for 5 minutes. At this point, add a glass of pasta cooking water ;
salt , pepper , also add the cubed potatoes and continue cooking for another 20 minutes or until the potatoes are tender.
Now place a pan full of salted water on the heat, and when it comes to a boil, cook the pasta al dente following the time indicated on the package. Once the pasta is al dente, drain it directly into the pan with the sauce . Stir to flavor and finish cooking. Serve the pasta with onions and potatoes hot .
If the pasta with onions and potatoes is left over, you can store it in the refrigerator for one day in an airtight container.
Those who love strong flavors can add a bit of chili pepper to give the dish a spicy note.
