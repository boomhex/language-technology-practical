name:
Blueberry Cake
ingredients:
Type 00 flour: 2cups(240 g)
Eggs: 5.6oz(160 g)- (3 medium)
Butter: ½cup(115 g)- soft
Greek yogurt: 1cup(250 g)
Brown sugar: 0.7cup(155 g)
Lemon peel: 1
Vanilla bean: 1
Fine salt: 1pinch
Baking powder: 3 ½tsp(16 g)
Blueberries: 2 ¾cups(400 g)
Brown sugar: ⅛cup(25 g)

info:
Difficulty: Easy
Prep time: 20 min
Cook time: 55 min
Serving: 8
Cost: Average

instructions:
To prepare the blueberry cake, all ingredients should be at room temperature. Rinse the blueberries under running water and let them drain . In a bowl, add the butter and sugar .
Start working with electric beaters ; then add the lemon zest , vanilla seeds , and a pinch of salt.
Work until you get a creamy mixture . Then add the eggs one at a time, waiting for the first to be well absorbed before adding the next . Also add the yogurt and continue working with the electric beaters.
Now sieve the flour and baking powder . Gradually add the dry ingredients and mix again with the beaters .
Add 9 oz of blueberries and mix with a spatula . Pour the mixture into the already greased and floured 9-inch cake tin , level well.
And pour the rest of the blueberries on top . Sprinkle with brown sugar and bake in a preheated static oven for 55 minutes at 356°F on the lower rack. Always perform the toothpick test before removing the cake from the oven . Let it cool before removing it from the pan and serving your blueberry cake.
The blueberry cake can be stored for 2-3 days under a glass dome.
Freezing is not recommended.
Serve the cake with ice cream or a small bowl of Chantilly cream !
