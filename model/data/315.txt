name:
Squid Risotto
ingredients:
Carnaroli rice: 1.6cups(320 g)
Calamari (squid): 8.8oz(250 g)- (already cleaned and whole)
Shallot: 1
Tomato paste: 2tsp(10 g)
Vegetable broth: 4 ¼cups(1 l)- (or fish)
White wine: 1.75oz(50 g)
Parsley: to taste
Butter: to taste
Parmigiano Reggiano PDO cheese: 1.75oz(50 g)
Butter: 3 ½tbsp(50 g)

info:
Difficulty: Easy
Prep time: 15 min
Cook time: 20 min
Serving: 4
Cost: Average

instructions:
To prepare the squid risotto, first make the Vegetable broth and keep it warm. Move on to the squid (if they are not already cleaned, consult our guide "How to clean squid" for more details): detach the head and tentacles , remove the cartilage pen and rinse everything under running water. Then cut the body into rings and the tentacles into pieces.
Chop the parsley leaves , then peel and chop the shallot . In a pot, add a knob of butter and the chopped shallot .
Let it season for a couple of minutes , then add the rice and deglaze with the white wine .
Add a ladle of broth , salt and add the squid . Cook the rice by moistening it with the broth a little at a time, waiting each time for the liquid to be absorbed before adding more.
Add the tomato paste and complete cooking . When the risotto is cooked, turn off the heat and cream with butter and grated Parmigiano Reggiano .
Scent with chopped parsley , stir to flavor , and serve your squid risotto hot !
Squid risotto can be stored in the refrigerator for up to 2 days.
Freezing is not recommended.
If you prefer, you can replace the tomato paste with halved cherry tomatoes or a light tomato sauce.
For an aromatic touch, add some grated lemon zest at the end of cooking!
