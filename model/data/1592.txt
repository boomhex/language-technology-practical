name:
Stewed baby octopus
ingredients:
Baby octopus: 2.6lbs(1.2 kg)- cleaned
Peeled tomatoes: 6 Â¼cups(1 kg)- datterini (cherry tomatoes)
Water: 0.67cup(150 g)
White wine: 1.8oz(50 g)
Dried chili pepper: 5- (small)
Garlic: 1clove
Basil: 6leaves
Extra virgin olive oil: to taste
Fine salt: to taste

info:
Difficulty: Easy
Prep time: 15 min
Cook time: 2 h 35 min
Serving: 4
Cost: Average

instructions:
To make the stewed baby octopus, first get a large saucepan, pour in the oil, the peeled whole garlic clove , and the dried chili peppers . Let it flavor for a few moments, then add the cleaned baby octopus
and let them brown for a few minutes over high heat, stirring them with the help of kitchen tongs . At this point, deglaze with the white wine , let it evaporate for a couple of minutes, then add the canned tomatoes .
Pour the 150 ml of water into the bowl containing the tomatoes to collect the remaining pulp residues, and pour everything into the pot . Salt and season with the washed and dried basil leaves .
Cover the baby octopus with the lid and cook over low heat for about 2 hours. After this time, remove the lid and continue cooking for another 30 minutes, keeping the heat low. Once the cooking is complete , you can serve your stewed baby octopus hot .
The stewed baby octopus can be stored in the refrigerator for 2 days. You can freeze the preparation if you used fresh products.
If you have leftover stewed baby octopus, use them to dress a nice plate of spaghetti; you'll get a really appetizing first course!
