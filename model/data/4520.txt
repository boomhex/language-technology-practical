name:
Cocoa and Strawberry Clafoutis
ingredients:
Nestlé condensed cocoa milk: 0.7cup(200 g)
Type 00 flour: 1 ½cup(200 g)
Eggs: 5.8oz(165 g)- (approximately 3 medium)
Strawberries: 0.67cup(90 g)
Powdered sugar: to taste

info:
Difficulty: Easy
Prep time: 15 min
Cook time: 25 min
Makes: 4 pieces

instructions:
If you are already familiar with the cherry clafoutis , the famous French dessert, then you're ready for a new and simple, quick, and incredibly delicious version! Only 4 ingredients are needed for your cocoa and strawberry clafoutis. Its almost creamy texture, given by the cocoa condensed milk, is perfect for a delightful snack or to beautifully finish a meal. The soft dough enriched with fresh and fragrant strawberries creates an intriguing play of flavors!
If the cocoa and strawberry clafoutis impressed you, discover all the other sweet and savory variations as well:
To prepare the cocoa and strawberry clafoutis, start by breaking the eggs into a bowl, pour the cocoa condensed milk and mix with a whisk . Sift the flour .
Incorporate the flour well , until you obtain a soft and homogeneous cream . Clean the strawberries and slice them into rounds .
Add the strawberries to the mixture and gently incorporate them . Butter 4 ceramic molds, 5 inches in diameter and 0.75 inches in height, and transfer the mixture .
Proceed to baking : the cocoa and strawberry clafoutis bake in a static oven, preheated to 350°F, for about 25 minutes . Once baked and warm, enjoy them with a bit of powdered sugar on top .
The cocoa and strawberry clafoutis can be stored at room temperature for 1 day.
Try adding some chopped nuts to the mixture: choose your favorite!
