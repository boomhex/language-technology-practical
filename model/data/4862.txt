name:
Baked Kale Chips
ingredients:
Italian kale (cavolo nero): 10cups(300 g)
Extra virgin olive oil: 2spoonfuls
Dried chili pepper: to taste
Black pepper: to taste
Fine salt: to taste
Salt: to taste

info:
Difficulty: Very easy
Prep time: 10 min
Cook time: 8 min
Serving: 4
Cost: Very low

instructions:
Baked kale chips are a tantalizing appetizer, a surprisingly healthy and wholesome alternative to the classic fries or packaged snacks. Prepared with love and care, these chips offer an extraordinary combination of crispiness and enticing flavors that make them irresistible. The kale, rich in antioxidants and essential nutrients, becomes the protagonist of this delicious snack, providing a feeling of pleasure and well-being with every bite.

To prepare baked kale chips, first wash and dry the leaves, then remove the central rib to keep only the tender part . Place them in a bowl and dress with oil .
Add salt and dried chili pepper . Now mix with your hands to blend the seasoning .
Spread the kale leaves on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper, making sure not to overlap them , and bake in a preheated static oven at 400Â°F for 7-8 minutes. Remove the baked kale chips from the oven and let them dry, then sprinkle with salt flakes and serve !
Baked kale chips can be stored at room temperature for 4-5 days.
You can flavor the chips with sesame seeds, garlic powder, or other spices of your choice.
