name:
Raw marinated zucchini
ingredients:
Zucchini: 8
Garlic: 2cloves
Apple cider vinegar: 2spoonfuls
Lemon juice: 2oz(60 g)
Mint: to taste
Extra virgin olive oil: 4spoonfuls
Fine salt: to taste
Black pepper: to taste

info:
Difficulty: Very easy
Prep time: 10 min
Serving: 4
Cost: Very low
Note + marinating time (30 min.)

instructions:
To prepare the raw marinated zucchini, first wash the zucchini, trim them, and dry them well. Slice them thinly lengthwise using a mandolin . Wash and chop the mint .
Pour the oil and the strained lemon juice into a bowl , then add salt and pepper .
Add the apple cider vinegar , the garlic cloves cut into pieces , and the chopped mint . Mix with a whisk to combine everything.
Transfer the zucchini to a baking dish and pour the marinade over them . Cover with plastic wrap and let it rest in the refrigerator for at least half an hour. After this time, remove the garlic, drain the marinade well, and serve your raw marinated zucchini at room temperature !
You can store the raw marinated zucchini for 1-2 days in the refrigerator covered with plastic wrap.
Freezing is not recommended.
If you don't like mint or want to vary, you can replace it with other fresh herbs such as basil, parsley, or oregano: they will give your marinated zucchini a different fragrance, but always fresh and perfect for summer.
