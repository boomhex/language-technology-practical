name:
Salt-Roasted Spring Chicken
ingredients:
Young rooster: 1.1lbs(500 g)- whole
Coarse salt: 4.4lbs(2 kg)
Rosemary: 20tsp(20 g)
Marjoram: 3tsp(15 g)
Parsley: 1 ½tbsp(10 g)
Thyme: 10tsp(10 g)
Sage: 2tsp(10 g)
Bay leaves: 0.4oz(10 g)
Ground white pepper: 9tsp(20 g)
Pink peppercorn: 2tsp(10 g)
Sun-dried tomatoes in oil: 1.5oz(40 g)
Parsley: 0.4oz(10 g)
Garlic: 2cloves
Lemons: 1slice
Extra virgin olive oil: 0.7tbsp(10 g)
Sweet Paprika: 1 ¼tsp(5 g)

To prepare the salt-roasted spring chicken, start by chopping the aromatic herbs: first, the sage with parsley and marjoram , then the rosemary with thyme and bay leaf . Mix the chopped herbs with the coarse salt in a large bowl .
Also chop the white and pink peppercorns and add them to the flavored salt , then mix well .
Proceed with the stuffing: place the spring chicken on the cutting board and insert the garlic cloves , lemon , and sun-dried tomatoes inside.
Finally, add the parsley . Pour the oil and paprika into a small bowl and mix with a brush .
Brush the spring chicken with the paprika oil, covering all parts well . Now take a cast-iron pot about 8 inches in diameter and cover the bottom with some of the flavored salt, then place the stuffed spring chicken and cover it completely with the remaining salt .
Bake in a preheated static oven at 392°F for 45 minutes . After this time, remove from the oven and let cool at room temperature for at least 30 minutes, then you can remove the salt with a spoon and serve your salt-roasted spring chicken !
The salt-roasted spring chicken can be stored in the refrigerator for 2 days in an airtight container.
Enhance the salt with herbs and aromas of your choice!
You can substitute sweet paprika with spicy paprika for a stronger flavor.
