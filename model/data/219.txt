name:
Carrots in Air Fryer
ingredients:
Carrots: 2.1cups(500 g)
Rosemary: 1sprig
Sage: 2leaves
Extra virgin olive oil: ½tbsp(10 g)
Fine salt: 1pinch
Black pepper: 1pinch
Natural plain yogurt: 2spoonfuls
Extra virgin olive oil: 1filo
Lemon juice: 2tsp(10 g)
Chives: 4fili

info:
Difficulty: Easy
Prep time: 10 min
Cook time: 12 min
Serving: 4
Cost: Low

instructions:
To prepare air fryer carrots, preferably use small and tender carrots. Start by removing the ends , then peel them with a vegetable peeler or a sharp knife . After peeling, wash them thoroughly .
Cut the carrots lengthwise into sticks about 1.5 inches long , then wash the herbs, remove the woody central part of the rosemary , and chop only the needles along with the sage leaves .
Now transfer the carrots to a large bowl and add the previously chopped herbs . Then adjust the salt and pepper .
Next, season with a drizzle of extra virgin olive oil . Mix everything well . Transfer the carrots to the air fryer basket; you can use perforated parchment paper or place them directly in the basket .
At this point, cook the carrots for about 12 minutes at 392°F, making sure to check them during cooking and stir if necessary . The carrots should be golden . To prepare the yogurt sauce to accompany the carrots, pour two tablespoons of plain or Greek yogurt into a bowl .
Add the chives , lemon juice , and salt .
Finally, after adding one last drizzle of oil , just mix everything . Serve the air fryer carrots with the homemade yogurt sauce .
The air fryer carrots can be stored in a bowl covered with plastic wrap or in an airtight container for up to two days in the refrigerator.
Air fryer carrots can be prepared with various herbs, you can also add garlic powder and sweet or hot paprika to taste.
If you want to add crunchiness to the carrots, you can prepare them the same way with the addition of a tablespoon of breadcrumbs or cornmeal.
The yogurt sauce can be prepared with the addition of a teaspoon of mustard.
