name:
Stuffed Zucchini with Grains and Quinoa
ingredients:
Mix of grains and quinoa: 1 ¼cup(180 g)- with buckwheat
Round zucchini: 4- small
Pizzutelli tomatoes: ½cup(100 g)
Taggiasca olives: 0.7oz(20 g)
Anchovies: 2.5oz(70 g)
Basil: to taste
Mint: to taste
Wild fennel: to taste
Lemon peel: to taste
Extra virgin olive oil: to taste
Fine salt: to taste
Black pepper: to taste

info:
Difficulty: Very easy
Prep time: 10 min
Cook time: 30 min
Serving: 4

instructions:
To prepare the stuffed zucchinis with grains and quinoa, first cook the mix in boiling, salted water to taste , for the time indicated on the package. Then drain it, transfer it to a bowl, and add a drizzle of oil . Mix and let it cool in the refrigerator.
At this point, wash the zucchinis, then with a knife cut off the top cap, as if it were a sort of hat . Hollow out the zucchinis inside , to completely empty them. Finally, slightly score them at the base to straighten them. Place them upside down on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper ;
season with salt and oil and bake at 400°F for about 15 minutes. Then remove them from the oven and let them cool . In the meantime, proceed to the How to Clean Anchovies , remove the head with your hands
and by gently spreading them apart, also extract the central bone . Then let a pan heat up and place the anchovies inside .
Cook them for about 4 minutes then transfer them to a plate and let them cool. Then cut the cherry tomatoes into small cubes and finely chop all the herbs together .
Then coarsely chop the anchovies as well . Take the cold grain mix, add the cherry tomatoes, anchovies , olives, the herb mix, and lemon zest .
Mix everything and use it to fill the zucchinis . At this point, you can serve them at room temperature or keep them in the refrigerator until ready to enjoy.
Keep the stuffed zucchinis in the refrigerator for up to 1-2 days.
Instead of fresh anchovies, you can use anchovies in oil. You can add caper flowers.
