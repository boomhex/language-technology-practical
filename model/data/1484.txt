name:
Sole rolls with herbs
ingredients:
Plaice filets: 14.1oz(400 g)- (8 fillets)
Breadcrumbs: 1cup(120 g)- (from fresh bread)
Garlic: 1clove
Parsley: to taste
Basil: 3leaves
Eggs: 2
Whole milk: 1spoonful
Butter: 5 Â½tbsp(80 g)
Fine salt: to taste
Black pepper: to taste
Mayonnaise: 0.4cup(100 g)
Low-fat Yogurt: 2spoonfuls
Salted capers: 1spoonful- desalted

info:
Difficulty: Easy
Prep time: 15 min
Cook time: 5 min
Serving: 4
Cost: Average

instructions:
To prepare the sole rolls with herbs, the first thing to do is prepare the breading. Transfer the breadcrumbs into a bowl, add the crushed garlic , the chopped parsley , salt, pepper, and the chopped basil .
Mix everything and set aside . Cut the sole fillets in half lengthwise . Spread a little bread mixture on each fillet ,
and drizzle with a little oil . Then roll up the sole fillets and secure them with a toothpick .
Repeat this operation for all the fillets . Break the eggs into a plate, add the milk , salt, pepper, and beat everything .
Dip the rolls first in the egg and then in the breadcrumb mixture , placing them on a plate as you go .
Melt the butter in a pan, place the sole rolls inside , and cook for 3-5 minutes, turning the rolls halfway through cooking . In the meantime, prepare the sauce. Transfer the mayonnaise to a bowl ,
add the yogurt and mix . Finally, add the desalted capers , mix again, and serve the sauce together with the sole rolls, now cooked .
The sole rolls can be stored cooked in the refrigerator for a maximum of 1-2 days.
The filling can be enriched with oil-packed anchovy fillets.
