name:
Asparagus in puff pastry with prosciutto
ingredients:
Asparagus: 1.75lbs(800 g)
Puff pastry: 8.8oz(250 g)
Prosciutto crudo: 8slices-
Grana Padano PDO cheese: 1oz(30 g)- shaved
Eggs: 1

info:
Difficulty: Very easy
Prep time: 30 min
Cook time: 25 min
Serving: 4
Cost: Low

To prepare the asparagus in puff pastry with prosciutto, start by cleaning the asparagus, removing the white parts of the stalks and scraping them to remove the tougher outer layers (click here to see how). Gather them into a bundle tied with kitchen string and boil them in salted water for 8-10 minutes, until they are cooked but still crunchy. Drain the asparagus, let them cool and dry. Roll out the puff pastry, gently flatten it with a rolling pin, and cut out 8 strips of dough about 1.18 inches wide. Lay out the prosciutto slices, sprinkle them with Parmesan flakes ,
place the asparagus on top and wrap the prosciutto around them . Use the puff pastry strips to wrap the asparagus with prosciutto in a spiral. Brush the asparagus in puff pastry with the beaten egg over the entire surface and place them on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper and bake for 15 minutes at 392°F, until the pastry is cooked and golden brown . Serve the asparagus in puff pastry with prosciutto hot!
To give an even more original touch to your asparagus in puff pastry with prosciutto, sprinkle them with sesame seeds once you've brushed them with egg, making sure they adhere well to the pastry!
