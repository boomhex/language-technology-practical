name:
Yogurt cake without butter
ingredients:
Natural plain yogurt: ½cup(125 g)- (unsweetened)
Type 00 flour: 2cups(250 g)
Sugar: 0.8cup(150 g)
Cornstarch: 8 ½tbsp(50 g)
Sunflower seed oil: 0.32cup(75 g)
Eggs: 7oz(195 g)- (3 medium)
Lemon peel: 1
Baking powder: 3 ½tsp(16 g)
Powdered sugar: to taste

info:
Difficulty: Easy
Prep time: 15 min
Cook time: 45 min
Serving: 6
Cost: Low

instructions:
To make the yogurt cake without butter, pour the eggs and sugar into a bowl , beat with an electric mixer until you obtain a light and fluffy mixture: it will take about 5 minutes. Pour in the vegetable oil and mix again. Sift all the dry ingredients.
Add the plain yogurt , the flour , and the cornstarch .
Continue by incorporating the baking powder and the grated lemon zest . Mix all the ingredients with the mixers, ensuring a smooth and homogeneous mixture without lumps .
Once ready , transfer the batter into an 8-inch cake pan lined with parchment paper and bake in a preheated oven at 350°F for about 45 minutes.
Remove the cake from the oven and let it cool slightly , then remove from the pan onto a wire rack and garnish with a simple dusting of powdered sugar . The yogurt cake without butter is ready to be enjoyed .
The yogurt cake without butter can be stored for 3-4 days under a glass dome. You can freeze it after baking.
You can also use fruit-flavored yogurt; if it's already sweetened, use 2 tablespoons less sugar.
