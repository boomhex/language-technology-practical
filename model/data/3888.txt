name:
Friggione
ingredients:
Lard: 2tsp
Salt: 1tsp
Sugar: 1tsp
White onions: 5.5lbs(2.5 kg)
Peeled tomatoes: ⅔cup(200 g)

info:
Difficulty: Very easy
Prep time: 15 min
Cook time: 3 h 30 min
Serving: 4
Cost: Very low
Note 4 hours for the marination of the onion

To prepare friggione, start by cleaning the white onions , then slice them very finely using a mandolin or a sharp knife . Place them in a large bowl and add the teaspoon of coarse salt
and the sugar , and let everything marinate for at least 4 hours, stirring occasionally. Put the onions and the liquid they have released into a pan, adding the lard , and let it cook over low heat for at least 2 hours, stirring occasionally .
When the onions have darkened, add the peeled tomatoes and continue cooking for at least another hour and a half; the final result should be a kind of cream , at this point, adjust with salt and pepper. Serve the friggione on crostini , or as a sauce to accompany meat or polenta.
