name:
Stewed Pork Loin with Onions and Chili
ingredients:
Pork loin: 1.3lbs(600 g)- sliced
Fresh chili pepper: 4- large, green and slightly spicy
Red onions: 2- small
Tomato pur√©e: 1cup(250 g)
Garlic: 1clove
Extra virgin olive oil: to taste
Fine salt: to taste
Basil: to taste

info:
Difficulty: Easy
Prep time: 20 min
Cook time: 20 min
Serving: 4
Cost: Low

instructions:
To prepare the stewed pork loin with onions and chili, start by rinsing the chili peppers, removing the stems , and cutting them into thin strips . Slice the peeled onion thinly as well .
In a saucepan, heat a little oil with a clove of garlic, then add the chili peppers and onions . Let them stew for a few minutes over low heat, then add the tomato puree and a pinch of salt .
Cook for 20 minutes until the sauce has thickened. Remove the garlic clove and immerse the pork loin slices . Gently turn the slices with tongs or two spoons to avoid piercing the meat .
Fragrance with basil and let cook for another 10-15 minutes, depending on the thickness of the meat, so it remains tender and juicy; ensure the salt is adjusted . Your stewed pork loin with onions and chili is ready to plate and enjoy right away .
It is recommended to consume the stewed pork loin with onions and chili immediately as the meat might become a bit dry when stored. However, if you prefer, you can store it in the refrigerator for a couple of days.
Freezing is not recommended.
Enhance the flavor of your stewed pork loin with onions and chili by adding a pinch of smoked paprika!
