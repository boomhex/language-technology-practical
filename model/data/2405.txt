name:
Quick Tomato Puff Pastry Breadsticks
ingredients:
Puff pastry: 8.1oz(230 g)- (rectangular)
Triple concentrated tomato paste: 1 ½tbsp(20 g)
Oregano: to taste
Thyme: to taste
Sesame seeds: to taste
Poppy seeds: to taste

info:
Difficulty: Very easy
Prep time: 15 min
Cook time: 25 min
Serving: 4

instructions:

To prepare the quick tomato breadsticks, first dilute the tomato paste with a splash of water and mix with a brush . Take the puff pastry and with a knife, cut it into strips 1 inch wide .
Brush all the strips you've obtained with the tomato paste , then season half of the strips with dried oregano and the other half with fresh thyme .
Roll the strips into spirals to form twisted breadsticks . Brush again with the paste and sprinkle half of the breadsticks with sesame seeds
and the remaining with poppy seeds . Bake in a preheated oven at 392°F for about 20 minutes (if using a convection oven, at 356°F for about 10 minutes). Let them cool slightly and serve your quick tomato puff pastry breadsticks .
Serve your quick tomato puff pastry breadsticks as soon as they're ready. Alternatively, you can store them away from moisture for up to a couple of days in a paper bag. Freezing is not recommended.
Replace the aromatic herbs with those you love the most; also try with rosemary and basil!
Use tomato paste in dishes where you want to enhance the flavor and the bright and vivid color of the tomato.
