name:
Baked Pasta with Gorgonzola Gratin
ingredients:
Cellentani pasta: 11.3oz(320 g)
Gorgonzola cheese: 10.5oz(300 g)
Walnut kernels: ½cup(50 g)
Whole milk: ¼cup(50 g)
Grana Padano PDO cheese: 1.8oz(50 g)- grated

info:
Difficulty: Very easy
Prep time: 10 min
Cook time: 10 min
Serving: 4
Cost: Very low

instructions:
To prepare the baked gorgonzola pasta, first cook the pasta al dente in a pot of boiling salted water. Take the gorgonzola, cut it into strips and then into chunks . In a pan, add the gorgonzola, pour in the milk to dilute the cheese and make it creamy and stir with a whisk .
Cook over moderate heat for 2 or 3 minutes, until you obtain a smooth cream. Once the pasta is ready, drain it and transfer it to the pan with the sauce , mixing everything well . Place the pasta in a 10x7 inch rectangular baking dish and sprinkle it with plenty of Grana .
Roughly chop the walnuts and distribute them over the pasta . Cook for 3-4 minutes in the oven in grill mode . Your baked gorgonzola pasta is ready to be served!
It is best to consume the baked gorgonzola pasta as soon as it is prepared, or you can store it in the refrigerator in an airtight container for a maximum of one day.
Freezing is not recommended.
To make the baked gorgonzola pasta even more tempting, try adding sautéed radicchio, or try the recipe for Baked pasta with speck and gorgonzola !
