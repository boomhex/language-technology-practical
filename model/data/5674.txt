name:
Soy Sauce Chicken
ingredients:
Chicken breast: 1.1lbs(500 g)
Soy sauce: Â¼cup(50 g)
Fresh ginger: 1tsp(5 g)
Wildflower honey: 4.3tsp(30 g)
Hot Paprika: to taste
Lime peel: 1
Lime juice: 1
Garlic: 1clove
Extra virgin olive oil: to taste
Chives: to taste

info:
Difficulty: Easy
Prep time: 20 min
Cook time: 10 min
Serving: 4
Cost: Low
Note + the marinating time in the refrigerator (2 hours)

instructions:
Soy sauce chicken is a main course with a bold character and an oriental flavor, perfect for bringing an exotic touch to the table without complications. It is a recipe that transforms a simple chicken breast into something special, thanks to the fragrant marinade and the glaze that forms during cooking due to the soy sauce and honey that caramelizes. Another great advantage of this preparation is its versatility: you can pair it with simple basmati rice to stay true to oriental atmospheres, or accompany it with stir-fried vegetables for a balanced one-dish meal. You can prepare it for a casual dinner with friends, but it also works great as a tasty alternative to the usual everyday main courses.

To prepare the soy sauce chicken, first cut the chicken breast into slices and then into chunks . Transfer them to a bowl and add the honey .
Pour the soy sauce into the bowl , add the spicy paprika and the garlic .
Add the zest and juice of the lime . Finally, grate the fresh ginger into the bowl .
Mix thoroughly , cover with plastic wrap , and let marinate in the refrigerator for at least 2 hours. Heat a little oil in a pan, then add the chicken with the marinade .
Cook for 10 minutes over high heat, stirring often. Taste, if necessary, you can add salt or more soy sauce . Serve and finish with chives . The soy sauce chicken is ready to be served !
Soy sauce chicken should be consumed immediately after preparation, but if you have leftovers, you can store them in the refrigerator, in a closed container, for 1-2 days.
You can customize the recipe by adding sesame seeds at the end of cooking for a crunchy touch; if you like it spicy, you can add fresh chili. You can replace the lime with lemon or orange for a slightly sweeter note.
