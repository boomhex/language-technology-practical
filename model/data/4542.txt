name:
Prawns in the microwave
ingredients:
Prawns: 12
Lemon juice: 2 ½tbsp(40 g)
Extra virgin olive oil: 4 ½tbsp(60 g)
Parsley: to taste
Salt: to taste
Black pepper: to taste

info:
Difficulty: Easy
Prep time: 20 min
Cook time: 11 min
Serving: 4
Cost: Very low

instructions:
Microwave prawns are an easy and tasty main course, but at the same time healthy and genuine. This recipe will allow you to serve a flavorful dish in no time. Microwave cooking is ideal for this kind of preparation, as it is quick and even, allowing to preserve the nutritional and organoleptic properties of the prawns. In this recipe, the delicacy of the shellfish combines perfectly with the aromatic freshness of the citronette, creating a dish capable of winning over even the most demanding palates.

To prepare the microwave prawns, first focus on the cleaning of the prawns . Using a pair of scissors, cut off the legs , remove the shell leaving the head and tail. Make a cut on the back and remove the intestine .
Wash the parsley and finely chop it with a knife . Squeeze a lemon to obtain the juice .
Prepare the citronette that will be used to season the prawns. Place the parsley in a bowl, add the lemon juice , the oil , and season with salt and pepper. Emulsify the sauce well and set aside.
Place the prawns in a baking dish, side by side , and season them with the citronette . Cook in the microwave at 650 W for 8 minutes, then continue cooking in grill mode for another 3 minutes. The microwave prawns are ready to be served.
It is advisable to consume the prawns hot, just out of the oven. If there are any leftovers, you can store them in the fridge, in a closed container, for one day.
You can customize the recipe according to your tastes by adding the spices you like or have available, for example, instead of the citronette, you can use paprika or pink pepper.
