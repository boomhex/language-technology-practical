name:
Muffins with feta and salmon
ingredients:
Feta cheese: 5.3oz(150 g)
Salmon: 5.3oz(150 g)
Whole milk: ⅓cup(100 g)
Eggs: 3- medium
Type 00 flour: 1 ½cup(200 g)
Grana Padano PDO cheese: 3.5oz(100 g)
Instant yeast for salted preparations: 1tsp(10 g)
Vegetable oil: ½cup(100 g)
Chives: 10fili
Fine salt: 2tsp(10 g)
Black pepper: to taste

info:
Difficulty: Very easy
Prep time: 20 min
Cook time: 30 min
Makes: 12 pieces
Cost: Low

To prepare the muffins with feta and salmon, start with the preparation of the salmon. Place the salmon on a cutting board and remove the skin using a knife . Now take tweezers and remove the salmon bones. Then cut the salmon into strips and then into cubes from these strips .
Now move on to preparing the feta. Take the feta and cut it into strips, then also into cubes . Now take a bowl and break three eggs into it, using whisks . Beat them and add the milk
and the vegetable oil until you get a homogeneous mixture. Now sift the flour and add it to the combined mixture of eggs, oil, and milk . Then add the baking powder .
Now incorporate the finely chopped chives and the grated Grana Padano , which will give your muffins a more decided flavor. Continue to mix the ingredients to make the mixture homogeneous. Adjust with salt and pepper .
Then add the feta and also the diced salmon . Continue mixing, gently combining all the ingredients .
Now take some muffin liners. Transfer the obtained mixture into a pastry bag and fill the liners almost to the edge . Bake your muffins with feta and salmon in a preheated oven at 350°F for 25-30 minutes and... enjoy your meal!
Muffins with feta and salmon can be stored in the refrigerator, wrapped in plastic wrap for 2-3 days. If you have used only fresh ingredients, you can freeze them.
If the taste of chives is too strong for you, you can use any herbs you like.
