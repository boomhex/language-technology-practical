name:
Savory plumcake
ingredients:
Type 00 flour: 1.6cups(200 g)
Eggs: 3
Smoked pancetta: 5.3oz(150 g)- diced
Fresh liquid cream: ⅓cup(100 ml)
Whole milk: ⅓cup(100 ml)
Extra virgin olive oil: 4tbsp(60 ml)
Fine salt: to taste
Black pepper: to taste
Instant yeast for salted preparations: 1bag
Grana Padano PDO cheese: 3.5oz(100 g)- to grate
Gruyere cheese: 3.5oz(100 g)

info:
Difficulty: Easy
Prep time: 15 min
Cook time: 45 min
Serving: 6
Cost: Low

instructions:
To prepare the savory plumcake, start by sifting the flour and baking powder into a bowl , then add the finely grated Grana Padano cheese . Mix the ingredients well and incorporate the grated Leerdammer cheese
and the diced smoked bacon , mix everything together. Then add the extra virgin olive oil in a thin stream . In another bowl, beat the eggs with the cream and milk ,
salt and pepper , and add the liquid thus obtained to the flour, cheese, and bacon mixture. Stir with a wooden spoon until the ingredients are well combined . Butter and flour a loaf pan with a one-quart capacity and pour the mixture into it. Level it with the back of a spoon and bake in a preheated oven at 350°F for 45/50 minutes, performing the toothpick test to check its doneness. When the savory plumcake is cooked, wait until it is lukewarm before removing it from the mold, then let it cool completely on a rack. Serve your savory plumcake in slices!
You can enrich your savory plumcake with a pinch of chives or with your preferred finely chopped aromatic herbs!
