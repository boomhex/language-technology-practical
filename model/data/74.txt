name:
Brown rice with black cabbage and savoy cabbage
ingredients:
Whole rice: 1 ¼cup(240 g)
Italian kale (cavolo nero): 1.1lbs(500 g)- (to clean)
Verza (savoy cabbage): 1.1lbs(500 g)- (to be cleaned)
Carrots: 1
White onions: ½
Celery: ½rib
Garlic: 1clove
Pecorino cheese: 4tsp- without rennet
White wine: 4tsp
Extra virgin olive oil: 4spoonfuls
Fine salt: to taste
Black pepper: to taste

To prepare brown rice with black cabbage and savoy cabbage, first wash all the vegetables, then finely chop the garlic, carrot , celery , and onion .
Cut the black cabbage and savoy cabbage into strips.
In a non-stick pan, place the chopped carrots, garlic, onion, and celery , add 2 tablespoons of oil and 2 teaspoons of white wine , then stew over medium heat for about 5 minutes. Meanwhile, heat some lightly salted water in a pot.
Add the brown rice and stir to let it absorb the flavors , then deglaze with the remaining white wine .
When all the alcohol has evaporated, add the black cabbage and savoy cabbage , then cover with hot water .
Cover with a lid and cook over low heat for about 40 minutes or until the rice is cooked. With the heat off, stir in the remaining oil and the pecorino cheese .
Finish with a sprinkle of pepper and mix well . Your brown rice with black cabbage and savoy cabbage is ready to be served !
Brown rice with black cabbage and savoy cabbage can be stored in the refrigerator, in a closed container, for up to 2 days.
If you want to make the dish richer and more complete, you can add legumes such as chickpeas or cannellini beans.
Instead of brown rice, you can use farro, barley, or quinoa, adjusting the cooking times according to the chosen grain.
