name:
Penne alla Maitó
ingredients:
Smooth penne pasta: 12.7oz(360 g)
Tomato purée: 3 ¼cups(800 g)
Parmigiano Reggiano PDO cheese: 8.5oz(240 g)- (24 months aged)
Butter: 1cup(240 g)- (from Normandy, very cold)
Basil: 1cup(20 g)- fresh
Fine salt: to taste
Black pepper: to taste

info:
Difficulty: Easy
Prep time: 10 min
Cook time: 50 min
Serving: 4
Cost: Average

instructions:
To prepare penne alla Maitó, start by pouring the tomato purée into a large skillet (preferably stainless steel). Salt and pepper to taste. Let it cook gently for 20 minutes.
Meanwhile, take the cold butter out of the fridge and cut it into cubes . Add it to the sauce .
Stir continuously to melt it completely . Let the sauce cook on low heat for another 30 minutes: it should be smooth and silky. Toward the end of cooking, boil the penne for about 8 minutes (or for the time indicated on the package for al dente cooking) . Also grate the 24-month aged Parmigiano Reggiano PDO .
Drain the pasta directly into the sauce , add a ladle of cooking water, and stir , then turn off the heat. Season with some torn fresh basil and stir again.
Stir in part of the Parmigiano Reggiano PDO , mix , and repeat the operation several times so it blends better .
Plate , add plenty of sauce to each serving plate , and finish with fresh basil leaves . Penne alla Maitó are ready to be enjoyed!
It is recommended to consume penne alla Maitó immediately after preparation.

For a perfect result, we recommend using top-quality ingredients and very cold butter.
