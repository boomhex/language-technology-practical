name:
Venere Rice Salad with Vegetables and Shrimp
ingredients:
Black Venus rice: 1.6cups(320 g)
Shrimps: 16
Yellow peppers: 2
Red peppers: 2
Cluster tomatoes: 2
Zucchini: 2
Marjoram: to taste
Extra virgin olive oil: to taste
Salt: to taste
Black pepper: to taste

info:
Difficulty: Easy
Prep time: 20 min
Cook time: 30 min
Serving: 4
Cost: Low

instructions:
The Venere rice salad with vegetables and shrimp is a fresh, easy, and tasty first course. The Venere rice, with its distinctive color and unique taste, is the undisputed star of this recipe, and it pairs perfectly with the crunchiness of the vegetables and the delicacy of the shrimp. This rice salad is perfect for those who love to experiment in the kitchen, for those who want to impress their guests with something new and special, and for those who want to enjoy a nutritious meal without giving up lightness. Even if you don't have much experience in the kitchen, don't worry, this delicious version of Black Venus rice with vegetables is easy to make. By following our step-by-step instructions, you won't encounter any major difficulties!

To prepare the Venere rice salad with vegetables and shrimp, first cook the Venere rice in boiling salted water . Meanwhile, cut the yellow peppers, removing the stem, seeds, and inner filaments . Then cut them into strips, and then into small cubes .
Do the same with the red peppers . Cut the tomatoes in half and remove the seeds and pulp , then dice them .
Trim the ends of the zucchini , slice them , and then dice them .
Now focus on cleaning the shrimp. Remove the head, tail, and shell , remove the intestine , and cut the flesh into pieces .
Pour a drizzle of oil into a pan , add the shrimp , sear them for 1-2 minutes , and set aside.
In the same pan, add the peppers and zucchini . Season with salt and pepper and sauté.
Drain the rice and place it in a large bowl. Let it cool completely. Then add the sautéed vegetables and the tomatoes .
Add the shrimp , fresh marjoram , and a drizzle of oil .
Mix everything together and plate. Finish with a few more marjoram leaves . The Venere rice salad with vegetables and shrimp is ready to be served .
The Venere rice salad with vegetables and shrimp can be stored in the fridge, in a closed container, for up to one day.
Instead of marjoram, you can use freshly chopped parsley. You can add grated lemon zest to give a fresher touch and a citrus aroma to the dish. If you want a more complex flavor, you can add a splash of balsamic vinegar, which will give the dish a sweet and sour note.
