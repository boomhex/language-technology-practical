name:
Quick Calandraca
ingredients:
Veal: 1.3lbs(600 g)- (meat)
Colonnata lard: 3.5oz(100 g)- thinly sliced
Potatoes: 1.1lbs(500 g)
Tomato purée: 1 ¼cup(300 g)
Fine salt: to taste
Black pepper: to taste
Extra virgin olive oil: 3spoonfuls
Water: 1.67cups(400 g)

info:
Difficulty: Easy
Prep time: 30 min
Cook time: 40 min
Serving: 4
Cost: Average

To prepare the calandraca, cut the veal into slices , using a sharp knife. Then divide each slice into chunks that are not too small . In a saucepan, pour the oil and add the veal chunks .
Brown over high heat for about 5 minutes , stirring often with a wooden spoon. Meanwhile, cut the slices of bacon into strips and when the meat is well browned, add the bacon to the pan.
Turn the meat chunks and cook until the bacon is slightly melted . Meanwhile, wash and peel the potatoes , then cut them into thick slices
and from these, make wedges . Add the potatoes to the meat , mix everything and pour in the water . You will need about 12-14 oz.
Season with salt and pepper , mix and continue cooking for about 10 minutes.
Add the tomato sauce and continue to cook over medium heat, stirring occasionally until the potatoes and veal are tender . Serve the calandraca hot.
Calandraca can be stored in the refrigerator for 3 days in an airtight container. It can be frozen.
Add chili and aromatic herbs for a richer taste!
