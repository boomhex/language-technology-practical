name:
Casarecce with pumpkin, walnuts, and burrata
ingredients:
Casarecce pasta: 11.3oz(320 g)
Delica pumpkin: 0.7lb(300 g)
Powdered turmeric: 1 ½tsp(5 g)
Walnut kernels: 2.5oz(70 g)
Fine salt: to taste
Black pepper: to taste
Thyme: to taste
Burrata cheese: 4.5oz(125 g)
Water: ½cup(125 g)
Garlic: 1clove
Extra virgin olive oil: to taste

info:
Difficulty: Easy
Prep time: 20 min
Cook time: 25 min
Serving: 4

To prepare the casarecce with pumpkin, walnuts, and burrata, remove the pumpkin's skin and seeds , then cut it into irregular pieces about 3/4 inch .
Pour a drizzle of oil into a pan and add the garlic clove . Let it sauté for a couple of minutes, then add the pumpkin . Cook over high heat for 5 minutes, stirring occasionally, then add 1/2 cup of water .
Salt to taste and cover with a lid: cooking continues at a low heat for about 10 more minutes . Halfway through cooking, you can season with turmeric powder . Once cooked, remove the garlic .
Stir , add fresh thyme and a drizzle of raw oil , then adjust with pepper, turn off the heat, and keep warm.
Coarsely chop the walnut kernels . Meanwhile, boil the casarecce in salted boiling water , draining them directly into the pan with the pumpkin a couple of minutes before the pasta finishes cooking. Add a little cooking water , without turning the heat back on.
Mix well, add the walnut kernels , and stir again. Serve with the addition of fresh burrata on each serving plate , a drizzle of raw extra virgin olive oil, and a few fresh thyme leaves .
You can replace the walnuts with hazelnuts, to be toasted in a pan without additional fats.
It is recommended to consume the casarecce as soon as they are ready!
