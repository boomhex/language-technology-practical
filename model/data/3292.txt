name:
Irish bruschetta
ingredients:
Smoked Salmon: 12.7oz(360 g)
Arugula: 2cups(50 g)
Salted capers: 1.8oz(50 g)- desalted
Coarse salt: to taste
Lemons: 1
Black bread with grains: 0.9lb(400 g)
Butter: 4tbsp(60 g)

info:
Difficulty: Very easy
Prep time: 15 min
Serving: 4
Cost: Average

instructions:

To prepare the Irish bruschettas, take the loaf of multigrain brown bread and slice it about 0.4 inches thick; place them on the cutting board, cut them in half , and spread a layer of butter (about 1 tsp) on them using a knife . Repeat this step for all the bread slices .
Next, take the salmon and cut it into slices: each slice should weigh around 1 oz and be the same size as the bread slice . Then, place each salmon slice on the bread and add a few arugula leaves (about 1 tbsp per slice) on each bruschetta .
On each slice, add a teaspoon of capers (about 1 tsp) , finally season with a pinch of coarse salt and a squeeze of lemon . There you go, your Irish bruschettas are ready to be enjoyed!
The Irish bruschettas can be stored for up to 2 days in an airtight container.
If you can't find multigrain brown bread, you can prepare the Irish bruschettas with whole wheat bread. To prepare the bruschetta, we recommend using, if you can find it, Irish smoked salmon: the flavor will be greatly enhanced, and it will feel like you are truly in Ireland!
