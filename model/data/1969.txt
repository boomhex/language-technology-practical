name:
Mini penne with asparagus tips and spinach cream
ingredients:
Mini Penne Rigate pasta: 2cups(190 g)
Asparagus: 7oz(200 g)
Spinach: 7cups(200 g)
Basil: 20tsp(10 g)
Scamorza (provola) cheese: 1.1oz(30 g)
Extra virgin olive oil: to taste
Fine salt: to taste
Black pepper: to taste

info:
Difficulty: Easy
Prep time: 15 min
Cook time: 15 min
Serving: 4
Cost: Average

To prepare mini penne with asparagus tips and spinach cream, start by cleaning the asparagus. First, remove the end of the stalk , then set aside the tips and cut the rest into slices . Take the tips and cut them in half .
Set aside the asparagus for a moment and start cooking the spinach. Pour a drizzle of oil into a pan, add the spinach , cover with a lid , and let cook over low heat for 3-4 minutes, until wilted . At this point, place a pot filled with salted water on the stove, which will be used to cook the pasta.
Then pour a drizzle of oil into a pan , add the asparagus slices , and let them sauté over low heat for about 5 minutes .
In another pan, sauté the asparagus tips for about 6-7 minutes until they are nicely golden. Meanwhile, take the scamorza cheese and cut it into rough pieces .
As soon as the water starts to boil, cook your mini penne , following the cooking times indicated on the package. Transfer the still warm spinach into a blender cup , add the basil leaves ,
a ladle of cooking water and blend everything with an immersion blender until you get a smooth and velvety cream . Taste and adjust the salt if necessary.
As soon as the pasta is al dente, drain it, keeping some cooking water aside, and transfer it directly into the pan with the asparagus slices ; adjust with pepper , add a ladle of cooking water to bind everything and sauté for a few moments. If you prefer, you can add a drizzle of oil at this stage.
Lastly, add the pieces of provolone cheese and continue to cook until it is completely melted . Place some spinach cream on each plate, add the pasta, and decorate with the asparagus tips. Your mini penne with asparagus tips and spinach cream are ready to be enjoyed .
It is recommended to consume the mini penne with asparagus tips and spinach cream immediately. Alternatively, they can be stored in an airtight container for up to 1 day.
If a bit of pasta is left over, no worries! You can gratin it in the oven by adding some Parmesan and other cheeses to taste! If you prefer, you can replace the spinach with another seasonal vegetable.
