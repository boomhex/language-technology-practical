name:
Warm rice salad with mushrooms
ingredients:
Roma rice: 1 ½cup(320 g)
Chanterelle mushrooms (chanterelle): 3 ½cups(400 g)
Leeks: 1
Spinach: 10cups(300 g)
Pumpkin seeds: to taste
Flax seed: to taste
Sesame seeds: to taste
Fine salt: to taste
Extra virgin olive oil: to taste

To prepare the warm rice salad with mushrooms, first boil the rice in plenty of salted boiling water, cooking it one minute less than the instructions on the package indicate . Drain it and let it cool slightly. Clean the leeks by cutting the base, removing the green leaves, and the first layer of skin .
Slice them thinly . Sauté the leeks in a hot pan greased with oil for about 5 minutes , then add the spinach , season with salt, and continue cooking for another two minutes.
Turn off the heat and set aside . Clean the mushrooms from the soil with damp paper , cut the larger ones in half .
Try to get mushrooms of the same size . Cook the mushrooms in a hot pan greased with oil for about 5 minutes , season with salt and set aside .
Combine in a bowl the rice, mushrooms , spinach, and leeks, adding pumpkin seeds , flaxseeds, and black sesame seeds .
Dress with a little oil , mix , and serve the warm rice salad with mushrooms .
Store in the refrigerator for up to two days in an airtight container.
We do not recommend freezing.
Do not throw away the green leek leaves, you can use them to make a broth. Instead of the proposed seed mix, you can also use other types of seeds or nuts, such as walnuts and hazelnuts.
