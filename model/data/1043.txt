name:
Flatbread with Taleggio and Caramelized Onions
ingredients:
Whole wheat flatbreads: 4
Taleggio cheese: 12.5oz(350 g)
White onions: 2lbs(900 g)
Brown sugar: 3tbsp(40 g)
Balsamic vinegar: 3 ½tbsp(50 ml)
Water: ⅔cup(150 g)
Fine salt: to taste
Extra virgin olive oil: to taste

info:
Difficulty: Easy
Prep time: 15 min
Cook time: 1 h 10 min
Serving: 4

instructions:

To make the flatbread with Taleggio and caramelized onions, first clean the onions, then slice them to a thickness of about 1/8 inch . In a pan, heat the onions with a drizzle of oil .
Add water , brown sugar , and adjust with salt .
Stir, cover with a lid , and cook for about 20 minutes on low heat, stirring occasionally. After this time, uncover and continue cooking for another 45 minutes on low heat, stirring frequently. When the onions are nicely softened, pour in the balsamic vinegar , let it evaporate, and then turn off the heat.
Cut the Taleggio into slices about 1/5 inch thick . Heat the whole-grain flatbread in a pan for about 30 seconds , flip the flatbread to heat it for another 30 seconds.
Place the slices of Taleggio on just one half , cover with the lid , and let the Taleggio melt over low heat .
Place the caramelized onions on the melted cheese and close the flatbread by folding it in half . Enjoy the flatbread with Taleggio and caramelized onions while hot .
We recommend consuming the flatbread with Taleggio and caramelized onions immediately. You can prepare the caramelized onions in advance and store them in the fridge for a couple of days, then reheat them as needed.
Brown sugar gives a more aromatic taste, but if you don't have it, you can replace it with the same amount of granulated sugar.
