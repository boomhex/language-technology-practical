name:
Zucchini and Cherry Tomatoes Pasta
ingredients:
Bow tie pasta: 11.3oz(320 g)
Zucchini: 2 ½cups(300 g)
Datterino tomatoes: 1 ¼cup(240 g)
Pecorino cheese: 1.1oz(30 g)- grated
Basil: to taste
Extra virgin olive oil: to taste
Fine salt: to taste
Black pepper: to taste

info:
Difficulty: Very easy
Prep time: 10 min
Cook time: 15 min
Serving: 4
Cost: Low

instructions:
To prepare the zucchini and cherry tomatoes pasta, first wash the vegetables well. Trim the zucchini and cut them first into slices and then into cubes about 1 centimeter (0.39 inches) large .
Also wash the cherry tomatoes and cut them in half . In a large pan, pour a generous swirl of oil . If you prefer, you can also add a clove of garlic or sliced onion. Then add the zucchini and sauté them for 5-7 minutes.
Add the cherry tomatoes , stir , and continue cooking for another 5-6 minutes. Meanwhile, also cook the pasta in plenty of boiling water, salted to taste .
When the cherry tomatoes are soft, salt , pepper, and scent with hand-torn basil . Then also add the farfalle, drained al dente .
Add a ladle of cooking water , the grated Pecorino cheese , and sauté everything .
Plate and garnish with a sprinkle of black pepper and basil leaves . Your zucchini and cherry tomatoes pasta is ready to serve !
The zucchini and cherry tomatoes pasta can be stored in the refrigerator for a maximum of 1-2 days.
Instead of Pecorino, you can use grated Grana Padano DOP. If you prefer, you can also add a chili pepper to the sauté.
