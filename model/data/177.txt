name:
Hummus without tahini
ingredients:
Precooked chickpeas: 2 ½cups(500 g)- (drained weight)
Garlic: 1clove
Lemon juice: 6.7tbsp(100 g)
Extra virgin olive oil: 5tbsp(70 g)
Water: ⅓cup(75 g)
Fine salt: to taste
Parsley: to taste
Sweet Paprika: to taste
Lemon peel: to taste
Extra virgin olive oil: to taste
Black pepper: to taste

info:
Difficulty: Very easy
Prep time: 5 min
Serving: 8
Cost: Low

instructions:
To prepare the hummus without tahini, place the drained pre-cooked chickpeas in a blender , then add the lemon juice and grate one clove of garlic .
Add the extra virgin olive oil , salt , and water .
Close the blender and blend until you get a creamy consistency . If it still seems lumpy, add a little water at a time. Now focus on the decoration: finely chop the parsley .
Transfer the obtained cream into a bowl and garnish with the chopped parsley , sweet paprika , and a grind of black pepper .
Season with lemon zest and finish with a drizzle of oil . Your hummus without tahini is ready to be served !
The hummus without tahini can be stored in the refrigerator for 2 days in an airtight container.
For an even creamier consistency, you can add a tablespoon of Greek yogurt.
