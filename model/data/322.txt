name:
Farfalle with Smoked Salmon
ingredients:
Bow tie pasta: 11.3oz(320 g)
Smoked Salmon: 7.1oz(200 g)
Fresh liquid cream: ¾cup(200 g)
Shallot: ½
Tomato paste: 1spoonful
White wine: ½glass
Butter: 1 ½tbsp(20 g)
Salt: to taste
Black pepper: to taste

info:
Difficulty: Very easy
Prep time: 10 min
Cook time: 15 min
Serving: 4
Cost: Average

instructions:
To prepare the farfalle with smoked salmon, first finely chop the smoked salmon with a knife , then clean and finely chop the shallot . In a pan, melt the butter .
Add the shallot and stew it for a few minutes, then deglaze with white wine . When all the alcohol has evaporated, add the salmon and cook for 2 minutes.
Add the cream , then season with pepper and the tomato paste .
Mix to combine the ingredients and cook for a few more minutes over low heat . In the meantime, cook the farfalle in plenty of salted water . Drain the pasta al dente and transfer it to the pan .
Briefly sauté everything . Plate the dish and finish with a grind of pepper . Your farfalle with smoked salmon is ready to be served !
It is recommended to consume the farfalle with smoked salmon immediately; if there are leftovers, you can store them in the refrigerator for 1-2 days in an airtight container.
If you love spiciness, you can add sliced chili peppers to the shallot.
For a touch of freshness, you can garnish with chopped chives at the end!
