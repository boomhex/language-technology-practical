name:
Roasted potatoes with apples, oranges and Grana Padano
ingredients:
Potatoes: 8- medium-large
Red cabbage: 6.3oz(180 g)
Apples: 4.2oz(120 g)- (red and yellow)
Oranges: 1
Sichuan peppers: to taste
Fine salt: to taste
Rosemary: to taste
Oregano: to taste- fresh
Grana Padano PDO cheese: 0.7oz(20 g)- (Reserve over 20 months) grated
Grana Padano PDO cheese: 1.8oz(50 g)- (Reserve over 20 months) in flakes

info:
Difficulty: Very easy
Prep time: 30 min
Cook time: 60 min
Makes: 8 pieces

instructions:

To prepare the roasted potatoes with apples, oranges, and Grana Padano, start by boiling the potatoes for about 30 minutes . Once cooked, without peeling them, remove the top , hollow them out with a teaspoon or a melon baller , leaving about 0.4 inches of edge.
Once hollowed, place them on a baking tray lined with parchment paper and sprinkle the inside base with grated Grana Padano PDO . Drizzle with a little olive oil and bake in a preheated static oven for 25 min at 392°F. Then grill them for another 5 minutes. Meanwhile, prepare the filling: chop the potato pulp .
Thinly slice the cabbage , clean and thinly slice the shallot as well , and sauté it for 2-3 minutes in a pan with a dash of olive oil. Then add the cabbage .
Add half of the potato pulp . Mix , salt to taste, and also season with oregano and rosemary. Cook for about 10 minutes, stirring frequently. Prepare the rest of the dressing by cutting the apples first into thin slices and then into smaller pieces.
Peel the orange , divide it into segments , and cut them in half .
Add the orange to the apples in a bowl , also pour the warmed cabbage, the remaining potato pulp, and season with freshly ground Sichuan pepper. Mix well . Shave thin slices of Grana Padano PDO
and add them to the rest of the dressing . Fill the potato shells with the salad and decorate with fresh oregano and rosemary to taste. Your roasted potatoes with apples, oranges, and Grana Padano are ready to be enjoyed .
It is recommended to consume immediately.
In the refrigerator, they can be stored for a day in an airtight container.
You can replace the apple with a pear, and the cabbage with escarole slightly sautéed in a pan.
