name:
Hazelnut Muffins
ingredients:
Type 00 flour: 1cup(200 g)
Nut flour: 0.875oz(25 g)
Brown sugar: 0.6cup(130 g)
Butter: 0.67cup(150 g)- melted
Eggs: 5.8oz(165 g)- (about 3) at room temperature
Whole milk: ½cup(120 g)- at room temperature
Chopped hazelnuts: 0.83cup(120 g)
Baking powder: 2 ½tsp(10 g)
Fine salt: 1pinch
Chopped hazelnuts: to taste
Hazelnuts: to taste

info:
Difficulty: Easy
Prep time: 10 min
Cook time: 35 min
Makes: 12 pieces
Cost: Low

instructions:
To prepare the hazelnut muffins, first, pour the eggs into a bowl . Begin to whisk them with an electric mixer while adding the brown sugar . Continue beating until they are fluffy and frothy .
Next, pour in the melted butter , add the salt , and mix again with the beaters .
Sift the all-purpose flour and baking powder directly into the bowl. Incorporate them with the other ingredients using the electric beaters .
When they are completely absorbed , pour in part of the milk , mix, and add the hazelnut flour .
Pour in the remaining milk and mix again with the beaters. Then add the chopped hazelnuts and mix with a spatula .
Transfer the mixture into a pastry bag and use it to fill 12 muffin cups . Decorate with the chopped hazelnuts ,
and with whole hazelnuts. Bake the muffins in a preheated static oven at 350°F for 35 minutes on the middle rack. Then remove from oven , let cool slightly, and serve your hazelnut muffins !
Store the hazelnut muffins for 2 days under a glass dome. They can be frozen after baking.
It is possible to add dark chocolate chips or blueberries to the batter.
