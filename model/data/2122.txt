name:
Grilled cheese sandwich
ingredients:
White bread loaf: 8.5oz(240 g)- (8 slices)
Cheddar cheese: 5.6oz(160 g)- white paste
Butter: to taste- at room temperature

info:
Difficulty: Very easy
Prep time: 5 min
Cook time: 8 min
Makes: 4 pieces
Cost: Low

instructions:

To prepare the grilled cheese sandwich, first slice the sandwich bread, you should get 8 slices about 0.6 inches (1.5 cm) thick . Then butter all 8 slices on only one side . With a large-hole grater, grate the cheddar cheese .
and generously distribute it on 4 slices, sprinkling it on the unbuttered side . Close with the other slice of bread, with the buttered side facing out : this way, all 4 sandwiches will have cheese inside and the buttered part on the outside. Let a non-stick pan heat well over moderate heat and place your sandwich in it .
Cover with a lid and wait a couple of minutes. Flip the sandwich, press it slightly to aid the cheese melting; cover again and cook for another minute.
When the cheese is completely melted, your grilled cheese sandwich will be ready. Transfer it to a cutting board and cut it in half, creating 2 triangles . Now you just have to enjoy the grilled cheese sandwich while it is hot and gooey !
It is recommended to enjoy the grilled cheese sandwich immediately; any form of storage is not recommended.
Use the cheese you love most: Emmental or Caciocavallo will work perfectly! We used a lightly aged cheddar, about 8-10 months, which has a lighter color than the classic one and a milder flavor.
