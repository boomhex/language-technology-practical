name:
Maccheroncini al pettine with culatello and parmesan cream
ingredients:
Maccheroncini al pettine pasta: 14.1oz(400 g)
Culatello ham: 7oz(200 g)
Butter: 1oz(30 g)
Sage: 15leaves
Whole milk: 2.1cups(500 ml)
Butter: 2tbsp(30 g)
Type 00 flour: ¼cup(30 g)
Parmigiano Reggiano PDO cheese: 1.75oz(50 g)- to grate

info:
Difficulty: Easy
Prep time: 20 min
Cook time: 30 min
Serving: 4
Cost: Average

instructions:
To prepare Maccheroncini al Pettine with culatello and Parmesan cream, start with the Parmigiano Cream . In a saucepan, melt the butter and once melted, add the flour gradually , stirring with a whisk to obtain a well-blended roux. At this point, add the milk , which you have heated in a saucepan,
and mix the entire mixture, stirring well with the whisk . Continue stirring and cook the mixture over low heat until it thickens. Remove the saucepan from the heat and gradually add the grated Parmigiano Reggiano . Mix the Parmesan well into the béchamel sauce and set it aside.
Cut the culatello into strips and prepare a pan where you will flavor 2 tablespoons of butter with well-cleaned sage leaves . Add the culatello strips to the pan, reserving some for the final decoration of the dishes, and sauté for 10 minutes over low heat .
Cook the maccheroncini al pettine in boiling salted water for 7 minutes , drain them, and add them to the pan with the culatello and sage . Finally, add the Parmesan cream and mix all the ingredients well. Serve the maccheroncini al pettine with culatello and Parmesan cream hot, decorating the dishes with some strips of culatello.
