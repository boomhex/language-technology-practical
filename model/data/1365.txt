name:
Green tea and chocolate cake
ingredients:
Almond flour: 1cup(100 g)
Butter: ½cup(100 g)- soft
Eggs: 4oz(110 g)- at room temperature (about 2 medium)
Brown sugar: 6tbsp(70 g)
Fine salt: 0.375tsp(2 g)
Fresh liquid cream: 6.1tbsp(90 g)
Green Tea: 0.2oz(6 g)- in leaves
Rice flour: ⅓cup(50 g)
Baking powder: 0.7tsp(3 g)
Dark chocolate 55%: 4.25oz(120 g)
Fresh liquid cream: ½cup(120 g)
White chocolate: 5.25oz(150 g)
Fresh liquid cream: 1.875cups(450 g)
Green Tea: 6tsp(12 g)
Gelatin in sheets: 0.3oz(8 g)
Almonds: 0.35cup(50 g)
Sugar: ¼cup(50 g)
Water: 0.7oz(20 g)

info:
Difficulty: Very difficult
Prep time: 60 min
Cook time: 30 min
Serving: 10
Cost: High
Note + 2 and a half hours of cooling

To make the green tea and chocolate cake, first pour the cream and the tea into a small saucepan . Bring to a boil , remove from heat, and let it cool slightly. In a bowl, pour the butter and sugar .
Work with the electric beaters for a couple of minutes . Add the eggs and mix until fully absorbed, obtaining a smooth cream. Add the almond flour ,
then strain the cream and tea mixture through a sieve and add it to the mixture . Work with the beaters until you obtain a smooth batter and add the rice flour and baking powder . Work for about a minute.
The mixture should be well combined . Line a baking tray with parchment paper and place a 9-inch pastry ring with the edges lined with parchment paper on top. Pour the batter, leveling the surface with a spatula . Bake in a preheated static oven at 350°F for 25 minutes. Meanwhile, prepare the ganache. Chop the dark chocolate .
Bring the cream to a boil and pour it over the chocolate in two additions . Mix well with the spatula until you get a smooth cream .
Remove the cake from the oven and after letting it cool for 5 minutes, pour the ganache on top. Spread well over the entire surface to the edges . Let it cool completely in the refrigerator for about 1 hour. Now prepare the mousse. Finely chop the white chocolate .
Soak the gelatin sheets in cold water . Simmer 0.4 cups of cream and the tea in a small saucepan .
Strain the cream directly onto the chopped white chocolate and stir. Heat 0.4 cups of cream in the saucepan, add the squeezed gelatin and stir to dissolve. Pour the cream with gelatin over the white chocolate and mix to obtain a smooth cream.
Blend the cream with an immersion blender . Whip the remaining 1 cup of cream with the beaters and fold it into the mixture gently with a rubber spatula.
You should obtain a smooth and uniform mousse . Pour the mousse over the now cold cake and let it rest for at least an hour and a half in the refrigerator .
For the decoration: pour the sugar and water into a pan and when the sugar is completely dissolved, add the almonds . Stir with a wooden spoon until the water has completely evaporated and the sugar has crystallized . Transfer the praline almonds to a container to cool.
Take the now cold cake , remove the ring and transfer it to a serving plate. Finally, decorate the green tea and chocolate cake with the praline almonds .
The green tea and chocolate cake keeps in the refrigerator for 3 days. Freezing is not recommended.
You can substitute green tea with a more robust flavored tea for a more aromatic cake. If you do not have a pastry ring, you can use a springform pan.
