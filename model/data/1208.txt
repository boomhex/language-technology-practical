name:
Artichoke Rösti and Fried Eggs
ingredients:
Purple artichokes: 2- (about 260 g when cleaned)
Eggs: 2
Potato starch: 1 ½tbsp(20 g)
Grana Padano PDO cheese: 2.5oz(70 g)
Fine salt: to taste
Black pepper: to taste
Thyme: to taste
Eggs: 6
Extra virgin olive oil: to taste
Lemon peel: to taste- (optional)

info:
Difficulty: Easy
Prep time: 30 min
Cook time: 10 min
Serving: 6
Cost: Average

instructions:
To prepare the artichoke rösti with fried eggs, start with the How to clean artichokes . Remove the outer leaves until you reach the softer ones then peel the stem and the base and cut off the tips .
Cut in half and if there is any choke, remove it with a small knife . Slice thinly and set aside for a moment . In a bowl, combine the eggs, grated cheese ,
potato starch , thyme leaves, salt and pepper . Mix everything well and then add the artichoke strips .
Mix again and proceed to cooking . In a pan, heat a little oil and then pour a portion of the mixture, you should get a pancake about 5 inches in diameter . Let it brown for 2-3 minutes on one side, then flip and continue cooking for the same time .
Once the first one is ready, drain it on absorbent paper and continue cooking all the others . When all the pancakes are ready, arrange them on the serving plate, keep warm and proceed to cook the Eggs al tegamino . Heat a drizzle of oil in a pan and crack the first egg . Let it cook for a few minutes and then season with salt, pepper, and a few thyme leaves .
Transfer the first fried egg onto one of the rösti and continue with all the others. Finally, grate some lemon zest on the surface of each egg and your artichoke rösti with fried eggs are ready: enjoy your meal !
It is not recommended to store artichoke rösti and fried eggs.
If you want to add an exceptional touch of flavor, add a pinch of smoked paprika or cumin to the batter of your artichoke rösti with fried eggs!
