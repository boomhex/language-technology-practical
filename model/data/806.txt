name:
Easy-to-make white chocolate fondant
ingredients:
Soluble cappuccino: 8tsp
Water: 0.8cup(200 g)
Ice: 0.9cup(200 g)
White chocolate: 2.8oz(80 g)- melted
White chocolate: to taste- in flakes
Ice: 24- cubes
Powdered coffee: to taste
Fresh liquid cream: 0.4cup(100 g)

info:
Difficulty: Easy
Prep time: 10 min
Cook time: 2 min
Serving: 4

instructions:
To make the white chocolate drink, heat the water in a small pot until it reaches a temperature of 185Â°F . Once the temperature is reached, pour the instant cappuccino into the water and mix well. Wait about 20 seconds, mix again, and let it cool slightly .
Pour the ice cubes , the lukewarm cappuccino , and the melted chocolate , melted in a bain-marie, into the blender glass. Turn on the blender and blend the mixture for about 60 seconds.
Set aside and in the meantime whip the cream with the electric whisk until it is fluffy .
Transfer the whipped cream into a piping bag with a star tip. Assemble your white chocolate drink: in a glass, pour 5 ice cubes, the blended mixture , and garnish with the cream .
Sprinkle with coffee powder and decorate with white chocolate flakes . The white chocolate drink is ready to be enjoyed .
We recommend consuming the white chocolate drink immediately.
If you love spicy notes, you can flavor the white chocolate drink with ground cinnamon.
