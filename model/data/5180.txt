name:
Puff Pastry Mini Pizzas
ingredients:
Puff pastry: 8.1oz(230 g)- (1 round roll)
Tomato purée: ½cup(100 g)
Oregano: to taste
Extra virgin olive oil: to taste

info:
Difficulty: Very easy
Prep time: 10 min
Cook time: 20 min
Makes: 14 pieces
Cost: Low

instructions:
For a birthday party or an appetizer, snacks and mini pizzas are always a must... if time is short, the solution is puff pastry mini pizzas! An appetizer with ready-made puff pastry, easy and quick to make even at the last minute. We offer you the classic version: only two ingredients, tomato and oregano, a basic recipe that you can personalize by adding your favorite ingredients. Perfect for whetting the appetite even on the most important holidays, such as Christmas and New Year's, these puff pastry mini pizzas will delight your guests!
Try also these delicious mini pizza variants:

To make the puff pastry mini pizzas, roll out the puff pastry sheet on the work surface and cut it out with a 2.75-inch cookie cutter : you will get about 14 discs . Transfer the discs to a baking tray lined with parchment paper, spacing them a few inches apart
Prick the puff pastry with a fork . Spread a tablespoon of tomato sauce on each disc , then season with a dash of oil .
Add a pinch of oregano and bake in a preheated static oven at 400°F for about 20 minutes, on the middle shelf . When they are nicely golden, take the puff pastry mini pizzas out of the oven and let them cool slightly before serving!
The puff pastry mini pizzas can be stored in the refrigerator for 2 days, in an airtight container. Before serving, warm them up in the oven at 300°F for a few minutes.
If you prefer a more golden result, you can brush the puff pastry circles with beaten egg before seasoning and baking them.
You can enrich the puff pastry mini pizzas with your favorite ingredients, for example, olives, capers, tuna, anchovies, mozzarella, or diced ham.
