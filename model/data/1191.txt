name:
Aglianico Stew
ingredients:
Pork: 3.3lbs(1.5 kg)- (fish-bell cut)
Red wine: 5glasses(750 ml)- Aglianico
White onions: 1
Carrots: 2
Celery: 2ribs
Type 00 flour: 1spoonful
Bay leaves: 2leaves
Sage: 2leaves
Rosemary: 1sprig
Extra virgin olive oil: to taste
Fine salt: to taste
Water: ¾cup(200 g)
White onions: ½
Carrots: ½
Celery: ½rib
Cloves: 2

info:
Difficulty: Average
Prep time: 30 min
Cook time: 2 h 20 min
Serving: 4
Cost: High
Note + 1 night of marination

instructions:
To prepare the Aglianico stew, first, cut the meat into coarse pieces , then place it in a baking dish and cover it with the wine .
Season with bay leaf, sage, and rosemary , cover with plastic wrap and let the meat marinate overnight in the fridge. The next day, continue with the preparation: prepare the sauté by finely chopping onion, carrot, and celery and set aside.
Drain the meat, keeping the marinade . Flour the morsels and then prepare the broth by placing the reserved marinade, water, cloves, half an onion, half a carrot, and celery in a pot. Salt , and cook for 10 minutes.
Strain the broth and keep it warm. Now pour the sauté into a large pot, stew for 2 minutes with a drizzle of oil, then add the meat and let it brown on all sides for about 7 minutes. At this point, add the broth .
Cover with the lid and cook for 2 hours on low heat. When cooking is complete, adjust the salt and serve the Aglianico stew hot .
Aglianico stew can be stored in the fridge for a couple of days. You can freeze it after cooking if you have used fresh ingredients.
Accompany the Aglianico stew with mashed potatoes or a skillet of seasonal vegetables!
