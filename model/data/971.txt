name:
Savoy Cabbage Salentina Style
ingredients:
Verza (savoy cabbage): 1.1lbs(500 g)
Cherry tomatoes: 8
Fresh chili pepper: 1
Red onions: 3.5oz(100 g)
Water: 1.3cups(300 g)
Extra virgin olive oil: 5spoonfuls

info:
Difficulty: Easy
Prep time: 15 min
Cook time: 40 min
Serving: 4
Cost: Low

instructions:
To make savoy cabbage Salentina style, first remove the outer tougher leaves of the cabbage , cut it in half, and then slice it into strips . Rinse it under running water and set it aside.
Peel and slice the onion . Wash the cherry tomatoes and cut them in half . Also, slice a fresh chili .
Heat 5 tablespoons of extra virgin olive oil in a pan , add the onion , and let it wilt over high heat for two minutes. Add the halved cherry tomatoes
the drained cabbage , and the sliced chili , salt, and cover with water .
Cook for 30 minutes with the lid on and finish cooking for another ten minutes without the lid to let the excess water evaporate. The savoy cabbage Salentina style is ready , serve it hot at the table .
You can store savoy cabbage Salentina style in the refrigerator for 3-4 days. It can be frozen after cooking.
The stewed savoy cabbage Salentina style is a winter side dish that you can serve with meat dishes like Sweet and sour rabbit stew and Pork loin roast or a good Foil-baked sea bream .
