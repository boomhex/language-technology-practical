name:
Cold pasta with zucchini cream, peaches, and stracciatella
ingredients:
Rigatoni: 11.3oz(320 g)
Nectarines: 2
Stracciatella cheese: 3.5oz(100 g)
Walnut kernels: 0.3cup(40 g)
Zucchini: 5cups(600 g)
Extra virgin olive oil: 1 ½tbsp(20 g)
Fine salt: 1 ¾tsp(10 g)

To prepare the cold pasta with zucchini cream, peaches, and stracciatella, first trim the zucchini and chop them roughly . Place the zucchini in boiling salted water and cook for about 5 minutes.
Drain the zucchini and transfer them to a pitcher, then add a ladle of cold water and a pinch of salt .
Also add the oil and blend with an immersion blender until you get a smooth cream. Cook the pasta in the same water in which you boiled the zucchini .
Toast the walnuts in a static oven at 284°F for about 10 minutes , then let them cool. Meanwhile, cut the peaches into half-inch cubes . Drain the pasta al dente into a bowl, drizzle with a bit of oil, and let it cool.
When the pasta is cold, add the zucchini cream and mix well . Plate and add the walnuts broken by hand .
Finish with the peach cubes , a few dollops of stracciatella , and a drizzle of oil. Serve your cold pasta with zucchini cream, peaches, and stracciatella immediately !
We recommend consuming the cold pasta with zucchini cream, peaches, and stracciatella immediately.
You can prepare the zucchini cream in advance and store it in the refrigerator for a couple of days.
If you wish, you can add some chopped fresh mint leaves to the zucchini cream or at the moment of plating.
