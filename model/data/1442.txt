name:
Troccoli with Broad Bean Pesto
ingredients:
Troccoli pasta: 0.9lb(400 g)
Pistachios: ½cup(60 g)- shelled
Burrata cheese: 1.8oz(50 g)
Lemon peel: to taste
Fava beans: 1 ½cup(150 g)- shelled (2.2 lbs to shell)
Pecorino Romano PDO cheese: 2.5oz(70 g)- grated
Basil: 1cup(20 g)
Extra virgin olive oil: 5tbsp(70 g)
Thyme: to taste
Black pepper: to taste
Fine salt: to taste

info:
Difficulty: Easy
Prep time: 20 min
Cook time: 10 min
Serving: 4
Cost: Low

instructions:
To prepare the troccoli with broad bean pesto, you will need 5.3 oz of shelled broad beans, without the outer skin . Pour them into the container of a mixer , add some thyme leaves ,
the basil leaves , the grated pecorino cheese , the oil , salt, and pepper.
Add a little water to obtain a smoother pesto . Then blend everything to get a cream and if it is too thick, add a bit more water. At this point, pour the pistachios into a hot pan
and toast them for about 4 minutes . Let cool slightly, then chop them coarsely . At this point, cook the troccoli in plenty of boiling salted water to taste .
In the meantime, pour the pesto into a pan , add a ladle of pasta cooking water , and heat over low flame. When the pasta is al dente, drain it and transfer it to the pan .
Sauté it for a few moments . Plate the troccoli and garnish each dish with some burrata tufts , the pistachio crumbs ,
lemon zest , and a pinch of pepper . The troccoli are now ready to serve .
The broad bean pesto can be stored in the refrigerator for 1-2 days. It is recommended to consume the dressed pasta immediately.
Add some raw cherry tomatoes!
