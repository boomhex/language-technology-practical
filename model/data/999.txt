name:
Carrot cake without eggs
ingredients:
Carrots: 3cups(380 g)- whole
Type 0 flour: 2cups(250 g)
Sugar: 0.7cup(140 g)
Potato starch: ⅓cup(50 g)
Sunflower seed oil: ⅓cup(80 g)
Orange juice: ¼cup(60 g)
Water: ¼cup(60 g)
Baking powder: 2.4tsp(10 g)
Butter: to taste
Type 00 flour: to taste
Powdered sugar: to taste

instructions:

To make the eggless carrot cake, first peel the carrots, cut them into chunks, and place them in the mixer , blend until they become shavings . Squeeze the orange ; you will need 60 grams (about 1/4 cup) of juice.
In a bowl, pour the water, orange juice , and sugar , then mix with a whisk .
Also add the sunflower seed oil and mix again. Sift the flour and add it to the liquids , then do the same with the cornstarch and the baking powder .
Mix until you get a smooth and lump-free batter . Finally, incorporate the carrots and blend them into the mixture .
Butter and flour a 9-inch (23 cm) mold , pour the batter in , and level with a spatula. Bake in a preheated static oven at 356°F (180°C) for about 30 minutes.
Remove from the oven, let cool , and dust with powdered sugar . The eggless carrot cake is ready to be enjoyed .
You can store the eggless carrot cake under a glass dome for up to 3-4 days. Freezing is not recommended.
You can flavor the batter with the seeds of a vanilla pod.
If you don't have a mixer, you can use a grater.
