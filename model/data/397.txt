name:
Microwave rabbit
ingredients:
Rabbit: 2.6lbs(1.2 kg)- in pieces
Potatoes: 1.8lbs(800 g)
Red onions: 4.2oz(120 g)
Vegetable broth: ⅓cup(100 g)
White wine: 1.4oz(40 g)
Garlic: 1clove
Rosemary: 4sprigs
Thyme: 4sprigs
Sage: 1leaf
Extra virgin olive oil: 4 ¾tbsp(70 g)
Salt: to taste
Black pepper: to taste

info:
Difficulty: Very easy
Prep time: 20 min
Cook time: 50 min
Serving: 4
Cost: Very low

instructions:
To prepare the microwave rabbit, first peel the potatoes, cut them into quarters , and then into pieces . Slice the onion julienne style and set aside.
In a bowl, place the rabbit pieces, add the oil , rosemary , and season with salt and pepper .
Also pour the white wine and vegetable broth into the bowl. Add the onions and potatoes . Stir to season.
Transfer everything into a lightly greased baking dish, add the garlic , thyme, and sage . Cook in the microwave at 800 W for 40 minutes. Then continue cooking for another 10 minutes in grill mode. The microwave rabbit is ready to be served .
The microwave rabbit can be stored in the fridge, in a closed container, for up to 1 day. Freezing is not recommended.
Instead of thyme, you can use rosemary. To personalize the recipe, you can add other spices such as paprika or curry.
Additionally, you can add more complexity to the dish by adding citrus juice like orange or lemon.
