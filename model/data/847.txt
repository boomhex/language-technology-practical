name:
Chicken slices with curry
ingredients:
Chicken breast: 1.75lbs(800 g)
Curry: 1.6tsp(8 g)
Sweet Paprika: 0.7tsp(3 g)
Garlic: 1clove
Vegetable broth: 0.7cup(150 g)
Fresh liquid cream: 1cup(250 g)
Parsley: to taste
Extra virgin olive oil: to taste
Fine salt: to taste
Basmati rice: 1 ½cup(250 g)
Water: 4 ¼cups(1 l)

info:
Difficulty: Easy
Prep time: 20 min
Cook time: 20 min
Serving: 4
Cost: Average
Note + 15 minutes of marinating

instructions:
To prepare the chicken strips with curry, first cut the chicken into strips , place it in a large bowl, add the oil , and salt .
Season with curry and paprika , mix with a spoon to evenly coat the chicken with the spices. Let it rest for 15 minutes.
Meanwhile, prepare the rice. Rinse the rice and put it in a pot, cover with water , and cook for 10 minutes once boiling .
Once the rice is ready, remove it from the cooking water with a slotted spoon and let it cool in a bowl. Season it with salt and oil . Heat some oil in a large pan, add the crushed garlic clove , and sauté for a few minutes to flavor the oil.
Remove the garlic, add the chicken , and brown over moderate heat for about 8 minutes, stirring often. Once the chicken is browned on all sides, add a sprinkle of pepper, stir, and then add the hot broth and cream .
Cook for another 10 minutes , stirring occasionally until the sauce thickens . Turn off the heat and let it cool slightly. Plate and serve the chicken with the rice, decorating the dish with parsley leaves. The chicken strips with curry are ready to be enjoyed .
Store the chicken strips with curry in the refrigerator for up to 1-2 days. Freezing is not recommended.
For an even more intense flavor, you can add chopped chili when marinating the chicken.
