name:
Overnight Porridge
ingredients:
Oat bran: Â½cup(40 g)
Rice milk: 0.6cup(150 g)
Blueberries: 0.4oz(10 g)
Greek yogurt: 5tbsp(75 g)
Blueberries: 0.1cup(20 g)
Almond slices: to taste
Mint: to taste

info:
Difficulty: Very easy
Prep time: 10 min
Serving: 1
Cost: Low
Note + one night of rest in the refrigerator

instructions:
To prepare the overnight porridge, pour the oat bran into a glass jar , then add the rice milk . Wash and halve the blueberries .
Add the blueberries , stir , and cover with the lid . Let sit in the refrigerator overnight.
The next morning, remove the lid , then top with the Greek yogurt and the whole blueberries .
Finish with some almond slivers and a few mint leaves : your overnight porridge is ready to be enjoyed !
Overnight porridge can be stored in the refrigerator for one night, after which it is recommended to consume it.
Oat bran has a finer texture compared to oat flakes: the former is more suitable for this preparation method, while the latter is more recommended for the cooked version of porridge.
We chose rice milk because it is naturally sweet and is therefore ideal for preparing porridge without added sugars. However, if you prefer, you can use cow's milk or another type of plant milk.
If you wish to sweeten the porridge, you can add white or brown sugar, stevia, honey, or maple or agave syrup.
