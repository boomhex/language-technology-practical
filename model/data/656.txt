name:
Cold porridge
ingredients:
Rolled oats: 0.6cup(60 g)
Chia seeds: 1spoonful
Water: ½cup(120 g)- at room temperature
Agave syrup: 2tsp
Soy yogurt: 2spoonfuls
Granola: 2spoonfuls- with chocolate
Blueberries: 5

To make the cold porridge, first pour the oat flakes and chia seeds into a glass jar , then add the agave syrup and room-temperature water .
Mix well , then close with the lid and let it rest in the refrigerator overnight (or at least 6 hours). After resting, transfer the porridge to a bowl and garnish with soy yogurt , chocolate granola, and blueberries . Your cold porridge is ready to be enjoyed!
Cold porridge can be stored in the refrigerator, sealed in a jar, and without additional ingredients, for up to 24 hours.
You can enrich the cold porridge with other fresh seasonal fruits or dried fruits, for example!
Agave syrup can be replaced with maple syrup, honey, white sugar, or brown sugar.
