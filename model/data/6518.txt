name:
Bellini cocktail
ingredients:
Spumante: 2tbsp(30 g)- brut (or Prosecco wine)
Peach: ½cup(125 g)- white-fleshed purée

info:
Difficulty: Very easy
Prep time: 10 min
Makes: 3 pieces
Cost: Low

instructions:
To prepare a Bellini, start by washing a white peach under plenty of running water . Then dry with a clean cloth , and cut into segments without removing the skin . Remove the pit.
Now place the peach into the mixer and blend until it is p ur é ed . Filter the peach purée with a narrow-mesh strainer placed over a bowl .
A uniform p ur é e will be obtained . Pour it into a shaker and add very cold brut sparkling wine (or Prosecco wine) . Stir the ingredients briefly with a long-handled spoon .
Place the strainer over the mouth of the shaker and filter the cocktail directly into the flutes . Now serve your Bellini . Cheers!
The Bellini cocktail should be consumed as soon as it's prepared.
Would you like a Bellini with royal allure? Use Champagne instead of Prosecco wine to make a Bellini Royal! Would you prefer the non-alcoholic version? Simply replace the wine with soda! Serve with a slice of peach for an irresistible cocktail!
