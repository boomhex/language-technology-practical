name:
Fettuccine with artichokes and bacon
ingredients:
Egg fettuccine pasta: 8.8oz(250 g)
Red onions: 2.8oz(80 g)
Artichokes: 4
Smoked pancetta: 5.3oz(150 g)
White wine: 1.4oz(40 g)
Fine salt: to taste
Black pepper: to taste
Extra virgin olive oil: 2tbsp(30 g)
Saffron: 1tsp(0.125 g)- 1 sachet
Thyme: 3sprigs
Lemons: 1

info:
Difficulty: Easy
Prep time: 20 min
Cook time: 15 min
Serving: 4
Cost: Low
Note + cooking the pasta

To prepare fettuccine with artichokes and bacon, start by bringing a pot of salted water to a boil and by How to clean artichokes , but before touching them, protect your hands with latex gloves or rub lemon on your skin to prevent them from turning black. Remove the thicker outer leaves until you reach the tender ones, then, with a knife, cut off the tougher tips and also remove the stem. Place the flowers and stems in a basin filled with water and lemon to prevent them from browning . Now peel the stems, removing the most fibrous outer part, and chop them finely ,
then cut the artichoke hearts in half, remove the inner choke, and cut them into quarters . Clean and slice the red onion into julienne strips and sauté it in a large pan drizzled with olive oil .
When the onion is well softened, add the carefully drained artichokes , season with salt and pepper , add a little water, then deglaze with white wine and let the artichokes cook for 10 minutes over high heat.
Meanwhile, cut the bacon into thin strips . Sauté it in a non-stick pan without adding any additional fat, to render it crispy : it will take just a few minutes, and when it's ready, you can add it to the artichokes and continue cooking the sauce over low heat, mixing the ingredients well.
In a bowl, place the saffron , dilute it with a little water , and after dissolving it with a teaspoon, set it aside. Cook the fettuccine in the boiling salted water : it will take just a few minutes.
When they are cooked, drain the fettuccine directly into the sauce using a slotted spoon . Add the diluted saffron , carefully mix the ingredients over high heat to distribute the flavors. The fettuccine with artichokes and bacon is ready : serve it with a sprinkle of fresh thyme.
You can store the plain sauce for 2-3 days in the refrigerator, sealed in an airtight container. If you used only fresh ingredients, you can also freeze it in the same way. Once the pasta is added, it is recommended to consume it immediately.
In addition to wine, you can deglaze the artichokes using vegetable broth instead of water. Try adding a chopped fresh chili pepper: the spicy note will give it an extra kick! If you like, you can easily substitute the thyme with fresh parsley.
