name:
Reginette with ricotta cream and swordfish dice
ingredients:
Reginette pasta: 11.3oz(320 g)
Ricotta cheese: 2 ½cups(600 g)
Swordfish: 14oz(400 g)
Fine salt: 1pinch
Red pepper: 1pinch
Garlic: 1clove
Extra virgin olive oil: 4tbsp(60 g)
White wine: 2tbsp(30 g)
Marjoram: 4leaves

info:
Difficulty: Easy
Prep time: 6 min
Cook time: 10 min
Serving: 4
Cost: Very low

instructions:

To prepare the reginette with ricotta cream and swordfish dice, first put a pot of abundant salted water on the stove, which will be used for cooking the pasta. In the meantime, focus on preparing the swordfish. Remove the skin from the fish and cut it first into strips and then into cubes .
Sauté a clove of garlic in a pan with some extra virgin olive oil , then remove it and add the swordfish dice . Add salt ,


pepper , deglaze with white wine and cook for no more than 5 minutes. Then cook your reginette
and in the meantime coarsely chop the ricotta , add some oil in a large pan and pour in the ricotta .
Thin it with some of the pasta cooking water , blend it with a whisk to remove lumps , add pink pepper , and stir until it becomes a smooth and homogeneous cream.
When the pasta is ready, drain it and pour it into the pan with the ricotta cream , mix everything well and then add the swordfish dice .
Add marjoram leaves , mix to combine all the ingredients well , and your reginette with ricotta cream and swordfish dice are ready to be served!
It is preferable to consume the reginette with ricotta cream and swordfish dice freshly prepared; otherwise, you can store them in the refrigerator in an airtight container for a maximum of one day.
If you wish, you can add an extra touch of flavor with a sprinkle of parsley, capers, and black olives!
