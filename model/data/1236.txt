name:
Gingerbread bundt cake
ingredients:
Type 00 flour: 1 ½cup(180 g)
Unsweetened cocoa powder: 4tbsp(50 g)
Powdered ginger: 1tsp
Cinnamon powder: 1tsp
Nutmeg: 1pinch
Cranberries: 1.75oz(50 g)- dehydrated
Baking powder: 1 ½tsp(8 g)
Brown sugar: ¾cup(180 g)
Eggs: 6- at room temperature
Butter: 0.9cup(200 g)- at room temperature
Fine salt: 1pinch
Dark chocolate: 5.3oz(150 g)- to melt
White chocolate: 7oz(200 g)
Fresh liquid cream: ½cup(100 g)
Cranberries: 1.1oz(30 g)- dehydrated
Sugar: 2tsp
Brown sugar: 2tsp
Rosemary: to taste

info:
Difficulty: Easy
Prep time: 15 min
Cook time: 45 min
Serving: 8
Cost: Average

instructions:

To prepare the gingerbread bundt cake, first melt the dark chocolate in the microwave or a double boiler, then let it cool slightly. In a large bowl, sift the flour , cocoa , and baking powder.
Season the dry ingredients with ground ginger, ground cinnamon , and a pinch of grated nutmeg. In the mixer bowl, add the brown sugar .
Add the previously sifted dry ingredients and 1/3 cup of dried cranberries . Give it a quick pulse to mix. Now also add the eggs and the butter cut into pieces , pulse again to blend the ingredients.
While the mixer is running, drizzle in the melted chocolate . Once you have a homogeneous mixture, stop the mixer . Butter and flour a bundt pan with a diameter of 9.5 inches, pour the batter in .
Bake the cake in a preheated static oven at 356°F for 40 minutes . Meanwhile, chop 1 ounce of dried cranberries , season the cranberries with 2 tablespoons of granulated sugar and 2 of brown sugar, also add some rosemary needles cut into pieces . Set aside, it will be used to garnish the cake.
In a small saucepan, heat the cream with a teaspoon of cinnamon and then pour it hot over the chopped white chocolate , stir with a whisk to melt the chocolate and achieve a smooth and homogeneous sauce.
In the meantime, the cake will be done, take it out of the oven , let it cool, then unmold it onto a cutting board lined with parchment paper or a tray . Pour the white chocolate glaze over the cake .
Cover the surface , immediately garnish with the sugared cranberries . The gingerbread bundt cake is ready to be enjoyed .
The gingerbread bundt cake can be stored under a glass dome for 2-3 days. If you want, you can slice and freeze it without glazing, then thaw only the amount you need.
To be called a bundt cake, a cake should be baked in a special pan characterized by particular grooves. You'll find a rich variety of pans with different patterns, arabesques, and ridges available in stores.
