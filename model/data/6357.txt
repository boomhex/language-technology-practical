name:
Pasta with smoked pancetta sauce
ingredients:
Sedanini Rigati pasta: 0.75lb(320 g)
Smoked pancetta: 0.5lb(250 g)
Peeled tomatoes: 1lb(450 g)
Celery: 1cup(100 g)
Onions: 2cups(100 g)
White wine: 0.3cup(75 g)
Fresh chili pepper: 1
Basil: to taste
Black pepper: to taste
Fine salt: to taste
Extra virgin olive oil: to taste

info:
Difficulty: Easy
Prep time: 20 min
Cook time: 30 min
Serving: 4
Cost: Low

instructions:
To make the pasta with smoked pancetta sauce , start by cleaning and chopping the ingredients to be cooked: Finely chop the celery and the onion . Then chop the chili as well , removing the seeds to take away some of the heat.
Remove the rind on the smoked pancetta and chop the pancetta into tiny pieces . Finally, finely chop the peeled tomatoes and then head over to the stove. Put a pot full of water on to boil to cook the pasta.
Heat a glug of oil in another pot, then brown the pancetta with the onion, celery , and chili , over low heat.
Let them cook for around 10 minutes, stirring occasionally, so the vegetables soften and the pancetta browns . Deglaze with the wine and allow the alcohol to evaporate completely , then add the peeled tomatoes .
Shred the basil by hand , add to the pot, and season with salt and pepper . Cook the sauce for around 20 minutes or until it is dry enough or has reached the desired consistency. In the meantime, cook the sedanini rigati in salted boiling water .
As soon as the pasta is al dente, drain and pour it into the sauce . Now, just add a pinch of pepper, a drop of oil , and a few basil leaves to your plates of pasta with smoked pancetta sauce . Buon appetito !
We recommend eating the pasta with smoked pancetta sauce right away, but if you prefer, you can store it for up to 2 days. The sauce can be stored in the fridge for 2-3 days. If needed, you could also freeze the sauce and use it on freshly cooked pasta.
