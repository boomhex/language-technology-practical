name:
Pullastiello-style Zucchini
ingredients:
Zucchini: 3 ¼cups(400 g)
Smoked provola cheese: 4.6oz(130 g)
Neapolitan salami: 2.5oz(70 g)- (14 slices)
Eggs: 2
Fine salt: to taste
Black pepper: to taste
Type 00 flour: to taste
Sunflower seed oil: to taste

info:
Difficulty: Easy
Prep time: 20 min
Cook time: 10 min
Makes: 14 pieces
Cost: Low

instructions:
To prepare pullastiello-style zucchini, start by cleaning the zucchini , cut them diagonally into slices about 1/4 inch thick . Prepare for the first frying, immerse plenty of oil and bring it to 320°F, immerse a few pieces at a time .
In a couple of minutes, they will be golden brown , drain them on absorbent paper until all are cooked . At this point, slice the smoked provola into slices 1/8-1/6 inch thick . As you go, make sure to dry them with absorbent paper.
Also slice the cleaned Neapolitan salami, obtaining 14 slices about 1/16-1/12 inch thick . On a fried zucchini slice, add the broken provola and the salami .
Close with another zucchini slice and continue like this until you have 14 pieces in total . Break the eggs into a shallow, wide bowl, season with salt and pepper, and beat them .
Dip the stuffed zucchini first in the eggs and then in the flour , ensuring it adheres well: it should be very compact. Place on a tray as you go .
Dip in the hot oil at 320°F, a few pieces at a time . In 3-4 minutes, they will be ready to drain , so continue until all the pullastiello-style zucchini are finished and enjoy them hot .
It is recommended to consume the pullastiello-style zucchini immediately.
Let your imagination run wild when it comes to filling the pullastiello-style zucchini.
