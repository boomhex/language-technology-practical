name:
Tiramisu with Pan di Stelle® cookies
ingredients:
Pan di Stelle® cookies: 2 ½cups(250 g)
Whole milk: 0.42cup(100 g)
Unsweetened cocoa powder: 4tbsp(20 g)
Sugar: 2 ½tsp(10 g)
Mascarpone cheese: 1.67cups(400 g)
Sugar: ½cup(100 g)
Eggs: 4- (medium, very fresh)
Unsweetened cocoa powder: to taste

info:
Difficulty: Easy
Prep time: 30 min
Makes: 4 pieces
Cost: Average

instructions:
To prepare the tiramisù with Pan di Stelle® cookies, start by separating the egg yolks from the whites . Beat the whites with the mixer until you get firm peaks .
Then do the same with the yolks, add the sugar and beat with the mixer for at least 5 minutes to obtain a light foam .
At this point, combine the beaten yolks and sugar with the mascarpone placed in a large bowl , mix to obtain a smooth cream , and do the same with the whipped egg whites .
Be sure to incorporate them gently with a spatula, making gentle movements from bottom to top . Prepare the cookie dip by pouring the cocoa powder and sugar into a bowl . Stir and add the milk .
Keep stirring to prevent lumps . Transfer the cream to a piping bag without a nozzle and pour some into the bottom of 4 glasses . Quickly dip the cookies, two for each glass, and place them on the cream .
Add some more mascarpone cream and then again 2 dipped cookies per glass . Cover with the remaining cream .
Finally, sprinkle with a little cocoa powder . To decorate, you can use an undipped cookie, placed vertically in each glass : here are your tiramisù glasses with Pan di Stelle® cookies ready. They can be left to rest in the fridge for about half an hour before enjoying them !
It is recommended to consume the tiramisù with Pan di Stelle® cookies within 1 day of preparation.
No children around and want to use coffee? It will work perfectly instead of milk and cocoa!
If you wish, you can also How to pasteurize eggs for a safer result.
