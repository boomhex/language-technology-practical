name:
Candy filled with potatoes and sausage with aromatic herbs
ingredients:
Pasta disks: 16
Sausage: 5.5oz(150 g)
Potatoes: 5.3oz(150 g)
Butter: 3 ½tbsp(50 g)
Water: 0.75oz(20 g)
Thyme: to taste
Marjoram: to taste
Fine salt: to taste
Black pepper: to taste

info:
Difficulty: Easy
Prep time: 20 min
Cook time: 10 min
Makes: 16 pieces

instructions:

To prepare the candies filled with potatoes and sausage with aromatic herbs, start with the potatoes: peel the potatoes and first slice them then dice them .
Blanch the potato cubes in plenty of boiling salted water to taste for 5 minutes , then once cooked, drain them thoroughly . In the meantime, score a sausage to then remove the casing
then crumble it and place it to brown in a pan for about 5 minutes ; when well-browned, add the potatoes and cook for about ten minutes; season with salt and pepper to taste.
Take the pasta disks and fill each one with the potato and sausage filling (about 25 grams or 1 oz for each disk; you can set aside any leftover filling) ; then wrap the filling to form a small cylinder.
Then pinch the part near the filling to get the candy shape , taking care to seal well so the filling doesn’t spill out. Then blanch the filled candies in plenty of boiling salted water to taste . Then melt the butter in a pan and when it is melted, pour the aromatic herbs in and sauté for 1 minute .
Then drain the candies and place them in the pan to flavor them for a few moments ; if you still have it, you can also add the leftover filling , to further season the candies filled with potatoes and sausage with aromatic herbs .
It is recommended to consume the candies filled with potatoes and sausage with aromatic herbs as soon as they are ready.
They can be stored cooked for one day in the refrigerator.
Freezing is not recommended.
To make the filling even tastier and melt-in-your-mouth, you can add smoked scamorza cheese cut into cubes!
