name:
Homemade Jelly
ingredients:
Potato starch: 3tsp(7 g)
Sugar: 4tbsp(50 g)
Water: Â½cup(125 g)
Lemon juice: to taste

info:
Difficulty: Very easy
Prep time: 5 min
Cook time: 5 min
Serving: 10
Cost: Very low

instructions:
To prepare homemade jelly, first put the sugar and potato starch in a small pot . Add part of the water required, and stir to dissolve the starch and sugar without forming lumps . Pour in the remaining water while continuing to stir .
Heat over low flame and bring to a boil, stirring constantly. Once boiling, turn off the heat, and the liquid will start to thicken . Add a few drops of lemon juice , stir one last time, and let it cool slightly. Your homemade jelly is ready to be used .
Homemade jelly can be stored in the refrigerator, in a closed container, for 1 week.
Add a few drops of vanilla, orange, or lemon extract for an extra touch of flavor. You can also use citrus peels during cooking for a more intense aroma. Potato starch can be replaced with cornstarch or rice starch. For coloring, you can add natural colorants like beet juice, turmeric, or spirulina for a colorful and healthy result. For savory preparations, instead of sugar, add vegetable or meat broth, ideal for aspics or to give a glossy finish to savory dishes.
