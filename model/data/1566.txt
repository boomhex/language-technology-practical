name:
Cake with pears and amaretti
ingredients:
Type 00 flour: 1 ½cup(190 g)
Eggs: 9.2oz(260 g)- (approximately 5)
Vegetable oil: ½cup(120 g)
Sugar: 0.9cup(180 g)
Baking powder: 2tsp(10 g)
Amaretti cookies: 1.5oz(40 g)
Coscia pears: 14.1oz(400 g)- peeled
Powdered sugar: to taste- for garnishing

info:
Difficulty: Average
Prep time: 20 min
Cook time: 45 min
Serving: 8
Cost: Average

instructions:

To prepare the cake with pears and amaretti, start with the latter. Transfer them into a mixer and blend them until they become powder .
In another bowl, pour the flour, the baking powder , the crushed amaretti and mix with a spoon to even out the powders .
Break the eggs in a large bowl, add the sugar and whisk everything with an electric mixer until you get a fluffy and frothy mixture .
While continuing to whisk, pour the oil in a thin stream , and when it is well incorporated, stop. Then add the flours , mixing from bottom to top .
The mixture should be smooth and still whipped . Then peel the pears , remove the central core, and cut the flesh into cubes .
Add them to the batter and mix gently to incorporate them. Butter and flour a 9.5x9.5 inch mold, then pour the batter inside . Bake in a preheated static oven at 356°F for 45 minutes. Take the cake out of the oven .
and let it cool on a wire rack . Then cut it into 16 cubes, sprinkle with powdered sugar and serve your pear and amaretti cake .
The pear and amaretti cake can be stored under a cake dome for up to 2-3 days. It can also be frozen in portions.
To make the pear and amaretti cake even more delicious, you can add dark chocolate chips.
