name:
Shrimp sautéed in teriyaki sauce with snow peas and baby carrots
ingredients:
Shrimps: 12
Snap peas: 9oz(250 g)
Baby carrots: 2cups(250 g)
Teriyaki sauce: 3 ½tbsp(50 g)
Extra virgin olive oil: 2tbsp(30 g)
Thyme: 1sprig
Black pepper: 1pinch

info:
Difficulty: Easy
Prep time: 15 min
Cook time: 5 min
Serving: 4

To prepare the shrimp sautéed in teriyaki sauce with snow peas and baby carrots, start by thoroughly washing the baby carrots under plenty of running water . Place them on a cutting board, trim them with a small knife , and lightly scrape the skin; shorten the green stem and set aside .
Take the snow peas, remove the two ends , then rinse them under running water. Place a pot with plenty of salted water on the stove: when the water is about to boil, cook the snow peas for 5 minutes so they soften but do not disintegrate . Once ready, drain them, keeping the cooking water .
Leave the snow peas in the strainer to cool and drain well from their liquid . Then boil the carrots for 7-8 minutes in the reserved cooking water of the snow peas . Meanwhile, proceed to the How to clean shrimp : leave the head and tail intact, and with your fingers remove the shell ;
once cleaned externally, gently remove the internal intestine (the black filament) with the help of a toothpick (you can also cut the back of the shrimp with a small knife and remove it by gently pulling). In a non-stick pan, place a drizzle of oil and the whole sprig of thyme .
Sear the shrimp over high heat for 2 minutes on each side, turning them with kitchen tongs for even cooking . When the carrots are ready, drain them well and add them to the shrimp along with the snow peas ;
Season with pepper to your liking and deglaze with teriyaki sauce , letting it infuse for 2-3 minutes; turn off the heat and finally plate and serve your shrimp sautéed in teriyaki sauce with snow peas and baby carrots! .
It is recommended to consume the shrimp sautéed in teriyaki sauce with snow peas and baby carrots immediately. You can store them in the refrigerator for up to 1 day covered with plastic wrap. Freezing is not recommended.
If the shrimp are fresh, you need to sear them for a very short time: therefore, avoid prolonging the cooking! Looking for the perfect accompaniment for this tasty dish? Try with some good pilaf rice!
