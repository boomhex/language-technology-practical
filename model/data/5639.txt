name:
Baked Bolognese
ingredients:
Unsalted butter: 1tbsp
Olive oil: 2tbsp
Red onion: 1- finely chopped
Carrots: 2- small, peeled and finely chopped
Celery: 1rib- finely chopped
Kosher salt: 1 ¼tsp- divided
Ground chuck: 1.5lbs
Garlic: 2cloves- chopped
Calabrian Chili Paste: 1 ½tsp- optional
Tomato paste: ¼cup
Whole milk: 2cups
Red wine: 1cup- dry, such as Chianti
San Marzano tomatoes: 28oz- (or 2 14-ounce cans carbara pomodorini tomatoes) crushed by hand
Bay leaves: 1
Parmigiano Reggiano cheese rind: 1- 1 piece (3-inch)
Nodi Marini pasta: 17.5oz- 1 package, such as Giadzy
Parmigiano Reggiano PDO cheese: 1 ½cup- freshly grated, divided
Mozzarella cheese: 1 ½cup- shredded

info:
Prep time: 30 min
Cook time: 4 h
Serving: 8

instructions:
Bolognese is always a delightful choice, and it becomes even more appealing when you can prepare it entirely in advance. Imagine the convenience of assembling this rich, flavorful dish ahead of time, with the assurance that baking it on the day of your event will yield results as sumptuous as if it were made fresh that same day. That’s not just smart cooking; it’s sheer brilliance! To add an extra touch of charm, this recipe features fun sailors knot pasta shape, ensuring that each forkful is packed with joyful, savory satisfaction. Perfect for gatherings, this make-ahead Bolognese promises to be a crowd-pleaser, leaving your guests impressed with each perfectly seasoned bite!
