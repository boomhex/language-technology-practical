name:
Egg White Omelette
ingredients:
Egg whites: 1 ¼cup(300 g)
Onions: 0.7oz(20 g)
Parmigiano Reggiano PDO cheese: 1oz(30 g)- (to be grated)
Pecorino cheese: 0.7oz(20 g)
Whole milk: 2.7tbsp(40 ml)
Potato starch: ¼cup(30 g)
Extra virgin olive oil: to taste
Sage: to taste
Mint: to taste
Thyme: to taste
Fine salt: to taste
Black pepper: to taste

instructions:

To prepare the egg white omelette, clean and chop the onion, then add it to a pan with a splash of oil . Sauté for a few minutes over medium heat, stirring occasionally, until softened . Let the sautéed onion cool. Chop the herbs .
Pour the egg whites into a bowl , add the grated Parmigiano Reggiano DOP and Pecorino . Mix everything together .
Add the potato starch , stir until absorbed , then add the milk .
Salt and incorporate the chopped herbs . Meanwhile, the onion will have cooled, add it to the egg whites .
Mix carefully and pour the mixture into a round 7-8 inch pan lined with parchment paper (depending on the height you want to give the omelette) . Bake in a preheated static oven at 350°F for 25 minutes. Once ready, let it cool slightly before removing from the mold . The egg white omelette is ready to be enjoyed.
It's recommended to consume the egg white omelette immediately. You can store the egg white omelette in the refrigerator for up to one day. Freezing is not recommended.
With the leftover yolks, you can prepare a tasty Spaghetti Carbonara or a delicious Pastry cream !
