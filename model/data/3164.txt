name:
Pandoro French toast with Nutella®
ingredients:
Pandoro pastry: 1
Maple syrup: ⅓cup(80 g)
Nutella®: 4tbsp(60 g)
Blackberries: ⅓cup(50 g)
Raspberries: ½cup(50 g)
Butter: 1tbsp(15 g)- (to grease the pan)

info:
Difficulty: Easy
Prep time: 20 min
Cook time: 10 min
Serving: 6

To prepare Pandoro French Toast with Nutella®, cut out a cardboard template in the shape of a brioche bread slice. Then cut the pandoro into slices about 3/4 inch thick . Cut out the shapes with the help of a small knife, placing the cardboard on each pandoro slice . From the first 3 slices, you will obtain two shapes and one from the smaller subsequent ones.
You will have a total of 9 shaped slices . Heat a large non-stick pan and grease it with butter . Toast the slices .
It will take a few minutes per side until golden brown . Plate by stacking 3 slices for each serving , sprinkle with maple syrup .
Garnish with blackberries and raspberries , dollops of Nutella® using a piping bag . Your Pandoro French Toast with Nutella® is ready !
Pandoro French Toast with Nutella® should be consumed immediately.
You can use the leftover pandoro to accompany a custard, toasted in the oven or in a pan to serve as a delicious snack!
You can use blueberries instead of blackberries.
You can drizzle the French toast with agave syrup instead of maple syrup.
You can grease the pan with coconut oil instead of butter!
