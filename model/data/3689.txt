name:
Orecchiette with aromatic herbs
ingredients:
Orecchiette pasta: 11.3oz(320 g)
Pecorino Romano PDO cheese: 3spoonfuls- grated
Butter: ½cup(120 g)- at room temperature
Chives: 2tsp- chopped
Thyme: 2spoonfuls- minced
Parsley: 2spoonfuls- minced
Fine salt: to taste
Black pepper: to taste

info:
Difficulty: Very easy
Prep time: 8 min
Cook time: 6 min
Serving: 4
Cost: Very low

To prepare orecchiette with aromatic herbs, finely chop the herbs . In a small bowl, pour the softened butter and work it into a cream with a spatula .
Add the finely chopped aromatic herbs to the butter , salt , and pepper to taste, then mix the ingredients .
Once the cream is homogeneous , set it aside covered with plastic wrap and boil the orecchiette in plenty of salted water to taste . Drain them while still al dente (keep a glass of cooking water aside). Combine the orecchiette with the prepared seasoning
and mix everything well with a spatula , add the grated pecorino , incorporate it well, and if the pasta is too dry, add a few tablespoons of cooking water. Serve the orecchiette with aromatic herbs hot, garnishing the serving plates with a few sprigs of aromatic herbs .
It is recommended to consume the orecchiette with aromatic herbs immediately after preparation!
If you prefer, you can use grated Parmesan (or another cheese of your choice) instead of pecorino. If you do not like grated cheese, you can also omit it.
