name:
Crispy Salmon
ingredients:
Salmon fillets: 2.2lbs(1 kg)- (4 of 250 g each)
Bread: 3.5oz(100 g)- day-old
Parsley: 1tuft
Dill: 1tuft
Thyme: 4sprigs
Rosemary: 2sprigs
Lemon peel: 1
Extra virgin olive oil: 3 ½tbsp(50 g)
Ground white pepper: 1tsp
Fine salt: to taste

info:
Difficulty: Very easy
Prep time: 10 min
Cook time: 20 min
Serving: 4
Cost: Average

instructions:
With a few simple steps, a common ingredient can become the star of a surprising dish... and crispy salmon is proof of that! Perfect for special occasions and holidays, just choose fresh, quality salmon fillets, wrap them in a crispy herb-flavored breadcrumb coating, and bake. The result? Golden, perfectly cooked salmon! Accompany this main course with a light seasonal side dish such as agretti with anchovies, sautéed chicory , or a simple salad. In just a few moves, you can bring a delicious seafood main course to the table in about 30 minutes!

To make crispy salmon, first prepare the breadcrumb coating: cut the bread into pieces and put it in a food processor , then add the dill and fresh thyme leaves .
Also add the rosemary needles and parsley . Pour in the oil .
Then add the lemon zest , salt, and white pepper . Blend until you get a coarse consistency .
Now take care of the salmon fillets: remove the skin with a thin-bladed knife and remove the bones using kitchen tweezers , then transfer the fillets to a baking sheet lined with parchment paper and cover them with the breadcrumb coating, pressing it well with your hands .
After evenly coating the fillets , bake in a preheated convection oven at 375°F for about 20 minutes. Once cooking time has passed, remove from the oven and serve your crispy salmon hot !
Crispy salmon can be stored in the refrigerator, in an airtight container, for up to 2-3 days.
If you want to make your salmon even crispier, you can add sesame or poppy seeds to the breadcrumb coating!
