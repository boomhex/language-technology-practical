name:
Tomato Piccatine
ingredients:
Veal rump: 1.25lbs(550 g)
Peeled tomatoes: 1cup(400 g)
Garlic: 1clove
Fine salt: to taste
Black pepper: to taste
Basil: 10leaves
Butter: 1.4tbsp(20 g)
Parsley: to taste
Prosciutto cotto: 4.2oz(120 g)
Mozzarella cheese: 1 Â¼cup(160 g)

info:
Difficulty: Easy
Prep time: 15 min
Cook time: 30 min
Serving: 4
Cost: Average

instructions:
To make tomato piccatine, first cut the veal cutlet into slices to get 4 pieces . Wrap each slice in parchment paper , then pound it with a meat mallet .
This will give you thin slices . In a saucepan, heat the oil, add the crushed garlic clove , and let it flavor for a few minutes, then add the tomato and salt.
Flavor with 4-5 basil leaves and cook for about 10-15 minutes covered with a lid. At the end of cooking, turn off the heat and set aside . In another pan, melt the butter with a drizzle of oil .
Place the slices of meat , salt , and let them brown on both sides for a few minutes, just enough to brown the meat .
Slice the mozzarella and let it drain by placing it in a colander , this way it won't release water during cooking. In a baking dish of about 12x8 inches, spread 1-2 tablespoons of tomato on the bottom .
Place the meat and pour the rest of the tomato over it , so that it covers it .
Now distribute the sliced ham and mozzarella . Chop the previously washed and dried parsley .
Season the dish with the parsley and remaining basil . Place in a preheated oven in grill mode for about 5-10 minutes or until the mozzarella melts . The tomato piccatine is ready, enjoy them hot !
It is advisable to consume the tomato piccatine immediately, if you have leftovers, you can store them in the refrigerator for a couple of days at most. You can freeze the preparation if you have used fresh, non-defrosted ingredients.
In the warmer season, if you don't want to turn on the oven, you can heat the piccatine in a covered pan just long enough to melt the mozzarella.
