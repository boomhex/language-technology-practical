name:
Piadina with sun-dried tomatoes and buffalo mozzarella
ingredients:
Flatbread: 2- Puff Pastry with Extra Virgin Olive Oil
Mozzarella di bufala cheese: 3.3cups(400 g)
Dried tomatoes: 7.1oz(200 g)
Oregano: to taste
Extra virgin olive oil: to taste
Black pepper: to taste

info:
Difficulty: Very easy
Prep time: 10 min
Cook time: 5 min
Serving: 2
Note + 30 min. to drain the mozzarella

To prepare the piadina with sun-dried tomatoes and buffalo mozzarella, first cut the mozzarella into slices and let it drain in a colander for at least 30 minutes . After this time, place a pan on the stove and heat the first piadina for 30 seconds on one side .
Then heat for another 30 seconds on the other side, adding half of the mozzarella slices on one side of the piadina . Cover with a lid and wait for it to melt .
At this point, transfer the piadina to a cutting board and season with oil and pepper , then add half of the sun-dried tomatoes .
Sprinkle with a pinch of oregano and fold the piadina in half . Proceed in the same way to prepare the other one and enjoy your piadina with sun-dried tomatoes, buffalo mozzarella, and oregano !
It's recommended to consume the piadina with sun-dried tomatoes and buffalo mozzarella immediately or at most within a few hours.
You can also use cow's milk mozzarella instead of buffalo mozzarella, always making sure to let it drain to remove excess liquid.
