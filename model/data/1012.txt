name:
Egg-free lemon cake
ingredients:
Type 00 flour: 1cup(180 g)
Almond flour: ½cup(50 g)
Lemon juice: 0.4cup(100 g)
Lemon peel: 1
Butter: 6 ½tbsp(90 g)
Whole milk: ¼cup(50 g)- (at room temperature)
Brown sugar: 0.8cup(180 g)
Cornstarch: 6tbsp(50 g)
Baking powder: 1 ½tsp(8 g)

To prepare the egg-free lemon cake, melt the butter over low heat and let it cool. Then pour it into a bowl with the brown sugar , add the lemon zest . Work the ingredients with a whisk .
Add the all-purpose flour , almond flour , cornstarch, and baking powder .
Quickly mix, then also add the lemon juice and milk at room temperature . Work the ingredients until you obtain a creamy and homogeneous batter .
Transfer the batter into a slightly tapered 9-inch diameter pan, previously buttered and floured . Level the surface with the back of a spoon . Bake in a static oven at 338°F for about 55 minutes. Always perform the toothpick test before removing it from the oven (cooking times can vary), once ready wait for it to cool before removing from the mold.
Unmold and let cool completely on a wire rack . To decorate with powdered sugar, you can place a small plate on top of the cake in the center and sprinkle the powdered sugar around , so that it falls only on the outside. Then decorate with a slice of lemon and serve your egg-free lemon cake !
The egg-free lemon cake can be stored for 3 days under a glass dome. It can be frozen after baking.
Enrich your egg-free lemon cake with chocolate chips or raisins as you like!
