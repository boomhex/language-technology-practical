name:
Cake with Greek Yogurt
ingredients:
Greek yogurt: 0.8cup(200 g)
Eggs: 3
Type 00 flour: 1.8cups(225 g)
Sugar: 0.8cup(160 g)
Vegetable oil: 6tbsp(80 g)
Vanilla extract: 1tsp
Baking powder: 1 ¼tbsp(16 g)
Salt: 1pinch
Powdered sugar: to taste

instructions:

To prepare the Greek yogurt cake, start by pouring the whole eggs into a large bowl . Add the sugar and beat the eggs and sugar with an electric mixer .
When it becomes fluffy, light, and frothy , add the Greek yogurt and mix it into the batter with the mixer .

Next, add a pinch of salt , vanilla extract , and oil .

Continuing to work the batter with the electric mixer , add the flour, making sure to sift it and pour it in little by little , then mix again until the batter is creamy, smooth, and uniform.


Now you just need to add the baking powder , always sifting it with a sieve, and mix one last time until you get the right consistency .

Then pour the batter into a 20 cm diameter pan, previously lined with parchment paper . Bake in a preheated static oven at 356°F for 40 minutes on the middle shelf. Times may vary slightly depending on the oven, always do the toothpick test . Once ready, allow the Greek yogurt cake to cool .
Unmold your Greek yogurt cake onto a plate , decorate it with powdered sugar , and enjoy it !

The Greek yogurt cake can be stored on a plate with a glass dome for 3 or 4 days at room temperature.

If you prefer a different aroma than vanilla, you can use lemon or orange zest.
The Greek yogurt cake can be decorated and filled with whipped cream and fresh fruit, or with custard.
