name:
Figs and Grana Padano Crostini
ingredients:
Homemade bread: 5slices
Grana Padano PDO cheese: 1.5oz(40 g)- (in thin shavings)
Figs: 5- red
Walnut kernels: Â¼cup(30 g)
Rosemary: 2sprigs
Balsamic vinegar: to taste
Red pepper: to taste
Fine salt: to taste
Extra virgin olive oil: to taste

info:
Difficulty: Easy
Prep time: 15 min
Cook time: 10 min
Makes: 10 pieces

instructions:
The sweetness of figs pairs perfectly with the savory flavor of cheeses, enhancing their sugary note: after highly recommending the combination with pecorino and gorgonzola , today we present figs and Grana Padano crostini! A simple appetizer with well-defined flavors that create a harmonious balance: to the classic combination of figs with walnuts, we have added the sweet-and-sour touch of balsamic vinegar and the spicy hint of pink peppercorns for a crostini for true connoisseurs. Have we piqued your interest? Try the figs and Grana Padano crostini and let us know if you liked them!
To prepare figs and Grana Padano crostini, first wash and quarter the figs . Heat a grill pan well, place the figs on it , and grill them for about 2 minutes , then set aside.
Cut 5 slices of bread . In a pan, heat the oil with rosemary and a pinch of salt and toast the bread slices on both sides for about 2 minutes until golden .
Cut the toasted bread slices in half . Now place the walnut halves in another well-heated pan and toast them for about 2 minutes .
Finally, shave thin slices of Grana Padano DOP using a vegetable peeler . You are ready to assemble your crostini: place 2 fig quarters on each crostini, add a few shavings of Grana Padano DOP and a couple of walnut halves .
Drizzle with a little balsamic vinegar and finish with pink peppercorns . Your figs and Grana Padano crostini are ready to be served!
It is recommended to consume the figs and Grana Padano crostini immediately.
If you prefer, you can replace the balsamic vinegar with chestnut honey.
