name:
Olive Pastries with Sautéed Zucchini
ingredients:
Crispy baked puff pastry: 12- with olives
Zucchini: 2
Garlic: 1clove
Pine nuts: 1spoonful
Basil: 1tuft
Vegetable broth: to taste
Extra virgin olive oil: 2spoonfuls

info:
Difficulty: Easy
Prep time: 10 min
Cook time: 15 min
Serving: 4
Cost: Low

instructions:
To prepare olive pastries with sautéed zucchini, start by preparing the zucchini: wash them, remove the ends, and slice them about 1/16 inch thick . In a non-stick pan, heat the extra virgin olive oil, add the crushed garlic and let it turn golden, then add the zucchini , and let them soak in the flavor.
and if necessary, add a couple of tablespoons of vegetable broth . When the zucchini are cooked but still crunchy, turn off the heat, salt, and pepper to taste, and add the chopped basil . Top each pastry with some sautéed zucchini, a couple of pine nuts , and a basil leaf!
