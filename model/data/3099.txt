name:
Spelt muffins with strawberries and almonds
ingredients:
Spelt flour: 3cups(370 g)
Strawberries: 2.1cups(300 g)
Natural plain yogurt: 1 ¼cup(300 g)
Raw cane sugar: ¾cup(150 g)
Extra virgin olive oil: 0.3cup(75 g)
Almond slices: ¾cup(70 g)
Eggs: 2
Baking powder: 3 ½tsp(16 g)

info:
Difficulty: Easy
Prep time: 30 min
Cook time: 25 min
Makes: 14 pieces
Cost: Low

instructions:

To prepare spelt muffins with strawberries and almonds, in a bowl mix the spelt flour and raw cane sugar with the help of a whisk . Then add the baking powder and yogurt ,
next break the eggs and add them , then mix with the whisk . Finally, pour in the extra virgin olive oil slowly and blend the ingredients well.
Continue to mix with a spatula until you get a smooth and soft mixture . Wash the strawberries, trim them, and cut them into pieces (8-9).
Add the strawberries to the muffin batter , give it a stir , and lastly add the sliced almonds . Remember to set aside a handful of both strawberries and almonds for the final decoration.
In a muffin tin, place the liners and fill them to 3/4 of their height with the batter ; add a couple of strawberry pieces and almond slices on top of each muffin and bake the spelt muffins in a preheated oven at 356°F for 20-25 minutes (if using a fan oven, 320°F for 15-20 minutes). Take the spelt muffins with strawberries and almonds out of the oven , let them cool on a wire rack, and serve them!
Store spelt muffins with strawberries and almonds under a glass dome for 3-4 days. You can freeze them once cooked.
If you don't like strawberries, no problem: raspberries are an option! And if you want an even more spectacular effect, there's only one solution: powdered sugar. A generous dusting and you're done.
