name:
Spiced Stewed Vegetables
ingredients:
Carrots: 2 ½cups(300 g)
Potatoes: 0.7lb(300 g)
Zucchini: 2 ½cups(300 g)
Pumpkin: 1 ¼cup(300 g)
Leeks: 1
Mustard seeds: ½tsp
Cumin seeds: 1tsp
Sesame seeds: 2spoonfuls
Extra virgin olive oil: 3spoonfuls
Sugar: 1tsp
Fine salt: 2pinches
White wine vinegar: 3 ½tbsp(50 g)

info:
Difficulty: Easy
Prep time: 60 min
Cook time: 30 min
Serving: 4
Cost: Low

instructions:
To prepare the spiced stewed vegetables, trim the washed zucchini and slice it into rounds . Peel the potato, wash it, and dice it . Peel the carrots and cut them into sticks. Peel and slice the leek , transfer it to a colander, wash it, and let it drain well.
Peel the pumpkin and cut it into large cubes . In a large pan, pour the oil, add the chili peppers and the spices .
Cover the pan and sauté for a few minutes . Meanwhile, bring some salted water to a boil. Drop in the carrots and potatoes into the boiling water and cook them for 10 minutes.
Reduce the heat of the pan with the spices and add the leek . Stir and sauté for a few minutes. Now add the zucchini and pumpkin , stir again, and cook for a few minutes.
Salt the vegetables , then use a slotted spoon to lift the potatoes and carrots and transfer them, gradually, to the pan along with the other vegetables , stirring gently .
Salt, pepper, add the sugar and cook over medium heat for another 10 minutes. Stir occasionally. Finally, season with white wine vinegar . Serve the spiced stewed vegetables hot.
Store the spiced stewed vegetables in the fridge for a couple of days. It is possible to freeze them after cooking.
To flavor the vegetables, you can use the spices you prefer: paprika, nutmeg, powdered ginger, curry, and many others!
