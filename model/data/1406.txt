name:
Pasta salad with asparagus, melon and goat cheese
ingredients:
Whole wheat Fusilli pasta: 11.3oz(320 g)
Asparagus: 1.1lbs(480 g)- to clean
Melon: 1.1lbs(490 g)- to clean
Goat: 4.2oz(120 g)
Gaeta olives: 1.4oz(40 g)- pitted
Lemon peel: 1
Extra virgin olive oil: to taste
Fine salt: to taste
Black pepper: to taste
Basil: to taste
Radicchio: 6.7oz(190 g)

info:
Difficulty: Easy
Prep time: 10 min
Cook time: 15 min
Serving: 4

To prepare the whole wheat pasta salad with asparagus, melon, and goat cheese, first clean the asparagus: remove the white part of the stalk which is tougher . Peel the stalk with a vegetable peeler to remove the fibrous outer layer . Now cut the asparagus into slices excluding the tips.
Cut the tips in half lengthwise . Heat a little oil in a pan, add the sliced asparagus and sauté for 5 minutes over medium heat. Halfway through cooking, also add the tips which are softer and require a shorter time .
Set aside the cooked asparagus . Meanwhile, place a pot full of salted water on the stove and bring it to a boil, it will be used for cooking the pasta. Cut the previously washed and dried radicchio leaves into strips . Now take care of the melon: cut the skin after leveling the ends.
Remove the internal seeds of the melon and cut the pulp into small pieces . In the meantime, cook the pasta al dente , adjust according to the time indicated on the package.
Place the radicchio in a bowl, also add the melon , the olives , and the asparagus sautéed in the pan .
Flavor with basil leaves , salt, pepper , and dress with olive oil. Now place the goat cheese in another bowl and flavor with lemon zest .
Add also a little olive oil, salt, pepper, and mix . When the pasta is ready, drain it directly into the bowl with the dressing , mix, and serve the dish garnished with pieces of goat cheese .
The pasta salad keeps for a couple of days in the refrigerator, covered with food wrap. Freezing is not recommended.
If you don't like radicchio, you can replace it with arugula.
