name:
White Russian
ingredients:
Vodka: 6.8oz(200 ml)
Coffee liqueur: 2.7oz(80 ml)
Fresh liquid cream: (80 g)
Ice: to taste

info:
Difficulty: Very easy
Prep time: 10 min
Makes: 4 pieces
Cost: Low

instructions:
To prepare the White Russian, start by pouring 2-3 ice cubes, or enough to fill Â¾, into a low tumbler glass . Add 5 cl of plain vodka and 2 cl of coffee liqueur .
Stir with a teaspoon and set aside for a moment. Pour the cold cream into the shaker , close with the smaller cup
and shake for a few seconds to get a fluffier mixture . Tap the smaller cup to detach it from the larger one and place the strainer on the shaker. Strain the cream by pouring it into the glass and here is your White Russian ready: drink it immediately !
Any form of storage is not recommended.
Enhance your White Russian by grating chocolate directly over the glass or adding a pinch of licorice powder!
