name:
Sea bass in foil
ingredients:
Sea bass (sea bass): 1.75lbs(800 g)
Cherry tomatoes: 1cup(150 g)
Pine nuts: 15
Taggiasca olives: 1.5oz(40 g)
Garlic: 2
Lemons: 1
Parsley: 1tbsp(10 g)

info:
Difficulty: Easy
Prep time: 30 min
Cook time: 35 min
Serving: 2
Cost: High

instructions:
To prepare Sea bass in foil , wash and halve the cherry tomatoes . Slice the lemon . In a bowl, gather the cherry tomatoes, pine nuts, and olives .
Chop parsley and garlic previously peeled and add to the cherry tomatoes. Season with oil , salt , and pepper.
Now clean the fish by cutting off the fins , and descale it with the appropriate tool . Then, after gutting and rinsing it under running water both inside and out, stuff the inside with lemon slices .
On a surface, place a sheet of aluminum foil and then a sheet of parchment paper on top. Place the sea bass here and distribute the cherry tomatoes on the sides . Close the two sheets one over the other , taking care to seal the sides well. Bake in a preheated static oven at 340Â°F for 30/35 minutes.
Once cooked, remove the parcel from the oven, open it and clean the fish while still hot. Use a fork to lift and peel off the skin . Serve the sea bass fillets en papillote while still hot with the cherry tomato, pine nut, and olive dressing .
Sea bass in foil can be stored for a couple of days in the refrigerator. You can freeze it if you have used fresh ingredients.
The sea bass will be cooked when the skin lifts off effortlessly and the flesh is white and tender.
