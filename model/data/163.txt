name:
Summer crunch with tomatoes
ingredients:
Type 00 flour: 2cups(250 g)
Tomatoes: 0.7cup(120 g)
White onions: 0.8cup(120 g)
Pecorino Romano PDO cheese: 2.5oz(70 g)
Corn flour: 1spoonful
Tomato paste: 4 ½tsp(10 g)
Water: 1.3cups(300 g)
Extra virgin olive oil: 1.7tbsp(25 g)
Fine salt: 2tsp

info:
Difficulty: Easy
Prep time: 20 min
Cook time: 1 h 5 min
Serving: 8
Cost: Low

instructions:
To prepare the summer crunch with tomatoes, first slice the tomatoes and the onion and set aside. Put the flour in a bowl and slowly pour in the water, mixing with a whisk to avoid lumps .
Season with salt and tomato paste , then mix again. Next, add the grated pecorino cheese .
Add the tomatoes and the onion , then mix everything well .
Pour the mixture into a 12.5-inch non-stick pan, greased and floured , and level it well . Sprinkle the surface with cornmeal .
Drizzle with the oil and bake in a static oven at 390°F for 50 minutes, then increase the temperature to 445°F and continue baking in fan mode for another 10-15 minutes. Your summer crunch with tomatoes is ready to be enjoyed !
The summer crunch with tomatoes can be stored in the refrigerator, in a closed container or covered with plastic wrap, for 2-3 days.
The cornmeal can be substituted with the same amount of breadcrumbs.
You can use any type of tomatoes you prefer, such as regular or cherry tomatoes.
If you wish, you can enrich the crunch with black or taggiasca olives and desalted capers for a Mediterranean touch, or flavor the dough with fresh basil, oregano, or lemon thyme!
