name:
Microwave Gratinated Vegetables
ingredients:
Potatoes: 9.2oz(260 g)
Carrots: 1 ½cup(200 g)
Red peppers: 2 ⅓cups(350 g)
Zucchini: 8.8oz(250 g)
Eggplant: 4.2oz(120 g)
Red onions: 1 ½cup(220 g)
Pecorino Romano PDO cheese: 1.75oz(50 g)
Breadcrumbs: ⅛cup(15 g)
Garlic: 2cloves
Extra virgin olive oil: to taste
Black pepper: to taste
Fine salt: to taste
Parsley: to taste

info:
Difficulty: Easy
Prep time: 20 min
Cook time: 45 min
Serving: 6
Cost: Low

instructions:
To prepare the microwave gratinated vegetables, start by washing the vegetables. Peel the potatoes and cut them into large cubes of about 1 inch , do the same with the peppers and carrots .
Now trim the eggplant and also cut it into 1-inch cubes , do the same with the zucchini . Clean the onion and slice it into rounds about 0.5 inches thick .
Now finely chop the parsley . In a large bowl, pour in the grated Pecorino cheese and breadcrumbs , season with the parsley .
Mix and set aside. Gather all the vegetables in a bowl and also add the peeled garlic with the core removed .
Season the vegetables with pepper , salt , and extra virgin olive oil . Mix carefully to flavor.
Transfer the vegetables to a microwave-safe baking dish . Pour the cheese and breadcrumb mix over them , then cook in the microwave at 650 W for 40 minutes, finally finishing the gratin by setting the grill for 4 minutes. Remove and enjoy!
Microwave gratinated vegetables can be stored in the refrigerator for a couple of days. They can also be frozen when cooked; it is not advisable to freeze them raw.
In addition to varying the vegetables, you can make a different coating with other herbs like thyme or rosemary and replace the Pecorino with Parmesan.
