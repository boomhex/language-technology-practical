name:
Salad with Prawns and Avocado
ingredients:
Prawns: 0.9lb(400 g)- to clean
Valerian (songino): 13cups(400 g)
Avocado: 1
Lemons: 1
Extra virgin olive oil: to taste
Fine salt: to taste
Black pepper: to taste
Lemon juice: 2tbsp(30 g)
Lemon peel: to taste

info:
Difficulty: Easy
Prep time: 15 min
Cook time: 5 min
Serving: 4
Cost: Average

instructions:
To prepare the salad with prawns and avocado, first focus on How to clean shrimp : remove the head , then remove the shell and the intestine located on the back of the shellfish .
In a pan, heat a drizzle of oil , then lay the prawns and brown over medium heat on both sides, for a total of 5 minutes . Set aside.
Move on to the avocado cream: take the pulp of the avocado and transfer it to a bowl along with the lemon zest and juice , then salt and pepper .
Add the oil and blend with an immersion blender until smooth and homogeneous .
Now clean the second avocado, remove the pit and cut the flesh into slices . Place a bowl with the avocado cream in the center of a serving plate , then distribute the lamb's lettuce all around .
Place the avocado slices and lemon slices on the lamb's lettuce. Season with salt and pepper .
Finish with a drizzle of oil and finally add the prawns . Your salad with prawns and avocado is ready to be served !
It is recommended to consume the salad with prawns and avocado as soon as it is ready.
The avocado cream can be stored in the refrigerator, in an airtight container, for up to 2 days.
You can replace the prawns with langoustines, scampi, or shrimps.
For a more intense flavor, you can enrich the avocado cream with mustard, chives, or a few drops of soy sauce.
