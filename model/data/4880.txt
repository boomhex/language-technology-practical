name:
Savory pan-fried radicchio
ingredients:
Radicchio: 1- head
Curry: to taste
Extra virgin olive oil: to taste
Fine salt: to taste
Black pepper: to taste
Feta cheese: 1.1oz(30 g)
Parsley: to taste

info:
Difficulty: Very easy
Prep time: 10 min
Cook time: 10 min
Serving: 2
Cost: Low

instructions:
Savory pan-fried radicchio is an easy and quick side dish: a tempting and original idea for cooking this purplish-colored vegetable, characterized by a typical bitter taste. A feature not always appreciated by everyone, we decided to soften it with a pinch of curry, for a pleasant spicy note. The crumbled feta also adds a touch of freshness to the saut√©ed radicchio in a pan and creates a delicious color contrast. Great to pair with meat and fish main courses, savory pan-fried radicchio can also transform into a unique dish with a bit of creativity!

To prepare savory pan-fried radicchio, first wash the head of radicchio and remove the base, keeping the stem intact . Cut the head in half and then into quarters .
Transfer the radicchio to a bowl and season with oil , salt, pepper, and curry . Gently massage with your hands to distribute the seasoning evenly .
Heat a non-stick pan, place the radicchio and sear it for a few seconds over medium heat, then cover with a lid ; this way a bit of moisture will form, preventing the radicchio from burning. Cook for 4-5 minutes, occasionally shaking the pan, then turn the radicchio and continue cooking for another couple of minutes. Transfer the radicchio to a plate and garnish with crumbled feta .
Finish with chopped parsley, a drizzle of oil , and a grind of pepper . Your savory pan-fried radicchio is ready to be served !
Savory pan-fried radicchio can be stored in the refrigerator for one day in an airtight container.
If you wish, you can also add a pinch of cumin to enhance the spicy note. For a more scenic look, you can use tardive radicchio!
