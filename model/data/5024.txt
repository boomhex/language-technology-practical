name:
Cheese Balls
ingredients:
Caciocavallo cheese: 10oz(280 g)
Pecorino toscano cheese: 9.9oz(280 g)
Bread: 3.5oz(100 g)- crumb
Eggs: 2
Thyme: 4sprigs
Fine salt: to taste
Black pepper: to taste
Peanut seed oil: to taste

info:
Difficulty: Easy
Prep time: 20 min
Cook time: 20 min
Makes: 36 pieces
Cost: Average

instructions:
Looking for a tasty appetizer and finger food? Try our cheese balls, delicious fried bites with an explosive flavor! They are great to enjoy on their own or dipped in aromatic sauces, and they are perfect to serve for an aperitif. Among homemade recipes, meatballs are undoubtedly the most appreciated, thanks to their versatility and ease of preparation... cheese balls are also ideal to make with the little ones, who love getting their hands dirty!
Discover more meatball recipes:

To prepare the cheese balls, remove the crust from the cheeses and grate them with a fine-grated grater . Set the cheeses aside and move on to the bread.
Remove the crust from the bread and cut the crumb into cubes , then blend with a mixer .
Once you have crumbs , mix them with the cheeses and flavor with thyme .
Salt , pepper, and finally add the eggs . Knead with your hands until you get a fairly compact mixture .
Form balls weighing about 0.7 oz each . With these quantities, you will get 36 balls . Now fry in hot oil at 340Â°F . Fry a few balls at a time as they tend to stick together during cooking, and the temperature would drop.
Once golden, it will only take a few minutes, drain them and transfer them to absorbent paper . Serve the cheese balls piping hot .
We recommend consuming the cheese balls immediately.
If you prefer to prepare the balls the day before, you can store them in the refrigerator and fry them before serving.
You can use aged cheeses of your choice such as Parmigiano Reggiano or Grana Padano.
The balls can also be flavored with chopped rosemary, dried or fresh oregano.
