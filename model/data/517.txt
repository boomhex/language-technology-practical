name:
Pak choi in wok
ingredients:
Bak choi: 1lb(450 g)
Fresh ginger: 1.8oz(50 g)
Garlic: 2cloves
Oyster Sauce: 1spoonful
Sesame seeds: to taste
Peanut seed oil: 2spoonfuls- (or sunflower)
Fine salt: to taste
Black pepper: to taste

info:
Difficulty: Easy
Prep time: 10 min
Cook time: 10 min
Serving: 4
Cost: Average

instructions:
To prepare the pak choi in a wok, first wash the pak choi and remove the base of the stem, then cut away the leaves and set them aside . Divide the stalk into 4 parts lengthwise . Finely chop the garlic cloves .
Finally, peel the ginger root and cut it into sticks . Place the wok over the heat, and when it is very hot, pour in the vegetable oil .
Add the garlic , ginger , and pak choi stems .
Also add the leaves and stir-fry everything over high heat for a couple of minutes. Salt , pepper, and season with oyster sauce .
Continue cooking for about 8 minutes, stirring with chopsticks , and adding another dash of vegetable oil . When the pak choi stems are tender, turn off the heat and plate. Serve your pak choi in a wok garnished with sesame seeds !
The pak choi in a wok can be stored in the refrigerator for one day, in an airtight container.
Oyster sauce can be purchased at ethnic food stores or well-stocked supermarkets.
Pak choi is also excellent steamed and seasoned with soy sauce and ginger!
