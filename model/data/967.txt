name:
Coconut Bundt Cake
ingredients:
Eggs: 4
Coconut milk: 2cups(150 g)
Shredded coconut: 1 ¾cup(150 g)
Type 00 flour: 2cups(250 g)
Sugar: 1cup(200 g)
Baking powder: 4tsp(16 g)
Powdered sugar: to taste
Shredded coconut: to taste

info:
Difficulty: Easy
Prep time: 15 min
Cook time: 45 min
Serving: 8
Cost: Low

instructions:
To make the coconut bundt cake, pour the whole eggs into a large bowl , add the sugar and whisk with an electric mixer on high speed .
When the mixture is light and frothy, pour in the coconut milk , once incorporated, also add the shredded coconut , the sifted flour
and also the sifted baking powder . Once combined , pour the batter into a greased and floured 8-inch diameter and 4-inch height bundt pan .
Bake the bundt cake in a preheated static oven at 350°F for 40-45 minutes, depending on the power of your oven. Once baked, take the cake out of the oven and let it cool slightly , then turn it out onto a plate .
Garnish with powdered sugar and shredded coconut . The coconut bundt cake is ready to be enjoyed .
The coconut bundt cake can be stored out of the refrigerator for 3-4 days, covered with a glass dome. After baking, it can be frozen and kept for a month.
As an alternative to coconut milk, you can use the same amount of coconut yogurt. The recipe calls for shredded coconut, which is grated coconut, not to be confused with coconut flour, which has a finer texture and would give the batter a different consistency.
