name:
Baked Chickpea Omelette
ingredients:
Precooked chickpeas: 1cup(250 g)
Extra virgin olive oil: 4 ¾tbsp(70 g)
Fine salt: to taste
Black pepper: to taste
Parsley: 3tufts- to chop
Thyme: 3sprigs
Eggs: 8
Cow's milk ricotta cheese: (150 g)
Cluster tomatoes: 6.3oz(180 g)
Fennel seeds: 1tsp
Red onions: 2.5oz(70 g)
Fresh chili pepper: - half
Frozen peas: 1cup(150 g)

info:
Difficulty: Average
Prep time: 20 min
Cook time: 1 h 20 min
Serving: 4
Cost: Average

instructions:
To make the baked chickpea omelette, start by preparing all the ingredients: peel and chop the onion , wash the tomatoes and dice them , then finely chop the parsley, thyme, and half of a fresh red chili pepper (about a teaspoon) .
Sauté the onion in three tablespoons of extra virgin olive oil and, when it is golden, add the fennel seeds , the chopped chili pepper ,
the chickpeas , taken out of the can, rinsed with water and drained, the peas, which you will have defrosted in the meantime, and finally the tomato . Let everything cook over medium heat for 10 minutes.
Beat the whole eggs with a whisk , then add the ricotta , the thyme ,
the parsley and mix all the ingredients. When the vegetables are cooked, add them to the egg mixture and mix well . Rinse a sheet of parchment paper under running water to make it more malleable ,
then wring it out well and place it in a baking dish with a diameter of 11 inches , pour the egg and vegetable mixture and bake everything at 375°F for 60 minutes in a fan oven . The baked chickpea omelette is ready , serve it at room temperature, cut into slices or small squares.
You can store the baked chickpea omelette for a couple of days in the refrigerator, placed in an airtight container.
If you prefer, you can replace the parsley with finely chopped coriander.
