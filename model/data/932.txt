name:
Piadina without lard
ingredients:
Type 00 flour: 2 ¾cups(350 g)
Water: 0.7cup(175 g)
Extra virgin olive oil: 2 ½tbsp(35 g)
Fine salt: 1pinch

info:
Difficulty: Easy
Prep time: 10 min
Cook time: 20 min
Serving: 4
Cost: Very low
Note + 30 min. of dough resting

instructions:
To prepare the piadina without lard, take a large bowl and pour in the flour, water , olive oil , and salt .
Knead quickly with your hands to obtain a compact and homogeneous dough , then transfer the dough to the work surface and divide it into 4 parts with a dough scraper . Quickly roll each portion of dough between your hands to form a ball .
Place the obtained balls on a tray, cover with plastic wrap and let them rest at room temperature for 30 minutes . After this time, roll each ball with a rolling pin on a lightly floured surface to a thickness of 1/8 inch .
Heat a large pan well and cook one piadina at a time for about 2-3 minutes per side, rotating it with your hand to prevent air bubbles from forming . Stack the piadinas on top of each other as they are ready . Your piadina without lard is ready to be filled and enjoyed!
It is recommended to consume the piadina without lard as soon as it is ready, to best enjoy its fragrance. They can be stored well covered for up to 2-3 days.
You can freeze them after cooking and letting them cool.
If you wish, you can add a pinch of baking soda to the dough to obtain a more fragrant piadina!
