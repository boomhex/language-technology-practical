name:
Red mullets Livorno style
ingredients:
Red mullet: 2.2lbs(1 kg)
Canned tomatoes: 1.7cups(400 g)
Garlic: 3cloves
Parsley: ½cup(30 g)
Extra virgin olive oil: to taste
Fine salt: to taste
Black pepper: to taste

info:
Difficulty: Easy
Prep time: 15 min
Cook time: 20 min
Serving: 4
Cost: Low

instructions:
To prepare red mullets Livorno style, first peel the garlic cloves and chop them . Wash and chop the parsley leaves .
Take care of cleaning the red mullets: cut the belly with scissors and remove the innards , then rinse them carefully in the sink .
In a pan, pour a drizzle of oil, the chopped garlic and parsley , briefly sauté , then pour in the tomato pulp .
Add salt and add the whole eviscerated red mullets . Cook on a very low heat for about 20 minutes, taking care to occasionally cover the fish with the sauce .
Halfway through cooking, gently turn the red mullets, as they tend to flake . Once ready, pepper to taste and serve the red mullets Livorno style garnished with fresh parsley .
Consume the red mullets Livorno style as soon as they are ready, but if you prefer, you can store them for one day in the refrigerator in an airtight container.
Freezing is not recommended.
If you wish, you can add fresh chili pepper to spice up the sauce!
