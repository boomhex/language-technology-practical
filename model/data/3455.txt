name:
Gnocchetti with crab meat and shrimp
ingredients:
Crab: 5.3oz(150 g)- meat
Shrimps: 3.5oz(100 g)
Potato gnocchetti pasta: 2cups(350 g)
Shallot: 1
Butter: 0.7tbsp(10 g)
Tomato purée: ¾cup(200 g)
Cream cheese: 1spoonful
Parsley: 1tuft
White wine: ½glass

info:
Difficulty: Very easy
Prep time: 10 min
Cook time: 20 min
Serving: 4
Cost: Average

instructions:
To prepare the gnocchetti with crab meat and shrimp, start by cleaning and boiling the shrimp in salted water . Then chop the shallot and sauté it with the oil and a pat of butter , then add the crab meat
and the shrimp . Then pour in the white wine and let it evaporate. Now add the tomato puree and salt.
Once the sauce has thickened, add a generous tablespoon of classic Philadelphia and mix everything together. Cook the gnocchi in plenty of salted water , drain them and toss them in the pan with the sauce. Finally, add a handful of chopped parsley and serve the gnocchetti immediately!
