name:
Savory Snails with Vegetables and Bacon
ingredients:
Water: 1 ¼cup(300 ml)- lukewarm
Type 00 flour: 4cups(500 g)
Fresh brewer's yeast: 0.5oz(12 g)
Extra virgin olive oil: 3spoonfuls
Fine salt: 2tsp(10 g)
Sugar: 1tsp
Bell peppers: - half red and half yellow
Zucchini: 0.8cup(100 g)
Salt: to taste
Olive oil: 1spoonful
Caciocavallo cheese: - (lerdammer, provola, emmentaler, etc...) sliced 250 gr
Pancetta: - thinly sliced coppata 125 gr

info:
Difficulty: Easy
Prep time: 30 min
Cook time: 15 min
Makes: pieces
Cost: Low
Note + 4 hours of rising time

Prepare the Bread dough by following the steps you find by clicking Bread dough then let it rise until it doubles in volume . In the meantime, wash and cut the vegetables into small strips, and sauté them for a few minutes in a pan with a tablespoon of oil, then salt them and turn off the heat. Divide the dough into two parts, which you will roll out with a rolling pin to form two rectangles about a quarter of an inch thick .
On one rectangle of dough, spread the vegetables leaving a couple of inches on the edges , which you will brush with a bit of water . Roll the filled dough onto itself to form a roll that you will seal and let rise with the seam facing down.
Take the second rectangle of dough, fill it with slices of cheese and bacon , and roll it up brushing the edges with water to seal it.
Let the two rolls rise for at least an hour, then cut them into slices at least an inch thick which you will place on baking sheets lined with parchment paper . Let the obtained slices rise for another hour, then bake them in a preheated oven at 356°F for about 15 minutes. Once baked, let the snails cool and then serve them.
Store the cooled snails in a plastic bag or in a tin box closed with a lid.
Savory snails are a classic for picnics or day trips, but also a tasty snack to serve before dinner.
