name:
Baked pasta with zucchini cream
ingredients:
Sedani Rigati pasta: 11.3oz(320 g)
Zucchini: 2 ½cups(300 g)
Edam cheese: 7.1oz(200 g)- (to grate)
Parmigiano Reggiano PDO cheese: 1.8oz(50 g)- (to grate)
Fresh liquid cream: ¾cup(200 g)
Garlic: 1clove
Fresh chili pepper: 1
Fine salt: to taste
Mint: to taste
Thyme: to taste
Extra virgin olive oil: to taste

info:
Difficulty: Easy
Prep time: 20 min
Cook time: 20 min
Serving: 4
Cost: Low

instructions:
To prepare the baked pasta with zucchini cream, first wash, trim, and slice the zucchinis into rounds about 1/4 inch thick . Pour a drizzle of extra virgin olive oil into the pan, add the garlic and the crushed but not cut chili pepper . Let it sauté for 2-3 minutes.
Then add the zucchinis and the aromatic herbs. Cook over high heat for 5 minutes. Salt to taste , flavor with fresh mint and thyme leaves. Then remove the garlic and chili pepper .

Take half of the zucchinis, transfer them to a tall glass with 2 tablespoons of extra virgin olive oil and some mint leaves to taste . Blend with an immersion mixer to obtain a cream.
Pour the obtained cream into a large bowl . Grate the Edamer with a wide-hole grater and set aside. In another tall glass, pour the finely grated Parmigiano Reggiano DOP .
Add the fresh liquid cream and blend with the immersion mixer to obtain a cream. Then pepper to taste and set aside .

Cook the pasta in boiling salted water to taste and drain al dente . Drain the pasta into the bowl with the zucchini cream and also add the remaining zucchinis .
Finally, pour the cream of cream and Parmigiano Reggiano DOP . Mix well and assemble the baking dish of pasta: pour half of the seasoned pasta into the baking dish and sprinkle the surface with some of the grated Edamer. Cover with the remaining pasta .
Finish with the remaining grated Edamer , zucchini rounds, and a sprinkle of pepper . Bake in a static oven at 410°F for 5 minutes, then another 5 minutes under the grill. Remove from oven and scent with fresh aromatic herbs .
The baked pasta with zucchini cream keeps in the fridge for a couple of days. We do not recommend freezing it.
To flavor the pasta, you can also use Pecorino, Caciocavallo, or Fontina, based on your taste.
