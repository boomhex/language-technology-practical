name:
Pasta with Chicken and Mushrooms
ingredients:
Bow tie pasta: 11.3oz(320 g)
Chicken breast: 8.8oz(250 g)
Champignon mushrooms: 14.1oz(400 g)- to clean
Type 00 flour: 1 ⅓tbsp(20 g)
Garlic: 1clove- chopped
Dried chili pepper: to taste
Salt: to taste
Extra virgin olive oil: to taste
White wine: to taste
Thyme: to taste
Butter: 2tbsp(30 g)
Fresh liquid cream: 0.6cup(150 g)
Chicken broth: 1cup(250 g)
Dijon mustard: 1tsp
Oregano: to taste- dry
Parsley: to taste- chopped
Parmigiano Reggiano PDO cheese: ¼cup(50 g)- grated
Black pepper: to taste
Basil: to taste

info:
Difficulty: Easy
Prep time: 20 min
Cook time: 35 min
Serving: 4
Cost: Low

instructions:
Pasta with chicken and mushrooms is a creamy first course inspired by the famous mushroom chicken pasta, a dish from American cuisine that is easy and delightful. In this version, the chicken and champignon mushrooms are cooked in a cream based on butter and cream, flavored with mustard and herbs. It's perfect for a family meal, with friends, or whenever you want to serve a tasty and hearty dish that anyone can enjoy. Try the pasta with chicken and mushrooms and let yourself be won over by its irresistible taste!

To prepare pasta with chicken and mushrooms, first clean the champignons and cut them into quarters . In a non-stick pan, pour the oil and add the minced garlic and dried chili pepper .
Add the mushrooms , season with salt , and sauté over high heat for 10 minutes .
When the mushrooms are ready, remove them from the pan and set aside . In the same pan, pour more oil, add the chicken cut into strips , and season with salt .
Add thyme and flour . Deglaze with white wine , allow the alcohol to evaporate completely, then remove the chicken from the pan and set aside.
In the same pan, add butter and let it melt over medium-low heat. Add mustard and chicken broth .
Finally, add cream and bring to a boil. Add the mushrooms and chicken . Cook over low heat for 10 minutes.
Cook the pasta in plenty of salted boiling water . Once ready, drain it directly into the pan . Add oregano and chopped parsley .
Add grated Parmesan and a sprinkle of pepper . Plate and finish with a few basil leaves. Your pasta with chicken and mushrooms is ready to be enjoyed !
Pasta with chicken and mushrooms is best consumed immediately, but if you have leftovers, you can store it in the refrigerator in an airtight container for up to 2 days. When you want to serve it again, heat it up by adding a splash of milk or broth to restore its creaminess.
For a lighter version, replace the cream with milk and a tablespoon of cornstarch to thicken the sauce. A pinch of smoked paprika or curry can give it an original touch.
