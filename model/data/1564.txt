name:
Dressed Potatoes
ingredients:
Potatoes: 4- medium
White onions: 1- large
Salted capers: 1tbsp(15 g)
Green olives: 5.6oz(160 g)
Extra virgin olive oil: 2spoonfuls
White wine vinegar: 3.4tbsp(50 ml)
Sugar: 2tsp
Fine salt: to taste

info:
Difficulty: Easy
Prep time: 20 min
Cook time: 20 min
Serving: 4
Cost: Low

instructions:
To prepare the dressed potatoes, first peel the onion. Then cut it in half and then into slices (1-2). Transfer them to a pan, add the oil and set them aside.
Move on to the potatoes: peel them and gradually transfer them into a bowl full of water . Then cut them into large cubes about an inch (2.54 cm) in size .
and transfer them along with the onions . Move everything to the heat , and add salt .
As soon as the oil begins to sizzle, cover with a lid and cook for 15 minutes. In the meantime, desalinate the capers and drain the olives .
Then in a small bowl, pour the vinegar and add the sugar , then stir to dissolve it. After 15 minutes, remove the lid and add capers ,
olives , and the vinegar mixed with sugar . Let it cook for another 5 minutes on high heat without the lid. Turn off the heat, let the potatoes cool, plate and serve .
Dressed potatoes can be stored in the refrigerator, in an airtight container, for 2 days. Freezing is not recommended.
Instead of green olives, you can use black olives.
