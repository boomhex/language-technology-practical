name:
Panettone muffins
ingredients:
Panettone: 5.3oz(150 g)
Baking soda: ½tsp
Butter: 0.8cup(190 g)- at room temperature
Type 00 flour: 3 ¼cups(400 g)
Whole milk: ¾cup(200 ml)
Baking powder: 1bag
Fine salt: 1pinch
Eggs: 3- medium
Orange peel: 1
Sugar: 1cup(200 g)

info:
Difficulty: Easy
Prep time: 15 min
Cook time: 25 min
Makes: 15 pieces
Cost: Low

To prepare the panettone muffins, first shred the panettone into small pieces and set it aside . In a bowl, sift the flour together with the baking powder, baking soda, and salt . Using a stand mixer or an electric beater, cream the butter with sugar until it becomes creamy .
Add the eggs one at a time , the dry ingredients , and finally the milk in a thin stream , continuing to mix.
When the ingredients are well combined, add the grated zest of an untreated orange and the previously shredded panettone , then incorporate it into the mixture with a spatula .
Transfer the mixture into a piping bag , prepare a muffin pan with liners, and fill them to the edge . Bake them in a preheated oven at 350°F for 25 minutes : here are your panettone muffins ready!
The muffins can be frozen after baking and then thawed at room temperature and warmed in the oven for a few minutes. Once made, let them cool and store them under a glass dome or in a tin box for 4 days.
To make your muffins richer, you can add raisins or candied orange peel. They are excellent accompanied by a spoonful of custard.
