name:
Fried Spring Chicken
ingredients:
Young rooster: 1.1lbs(500 g)- whole
Rice flour: 0.375cup(60 g)
Water: ½cup(100 g)
Breadcrumbs: ¾cup(100 g)
Peanut seed oil: to taste
Fine salt: to taste

To make the fried spring chicken, take the whole chicken, place it on the cutting board with the breast facing up, and cut along both sides of the central bone , then give it a few taps to break the bone. Open the chicken and remove the bone . Turn it over and lightly press it with your hands .
Now prepare the batter: in a bowl, pour the flour and water , mix with a whisk until a well-blended mixture is obtained .


Dip the chicken in it and move it well so that the batter completely covers it . Then pass it through the breadcrumbs , which you have previously poured onto a plate, making sure not to leave any part uncovered.
Put the chicken on a clean plate and leave it in the refrigerator for at least 30 minutes; this step will help the breading adhere better. Meanwhile, heat the oil until it reaches 340°F (the perfect temperature for frying). After the necessary time, immerse the chicken in the oil and let it fry for about 5 minutes on one side without moving it, then turn it and let it fry until it is completely golden and crispy.
Drain it with a slotted spoon and place it on a tray lined with paper towels . Salt to taste and serve the fried spring chicken piping hot .
The fried spring chicken can be stored in the fridge for 2 days when closed, but it is recommended to consume it immediately to preserve its crispiness.
You can substitute rice flour with all-purpose flour.
