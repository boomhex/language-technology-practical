name:
Hummus with Artichoke Stems
ingredients:
Artichoke stalks: 2
Precooked chickpeas: ¾cup(200 g)
Lemon juice: ½
Tahini: 1spoonful
Garlic: 1clove- (optional)
Extra virgin olive oil: to taste
Fine salt: to taste
Mint: to taste

info:
Difficulty: Easy
Prep time: 10 min
Serving: 4
Cost: Low

instructions:
To prepare the hummus with artichoke stems, start with the How to clean artichokes . Using a peeler, remove the outermost part, which would be bitter . If there is still an artichoke present, cut it and set it aside for your recipes. Cut the artichoke stems into pieces .
Boil or steam them until they are tender . Place the chickpeas in a blender container , add the tahini and, if you prefer, garlic with the core removed.
Add the juice of half a lemon , the artichoke stems , and half a cup of oil .
Add salt and blend intermittently until you get a smooth cream . Transfer the hummus to a bowl, season with oil and mint leaves .
Hummus with artichoke stems can be stored in the refrigerator for up to one day.
You can enhance the hummus by adding paprika, either inside the cream or just as a decoration.
Discover other variations for all seasons, such as Pea hummus or Cherry Tomato Hummus !
