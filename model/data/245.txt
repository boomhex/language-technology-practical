name:
Black Venus rice with vegetables
ingredients:
Black Venus rice: 1.6cups(320 g)
Carrots: 7.1oz(200 g)- (to clean)
Zucchini: 2 ½cups(300 g)- (to be cleaned)
Peas: 1 ⅛cup(180 g)
Red onions: 3.2oz(90 g)- (to clean)
Feta cheese: 2oz(60 g)
Parsley: to taste
Extra virgin olive oil: to taste
Salt: to taste

info:
Difficulty: Easy
Prep time: 10 min
Cook time: 25 min
Serving: 4
Cost: Low

instructions:
To prepare Black Venus rice with vegetables, first cook the rice in salted boiling water for the time indicated on the package . Meanwhile, peel the carrots and cut them into cubes about 1/2 inch . Trim and cut the zucchini first into sticks and then into cubes of the same size.
Clean and thinly slice the onion . Sauté the onion for 2 minutes in a pan with a dash of oil , then add the carrots and zucchini .
Add the peas , salt , stir and cook over medium-high heat, stirring often, for 10 minutes .
At the end of cooking, add the chopped parsley for aroma . Drain the rice and transfer it to the pan , then stir to combine the ingredients and plate.
Finish with crumbled feta , a few parsley leaves, and a dash of oil . Your Black Venus rice with vegetables is ready to be enjoyed !
Black Venus rice with vegetables can be stored in the refrigerator in a closed container for 2 days.
For a crunchier note, you can add dried fruits like toasted almonds, walnuts, or sesame seeds.
For a vegan version, omit the feta.
Vary the recipe by using seasonal vegetables: for example, pumpkin and broccoli in winter, asparagus and snow peas in spring, cherry tomatoes and eggplants in summer!
