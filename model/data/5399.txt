name:
Pasta with Anchovy Cream
ingredients:
Bread: 4.2oz(120 g)
Anchovies in oil: 3.5oz(100 g)
Pine nuts: 1spoonful
Garlic: 1clove
Parsley: to taste
Whole milk: 1cup(70 g)
Extra virgin olive oil: 5 ½tbsp(80 g)
Spaghetti: 11.3oz(320 g)

info:
Difficulty: Easy
Prep time: 10 min
Cook time: 10 min
Serving: 4
Cost: Low

instructions:
Express pastas are our specialty: easy, fast, and super creamy, like this pasta with anchovy cream, a quick dish that is prepared with just a few ingredients that are always available in the pantry and fridge. This enveloping and tasty sauce is made in no time with the mixer, while the spaghetti cooks, optimizing your time in the kitchen. Try this pasta with rustic yet timeless flavors and don't miss other anchovy recipes :
To prepare the pasta with anchovy cream, cut the bread into slices , separate the crumb from the crust . Place the crust in the mixer and blend it coarsely .
Once the bread crumbs are ready , set them aside , and in the same mixer, place the crumb .
Add the anchovies, pine nuts , parsley, peeled and de-germed garlic, and milk .
Finish with the olive oil and blend to get a cream . Meanwhile, cook the spaghetti and finish preparing the sauce.
In a pan, heat a splash of olive oil and add the bread crumbs , sautéing just enough to brown them , and transfer them to a bowl. In the same pan, add the anchovy cream .
Thin with a small ladle of cooking water , heat over low heat and stir . Once ready, drain the spaghetti here , being sure to reserve the cooking water.
Thin further with cooking water, adjusting based on the consistency of the sauce . Sauté the pasta for a few more moments to flavor it . Serve the pasta with anchovy cream hot, garnishing the plates with chopped parsley and toasted bread crumbs .
We recommend consuming the pasta with anchovy cream immediately.
You can replace the pine nuts with other nuts to taste.
