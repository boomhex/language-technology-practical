instructions:
Pesto with zucchini flowers is a refined and surprising variant of the classic Pesto alla Genovese . This creamy and delicate sauce uses zucchini flowers, typical ingredients of Mediterranean cuisine, to create a fresh and original flavor. Pesto with zucchini flowers is perfect for dressing pasta dishes, but it can also be used to accompany bruschetta or enrich summer salads.
Its versatility makes it an excellent choice for those who wish to explore new combinations of flavors while maintaining the authenticity of Italian culinary tradition. With its soft and slightly floral taste, pesto with zucchini flowers is ideal for those looking for a gourmet alternative to traditional basil pesto!

To prepare the pesto with zucchini flowers, first clean the flowers with a brush or a slightly damp cloth, then open them and remove the inner pistil . In a mortar, place the peeled garlic clove and almonds .
Add a pinch of salt and crush with the pestle until you get a fine consistency .
Add the zucchini flowers to the mortar and gently crush until you have a homogeneous mixture . Add 1/2 cup of grated Parmesan cheese and continue to work the pesto, mixing well with the pestle.
Finally, pour 1/3 cup of extra virgin olive oil and mix again ; you should obtain a smooth and homogeneous cream . Finish with a few drops of lemon juice to taste. The pesto with zucchini flowers is ready to be used in your summer recipes!
If you don't consume the pesto with zucchini flowers immediately, store it in a well-covered container (preferably glass) in the refrigerator for 2 days.
You can replace almonds with pine nuts or walnuts.
Instead of Parmesan, you can opt for Pecorino if you prefer a more intense and bold flavor.
