name:
Turkey slices with fennel and pomegranate
ingredients:
Turkey breast: 1.8lbs(800 g)- whole
Fennel: 0.88lb(400 g)
Pomegranate: 0.9lb(400 g)- (1 medium)
Extra virgin olive oil: 6tbsp(80 g)
Black pepper: to taste
Fine salt: to taste
Dill: to taste
Fleur de sel: to taste

info:
Difficulty: Easy
Prep time: 25 min
Cook time: 50 min
Serving: 4
Cost: Low

instructions:

To prepare the turkey slices with fennel and pomegranate, start with the meat. On a hot grill, pour a drizzle of oil . Place the turkey on it and cook it over medium heat on the first side for about 25 minutes. After the first 25 minutes, turn it
and cook it for another 25 minutes on the other side . Meanwhile, prepare the dressing: take the pomegranate, cut it in half , and deseed it by collecting the seeds in a bowl ; set some aside for the final decoration.
Pour the rest into a blender and puree. Then pass the obtained puree through a sieve to filter the juice, which you can put in a tall glass. Add 40 g of oil , salt, and pepper.
Blend everything (10-11). Wash and trim the fennel to remove the green part .
Cut it in half, then slice it finely (13-14), and transfer it to a bowl to season with 2 tablespoons of oil , salt, and pepper.
Flavor with dill broken by hand . Mix and distribute on a serving plate .
Take the turkey, slice it to a thickness of about 0.4 inches for each slice (19-20), and distribute it on the bed of fennel .
Garnish with the pomegranate seeds set aside, sea salt flakes , and drizzle with the pomegranate and oil emulsion ; your turkey slices with fennel and pomegranate are ready to be served .
You can store the turkey slices for 1 day in the refrigerator.
Freezing is not recommended.
Add orange slices and orange juice to the fennel salad.
