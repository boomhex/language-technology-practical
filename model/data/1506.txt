name:
Stuffed figs covered in chocolate
ingredients:
Dried figs: 14
Dark chocolate: 10.6oz(300 g)
Peeled almonds: 4
Walnuts: 5
Whole peeled hazelnuts: 5

info:
Difficulty: Easy
Prep time: 15 min
Cook time: 10 min
Makes: 14 pieces
Cost: Low
Note + the time required for the chocolate coating to set

instructions:

To prepare the stuffed figs covered in chocolate, start by opening the back of the fig and stuffing it with a walnut half , a hazelnut , or an almond. Close it and continue in the same way with all the others.
Melt 7 oz of dark chocolate in the microwave (or bain-marie) at 113°F . Once melted, add the remaining 3.5 oz of chocolate to the bowl and mix well. With wide movements of the spatula, cool the chocolate down to 84-86°F. At this point, dip the stuffed figs one at a time
and with the help of a fork, let the excess chocolate drip off . Gradually place the figs on a tray with acetate or parchment paper . Let the chocolate crystallize and once hardened, serve your stuffed figs covered with chocolate .
The stuffed figs covered in chocolate can be stored for 1 week at room temperature in a cool, dry place.
Freezing is not recommended.
You can also chop walnuts, hazelnuts, and almonds together, add some orange zest, and stuff each fig with this mix.
