name:
Quick pasta with robiola and bresaola
ingredients:
Pasta: 11.3oz(320 g)
Robiola cheese: 7.1oz(200 g)
Bresaola: 5.3oz(150 g)- (sliced)
Arugula: 2cups(40 g)
Extra virgin olive oil: to taste
Fine salt: to taste
Lemon peel: to taste

info:
Difficulty: Easy
Prep time: 15 min
Cook time: 10 min
Serving: 4
Cost: Low

instructions:
To prepare the quick pasta with robiola and bresaola, bring water to a boil to cook the pasta. In the meantime, prepare the sauce by peeling the lemon zest with a peeler, making sure to take only the yellow part . Finely chop the zest with a knife . In a bowl, mix the robiola to soften it, then season with salt and pepper.
and a drizzle of olive oil . Finally, flavor with some of the chopped zest , keeping the rest aside to garnish the plates when serving. Cut the bresaola slices into thin strips . Set some aside,
and add the rest to the robiola cream , then mix to combine . Cook the pasta for the cooking time indicated on the package .
Thin the sauce with a ladle of cooking water to make it more fluid . Once the pasta is cooked, drain it directly into the bowl with the sauce . Tear the arugula with your hands
and add it to the pasta . Mix to flavor and serve the quick pasta with robiola and bresaola immediately, garnishing the plates with lemon zest and bresaola strips set aside .
We recommend consuming immediately, but it is also possible to enjoy the pasta cold. Freezing is not recommended.
To enjoy the quick pasta with robiola and bresaola cold, simply drain the pasta, season it with a little oil, and let it cool to room temperature.
Then add the raw sauce to create a quick and tasty summer dish!
