name:
Coffee milkshake with almond milk
ingredients:
Almond milk: 1cup(250 g)
Instant coffee: 2spoonfuls- Nescafè Classic
Vanilla ice cream: 2 ¼cups(300 g)
Raspberries: ¾cup(100 g)
Brown sugar: 1spoonful
Fresh liquid cream: to taste- whipped
Dark chocolate: to taste
White chocolate: to taste
Almond slices: to taste
Raspberries: 3

info:
Difficulty: Easy
Prep time: 15 min
Cook time: 10 min
Serving: 1

instructions:
When summer turns up the heat in Italy, everyone’s looking for something cold and delicious. And you know what? A coffee frappé with almond milk and vanilla ice cream is just the ticket. Seriously good. This refreshing drink has a unique flavor, thanks to the smooth, nutty taste of almond milk and a scoop of creamy vanilla ice cream that melts perfectly into the coffee.

To prepare the coffee milkshake with almond milk, first heat about 2 tablespoons of almond milk . Add the instant coffee and stir until it's dissolved . Let it cool completely.
In the meantime, prepare the raspberry coulis: pour the raspberries into a small pan , add the brown sugar , and cook for 5 minutes over low heat .
After 5 minutes, strain the mixture and collect the coulis in a small bowl .
At this point, melt the dark chocolate and the white chocolate in 2 separate bowls in the microwave. Take a glass and decorate the inside with drizzles of white chocolate using a teaspoon .
Do the same with the dark chocolate . Then dip the rim of the glass in the remaining dark chocolate and decorate it with the almond flakes . Set aside until completely solidified.
Proceed to prepare the milkshake. In the bowl of a blender, pour the vanilla ice cream , the cold coffee latte , and the remaining almond milk , then close with the lid and blend everything.
When you have achieved a nice creamy consistency , pour your milkshake into the glass . Decorate with the whipped cream that you previously whipped .
Garnish the cream with the raspberry coulis and fresh raspberries . Add a straw and serve your coffee milkshake with almond milk immediately !
We recommend preparing the milkshake on the spot.
