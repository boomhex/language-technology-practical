name:
Spinach dumplings in taleggio sauce
ingredients:
Potatoes: 1.5lbs(700 g)- starchy
Type 00 flour: 1.6cups(200 g)
Spinach: 4 Â½cups(400 g)
Fine salt: to taste
Taleggio cheese: 7oz(200 g)
Fresh liquid cream: 1.1cups(250 ml)
White pepper: to taste
Chives: to taste

info:
Difficulty: Easy
Prep time: 20 min
Cook time: 10 min
Serving: 4
Cost: Average
Note + the preparation time for the green dumplings (about 1 h)

instructions:
Prepare the spinach dumplings following our recipe found by clicking Green gnocchetti . Remove the rind from the taleggio and cut the cheese into pieces ; pour the cream into a pan
and add the taleggio cut into pieces and let it melt over low heat, then let it thicken slightly, add ground white pepper , adjust salt if necessary, and turn off the heat. Then boil the dumplings in plenty of salted water ,
when they float to the surface , drain them well and toss them into a high-sided pan along with the previously prepared taleggio sauce . Mix for a few seconds until everything is combined and plate by sprinkling with chopped chives and garnishing with a few whole strands of the same.
