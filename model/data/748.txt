name:
Gong Bao Chicken
ingredients:
Chicken breast: 0.9lb(400 g)
Green peppers: 1 ¼cup(110 g)
Red peppers: 4oz(110 g)
Onions: 3oz(80 g)
Peanuts: ½cup(70 g)
Soy sauce: 3spoonfuls
Hot sauce: 2tsp
Potato starch: 1spoonful
Fine salt: to taste
Black pepper: to taste
Rice wine: 2spoonfuls
Soy sauce: 2spoonfuls
Potato starch: 2spoonfuls

To prepare gong bao chicken, start by cutting the chicken breast first into strips and then into cubes . Place the chicken cubes in a bowl and season with rice wine and soy sauce .
Season with salt and pepper , mix, and add potato starch . Mix again and let it rest for 30 minutes covered with plastic wrap in a cool, dry place .
Take the bell peppers and cut them first into strips and then into pieces. Clean and cut the onion into pieces as well .
Heat a wok, then pour a little vegetable oil and add the vegetables . Briefly stir-fry the vegetables, then add the chicken .
Stir and stir-fry over high heat, then deglaze with soy sauce . Also add the spicy sauce and the peanuts .
In a small bowl, pour water and potato starch , then mix well . When the chicken is well cooked, pour the mixture into the wok and cook for a couple more minutes.
At the end of cooking, a dash of vegetable oil to give shine to the dish . Your gong bao chicken is ready to serve accompanied by steamed rice !
Gong bao chicken can be stored in the refrigerator for one day in an airtight container.
Rice wine can be replaced with sake or mirin.
