name:
Mini Panettone cakes with Nutella®
ingredients:
Panettone: ½
Nutella®: 4 ¼tbsp(60 g)
Cream cheese: 1cup(250 g)
Fresh liquid cream: 0.4cup(100 g)
Honey: 2tbsp(30 g)
Pomegranate: ½
Powdered sugar: to taste

info:
Difficulty: Easy
Prep time: 20 min
Makes: 4 pieces

instructions:
The magic of the holidays is in the air, and so is the scent of delicious desserts! If you’re looking for a festive, no-bake treat that’s quick to prepare and sure to impress, these Mini Panettone Cakes with Nutella® are just what your Christmas table needs.
Inspired by the classic Italian panettone, this recipe transforms holiday leftovers into elegant, bite-sized desserts. Each mini cake features soft panettone rounds layered with a generous swirl of Nutella® and a smooth, tangy cream cheese filling. The result? A rich, bicolor dessert that’s both eye-catching and crowd-pleasing.
These individual holiday treats are perfect for parties, family dinners, or gifting. Best of all, they're easy to assemble ahead of time and require no baking — making them a lifesaver during the busy holiday season.
Want more Italian-inspired Christmas ideas? Try our Pandoro Tiramisu , Panettone French Toast , or Panettone skewers with chocolate fondue for sweet holiday magic!
To prepare the mini panettone cakes with Nutella® , first cut the panettone into slices 0.4 inches thick . From the slices, cut out 12 discs with a 2.4-inch diameter pastry cutter (2-3).
In a bowl, pour the cream cheese , heavy cream , and honey .
Whip with an electric mixer until you obtain a firm and frothy cream , then transfer it to a piping bag with a plain nozzle .
Now take each panettone disc and fill it with Nutella® . Pipe dollops of cream on top to cover the entire surface. Add some pomegranate seeds
and proceed with a second identical layer, filled with Nutella® , cream dollops , and pomegranate. Continue with a third and final layer, again filled with Nutella® ,
cream, and pomegranate . Decorate with powdered sugar on top . The mini panettone cakes with Nutella® are ready to be served .
We recommend consuming the mini panettone cakes with Nutella® immediately.
To make the cream, you can replace the honey with powdered sugar.
