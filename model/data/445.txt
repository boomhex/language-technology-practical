name:
Vegan Saffron Penne
ingredients:
Whole wheat Pennette Rigate pasta: 11.3oz(320 g)
Leeks: 1.5lbs(700 g)- to be cleaned
Saffron: 2bags
Soy cream: 0.85cup(200 g)
Extra virgin olive oil: 2tbsp(30 g)
Salt: to taste
Black pepper: to taste

To prepare vegan saffron penne, first cut the roots off the leeks , remove the first tough layer , then slice them into rounds .
Cook the pasta in plenty of salted boiling water for the time indicated on the package. In a pan, pour the oil, add the leeks and a ladle of the pasta cooking water . Cook for 10 minutes.
Pour the soy cream into the pan and continue cooking for another 5 minutes. Dissolve the saffron packets with a ladle of pasta cooking water, add it to the leeks , and stir. Drain the pasta al dente and transfer it to the pan .
Stir to combine the ingredients and finish cooking; if needed, add more pasta cooking water. Adjust with salt and pepper , then plate. Vegan saffron penne are ready to be enjoyed .
The vegan saffron penne should be consumed immediately once prepared. The sauce can be stored in the fridge, in a closed container, for up to 2 days.
You can enrich the sauce by adding your favorite spices and herbs. You can customize the recipe by reducing the amount of leeks and adding other vegetables such as zucchini, bell peppers, carrots, or you can add mushrooms such as porcini, champignon, or oyster mushrooms. For a more intense flavor, dice smoked tofu, sauté it in a pan until golden, then add it to the pasta before serving.
