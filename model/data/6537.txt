name:
Pasta with tuna
ingredients:
Mezze Maniche Rigate pasta: 12oz(320 g)
Tuna in oil: 9oz(250 g)- drained
Tomato pur√©e: 14oz(400 g)
Garlic: 1clove
Parsley: 1sprig- to be chopped
Fresh chili pepper: 1
Salt: to taste
Extra virgin olive oil: 3tbsp(40 g)
Anchovies in oil: 1tbsp(15 g)

info:
Difficulty: Easy
Prep time: 10 min
Cook time: 15 min
Serving: 4
Cost: Low

instructions:
To pasta with tuna, boil plenty of salted water to cook the pasta. In a pan, let the whole peeled garlic sizzle for a few moments in oil, then remove it . Add the drained anchovy fillets in oil and melt them with a little cooking water from the pasta . Then add the fresh chili pepper cut into strips, ideally without the seeds to reduce its spiciness .
While everything is seasoning, drain the oil from the tuna and place the tuna into the pan . Let it season, then wet with a bit of cooking water from the pasta .
Add the tomato puree and cook for about ten minutes. You have just enough time to cook the pasta in boiling salted water . So as soon as the pasta is al dente, drain and add it to the sauce .
After few moments , add salt, pepper and chopped parsley to taste . Your pasta with tuna is ready. Buon appetito !
Pasta with tuna should be eaten fresh.
It can be stored in the refrigerator in an airtight container for 1 day.
Freezing is not recommended.
You can also add small capers and/or finely chopped black olives to give your pasta with tuna a stronger and more intense flavor.
