name:
Papassini sardi
ingredients:
Type 00 flour: 4cups(500 g)
Sugar: 1cup(200 g)
Lard: 7oz(200 g)
Raisins: ¾cup(130 g)
Peeled almonds: 1cup(130 g)
Eggs: 4.2oz(120 g)- (about 2 medium)
Whole milk: ⅓cup(80 g)- warm
Baker's ammonia: ¼tsp(1 g)
Almond extract: 0.4tsp(2 g)
Powdered sugar: 1 ¼cup(150 g)
Egg whites: ⅓cup(80 g)- ( about 2 )
Colored sprinkles: to taste

info:
Difficulty: Easy
Prep time: 20 min
Cook time: 30 min
Makes: 25 pieces
Cost: Low
Note + 1 hour of dough resting

instructions:

To make papassini, first, soak the raisins in water . Toast the almonds in a pan and then chop them coarsely .
Pour the ammonia into the warm milk and stir to dissolve it . Meanwhile, drain the raisins, which will have softened .
In a bowl, pour the flour and sugar , add the lard . Start working the dough with your hands and then pour in the eggs .
Flavor with almond extract , add the milk mixed with ammonia , and the toasted and chopped almonds .
Finally, incorporate the softened raisins and knead until you get a homogeneous dough ball . Wrap it with plastic wrap and place it in the refrigerator for at least an hour.
After this time, take the dough and roll it out with a rolling pin on a lightly floured work surface to a thickness of 3/8 inch . Cut out diamonds with a cookie cutter . Rework the dough scraps to make more cookies until the dough is used up. Place the cookies on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper and bake in a preheated static oven at 350°F for 30 minutes.
Once baking is complete, remove the papassini from the oven and let them cool slightly . Meanwhile, make the icing: in a bowl, pour the egg whites and powdered sugar , beat the mixture with electric whisks until it is white and thick, it should be spreadable .
Cover the cookies with the icing using a spatula . Garnish immediately with colored sprinkles and place on a rack to let the icing dry . The papassini are ready to be enjoyed .
Sardinian papassini can be stored in a tin box for a week. It is possible to freeze the raw dough.
Lard can be replaced with the same amount of butter.
