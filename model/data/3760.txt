name:
Meringue Bones for Halloween
ingredients:
Egg whites: ½cup(100 g)- (from about 3 medium eggs)
Salt: 1pinch
Vanilla: 1bag
Powdered sugar: 1.67cups(200 g)
Lemon juice: a few- drop

info:
Difficulty: Easy
Prep time: 30 min
Cook time: 2 h
Makes: 60 pieces
Cost: Low
Note + 2 hours of resting in the oven

instructions:
To prepare meringue bones for Halloween, preheat the oven to 194°F. Separate the yolks from the whites and place the whites in a stand mixer , along with a pinch of salt. Turn on the mixer and beat the whites until very stiff, then incorporate the powdered sugar one tablespoon at a time .
and the vanilla powder ; finally, add a few drops of lemon juice . Mix gently to incorporate all the ingredients; once ready, the meringue mixture will be compact as shown in the photo .
Now pour the mixture into a pastry bag equipped with a medium, plain nozzle , then line a baking tray with parchment paper and start forming the bones, shaping them with the pastry bag . Bake everything at 194°F for about 2 hours , then turn off the oven and let the meringues cool inside for another two hours, keeping the oven slightly open. When the meringues are dry and cool, remove them from the oven and ...Happy Halloween!
If you don't plan to use the meringue bones immediately after preparing them, store them in a tin box with a lid. They can be kept for 7-10 days.
If you wish, you can make classic meringues with the same mixture. For the French meringue recipe French meringue (basic dough)
