name:
Rice soup with legumes
ingredients:
Rice: 1 ¼cup(240 g)- for risottos
Mixed vegetables: 1 ¾cup(260 g)- precooked
Potatoes: 1lb(430 g)
Carrots: 1cup(120 g)
Celery: 2.8oz(80 g)
Shallot: 3oz(80 g)
Tomato purée: 1cup(250 g)
Water: 3.3cups(800 g)
Thyme: to taste
Extra virgin olive oil: to taste
Fine salt: to taste
Black pepper: to taste

instructions:

To prepare the rice soup with legumes, wash and finely dice the vegetables for the soffritto, namely celery, carrot, and onion . Peel the potatoes and dice them into cubes of about 3/4 inch . In a saucepan, pour the oil and the minced vegetables for the soffritto .
Sauté gently for about 10 minutes , then add the potatoes and stir .
Add the tomato puree and the legumes . Cook for a couple of minutes, then pour in the water .
Cover with a lid and cook over medium heat for 20 minutes. After the cooking time, add the rice and stir, then adjust the salt . Cover with the lid and let it cook for another 10 minutes, stirring occasionally.
After 10 minutes , serve your rice soup with legumes, finishing with a sprinkle of black pepper, a drizzle of oil , and some thyme leaves !
The rice soup with legumes can be stored in the refrigerator for one day in an airtight container. It can be frozen, preferably without pasta, for about 2-3 weeks.
You can use a mix of dried legumes as an alternative to pre-cooked ones; in this case, you will need to cook them in the broth for about an hour, or until they are tender.
