name:
Harissa
ingredients:
Fresh chili pepper: 8.8oz(250 g)- hot red
Garlic: 4cloves
Coriander: 1spoonful- fresh leaves
Coriander: 1spoonful- powdered
Dried mint: 1spoonful
Extra virgin olive oil: to taste
Fine salt: 1spoonful
Caraway: 1spoonful- seeds

info:
Difficulty: Very easy
Prep time: 10 min
Serving: 6
Cost: Low
Note + 1 hour of soaking

instructions:

To prepare Harissa, remove the stems from the chili peppers, wash them, cut them lengthwise, remove the seeds, and soak them in a little water for at least 1 hour. After an hour, drain them and crush them together with the other ingredients, or put everything in a mixer, adding enough oil to create a very thick paste. Place the mixture in a glass jar and cover the surface with oil, which will help preserve the Harissa.
Every time you take some sauce from the jar of Harissa, you should restore the oil level to ensure the Harissa is well-preserved. If you can't find fresh hot chili peppers, or don't want to try this very spicy recipe, you can buy ready-made Harissa at any large supermarket.
