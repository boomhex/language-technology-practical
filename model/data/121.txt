name:
Cuba libre
ingredients:
White Rum: 3.5oz(100 ml)
Coca Cola: 1cup(240 ml)
Lime juice: 4tsp(20 ml)
Lime: 4cloves
Ice: to taste

info:
Difficulty: Very easy
Prep time: 5 min
Serving: 2
Cost: Low

instructions:
To prepare the Cuba Libre, start with the lime. Squeeze its juice using a citrus juicer and pass the obtained liquid through a strainer . Cut the lime into wedges to use for garnishing the cocktail.
In a highball glass, add ice cubes , pour in half of the lime juice , and also add half of the white rum .
Now, you only need to pour the Coca-Cola . With our quantities, you will get 2 cocktails. Garnish and scent your cocktail with lime wedges , and you can already enjoy your refreshing Cuba Libre !
The Cuba Libre should be consumed immediately.
Many non-alcoholic products are available on the market, allowing everyone to enjoy the taste of cocktails. Look for rum made with 0% alcohol and enjoy your Virgin Cuba Libre.
