name:
Grilled Vegetable Skewers
ingredients:
Zucchini: 1
Eggplant: 1- small
Red peppers: 1
Yellow peppers: 1
Champignon mushrooms: 10
Cherry tomatoes: 20
Mozzarelline ciliegine cheese: 20
Capers in vinegar: 2tbsp(30 g)
Parsley: 1bunch
Extra virgin olive oil: 4spoonfuls
Lemon juice: 1
Garlic: 1

info:
Difficulty: Very easy
Prep time: 15 min
Cook time: 20 min
Makes: 20 pieces
Cost: Low

To prepare the vegetable skewers, start by cleaning the mushrooms and cutting them in half , wash all the vegetables and cut the zucchini into 3/8-inch slices , slice the eggplant and cut each slice into quarters , also 3/8-inch thick,
finally, cut the peppers into pieces that match the proportions of the other vegetables . Brush a large grill with oil and cook the vegetables over high heat . In the meantime, prepare the sauce by placing the parsley leaves, capers , oil,
lemon juice , and garlic in a blender, blend all the ingredients until you get a creamy mixture . Now start assembling the skewers by inserting a parsley leaf and then alternating the rest of the components: the tomatoes, zucchini, peppers, eggplants, mushrooms, and mozzarella bites. Place the skewers on a serving platter and accompany them with the parsley and caper sauce.
