name:
Apple and mascarpone cake
ingredients:
Golden delicious apples: 14oz(400 g)
Mascarpone cheese: 7oz(200 g)
Type 00 flour: 1 ½cup(250 g)
Sugar: 0.65cup(150 g)
Eggs: 0.65cup(120 g)- (approximately 2)
Baking powder: 2 ¾tsp(16 g)
Vanilla bean: 1
Fine salt: 1pinch
Butter: to taste
Type 00 flour: to taste

info:
Difficulty: Easy
Prep time: 30 min
Cook time: 50 min
Serving: 12
Cost: Low

instructions:
Simply delicious! This is how we would define our apple and mascarpone cake , a dessert for everyone to prepare at home, a butter-free variant of the more traditional apple pie in which the addition of creamy mascarpone cheese ensures its softness and delicate taste. Enjoy it with a dusting of powdered sugar for breakfast or a snack, and you will make the whole family happy. If accompanied with a scoop of cream ice cream or vanilla sauce, it will become a perfect dessert to end a dinner on a sweet note!

To make the apple and mascarpone cake , first peel the apples, remove the core and cut them into irregular cubes . Pour the whole eggs and sugar into a bowl.
Add a pinch of salt and whip with hand mixer until frothy . Add the mascarpone cheese and whip.
Next add the sifted flour a little at a time , then the yeast . While whipping, flavor with the seeds of the vanilla bean seeds .
To finish, add the apple cubes and mix them in the dough . Butter and flour an 8-inch (20 cm) cake pan .
Pour the dough inside and level it with a spatula or the back of a slightly dampened spoon . Cook in a static oven preheated to 350°F (180°C) for 50 minutes. When it is cooked, bake the apple and mascarpone cake , let it cool down and then remove from the pan and garnish with powdered sugar to taste .
The apple and mascarpone cake can be stored under a glass cloche at room temperature for 2-4 days.
Leave the mascarpone to soften at room temperature before adding it to the dough, it will be easier to work with!
