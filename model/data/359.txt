name:
Crostino with Marinated Anchovies
ingredients:
Anchovies: 8.8oz(250 g)- already cleaned and frozen
Burrata cheese: 1cup(300 g)
Baguette: 1
Lemon peel: to taste
Orange peel: to taste
Lemon juice: 3 ½tbsp(50 g)
Orange juice: 1.7oz(50 g)
Garlic: 2cloves
Red onions: 1
Wild fennel: to taste
White wine vinegar: 2tsp(10 g)
Extra virgin olive oil: 5tbsp(70 g)
Salt: to taste
Black pepper: to taste
Mint: 1bunch

info:
Difficulty: Very easy
Prep time: 30 min
Cook time: 15 min
Makes: 20 pieces
Cost: Low
Note plus the anchovies' marinating time (about 1 hour)

instructions:
To prepare the crostino with marinated anchovies, first slice the onion and the garlic . Finely chop the fennel .
In a bowl, pour the oil, add the white wine vinegar , the grated zest of an orange and a lemon . Add the juice of one orange .
Add the juice of one lemon , season with salt and pepper , and mix .
Place the cleaned and butterflied anchovies in a baking dish , lay the onion and garlic on top . Pour the previously prepared mixture over the anchovies. Cover with plastic wrap and let it rest in the fridge for at least one hour .
Slice the baguette , transfer the slices onto a baking tray lined with parchment paper, drizzle with a little oil and toast in the oven at 350°F for 15 minutes, or until golden .
Meanwhile, remove the anchovies from the marinade . Strain the rest to obtain the marinade liquid . Retrieve and set aside the onions for decoration. Add fresh mint to the marinade liquid and blend until you get a smooth sauce.
Place the crostini on a serving plate and top with hand-torn burrata, lay the anchovies on top . Decorate with onions and fennel, and finish with the mint marinade . The crostino with marinated anchovies is ready to be enjoyed .
It's advisable to consume the crostini immediately after assembling. You can prepare the marinated anchovies in advance and store them in the refrigerator in an airtight container for a maximum of 2-3 days.
You can experiment by adding the spices and herbs you like to the marinade. If you like spicy, you can add a chopped chili pepper to the marinade.
