name:
Strawberry Milkshake
ingredients:
Strawberries: 1 ½cup(250 g)
Whole milk: ¾cup(180 g)
Sugar: 1tsp(5 g)
Ice: 1.8oz(50 g)

info:
Difficulty: Very easy
Prep time: 5 min
Serving: 2
Cost: Very low

instructions:

To prepare the strawberry milkshake, start by cleaning the fruit. Thoroughly wash the strawberries and remove the green parts , then cut them into quarters and set them aside .
At this point, pour the ice into a blender , add the previously cut strawberries and also the milk .
Finally, add the sugar and blend until you have a creamy and homogeneous mixture . Pour everything into glasses and decorate with a small strawberry, slicing it from the bottom . Serve the strawberry milkshake well chilled!
It is advisable to consume the strawberry milkshake immediately to prevent the fruit from oxidizing. Freezing is not recommended!
To make the strawberry milkshake creamier, you can add one or two scoops of ice cream; if it becomes too thick, add a little milk! To flavor your milkshake, add some mint leaves!
