name:
Eggplant caponata with cocoa sauce
ingredients:
Eggplant: 2
Red Tropea onions: 1
Celery: 1rib
Cherry tomatoes: 8
Red peppers: 1
Salted capers: 1spoonful
Green olives: 3.5oz(100 g)- pitted
Raisins: 5tbsp(40 g)
Pine nuts: ¼cup(30 g)
Orange blossom honey: 1spoonful
White wine vinegar: 3spoonfuls
Unsweetened cocoa powder: 2spoonfuls
Marsala wine: to taste
Extra virgin olive oil: to taste
Salt: to taste
Black pepper: to taste
Basil: to taste

To prepare the eggplant caponata with cocoa sauce, first, place the raisins in a bowl and cover them with Marsala wine . Let them soak for 30 minutes. Soak the capers in water for at least 10 minutes. Wash and slice the eggplants .
Place the eggplant slices under salt for 30 minutes. Then pat them dry with paper towels and cut them into cubes .
Peel the onion and chop it with a knife . Wash the celery and cut it into small cubes . Clean a bell pepper by removing the stem and seeds, wash it, and cut it into strips and then into cubes .
Wash the cherry tomatoes and cut them in half , remove the seeds and the pulp , then cut them into wedges .
In a pan, pour the oil and add the bell peppers . Cook for 10 minutes and then place them on paper towels . Put the eggplants in the pan, fry them in small batches over high heat, turning them slightly, until a crispy crust forms, then place them on paper towels .
In a pan, pour the oil, add the onions , the celery , and sauté for a few minutes. Add the well-drained capers .
Add the pine nuts , stir to toast them. Then add the raisins and the olives . Stir and cook for a few minutes.
Turn off the heat and add the eggplants, bell peppers , and cherry tomatoes to the pan. Season with salt and pepper, stir to flavor and blend the ingredients .
In a bowl, pour the white wine vinegar, add the orange blossom honey , and the unsweetened cocoa powder . Stir until you obtain a homogeneous mixture .
Pour the mixture into the pan , turn on the heat, stir and cook for 3-4 minutes , then let it rest for at least an hour. The eggplant caponata with cocoa sauce is ready, garnish with basil leaves and serve .
Eggplant caponata with cocoa sauce can be stored in the fridge, in a closed container or covered with plastic wrap, for up to 2-3 days. Before serving, it is advisable to bring it back to room temperature by keeping it out of the fridge for at least an hour.
You can prepare the cocoa sauce in advance and store it in the refrigerator for up to 3 days. You can also use the caponata as a dressing for a rice salad.
Orange blossom honey can be replaced with more common wildflower honey.
