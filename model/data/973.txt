name:
Meatballs with Pepper Sauce
ingredients:
Beef: 0.7lb(300 g)- (ground)
Sausage: 7oz(200 g)
Whole grain bread: 7oz(200 g)
White onions: 3.5oz(100 g)
Parmigiano Reggiano PDO cheese: 1oz(30 g)- (to grate)
Eggs: 1
Basil: 5leaves
Garlic: 1clove
Red peppers: 4cups(600 g)
Parsley: 1tuft
Extra virgin olive oil: to taste
Fine salt: to taste

instructions:

To make the meatballs with pepper sauce, first wash and clean the peppers by removing the top and the seeds inside , then cut them into small pieces . Clean and slice the onion .
In a pan, pour a little extra virgin olive oil, brown a clove of garlic inside , and as soon as it begins to brown, add the onions first and let them fry for a few minutes, then also add the peppers .
Season with basil and adjust the salt. Let it stew covered with a lid for about 20 minutes , keeping it moist with a little hot water as needed. In a separate bowl, soak the whole wheat bread in water .
In another bowl, mix the ground meat, sausage without casing , parsley , and Parmesan cheese .
At this point, also add the egg , the squeezed bread , and a pinch of salt, mix everything until you form a homogeneous mixture .
Form meatballs weighing about 1 oz each , you will get about 30. Heat a little oil in a pan , brown the meatballs .
Turn them when they are golden and continue until cooking is complete, it will take about 5-6 minutes. When they are all ready, pour the peppers into the glass of a blender , blend with the immersion blender until you get a cream .
In a serving dish, place the pepper sauce and on top arrange the meatballs . Enjoy the meatballs with pepper sauce immediately.
The meatballs with pepper sauce can be stored in the fridge for a couple of days. You can freeze the sauce and meatballs separately after cooking.
To make the meatballs more appetizing, you can fill them in the center with a cube of scamorza cheese!
