name:
Crispy hake with grapefruit salad
ingredients:
Hake fillets: 0.66lb(300 g)
Pink grapefruits: 10.6oz(300 g)
Quinoa: 0.15cup(25 g)
Taggiasca olives: 2.1oz(60 g)- pitted
Extra virgin olive oil: to taste
Fine salt: to taste
Black pepper: to taste

info:
Difficulty: Easy
Prep time: 20 min
Cook time: 25 min
Serving: 3

instructions:

To prepare the crispy hake with grapefruit salad, rinse the quinoa under running water, then toast it in a non-stick pan and scald it over low heat for 4-5 minutes, stirring occasionally until it becomes golden; set it aside . On a baking sheet, pour some oil and place the hake fillets ,
pepper and salt them and finally pour the previously toasted quinoa over each hake fillet . Bake in a preheated static oven at 392°F for 20 minutes .
While the hake fillets are baking in the oven, focus on the grapefruit salad. To start, you will need to peel the grapefruit raw. So cut the caps , then the rest of the peel being careful to also remove the white part . With a sharp knife with a smooth blade, extract the pulp into segments, following the veins of the skin
When you are finished, gather the segments in a bowl and add the black olives , then season with salt and pepper and mix everything . Finally, move on to the composition: on a serving plate, arrange some grapefruit and Taggiasca olive salad
and then place the fillets . Garnish with a splash of raw oil to your pleasure here is your crispy hake with grapefruit salad: enjoy your meal !
It is recommended to consume the crispy hake with grapefruit salad immediately. Freezing is not recommended.
You can enrich the salad by adding dried fruit of your choice. Or season the crispy hake with grapefruit salad with fresh aromatic herbs like thyme or parsley. If the idea of quinoa does not suit your taste, no problem! You can replace it with hazelnut crumbs!
