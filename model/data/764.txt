name:
Rice noodles with meat and vegetables
ingredients:
Rice spaghetti: 1 ¼cup(200 g)
Pork loin: 3.5oz(100 g)
Zucchini: ½cup(100 g)
Carrots: 0.9cup(100 g)
Onions: 2.1oz(60 g)
Eggs: 2
Sunflower seed oil: 6spoonfuls
Rice wine: 2spoonfuls
Soy sauce: to taste- optional
Fine salt: to taste
White pepper: to taste

info:
Difficulty: Easy
Prep time: 30 min
Cook time: 8 min
Serving: 4
Cost: Average

instructions:
To make rice noodles with meat and vegetables, first soak the rice noodles in plenty of warm water for about 30 minutes . Meanwhile, prepare the rest of the ingredients: cut the zucchini and carrots into strips .
Also cut the onion and pork loin into strips. Heat 5 tablespoons of vegetable oil in a wok over high heat, then add the onion .
Add the meat and brown for 30 seconds. Move the meat and onions to one side and pour in the beaten eggs . Keep stirring for 30 seconds, tossing everything .
Add the carrots and zucchini and cook for a minute and a half. At this point, drain the rice noodles and transfer them to the wok .
Season with salt, pepper , and rice wine . If you wish, you can also add soy sauce at this point: the important thing is to do it during cooking and not on the finished dish .
Continue to toss everything for about 5 minutes until the noodles are soft and no longer pale . Finally, add a tablespoon of vegetable oil off the heat, it will make the noodles shiny and glossy . Mix well and immediately serve your rice noodles with meat and vegetables !
It is recommended to consume the rice noodles with meat and vegetables immediately.
If you are short on time, you can skip the soaking and cook the rice noodles in boiling water for 2-3 minutes.
