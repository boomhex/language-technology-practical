name:
Melon and Bacon Cups
ingredients:
Winter yellow melon: ½
Smoked pancetta: 5.3oz(150 g)
Roasted salted peanuts: 1.75oz(50 g)

info:
Difficulty: Very easy
Prep time: 15 min
Cook time: 5 min
Serving: 4

To prepare the melon and bacon cups, first heat a non-stick pan, add the bacon and let it brown until golden . Transfer the bacon to a tray lined with paper towels to absorb the excess fat.
Meanwhile, take care of the melon: cut it in half, remove the seeds, then cut it into slices . Remove the rind and then cut the slices first horizontally and then vertically to obtain cubes about 1/2 inch .
Roughly chop the peanuts with a knife . In a large bowl, pour in the melon, peanuts , and bacon .
Mix everything to flavor , then fill small appetizer cups and serve .
The melon and bacon cups can be stored in the refrigerator for a maximum of a couple of days.

As an alternative to peanuts, you can use other nuts such as walnuts or pistachios.
