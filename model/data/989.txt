Verzini on the BBQ with peppers and onions
ingredients:
Verzini: 1lb(440 g)- (approximately 8 pieces)
Mayonnaise: 1 ⅓cup(320 g)
Stale bread: 4- (type ciabatta)
Red peppers: 1lb(250 g)
Yellow peppers: 1cup(250 g)
Red onions: 0.6lb(250 g)
Extra virgin olive oil: to taste
Thyme: to taste
Oregano: to taste
Lettuce: 4leaves

instructions:

To prepare the verzini on the BBQ with peppers and onions, first wash the peppers, remove the top and seeds inside , then cut them into pieces about 3/4 inch . Clean and cut the red onions into 3/4 inch pieces too .
Set the vegetables aside . Pour the mayonnaise into a bowl, flavor with thyme leaves and fresh oregano .
Mix to season . Form the skewers: first skewer a verzino, then the vegetables and alternate to complete the skewers .
Brush the skewers with olive oil . Heat the BBQ to about 482°F. Place the skewers on the grill , cook for 18 minutes, turning them every 4 minutes to brown evenly .
Once cooked, remove the skewers from the BBQ . Take the bread and cut it in half . Spread mayo on one side .
Place a lettuce leaf on top . Remove the verzini and vegetables from the skewers , then fill the sandwiches with the meat and vegetables.
Spread the mayo on the other slice of bread as well . Close the sandwich and enjoy the verzini on the BBQ with peppers and onions in your nice hot sandwich !
We recommend consuming the verzini on the BBQ with peppers and onions right away.
You can vary the herbs according to your taste: try chives, fennel, or cilantro!
