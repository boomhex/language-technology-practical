name:
Steamed Cod Fillet with Tomatoes and Caper Berries
ingredients:
Cod fillet: 1.8lbs(800 g)
Cherry tomatoes: 2 ½cups(400 g)
Caper berries: 1.75oz(50 g)- (caper berries)
Taggiasca olives: 2oz(50 g)
Basil: to taste
Extra virgin olive oil: to taste
Fine salt: to taste

instructions:

To prepare the steamed cod fillet with tomatoes and caper berries, first cut the cherry tomatoes into 4 parts , then distribute them on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper. Season with salt and oil and bake in a preheated fan oven at 356°F for 40 minutes.
In the meantime, cut the cod fillet into 3.5 oz portions . Salt the water in the steamer , place the basket on top , and bring to a boil.
Place the cod portions in the basket , cover with the lid , and steam for 7 minutes. After this time, remove the cod from the steamer and gently pat it dry with a paper towel to remove excess water. Meanwhile, cut the caper berries in half .
When the tomatoes are cooked , transfer half of them into a jug and blend with an immersion blender to obtain a smooth and homogeneous cream .
You are ready to plate: spread the roasted tomato cream at the base of the plate , then place 2 cod portions on top and garnish with the whole cherry tomatoes set aside .
Finally, add the caper berries , Taggiasca olives, a few basil leaves , and a drizzle of oil. Your steamed cod fillet with tomatoes and caper berries is ready to be served !
It is recommended to consume the steamed cod fillet with tomatoes and caper berries immediately or store it for up to one day in the refrigerator, in an airtight container.
If you cannot find caper berries, you can omit them or substitute them with desalted capers.
