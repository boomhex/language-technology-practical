To prepare crepes without butter, pour the whole eggs into a bowl and beat them with a whisk . Gradually pour in the milk .
Add a pinch of salt and the extra virgin olive oil , continuing to mix with the whisk .
Now incorporate the sifted flour through a sieve, a little at a time , always mixing with the whisk to avoid lumps and obtain a smooth and homogeneous batter . Pour a little oil into a pan with a diameter of approximately 7 inches .
Remove any excess oil with a kitchen paper . Pour a ladle of batter to cover the bottom .
After about a minute, keeping the flame medium, you will notice that the edges will begin to detach from the pan and will be slightly golden, flip the crepe with a spatula and cook it on the other side . Transfer the crepe to a plate, stacking them one on top of the other ( The crepes without butter are ready, and you can choose to serve them sweet or savory.

The crepes without butter can be stored in the refrigerator wrapped with plastic wrap for 2 or 3 days.
If you want to keep the crepes longer, you can also freeze them, then defrost them in the refrigerator before using them. You can freeze them all together or separate them with a sheet of parchment paper so that you can take out one at a time.

If you plan to use the crepes for sweet fillings, you can flavor the batter with cinnamon, lemon zest, or vanilla beans.
