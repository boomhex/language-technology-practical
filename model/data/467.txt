name:
Pasta with robiola and zucchini
ingredients:
Strozzapreti pasta: 11.3oz(320 g)
Zucchini: 3 ½cups(400 g)
Robiola cheese: 7oz(200 g)
Whole milk: 2spoonfuls
Extra virgin olive oil: 2spoonfuls
Garlic: 1clove
Fine salt: to taste
Black pepper: to taste

info:
Difficulty: Easy
Prep time: 10 min
Cook time: 20 min
Serving: 4
Cost: Low

instructions:
To prepare the pasta with robiola and zucchini, first wash the zucchinis, dry them, and remove the ends. Cut them in half , into sticks, and finally into cubes . In a large wok, sauté the garlic in the extra virgin olive oil .
Add the diced zucchini and salt, stir and cook for 5 minutes . Remove the garlic . In the meantime, cook the pasta in plenty of salted water following the cooking times indicated on the package .
Set aside two ladles of the pasta cooking water and pour them over the zucchini . Add the robiola and stir to melt it, along with two tablespoons of milk .
Cook the sauce for 10 minutes on low heat . Drain the pasta and place it in the wok . Mix well and serve with a sprinkle of black pepper .
Pasta with robiola and zucchini is best enjoyed hot, so it's advisable to prepare it on the spot. You can prepare the zucchini in advance and store them in the refrigerator.
You can flavor the pasta cooking water with spices like curry or saffron to add a touch of color to the dish.
