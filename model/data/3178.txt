name:
Toffee sauce
ingredients:
Sugar: ½cup(100 g)
Water: 5tsp(25 g)
Fresh liquid cream: 3 ½tbsp(50 g)- at room temperature
Butter: 0.4oz(10 g)- at room temperature

info:
Difficulty: Very easy
Prep time: 5 min
Cook time: 10 min
Makes: pieces
Cost: Very low

instructions:

To prepare the toffee sauce, pour the water into a saucepan, add the sugar , and stir to mix the two ingredients well , then turn on the heat and let it come to a boil over medium flame without stirring further .
When the mixture has reached a light amber color , remove the saucepan from the heat and slowly pour in the fresh liquid cream , stirring with a wooden spoon ; be sure to add the cream gradually as indicated, being very careful of the steam and bubbles that will form.
Finally, add the piece of butter and continue stirring to mix it into the mixture . Your toffee sauce is ready .
For chocolate lovers, we recommend a recipe that pairs chocolate with toffee sauce, creating a perfect and delicious combination: Chocolate toffee tartlets .
You can store the toffee sauce in a jar with a lid, in the refrigerator for 4-5 days.
