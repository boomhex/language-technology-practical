name:
Warm rice with chickpeas, roasted vegetables, and feta
ingredients:
Whole rice: 1 ¾cup(320 g)
Precooked chickpeas: ¾cup(200 g)
Zucchini: 1 ½cup(200 g)
Eggplant: 12.4oz(350 g)
Datterino tomatoes: 1 ¼cup(250 g)
Feta cheese: 2.8oz(80 g)
Extra virgin olive oil: to taste
Oregano: to taste
Extra virgin olive oil: 3 ½tbsp(50 g)
Aromatic herbs: to taste

instructions:

To prepare the warm rice with chickpeas, roasted vegetables, and feta, start by washing the vegetables, then trim the eggplants and cut them into cubes of about 3/4 inch . Do the same with the zucchinis . Pour the cherry tomatoes into a baking dish .
Add the eggplants and zucchinis as well , season with oil , salt, pepper, and fresh oregano , bake in a static oven at 450°F for 20 minutes.
Meanwhile, boil the whole grain rice in salted water for the time indicated on the package . In the meantime, prepare the flavored oil by placing the oil in a small saucepan with the fresh herbs you have at home (rosemary, chives, fennel, oregano, marjoram, basil, parsley, or thyme).
Let it go for 10 minutes on a very low flame. Remove the herbs and let it cool. Remove the vegetables from the oven . Drain the rice and transfer it to a bowl .
Pour the drained chickpeas into the rice , also incorporate the vegetables , and season with the flavored oil .
Crumble the feta and mix to flavor , adjust with salt and pepper, add some fresh oregano leaves. Your warm rice with chickpeas, roasted vegetables, and feta is ready to be served .
The rice keeps for a couple of days in the refrigerator.
If you do not want to use the oven, you can sauté the vegetables in a pan with a drizzle of olive oil.
