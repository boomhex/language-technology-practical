name:
Mixed fried vegetables and cheeses
ingredients:
Cauliflower: 1
Broccoli: 1
Artichokes: 4
Potatoes: 3
Zucchini: 3
Fine salt: to taste
Black pepper: to taste
Cow's milk ricotta cheese: 1cup(250 g)
Caciotta romana cheese: 1.75oz(50 g)
Provolone cheese: 1.75oz(50 g)
Nutmeg: to taste
Type 00 flour: ½cup(70 g)
Lemons: ½
Flour: 3cups(370 g)
Water: 2 ⅓cups(550 g)
Fine salt: 1pinch
Breadcrumbs: to taste
Type 00 flour: to taste
Egg yolks: 3
Vegetable oil: to taste

info:
Difficulty: Easy
Prep time: 30 min
Cook time: 20 min
Serving: 6
Cost: Average

instructions:

To prepare the mixed fried vegetables and cheeses, start by preparing the vegetables: clean and cut the cauliflower and broccoli into small pieces , cook the broccoli in boiling water for 3 minutes and the cauliflower for 5 minutes; they should remain crunchy. Slice the potatoes thinly and cook them for 1 minute in salted boiling water.
Now clean the artichokes following the instructions you can How to clean artichokes , cut them into quarters and place them in a bowl with lemon water to prevent them from turning brown . Wash the zucchini and slice them thinly . At this point, prepare the cheeses: in a large bowl, place the ricotta, finely sliced caciotta slices, and 2 tablespoons of grated provolone, flour, salt, pepper, and nutmeg
and mix everything to form balls of about 0.5 oz each . Finally, slice the remaining provolone . Prepare the ingredients for breading: in a bowl, beat the egg yolks with salt, and place flour and breadcrumbs on two large trays.
Start by breading the cheeses: pass them in the flour , then in the egg , and finally in the breadcrumbs and set them aside.
Continue similarly with half of the artichokes . Now prepare the batter: in a bowl, pour the water and gradually add the flour, mixing with a whisk to avoid lumps , let the batter rest in the refrigerator for about half an hour. Everything is ready for frying: place a large pan with plenty of vegetable oil on the stove, as soon as the oil is hot, start frying the potatoes first and then let them dry on absorbent paper.
Dip the cauliflower bites , broccoli, zucchini, and the remaining artichokes in the batter, fry them , and let them dry. Finish frying with the breaded cheeses and artichokes. Make sure to dry all the vegetables and cheeses on a sheet of absorbent paper to remove excess oil . Serve the hot and crispy fried vegetables and cheeses immediately!
