name:
Spring chicken with aromatic herbs and lard with beer potatoes
ingredients:
Young rooster: 1.1lbs(500 g)
Lard: 4oz(115 g)
Thyme: 5sprigs
Sage: 5leaves
Marjoram: 5sprigs
Fine salt: to taste
Black pepper: to taste
Blond beer: ¾cup(180 g)
New potatoes: 10.6oz(300 g)
Garlic: 2cloves
Extra virgin olive oil: to taste

info:
Difficulty: Average
Prep time: 20 min
Cook time: 50 min
Serving: 2

instructions:
To prepare the spring chicken with lard and beer potatoes, first chop the lard . Melt it in a saucepan . Meanwhile, finely chop sage and marjoram .
Strain the lard: keep the liquid aside and blend the residues . Salt the spring chicken .
Pepper both sides . Grease a baking sheet , place the chicken, and brush it with the melted lard .
Season with the herbs and the blended lard granules . Bake in a preheated oven at 392°F for at least 50 minutes. Every 15 minutes, collect the cooking juices and pour them over the chicken so it doesn’t dry out excessively. Meanwhile, take care of the beer potatoes: wash and cut the potatoes. In a pan, pour the oil and garlic and let it infuse .
Add the potatoes to the pan , salt, pepper, and season with thyme sprigs . Cook the potatoes by adding the beer little by little .
Continue cooking until they are golden, then remove the garlic . Meanwhile, the spring chicken will have finished cooking ; serve it hot with the potatoes .
It can be frozen once cooked. It is recommended to serve hot.
You can change the aromatic herbs according to your taste. Alternatively, you can bake the potatoes along with the chicken in the oven.
