name:
Chicken with tomatoes and avocado
ingredients:
Chicken breast: 1.25lbs(550 g)
Extra virgin olive oil: 1.3tbsp(20 g)
Fine salt: 1
Black pepper: 1tsp
Lime: 1
Oregano: 1tsp
Cluster tomatoes: 1.1lbs(500 g)
Avocado: Â¾cup(200 g)
Red onions: 3.5oz(100 g)
Fine salt: 1tsp
Paprika: 1tsp

info:
Difficulty: Easy
Prep time: 15 min
Cook time: 10 min
Serving: 4
Cost: Low

instructions:
To prepare the chicken with tomatoes and avocado, first slice the chicken breast , then pound them with a meat tenderizer to make them thinner . Transfer the meat to a baking dish, then season with oil, salt, and pepper .
Also add the lime zest and its juice , finally season with oregano . Mix well to flavor.
Cover with plastic wrap and set aside until cooking. Now prepare the tomatoes: after washing and drying them, cut them into wedges and then dice them . Clean and chop the red onion .
In a bowl, combine the tomatoes and onion . Now cut the avocado in half, score the flesh vertically and then horizontally to obtain cubes . Add the avocado to the bowl .
Season with oil , paprika , salt, and pepper. Now proceed to cooking: heat a grill well, place the chicken breasts and cook them for 5 minutes.
Then turn them over and continue cooking for another 5 minutes. They should be well grilled and have typical grill marks on the surface . Once cooked, serve the chicken immediately with the diced tomatoes and avocado .
We recommend consuming the chicken with diced tomatoes and avocado immediately.
Freezing is not recommended.
Try adding crumbled feta to enrich the dish with flavor!
