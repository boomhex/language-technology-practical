name:
Zucchini and Ricotta Pasta
ingredients:
Fusilli pasta: 11.25oz(320 g)
Cow's milk ricotta cheese: 1cup(240 g)
Zucchini: 4cups(500 g)
Shallot: 1
Basil: 6leaves
Grana Padano PDO cheese: 1.8oz(50 g)- grated
Lemon peel: to taste
Extra virgin olive oil: to taste
Salt: to taste
Black pepper: to taste

info:
Difficulty: Very easy
Prep time: 15 min
Cook time: 10 min
Serving: 4
Cost: Very low

instructions:
To prepare zucchini and ricotta pasta, first put the water on the stove and bring it to a boil. Meanwhile, finely chop the shallot . Cut the zucchini first into slices and then into 1/2 inch cubes .
In a large pan, pour a drizzle of olive oil, add the shallot and saut√© over medium heat. Then add a ladle of water to prevent it from burning . Then add the zucchini .
Season with salt and pepper and cook over medium heat for 4-5 minutes .
Drop the pasta into the boiling water . Meanwhile, in a bowl, place the ricotta, a drizzle of olive oil, and adjust with salt and pepper . Also add the lemon zest .
Pour a ladle of pasta cooking water and mix to obtain a cream . Finally, add some basil leaves torn by hand .
Drain the pasta one minute before the time indicated on the package and pour it into the pan , also add the ricotta cream and stir to combine the ingredients .
Add the grated Grana Padano cheese , a ladle of pasta cooking water , stir and plate. Finish with a few basil leaves, and your zucchini and ricotta pasta is ready to be served
It's best to consume zucchini and ricotta pasta immediately, but if there are leftovers, you can store it in the refrigerator in a closed container for up to 2 days.
For an even fresher flavor, you can replace the basil with fresh mint. For a stronger taste, you can substitute cow's milk ricotta with sheep or goat ricotta. The recipe lends itself to many variations depending on the seasonality of the vegetables. Try replacing zucchini with other seasonal vegetables, like asparagus in spring or pumpkin in fall, to create new and tasty versions of the dish.
