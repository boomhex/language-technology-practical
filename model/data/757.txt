name:
Pumpkin in the pan
ingredients:
Butternut squash: 0.9lb(400 g)
Extra virgin olive oil: to taste
Fine salt: to taste
Paprika: to taste
Rosemary: 2sprigs
Marjoram: 4sprigs
Garlic: 2

To prepare the pumpkin in the pan, wash the pumpkin. Then cut slices about 1/4 inch thick without removing the skin, and divide them in half .
In a large pan, pour the oil, garlic , and herbs . Let them flavor and add the pumpkin .
Salt and season with sweet paprika . Cook over medium-low heat for about 8-10 minutes or until you obtain the desired consistency. Gently, from time to time, turn the pumpkin for even cooking. The pumpkin in the pan is ready to be served .
Pumpkin in the pan can be stored in the fridge for 1-2 days. Freezing is not recommended.
This pumpkin is very tender, but you can use any type of pumpkin, adjusting the cooking times.
It is recommended to leave the skin on, so the pumpkin does not fall apart during cooking.
