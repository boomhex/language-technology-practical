name:
Warm whole grain rice
ingredients:
Whole rice: 1 ½cup(280 g)
Shrimps: 12
Leeks: 1
White wine vinegar: 1tsp(5 g)
Soy sauce: 1tbsp(15 g)
Peeled almonds: 0.7oz(20 g)
Brown sugar: 2tsp(10 g)
Black pepper: to taste
Black sesame seeds: to taste
Fine salt: to taste
Extra virgin olive oil: to taste

instructions:

To prepare the warm whole grain rice, thinly slice the leek about 1/4 inch thick, removing the end with the roots . Pour a drizzle of oil into a pan, add the leek, taking care not to overlap the slices. Salt and pepper.
Add the brown sugar and pepper. Cook over high heat for about 5 minutes, turning occasionally until golden, if necessary, add a little hot water . Meanwhile, add the rice to salted boiling water and cook for 10 minutes .
Once cooked , transfer the leek to a bowl and set aside. Remove the shrimp heads .
Remove the shells . In the same pan where you cooked the leek, pour a drizzle of oil and add the previously shelled shrimp .
Cook over high heat for 1 minute on each side. Add the soy sauce and cook for a couple of minutes. Transfer the shrimp to a baking dish and set aside. Drain the rice when cooked .
Toast whole almonds in a pan until golden and then coarsely chop them . Transfer the cooled rice to a large bowl, season with oil .

Add the leek , chopped almonds , and black sesame seeds .
Mix everything together. Plate using a 4-inch ring and gently press the rice inside . Finish the dish with soy sautéed shrimp and a drizzle of raw oil .
The warm whole grain rice can be stored in the refrigerator for one day.
You can add other dried fruits to the warm rice salad and use herb-infused or spicy oils.
