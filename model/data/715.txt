name:
Stir-fried Vegetables with Soy Sauce and Ginger
ingredients:
Onions: ½cup(80 g)
Zucchini: 2.5oz(70 g)
Cabbage: 10.6oz(300 g)
Carrots: 2.8oz(80 g)
Champignon mushrooms: ½cup(50 g)
Fresh ginger: to taste
Soy sauce: to taste
Sunflower seed oil: 3spoonfuls
Fine salt: to taste
Black pepper: to taste

instructions:

To prepare the stir-fried vegetables with soy sauce and ginger, first, wash the vegetables and cut them into strips, starting with the cabbage, removing its inner core . Clean and slice the onion . Trim and cut the zucchini into irregular pieces, about 1/8 inch thick .
Peel and trim the carrots, slicing them to the same thickness as the zucchini . Clean and slice the ginger as well . In the wok, heat the vegetable oil, then add the ginger .
Add the onions and let them simmer over low heat, then add the other vegetables: the cabbage , the carrots, the zucchini , and the cleaned mushrooms .
Salt and pepper , pour a little water , and stir-fry for about 8 minutes. Stir often to avoid burning. When the vegetables and mushrooms are cooked, add the soy sauce .
Drizzle with sunflower seed oil and stir-fry a few more moments to enhance the flavor . The stir-fried vegetables with soy sauce and ginger are ready, enjoy them hot .
Stir-fried vegetables with soy sauce and ginger can be stored in the fridge for a couple of days. You can freeze them after cooking.
Serve the stir-fried vegetables with rice or use them as a topping for noodles.
