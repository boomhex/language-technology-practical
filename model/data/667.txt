name:
Cabbage and beans
ingredients:
Verza (savoy cabbage): 1.5lbs(650 g)- (to clean)
Dried Borlotti beans: 1cup(150 g)
Pumpkin: 2cups(520 g)- (to clean)
Yellow onions: 1.5oz(40 g)
Celery: 1rib
Carrots: 1
Bay leaves: 1leaf
Vegetable broth: 4 ¼cups(1 l)
Extra virgin olive oil: to taste
Fine salt: to taste
Black pepper: to taste

info:
Difficulty: Easy
Prep time: 20 min
Cook time: 1 h 10 min
Serving: 4
Cost: Low
Note + 12 h soaking of dried beans and the preparation time of vegetable broth

instructions:
To make cabbage and beans, first soak the dried beans in cold water for 12 hours . After this time, drain and rinse them under running water. Meanwhile, prepare the Vegetable broth and the rest of the ingredients: strip the cabbage and cut it into thin strips , then clean the pumpkin and cut it into small cubes . You will need about 600 g (about 1.3 lbs) of cleaned cabbage and 400 g (about 14 oz) of pumpkin pulp.
Clean and chop the carrot , the celery , and the onion .
Place the chopped carrot, celery, and onion in a large pot and sauté with a little olive oil for a few minutes , then add the beans .
Add the cabbage , the bay leaf , and the pumpkin .
Cover everything with the hot broth and cook over low heat for about an hour, adding more broth if necessary . At the end of cooking, season with salt and pepper and serve your cabbage and beans piping hot !
Cabbage and beans can be stored in the refrigerator for about 3 days in an airtight container. You can also freeze individual portions.
To speed up cooking times, you can use a pressure cooker and cook everything for about 35 minutes.
If you wish, you can enrich cabbage and beans with smoked bacon bits!
