name:
Spaghetti with peppers, olives, and capers
ingredients:
Spaghetti: 11.3oz(320 g)
Red peppers: 10.5oz(300 g)
Yellow peppers: 2cups(300 g)
Black olives: 2.3oz(65 g)- pitted
Capers in oil: 1 ¾tbsp(25 g)
Anchovies in oil: 1.5oz(40 g)

info:
Difficulty: Easy
Prep time: 15 min
Cook time: 50 min
Serving: 4
Cost: Low

instructions:

To prepare spaghetti with peppers, olives, and anchovies, wash the peppers and wrap them in aluminum foil (1-2), place them on a baking sheet , and bake in a preheated static oven for 40 minutes at 428°F (or in a convection oven for 30 minutes at 392°F).
Meanwhile, drain the anchovies and capers in oil using a colander , slice the pitted black olives into rings , and set aside. When the peppers are cooked, remove them from the oven, discard the foil , and peel them.
Cut them in half, remove the stalk, and the seeds inside, and slice them into thin strips . Place the sliced peppers in a colander to drain the excess liquid and set it aside . In a large pan, gently fry the anchovies .
Add the pepper strips (10-11), and sauté for a couple of minutes. Pour in the pepper cooking water .
Also add the olives and the capers . Meanwhile, in a large pot, bring plenty of salted water to a boil, add the spaghetti , and cook for about 10 minutes or the time indicated on the package.
When cooked, drain the spaghetti and add them to the sauce , mix to season the pasta . Spaghetti with peppers, olives, capers, and anchovies is ready, serve immediately to enjoy it hot !
It is recommended to consume the pasta as soon as it's ready. If there are leftovers, store them in the refrigerator for up to two days, covered with plastic wrap. You can freeze the sauce if you have used fresh ingredients.
Choose the pasta shape you prefer; long pasta is perfect, and you can vary with linguine, tagliatelle, or tagliolini. Be careful not to over-salt the pasta because the sauce will already be very flavorful.
