name:
Orecchiette with artichokes
ingredients:
Orecchiette pasta: 11.3oz(320 g)- fresh
Artichokes: 5
Extra virgin olive oil: 4spoonfuls
White onions: 1
Whole milk: 1glass
Grana Padano PDO cheese: 1.4oz(40 g)
Fine salt: to taste
Mint: to taste

info:
Difficulty: Easy
Prep time: 15 min
Cook time: 30 min
Serving: 4
Cost: Low

To prepare orecchiette with artichokes, boil plenty of water in a pot and salt it once it's boiling. Clean the artichokes by removing most of the stem, leaving 1-2 inches . Cut the tips of the leaves by 1 inch , clean the stem
and remove the tougher outer leaves . First, cut the artichoke heart in half , then slice it .
In a bowl, pour cold water and squeeze a lemon ; you can dip the sliced artichokes in the acidulated water to prevent them from oxidizing . Clean and finely slice the onion .
Heat the oil in a pan, then add the onion and let it lightly sauté for 2 minutes on low heat . Drain the artichokes and add them to the pan . Let them cook for 3-4 minutes over medium heat, stirring often with a wooden spoon.
Pour a glass of milk into the pan , season with salt , cover the pan with a lid , and let the artichokes cook for about 16-18 minutes on low heat.
Cook the orecchiette in plenty of salted water for about 7-8 minutes (or the time indicated on the package) . Drain the orecchiette, pour them into the pan with the artichokes , stir well with a spoon .
Add grated Grana Padano and fresh mint leaves . Stir for a minute on low heat and serve the orecchiette with artichokes hot .
Consume the orecchiette immediately, or store them in the fridge for one day.
Freezing is not recommended.

If using dried orecchiette, consider the cooking times for dried pasta. For a more flavorful recipe, you can add 3.5 oz of ricotta previously thinned with 2-3 tablespoons of milk to the dressing. You can also replace the grated Grana with the same amount of Pecorino for a stronger taste.
