name:
Pasta with mushrooms and beans
ingredients:
Whole wheat Fusilli pasta: 11.3oz(320 g)
Fresh scallion: 1.75oz(50 g)
Champignon mushrooms: 4 ¼cups(300 g)
Pancetta: 6.4oz(180 g)- diced
Precooked Cannellini beans: 1 ½cup(370 g)
Water: 4tsp(20 g)
Rosemary: to taste
Extra virgin olive oil: to taste
Black pepper: to taste
Fine salt: to taste

info:
Difficulty: Easy
Prep time: 15 min
Cook time: 15 min
Serving: 4

instructions:
To make the pasta with mushrooms and beans, first place a pot full of salted water on the stove and bring it to a boil, which will be used for cooking the pasta. Meanwhile, prepare the sauce: thinly slice the light part of the spring onion after removing the outer leaves. Now, take care of the How to Clean Porcini Mushrooms : remove the base of the stem and pat them with a slightly damp cloth to remove any soil residues, then slice them thinly . Pour a drizzle of olive oil into a pan, add the spring onion
and let it brown over low heat for 2-3 minutes, then season with the rosemary leaves . At this point, also add the bacon and mushrooms and cook for 5-6 minutes.
then add the pre-cooked beans and add 20 g of water. Continue cooking for another 5 minutes, and in the meantime, cook the pasta until al dente and then drain it directly into the sauce .
Extend with a ladle of cooking water and sauté the pasta for a moment to blend the flavors . Serve your pasta with mushrooms and beans immediately .
Pasta with mushrooms and beans can be stored in the refrigerator for a maximum of one day. Freezing is not recommended.
Try replacing the cannellini beans with chickpeas and add fennel seeds to make the dish even more aromatic!
