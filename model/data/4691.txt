name:
Creamy garlic, oil, and chili pasta
ingredients:
Spaghetti: 0.7lb(320 g)
Crescenza cheese: 5.8oz(165 g)
Garlic: 3cloves
Fresh chili pepper: 3
Extra virgin olive oil: to taste

info:
Difficulty: Very easy
Prep time: 10 min
Cook time: 12 min
Serving: 4

instructions:
Creamy garlic, oil, and chili pasta is an irresistible variant of the famous spaghetti garlic, oil, and chili , a timeless dish of Italian cuisine. Perfect when you want to prepare a first course simple but with a twist: the addition of crescenza, with its delicate flavor and creamy consistency, enhances the bold character of garlic and fresh chili. The result is a velvety sauce with an intense and enveloping taste. Easy and quick to prepare in the time it takes to cook the spaghetti, creamy garlic, oil, and chili pasta is the ideal choice for those who love to experiment in the kitchen even with little time available!

To prepare creamy garlic, oil, and chili pasta, first cook the spaghetti in plenty of salted boiling water . Meanwhile, cut the garlic cloves in half, remove the core, and slice them thinly . Remove the stalk from the chili and cut it in half lengthwise, then into thin strips . If you prefer a less spicy result, remove the seeds first.
In a large pan, pour the oil, garlic , and chili . Saut√© on very low heat for 2 minutes. To brown them evenly, tilt the pan to gather the oil and seasoning in a small space .
Drain the pasta al dente and transfer it to the pan along with a ladle of cooking water , then toss everything together. Meanwhile, put the crescenza in a bowl, add 2 ladles of pasta cooking water , and blend with an immersion blender until you get a smooth cream .
Turn off the heat, add the cream , and mix well. Plate and finish with more seasoning . Your creamy garlic, oil, and chili pasta is ready to be enjoyed !
It is recommended to consume the creamy garlic, oil, and chili pasta immediately after preparation.
Tossing the pasta with the heat off is important to ensure the crescenza remains creamy.
You can flavor the dish with spices such as black pepper, smoked paprika, or a pinch of turmeric. If you desire to enrich it further, add crispy pancetta or guanciale!
