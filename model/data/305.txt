name:
Scallop carpaccio with citrus
ingredients:
Scallops: 12- (frozen and already cleaned)
Oranges: 1
Pink grapefruits: 1
Bergamots: 1
Pink peppercorn: to taste
Dill: to taste
Chili oil: to taste
Extra virgin olive oil: to taste
Fine salt: to taste

info:
Difficulty: Easy
Prep time: 30 min
Serving: 4
Cost: Average

instructions:
To prepare raw scallops with citrus, first grate the zest of the bergamot , orange , and pink grapefruit , and collect everything in a bowl.
Now peel the citrus fruits: with a knife, remove the peel and the white part , then extract the segments by inserting the blade between the membranes . Finally, squeeze the citrus remnants into the bowl with the zests . Set aside.
Lightly toast the pink peppercorns in a pan , then pour them into a mortar and crush them coarsely .
Add some of the pink pepper to the bowl with the zest and juice of the citrus fruits (adjust the amount to your taste) , then salt and dress with the oil .
Blend with an immersion blender to achieve a thick and creamy consistency . At this point, take the previously frozen and cleaned scallops and slice them into half-inch thick slices .
You're ready to plate: arrange the scallop slices in a circular pattern on a serving plate, then add the citrus segments and drizzle with the emulsion . Season with a pinch of pink pepper .
Garnish with some dill leaves and finish with a drizzle of spicy oil . Your raw scallops with citrus are ready to be served !
Raw scallops with citrus should be consumed immediately after preparation.
You can use your preferred citrus fruits.
Use a sharp knife to peel the citrus fruits and slice the scallops.
