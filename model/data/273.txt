name:
Capunet with rice
ingredients:
Verza (savoy cabbage): 1.1lbs(500 g)- (already cleaned)
Sausage: 9oz(250 g)
White onions: 1
Carrots: 1
Celery: 1
Eggs: 1
Parmigiano Reggiano PDO cheese: 2oz(60 g)- (to grate)
Extra virgin olive oil: to taste
Roma rice: 2cups(130 g)
Whole milk: 1 ¼cup(300 g)
Water: 1 ¼cup(300 g)
Fine salt: to taste
Tomato purée: 0.625cup(150 g)
Parmigiano Reggiano PDO cheese: to taste
Butter: to taste

instructions:

To prepare the capunet with rice, start with the latter: in a saucepan, combine the water and milk , add a pinch of salt , and heat over moderate heat. When it begins to simmer, pour in the rice and cook for 12-15 minutes.
The rice should be well-cooked, not al dente . Drain it and let it cool, being careful to keep the cooking liquid aside .
Take care of the cabbage: eliminate the outer and tough leaves, then set aside 10 whole leaves, which will be used to wrap the filling . Chop the inner core, which will be used in the filling of the roll . Blanch the whole leaves for a couple of minutes .
Drain them in ice water to stop the cooking . Now cut off the end of the central rib of the leaf, which is the toughest part , then lay them on a cloth .
Dry them well and set them aside. Prepare the filling: chop onion, celery, and carrot . In a saucepan, pour some oil and add the chopped onion .
Add celery and carrot and sauté for 5 minutes, then add the chopped core of the cabbage and salt .
Season with ground pepper and let it brown for 5 minutes. Now pour in some of the rice cooking liquid, just enough to soften the cabbage and aid the cooking. When the cabbage is soft, add the cooked rice .
Stir and cook for another 5 minutes, just enough to dry everything well . Remove from the heat and let it cool. Then complete with the egg, grated cheese , and crumbled sausage .
Stir to combine everything . Now assemble the capunet: spread the cabbage leaves on a cutting board , take a portion of filling and compact it in your hands to form a sausage shape , then place it in the center.
Fold over the side flaps and then roll it from the bottom , keeping the seam facing down. Set aside the 10 rolls. Separately, season the tomato sauce with salt and oil.
Take a baking dish and spread the tomato sauce at the bottom , then place the rolls and distribute some butter chunks on top .
Sprinkle with grated cheese and bake in a preheated convection oven at 350°F for 20 minutes. Take out your capunet with rice and let them cool slightly before serving them !
You can store the capunet with rice in the refrigerator for up to 2 days, in an airtight container.
It is possible to freeze them if you have used very fresh ingredients.
You can prepare the capunet with rice in advance, keep them in the refrigerator, and then bake them as needed.
To obtain compact capunet that do not open during cooking, tightly wrap the roll while rolling the leaf and place them in the dish with the seam facing down.
