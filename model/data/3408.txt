name:
Mussels Marinara
ingredients:
Mussels: 2.2lbs(1 kg)
Garlic: 1clove
White wine: ⅓cup(70 g)
Parsley: 1tuft- (to be chopped)
Extra virgin olive oil: 1.3tbsp(20 g)
Black pepper: 0.1tsp(0.3 g)

info:
Difficulty: Easy
Prep time: 15 min
Cook time: 10 min
Serving: 4
Cost: Low

instructions:
To prepare mussels marinara, first take care of the How to Clean and Open Mussels . Remove the beard and rinse them . Finely chop the washed parsley. Pour the oil into a high-edge pot .
Add the whole garlic, or crush it with the appropriate tool . Heat, let it flavor, and add the mussels . Stir and keep the heat medium-high.
Deglaze with white wine and let the alcohol evaporate, then cover with a lid and cook for about 5 minutes until they are all open. Occasionally shake the pot, without removing the lid, to facilitate opening. At this point, add the black pepper .
Add the chopped parsley , stir one last time , and serve your mussels marinara hot !
We recommend consuming the mussels marinara immediately.
If you love spicy food, add a chili pepper to the sauté! If you prefer a red version, you can add cherry tomatoes during cooking.
