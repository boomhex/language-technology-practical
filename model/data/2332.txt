name:
Farfalle with salmon, arugula, and cherry tomatoes
ingredients:
Bow tie pasta: 11.3oz(320 g)
Salmon fillets: 10.5oz(300 g)
Cherry tomatoes: 2 ¾cups(400 g)
Arugula: 2 ½cups(50 g)
Fine salt: to taste
Pepper: 1pinch- black
Pepper: 1pinch- pink
Extra virgin olive oil: 3tbsp(40 g)
Basil: 0.3cup(8 g)

info:
Difficulty: Easy
Prep time: 10 min
Cook time: 15 min
Serving: 4
Cost: Very low

instructions:
To prepare the farfalle with salmon, arugula, and cherry tomatoes, start by cutting the cherry tomatoes into 4 parts , then add the salt , the pepper
the extra virgin olive oil and the fresh basil, torn into pieces . Take the salmon fillet and with a knife remove the skin, trying not to cut away the flesh ,
then with the help of tweezers remove any bones present. Then cut the fillet first into strips and then into chunks of about 1x1 inch .
Cook the pasta and in the meantime in a pan with a drizzle of oil, sauté the salmon for no more than 2 minutes on each side, then add the salt ,
the pink pepper and in the bowl with tomatoes and basil also add the arugula . Once the pasta is cooked, pour it into the bowl with the vegetables ,
mix everything well , finally add the salmon and your farfalle with salmon, arugula, and cherry tomatoes are ready to be enjoyed!
It is advisable to consume the farfalle with salmon, arugula, and cherry tomatoes immediately after preparation, otherwise, you can store it for up to one day in the refrigerator, in an airtight container.
Instead of whole arugula, you can enhance your dish with a delicious arugula cream and if you want to replace the salmon with another fish, try monkfish!
