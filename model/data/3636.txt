name:
Rigatoni with artichokes, olives, and capers
ingredients:
Rigatoni: 3 ¼cups(320 g)
Artichokes: 6
Taggiasca olives: 7.1oz(200 g)- pitted
Anchovies: 1.25oz(35 g)
Salted capers: 2tbsp(30 g)
White wine: 0.4cup(100 g)
Extra virgin olive oil: 2tbsp(30 g)
Garlic: 2cloves
Fine salt: to taste
Black pepper: to taste
Thyme: 3sprigs

info:
Difficulty: Easy
Prep time: 20 min
Cook time: 15 min
Serving: 4
Cost: Low
Note + the cooking of the pasta

To prepare rigatoni with artichokes, olives, and capers, start by bringing abundant salted water to a boil and desalting the capers by rinsing them thoroughly. How to clean artichokes , but before handling them, protect your hands with latex gloves or rub lemon on your skin to prevent them from turning black. Remove the thicker outer leaves until you reach the tender ones, then, with a knife, cut off the tougher tops and also remove the stem . Now peel the stems removing the more fibrous outer part, then cut them into cubes or rings and place them in a bowl filled with water and lemon to prevent them from darkening.
Now cut the artichoke hearts in half, remove the inner choke, and cut them into quarters . Place these in the bowl as well . In a large pan drizzled with olive oil, brown the garlic cloves, then remove them when they start to turn golden .
Add the artichokes at this point . Sauté them, season with salt and pepper, and finally deglaze with the white wine . After 5 minutes of cooking, add the pitted olives and mix the ingredients well.
Chop the anchovies and desalted capers , then add both to the artichokes and cook for another 5 minutes. Turn off the heat and keep the sauce warm. Cook the pasta in the boiling salted water .
When it's cooked, turn the heat back on and drain the pasta directly into the sauce using a slotted spoon , taking care to keep the cooking water. Toss the ingredients together for a few minutes , adding a bit of the cooking water if necessary. Your rigatoni with artichokes, olives, and capers are ready : before serving, sprinkle with fresh thyme.
You can store the sauce alone: for 2 days in the refrigerator in an airtight container or in the freezer in the same manner if you've used only fresh ingredients. Once the pasta is added, it is advisable to consume the dish immediately.
Try adding a chopped fresh chili pepper to the sauce for a spicy touch. The ideal cooking time for the artichokes would be about ten minutes in total to maintain their crunchiness, but you can extend it according to your preference.
