name:
Pears Belle Helene
ingredients:
Bosc pears: 2- hard-boiled
Sugar: 1 ½cup(300 g)
Dark chocolate: 5.6oz(160 g)
Water: 4 ¼cups(1 l)
Cinnamon sticks: 2
Cream gelato: to taste
Almond slices: to taste

info:
Difficulty: Easy
Prep time: 10 min
Cook time: 25 min
Makes: 2 pieces
Cost: Very low

instructions:
To prepare Pears Belle Helene, first pour the water into a saucepan , add the sugar and the cinnamon sticks . Move to the stove and bring to a boil.
In the meantime, peel the pears and when the syrup starts boiling, immerse them inside (5-6).
Let them cook for 25 minutes over medium heat . They should be soft but still firm. At this point, let them cool in their cooking liquid. In the meantime, chop the dark chocolate .
and melt it in a bain-marie or in the microwave . As soon as the pears are lukewarm, you can plate them: transfer them onto a serving dish and pour the melted chocolate over them (11-12).
Place a scoop of ice cream next to it and cover the pear with sliced almonds . Serve your Pears Belle Helene immediately !
Pears Belle Helene can be stored in the refrigerator in their cooking liquid for 4 days.
You can flavor the cooking water with a dash of rum and add star anise to taste!
