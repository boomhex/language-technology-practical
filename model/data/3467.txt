name:
Shrimp Skewers
ingredients:
Shrimp paste: 17.5oz(500 g)
Extra virgin olive oil: 4spoonfuls
Zucchini: 2
Carrots: 2
Type 00 flour: to taste
Fine salt: to taste
Black pepper: to taste
White wine: Â½glass

info:
Difficulty: Easy
Prep time: 20 min
Cook time: 15 min
Makes: 6 pieces
Cost: Average

instructions:
To prepare the shrimp skewers, start by washing the zucchini well, remove the ends, and cut them into julienne strips. Peel the carrots and also cut them into julienne strips , then leave them in a colander to drain excess water. Shell the shrimp and after coating them in flour , thread them onto wooden skewers.
In a pan, put the oil and add the shrimp skewers , deglaze with wine , season with salt and pepper. Remove the skewers and keep them warm, add more oil to the pan, and add the vegetables which should cook for just a few minutes to remain rather crispy , then season with salt. Serve the shrimp skewers laid on a bed of vegetables and accompanied by lemon wedges.
