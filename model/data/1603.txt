name:
Chestnut Flour Cantucci with Almonds and Chocolate
ingredients:
Chestnut flour: 2cups(200 g)
Type 0 flour: 0.8cup(100 g)
Eggs: 2- medium
Sugar: 0.6cup(120 g)
Almonds: 0.7cup(100 g)
Dark chocolate chips: 3.5oz(100 g)
Butter: 1.1oz(30 g)- melted
Orange peel: 1
Baking powder: 1tsp

info:
Difficulty: Easy
Prep time: 20 min
Cook time: 25 min
Makes: 40 pieces
Cost: Average

instructions:
In a bowl, pour the 2 whole eggs together with the sugar , then work them with a whisk for 2-3 minutes (2-3).
Add the melted butter and beat again until you get a frothy mixture . Add the grated orange zest ,
the chestnut flour and all-purpose flour . Then add the baking powder ,
mix with a fork and add both the chocolate chips and the whole unpeeled almonds .
Mix again and if necessary add more all-purpose flour, then transfer the mixture onto a lightly floured surface and compact it with your hands .
Then divide the dough into 3 parts and shape each portion into a sausage about 10 inches long (17-18).
Arrange them on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper, spacing them apart . Bake in a static oven at 356Â°F for 20 minutes on the middle shelf, then take them out and wait a few minutes. When they are not hot, transfer the first log onto a cutting board and cut it into oblique slices about 0.6 inches thick .
place the cantucci back on the baking sheet and bake them again for another 5 minutes. Then take the cookies out and let them cool before serving .
You can store the cantucci for a week in a tin box.
For a more delicate flavor, use 150 g of chestnut flour and 150 g of all-purpose flour. You can also replace the chocolate chips with 3.5 oz of dark chocolate cut into small pieces.
