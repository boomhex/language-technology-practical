name:
Ruby Chocolate Tart
ingredients:
Type 00 flour: 2cups(250 g)
Egg yolks: 2- (of medium eggs)
Powdered sugar: 0.8cup(100 g)
Butter: 5.3oz(150 g)- cold
Vanilla bean: 1
Ruby chocolate: 14.1oz(400 g)- (in pearls)
Fresh liquid cream: 0.8cup(200 g)
Raspberries: to taste
Currants: to taste
Powdered sugar: to taste

To prepare the ruby chocolate tart, start with the Shortcrust pastry : in a bowl, combine the flour, powdered sugar , seeds from the vanilla bean , and cold butter cut into pieces .
Work the ingredients with your fingertips to obtain a coarse sandy mixture , then add the egg yolks and knead quickly .
Once you have a soft and homogeneous dough , wrap it in plastic wrap and let it firm up in the refrigerator for at least 30 minutes . Meanwhile, prepare the ruby chocolate ganache by heating the cream in a saucepan .
As soon as the cream reaches a simmer, pour it over the ruby chocolate pearls . Stir vigorously with a spatula , emulsifying everything slightly, then cover with plastic wrap and refrigerate for at least 2 hours .
Meanwhile, retrieve the shortcrust pastry, roll it out on a lightly floured surface to a thickness of about 1/8 inch , wrap the dough around the rolling pin, and unroll it into a 9-inch mold . Trim the edges using a smooth knife .
Place a sheet of parchment paper at the bottom of the dough and use ceramic weights for How to do blind baking : bake the tart in a static oven, preheated in static mode, at 350°F for about 20 minutes .
Remove from the oven and take out the parchment paper and weights, then bake again for 5 more minutes until golden . While the base cools, retrieve the ganache, and work vigorously with a spoon until it appears whipped . Transfer the cream into a pastry bag with a "Saint Honoré" tip and start decorating the tart from the outer edge .
Complete the decoration with tufts of ganache to fill in the center and finish by adding some berries on top and a dusting of powdered sugar . Your ruby chocolate tart is ready .
The tart can be stored in the fridge for up to 3 days.
The pastry can also be prepared up to a week in advance and kept in the fridge wrapped in plastic.
Add some chopped nuts or shredded coconut on top of your delicious tart if desired.
