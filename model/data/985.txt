name:
Easy and Quick Baked Zucchini
ingredients:
Zucchini: 6cups(750 g)
Mint: 6leaves
Garlic: 1clove
Extra virgin olive oil: 1 ½tbsp(20 g)
Breadcrumbs: 1cup(100 g)
Fine salt: 1 ¾tsp(10 g)

info:
Difficulty: Easy
Prep time: 10 min
Cook time: 20 min
Serving: 4
Cost: Very low

instructions:
To make the baked zucchini, first wash and trim the zucchini . Slice the zucchini lengthwise, making long slices about 1/4 inch thick. Then, cut each slice in half .
Cut the zucchini into sticks about 1/4 inch thick . Place the zucchini sticks in a bowl . Mince the garlic with a knife .
Add the minced garlic to the bowl , along with the salt , and chop the mint leaves .
Then flavor the zucchini with some of the chopped mint , finally add the breadcrumbs and mix with your hands .
The sticks should be well covered and breaded . Place the sticks on a baking tray covered with parchment paper; season with extra virgin olive oil . Flavor with the remaining mint. Bake in a preheated ventilated oven at 400°F for 20 minutes. Remove from the oven and serve the baked zucchini hot .
Consume the baked zucchini immediately; if there are leftovers, store them in the refrigerator for a maximum of one day. Freezing is not recommended.
Instead of mint, you can flavor with thyme, parsley, or marjoram.
