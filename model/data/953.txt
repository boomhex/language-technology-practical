name:
Digestive Canary
ingredients:
Water: 1.7cups(400 g)
Lemon peel: 1- untreated

info:
Difficulty: Very easy
Prep time: 5 min
Cook time: 10 min
Serving: 2
Cost: Very low

instructions:
To prepare the canary, first, thoroughly wash the lemon and peel it with a vegetable peeler, being careful to avoid the white part, otherwise, the beverage will taste bitter . Pour the water into a small pot, add the lemon peel , and bring it to a boil, then let it simmer for about 10 minutes .
When the water has taken on a yellowish color , you can pour the beverage into a glass or cup , straining it through a sieve if you prefer. Your digestive canary is ready to be enjoyed !
The digestive canary should be consumed hot, as soon as it's ready.
If you wish, you can flavor the digestive canary with a bay leaf.
• Can lemon juice also be added?
Yes, but only after cooking; otherwise, the beverage will taste bitter.
• And if I wanted to use ginger?
Just infuse a piece of fresh root together with the lemon peel.
• Which lemons should I choose?
Strictly those that are untreated, with edible peel, preferably organic.
• What are the benefits of lemon peel?
Besides being rich in vitamin C, it helps fight abdominal bloating and detoxify the body.
• Can I use other aromatic herbs as an alternative to bay leaf?
Sage or mint, for example.
