name:
Pasta alla cucunciata
ingredients:
Spaghetti: 11.3oz(320 g)
Cherry tomatoes: 2cups(400 g)
Eggplant: 10.5oz(300 g)
Green peppers: 1 ⅓cup(200 g)
Caper berries: 1.8oz(50 g)- (caper berries in salt)
Anchovies in oil: 1.5oz(40 g)
Black olives: 1.5oz(40 g)
Basil: 5leaves
Garlic: 1clove
Pecorino cheese: to taste
Extra virgin olive oil: to taste
Fine salt: to taste
Black pepper: to taste

instructions:

To prepare cucunciata pasta, start with the bell pepper: grill it on a grill plate or in the oven at 450°F for about 20 minutes . Meanwhile, prepare the sauce: wash the cherry tomatoes and cut them in half , wash and cut the eggplant into 1/2-inch cubes .

Pour a drizzle of oil into a non-stick pan and brown a clove of garlic over low heat . Sauté the eggplant in the pan with the garlic . When the eggplant is well browned, add the cherry tomatoes .

Cook over medium heat until they are well cooked . Meanwhile, in a tall, narrow glass, pour the desalted cucunci and anchovies .
Also add 1.4 oz of oil , a pinch of salt and pepper , and blend with an immersion mixer .
You should obtain a creamy consistency . Meanwhile, the bell pepper will be ready , let it cool for a few minutes and peel it, then cut it into 1- to 1.5-inch pieces, removing the seeds .
Pour it into the pan with the eggplant and tomato sauce . Cook the spaghetti in boiling salted water for the time indicated on the package . Remove the garlic clove from the sauce .


Drain the pasta directly into the pan with the sauce , add enough cooking water and the black olives .
Finally, add the anchovy and cucunci mixture . Fragrantly add fresh basil and season with Pecorino cheese to taste, mix well and plate. Your cucunciata pasta is ready to be served .
We recommend consuming cucunciata pasta immediately.
If you can't find cucunci, you can make the recipe with the more common well-desalted capers.
