name:
Greek Omelette
ingredients:
Eggs: 4
Feta cheese: 5.3oz(150 g)
Black olives: 0.3cup(50 g)- pitted
Piccadilly tomatoes: 8
Grana Padano PDO cheese: 1oz(30 g)- (to grate)
Mint: 3leaves
Extra virgin olive oil: to taste
Fine salt: to taste

info:
Difficulty: Easy
Prep time: 15 min
Cook time: 20 min
Serving: 2
Cost: Low
Note + 10 min. of resting time for the egg mixture

instructions:
To prepare the Greek omelette, crack the eggs into a bowl and add a pinch of salt , beat them for a few minutes with a fork and set aside while you prepare the rest of the ingredients. Slice the black olives into rings
and cut the cherry tomatoes into small pieces , then cut the feta into cubes (5-6).
Take the egg mixture and add the grated Grana Padano DOP , the black olives , the cherry tomatoes ,
the feta and the mint torn with your hands , then mix well with a fork and let the mixture rest for about ten minutes.
Now heat the oil in a non-stick pan with a diameter of 8-9 inches , pour in the egg mixture , cover with a lid , and cook over low heat for 15 minutes.
After this time, flip the omelette with the help of a flat plate , slide it back into the pan and continue cooking for about 5 minutes, always with the lid on. When the other side is cooked, your Greek omelette is ready to be served !
The Greek omelette can be stored in the refrigerator for one day, in an airtight container. Freezing is not recommended.
You can also add fresh basil to fragrance your Greek omelette!
