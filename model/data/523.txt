name:
Mapo tofu
ingredients:
Tofu: 1lb(450 g)- solid
Ground pork: 5.3oz(150 g)
Hot sauce: 2spoonfuls
Soy sauce: 2spoonfuls- light
Soy sauce: 2tsp- dark
Sugar: 1tsp
Potato starch: 1tsp
Water: 4spoonfuls
Fresh scallion: to taste- (the green part)
Sunflower seed oil: to taste
Fine salt: to taste
Black pepper: to taste

info:
Difficulty: Easy
Prep time: 10 min
Cook time: 15 min
Serving: 2
Cost: Average

instructions:
To prepare Mapo Tofu, first cut the tofu into cubes, being very careful not to break them . Blanch the tofu cubes in lightly salted water for one minute , then drain and set aside .
Heat a drizzle of vegetable oil in the wok , then add the minced meat and brown for a few minutes, stirring often .
Season with spicy sauce and pepper. Stir well to combine , then add the tofu .
Season with light soy sauce and dark soy sauce , then pour in 3 tablespoons of hot water .
Add sugar and stir-fry for a couple of minutes. Meanwhile, dilute potato starch with a tablespoon of water and pour it into the wok . Stir well and turn off the heat .
Slice the green part of the spring onion and use it to garnish the dish . Your Mapo Tofu is ready to be enjoyed !
It is recommended to consume Mapo Tofu immediately.
For a vegetarian version of Mapo Tofu, you can simply omit the meat or substitute it with mushrooms, for example!
