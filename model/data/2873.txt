name:
Sandwich Cake
ingredients:
Sandwich bread: 10.5oz(300 g)
Prosciutto cotto: 0.75lb(390 g)
Zucchini: 1 ½cup(300 g)
Radishes: 6.5oz(180 g)
Wild fennel: to taste
Mayonnaise: to taste
Fine salt: to taste
Black pepper: to taste
Cream cheese: 1.125lbs(500 g)
Spinach: 8cups(250 g)
Extra virgin olive oil: 3 ½tbsp(50 g)

info:
Difficulty: Average
Prep time: 60 min
Cook time: 5 min
Serving: 8

instructions:
The sandwich cake is a scenic appetizer that combines fresh flavors and vibrant colors, perfect for delighting both the eyes and the palate during spring festivities. Imagine an architectural masterpiece of perfectly balanced layers alternating delicate slices of white bread, soft shades of spinach, and crunchy radishes. The cake is completed with cooked ham, each slice is a hug of flavor and adds a soft sweetness that harmonizes with the other elements. But the surprise doesn't end here: the zucchini cover the cake with a cute and captivating checkerboard pattern. So, if you want to impress your guests with a spring appetizer that combines elegance and taste, look no further: the sandwich cake is the tasty and original idea that will make your meal unforgettable!
To prepare the sandwich cake, start by boiling the spinach in boiling water for 2 minutes . Drain them and transfer them to a bowl with ice water .
Let them cool for a few minutes, then drain the spinach and squeeze them to remove excess liquid . Pour the boiled spinach into a pitcher, add the oil , and blend with an immersion blender .
You need to achieve a smooth cream . Now incorporate the cream cheese and mix , then add salt and pepper . Set aside the spinach cream.
Take care of the radishes: trim and slice them thinly , then season with oil, salt, pepper, and fennel , mix to flavor and store in the fridge. With an 18 cm diameter ring, cut 2 slices of bread to create a disc . In total, you need to create 5 bread discs that will compose the cake.
Now start assembling the cake: place the first layer of bread on a serving plate , place the ring to maintain the shape during filling, then spread the bread with the spinach cream . Continue with a layer of radishes .
Place 2 slices of cooked ham and start again with a layer of bread , then spread the cream .
Continue with the radishes and ham and proceed until you achieve 4 layers. Close the cake with a layer of bread .
Spread the spinach cream on the surface and all around the edge of the cake , leveling it well with a spatula. Place the cake in the fridge and take care of the zucchini: wash them, trim them, and slice them thinly .
Season them with salt, pepper, and oil , then distribute the slices along the edge, making them adhere to the cream . Cover the surface as well .
Alternate the slices to create a checkerboard effect . To finish, squeeze puffs of mayonnaise and complete with ham rosettes . The sandwich cake is ready to be served!
It is recommended to consume the sandwich cake immediately.
Freezing is not recommended.
You can replace the sandwich bread with sliced bread and use peas instead of spinach.
Do not throw away the leftover bread scraps but use them to make canapés !
