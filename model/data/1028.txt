name:
Spicy pork stew with lentils
ingredients:
Pork loin: 0.75lb(300 g)
Dried lentils: ½cup(100 g)
Swiss chard: 1.1oz(30 g)- red
White onions: 1
Blond beer: 2.1cups(500 g)
Tomato paste: 1 ½tbsp(20 g)
Green Peperoncino: 1tsp- (of Cayenne)
Powdered turmeric: 1tsp
Cinnamon powder: 1tsp
Curry: 1tsp
Juniper berries: 3
Extra virgin olive oil: to taste
Fine salt: to taste

info:
Difficulty: Easy
Prep time: 15 min
Cook time: 1 h 10 min
Serving: 4
Cost: Average

instructions:
To prepare the spicy pork stew with lentils, first cut the loin into cubes of about 1 inch , then thinly slice the onion and chop the herbs into small pieces .
Pour a drizzle of oil into a saucepan, add the onions , and sauté over low heat until softened, stirring often. At this point, add the lentils and toast them for a few seconds, then add the meat cubes and brown them on all sides.
Add the herbs , then season with tomato paste and chili pepper .
Also add turmeric , cinnamon , and curry .
Add the juniper berries and mix well , then cover with the beer .
Salt and cook over low heat for about an hour: the consistency should be saucy but not liquid . Serve your spicy pork stew with lentils accompanied with basmati rice !
The spicy pork stew with lentils can be stored in the refrigerator for up to 3 days. It can be frozen if fresh ingredients have been used.
You can enrich the stew by adding diced potatoes, or you can substitute the tomato paste with fresh tomato.
