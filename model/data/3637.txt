name:
Cuttlefish with Artichokes
ingredients:
Octopi: 0.9lb(400 g)- already cleaned
Artichokes: 8
Extra virgin olive oil: to taste
Butter: 2tbsp(30 g)
Pine nuts: 1.1oz(30 g)
Thyme: 4sprigs
Garlic: 2cloves
White wine: 1glass
Fish broth: 1.1cups(250 g)- or vegetable
Parsley: 1tuft
Shallot: 1
Fine salt: to taste
Black pepper: to taste

info:
Difficulty: Easy
Prep time: 20 min
Cook time: 30 min
Serving: 4
Cost: Average

To prepare the cuttlefish with artichokes, start with the How to clean artichokes , remove the tougher outer leaves , then also remove the outer part of the stem and cut it a few inches below the base of the artichoke , also remove the tips, thus obtaining only the heart .
As soon as you finish cleaning an artichoke, to prevent it from darkening, rub it with half a lemon and then place it in a bowl with acidulated water (4-5), in this way you can proceed with cleaning all the artichokes without them darkening. Once the artichokes are cleaned, drain them well, cut them in half, remove the inner choke, and cut them into thin wedges .
In a pan, melt the butter and add the finely chopped shallot , and let it wilt for 10 minutes; when it is wilted, add the artichokes , brown them well, salt and pepper to taste .
Add a ladle of broth and let them cook until they become soft, then turn off the heat and set the artichokes aside. Meanwhile, wash the cuttlefish under running water and drain them well . Then, in another large pan, place the oil and crushed garlic (if you want to remove it later, leave it whole) .
Add the cuttlefish, let them brown for 10 minutes , and then add the glass of wine, allowing it to evaporate . Cook over very low heat for 10 minutes, then add the artichokes and mix everything well.
Let cook over low heat, covering with a lid until the cuttlefish become tender ; if necessary, add a ladle of broth to prevent it from drying out. Five minutes before turning off the heat, add the chopped thyme and parsley , mix well, then add the pine nuts and adjust the salt and pepper. Your cuttlefish with artichokes are ready to be enjoyed, serve them hot!
Cuttlefish with artichokes should be consumed within a few hours of preparation because once cooked, artichokes tend to develop toxic molecules. If the cuttlefish are fresh, and not defrosted, this dish can also be frozen. Before consuming, place it in a pan with two tablespoons of hot oil to make your cuttlefish with artichokes appetizing again!
Be careful not to overcook the cuttlefish, or they risk becoming tough!
