name:
Chicken bites, peppers and sun-dried tomatoes
ingredients:
Chicken breast: 1.1lbs(500 g)
Red peppers: 1
Yellow peppers: 1
Sun-dried tomatoes in oil: 1 Â½cup(150 g)
Garlic: 1
Extra virgin olive oil: to taste
Type 00 flour: to taste
Dry white wine: 0.67cup(150 g)
Fine salt: to taste

info:
Difficulty: Easy
Prep time: 20 min
Cook time: 30 min
Serving: 4
Cost: Low

instructions:
To prepare the chicken bites with peppers and sun-dried tomatoes, start by washing and cleaning the peppers, removing the stem and seeds inside, then cut them into cubes . Take a pan, add a little extra virgin olive oil and a whole peeled clove of garlic to the bottom . Put it on the heat and add the chopped peppers ,
add salt and also add the sun-dried tomatoes drained from their preservation oil . Cook everything for about 7-8 minutes, stirring occasionally. Take a blender and pour the peppers with the sun-dried tomatoes inside ,
blend to reduce everything into a cream . In a bowl, place some flour and coat the chicken bites in it , you need to flour them well. In the pepper cooking pan, place the floured chicken bites , cook for about 5 minutes, turning them occasionally, adding more oil if necessary.
Deglaze with wine halfway through cooking the chicken and let it evaporate completely. Finally, pour in the pepper and sun-dried tomato cream , thin the sauce with two tablespoons of water if it is too thick and finish cooking the chicken bites for another 7-8 minutes . Serve the chicken bites with their creamy pepper and sun-dried tomato sauce.
The chicken bites, peppers, and sun-dried tomatoes can be stored in the fridge for a maximum of 2-3 days.
Freezing is not recommended.
Add chili pepper to the sauce, as well as pitted black olives at the end of cooking to make it even more appetizing!
