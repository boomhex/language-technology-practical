name:
Vegan Butter
ingredients:
Soy milk: 0.6cup(150 g)- unsweetened
Coconut oil: 1 ⅓cup(300 g)
Lemon juice: 2tbsp(30 g)
Coconut milk: 1.1cups(250 g)- with at least 18% fat
Rice vinegar: 2tsp(10 g)
Sunflower seed oil: ¾tbsp(10 g)
Coconut oil: 2 ½tbsp(35 g)
Soy lecithin: 1 ½tsp(7.5 g)- granular

info:
Difficulty: Easy
Prep time: 15 min
Makes: pieces
Cost: High
Note + the long solidification times

instructions:
To prepare the soy vegan butter, first pour the soy milk into a jug . Then add the coconut oil and the lemon juice .
Blend everything with an immersion blender until you get a smooth cream . At this point, get a butter mold and line it with plastic wrap .
Then pour the mixture inside and let it solidify for at least 3 hours in the refrigerator. After this time, open the mold , remove the plastic wrap, and use your soy vegan butter for preparing sweet and savory dishes !
To prepare the coconut vegan butter, first pour the coconut milk into a jug, add the rice vinegar , the vegetable oil , and the coconut oil .
Blend everything with an immersion blender until you get a smooth cream ; then add the soy lecithin granules and emulsify everything again for about 2 minutes .
Cover with plastic wrap and place in the refrigerator for at least 18 hours. After this time, you will notice that the mixture has thickened and will be very creamy. At this point, you can transfer it to a small bowl and use it as you prefer !
Soy or coconut vegan butter can be stored in the refrigerator for 4 days.
Alternatively, it can be frozen for about 1 month.
To make soy vegan butter, you can replace the coconut oil with vegetable oil in the same amount. Additionally, it can be flavored with spices and herbs according to the preparation.
If you don't have a mold, don't worry; you can use a loaf pan or any bowl, always lined with plastic wrap.
