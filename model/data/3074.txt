name:
Flatbreads with shrimp, garlic, oil, and chili
ingredients:

Extra virgin olive oil: to taste

info:

Cook time: 15 min
Makes: 12 pieces

To make the flatbreads with shrimp, garlic, oil, and chili, pour a drizzle of extra virgin olive oil in a non-stick pan and sauté a previously peeled clove of garlic . Meanwhile, take the fresh chili and, after rinsing it, slice it thinly , then sauté it in the pan along with the garlic .
When the garlic is well golden, add the peeled shrimp and salt them to taste. Sauté them in the pan over high heat for a few minutes until they are well browned . Arrange the flatbreads on a cutting board and place the ingredients on top using a spoon. At this point, your flatbreads with shrimp, garlic, oil, and chili are ready to be served and enjoyed immediately!
We recommend consuming the flatbreads with shrimp, garlic, oil, and chili immediately, but if you wish, you can store them in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to one day.
You can garnish your flatbreads with shrimp, garlic, oil, and chili with a handful of parsley to add an extra note of color and flavor! You can use either frozen shrimp, cooking them directly in the pan without thawing for about 15 minutes, or fresh shrimp, which you will shell and cook in the pan for 10 minutes. In this case, you can shell them while keeping the tails.
