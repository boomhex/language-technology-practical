name:
Carrots with Mustard
ingredients:
Carrots: 4.7cups(600 g)
White wine vinegar: 0.6cup(150 g)
White wine: Â¾cup(200 g)
Water: 3cups(700 g)
Garlic: 2cloves
Extra virgin olive oil: 2spoonfuls
Parsley: 3tufts
Bay leaves: 4leaves
Mustard: 4tsp(20 g)
Fine salt: to taste
Sugar: 1tsp
Black pepper: to taste

info:
Difficulty: Easy
Prep time: 15 min
Cook time: 35 min
Serving: 4
Cost: Low

instructions:
To prepare the carrots with mustard, first wash the carrots well. Then trim them and peel them . Using a serrated blade, cut the carrots into slices, making a diagonal cut .
Continue this way until you've finished all the carrots . In a large pan, pour in the water, add the white wine vinegar , and the white wine
the oil , a pinch of salt , and a teaspoon of sugar .
Add the 2 garlic cloves , the bay leaves, and 2 sprigs of parsley . Bring to a boil, then add the carrots and let them cook over medium heat for about 15-20 minutes. They should be soft but firm.
At this point, use a sieve to drain the carrots and transfer them to a bowl . Let the cooking liquid reduce for another 10 minutes over high heat, then filter it ,
to remove the garlic and herbs . Let it cool, then add the mustard and mix to combine the ingredients .
Transfer the carrots to a serving dish, trying not to overlap them. Dress with the mustard sauce and add chopped parsley on top . The carrots are ready to serve !
Store the carrots in the refrigerator for a maximum of a couple of days. It's better not to dress them and keep the sauce separate in a small bowl.
You can substitute the parsley with a sprig of marjoram or rosemary. For a stronger flavor, add another tablespoon of mustard to the cooking liquid.
