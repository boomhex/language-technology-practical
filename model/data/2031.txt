name:
Baked plaice fillets
ingredients:
Plaice filets: 0.9lb(400 g)
Fresh scallion: 10.5oz(300 g)
Extra virgin olive oil: to taste
Fine salt: to taste
Black pepper: to taste
Rosemary: 1sprig
Sage: 4leaves
Thyme: 2sprigs
Lemons: 1

info:
Difficulty: Very easy
Prep time: 10 min
Cook time: 20 min
Serving: 4

instructions:
To prepare the baked plaice fillets, start by cleaning the scallions: trim the base , cut the green part of the stalk , and rinse them under running water .
Cut the scallions in half lengthwise and place them in a baking dish, season with oil , salt , and pepper.
Chop the sage, thyme, and rosemary with a knife , wash the lemon, dry it, and cut it into thin slices . Grease a baking tray with olive oil .
Place the plaice fillets, season with pepper and salt , flavor with the chopped aromatic herbs , then distribute the lemon slices over the fillets .
Finally, place the scallions between each fillet , salt, pepper, and bake in a preheated static oven at 425Â°F for 20 minutes. Once cooked, remove the baked plaice fillets from the oven , and before serving, drizzle with a little raw oil .
Consume the baked plaice fillets immediately. It is not recommended to freeze them.
To scent the baked plaice fillets, you can use your preferred aromatic herbs: mint and parsley are excellent substitutes!
