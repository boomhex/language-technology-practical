name:
Tasty pan-fried zucchini with onion
ingredients:
Zucchini: 5 ½cups(600 g)
Yellow onions: 1.3lbs(600 g)
Blond beer: 5oz(140 g)
Thyme: to taste
Extra virgin olive oil: to taste
Fine salt: to taste
Black pepper: to taste

To prepare the tasty pan-fried zucchini with onion, first clean and cut the vegetables: remove the inner part of the zucchini , then cut them into sticks about 2.5 inches long and 0.4 inches thick . Thinly slice the onions .
Heat a little oil in a pan, add the onions , and salt them , then cook over medium-low heat for about 10 minutes. Once softened , set them aside on a plate.
Pat the same pan with some kitchen paper and heat another bit of oil with the thyme sprigs . Add the zucchini and sauté over high heat for 7-8 minutes. When they are well browned, salt them and add the softened onions .
At this point, deglaze with the beer and let all the liquid evaporate . Adjust the salt if necessary, pepper to taste, and serve your tasty pan-fried zucchini with onion !
The tasty pan-fried zucchini with onion can be stored in the refrigerator for 2 days, in an airtight container.
Freezing is not recommended.
If you prefer, you can replace the beer with the same amount of white or red wine, or with vegetable broth.
