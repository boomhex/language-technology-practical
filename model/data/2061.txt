name:
Cod in Leek Leaf
ingredients:
Cod: 10.5oz(300 g)
Pumpkin: 0.6cup(150 g)
Leeks: 2leaves
Thyme: to taste
Dill: to taste
Fine salt: to taste
Black pepper: to taste
Extra virgin olive oil: 3tbsp(40 g)

info:
Difficulty: Easy
Prep time: 15 min
Cook time: 30 min
Serving: 2
Note you will need oven cooking bags

instructions:
To make the cod in leek leaf, take the pumpkin and cut it into slices, remove the peel , then cut it into cubes about 1 cm and set aside. Get 2 sheets of parchment paper for oven cooking, lay one sheet on a work surface, then slit the leek lengthwise and peel it, remove the outermost leaves and choose two leaves large enough to wrap the cod. Take a leek leaf, wash it, dry it, and lay it on the parchment paper ,
place the two cod fillets on the leaf , season with thyme leaves, salt , pepper, then roll the leaf around the fillets
completely wrapping them and keeping the seam on the bottom . Place the roll in the center of the parchment paper, also add the pumpkin cubes on the sides, salt, pepper , and season with dill .
Finally, drizzle with olive oil . Now close the sheet into a bundle (11-12)
and wrap a string around so that it remains well sealed. With the indicated quantities, you will obtain two bundles, place them on a baking tray and bake in a preheated static oven at 375Â°F for 35 minutes. After cooking, take the cod in leek leaf out of the oven, unwrap the parchment, and serve the dish hot .
It is preferable to consume the cod in leek leaf freshly prepared. Freezing the preparation is not recommended.
A parcel for all seasons? Replace the pumpkin with other vegetables: zucchini, potatoes, or cherry tomatoes!
