name:
Grilled Pumpkin and Robiola Bruschetta
ingredients:
Homemade bread: 4slices
Robiola cheese: 6.3oz(180 g)- (with truffle)
Prague cooked ham: 3.5oz(100 g)
Mantuan pumpkin: 5.25oz(150 g)
Spinach: 1.67cups(50 g)
Extra virgin olive oil: to taste
Fine salt: to taste
Black pepper: to taste

info:
Difficulty: Easy
Prep time: 15 min
Cook time: 15 min
Serving: 4

instructions:
To prepare the Bruschetta with grilled pumpkin and robiola, start with the pumpkin: slice it into approximately 0.2-inch pieces and remove the seeds, cut them in half and peel them .

Grill the pumpkin slices on both sides on a hot grill until they are well cooked . Transfer the pumpkin to a bowl and season it with oil ,
pepper, and salt , set it aside. Slice the bread and brush it with olive oil .

Toast the bread on the grill until it is golden brown . Start assembling the bruschetta: spread a layer of approximately 1.5 oz of robiola on each slice .
Then fill with baby spinach , Prague ham, and grilled pumpkin . The Bruschetta with grilled pumpkin and robiola is ready to be enjoyed .
We recommend consuming the bruschetta immediately.
Instead of baby spinach, you can use arugula for a more pronounced flavor.
