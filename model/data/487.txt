name:
Rice salad with parsley pesto and seafood
ingredients:
Mussels: 2.2lbs(1 kg)
Clams: 2.2lbs(1 kg)
Whole rice: 1 ½cup(320 g)
Parsley: 3sprigs
Extra virgin olive oil: 5tbsp(70 g)
White wine: ¼cup(60 g)
Fine salt: to taste
Black pepper: to taste
Garlic: 2cloves
Lemon peel: to taste

info:
Difficulty: Easy
Prep time: 30 min
Cook time: 20 min
Serving: 4
Note + the time for cleaning the shellfish

instructions:
To prepare the rice salad with parsley pesto and seafood, start by How to Clean and Open Mussels and How to clean clams . In a large pot, add a drizzle of oil, garlic , and the parsley stems . Sauté for a few minutes. Add the mussels and clams .
Deglaze with white wine and cover with a lid . Let them cook for a few minutes over medium-high heat until they open up .
Drain the seafood , keeping their liquid . Strain it with a paper kitchen towel to remove any impurities .
Pour the strained liquid into a pot and dilute it with water, bring to a boil without adding salt. When it boils, cook the brown rice for the time indicated on the package. In the meantime, shell the mussels and clams , keeping some with shells for plating.
Place the parsley leaves in a tall glass , add the oil . Blend to obtain a creamy pesto .
When cooking is finished, drain the rice and season it with the pesto , stir to flavor .
Adjust salt and pepper, and flavor with lemon zest . Now add the mussels and clams and mix everything together . The rice salad with parsley pesto and seafood is ready to be served .
It is recommended to serve the rice salad with parsley pesto and seafood warm. It can be stored in the fridge for 1 day. Freezing is not recommended.
Salt sparingly because the cooking water for the shellfish is already salty enough.
