name:
Tuna and Onion Pasta
ingredients:
Spaghetti: 11.3oz(320 g)
Tuna filet: 7oz(200 g)- fresh
Red Tropea onions: 2
Taggiasca olives: 1.8oz(50 g)
Marjoram: to taste
Extra virgin olive oil: to taste
Fine salt: to taste
Black pepper: to taste

instructions:

To prepare tuna and onion pasta, start with the onions: clean them and slice them thinly , then place them in a pan with the oil and a pinch of salt .
Add the pepper and gently stew them, adding a ladle of water . Cook on low heat with the lid on for about 5 minutes, or until they are wilted .
Meanwhile, cut the fresh tuna fillet first into slices about 1/2 inch thick , then into strips, and finally into cubes, all the same size . At this point, you can add the tuna to the onions .
Add the olives and sauté for about 5 more minutes over high heat . Meanwhile, cook the spaghetti in boiling salted water. Drain the pasta al dente and transfer it to the pan with the sauce , then briefly toss everything to combine.
Plate and garnish with a bit of marjoram for freshness . Your tuna and onion pasta is ready to be served!
It is recommended to consume the tuna and onion pasta immediately.
If you wish to add a hint of fresh acidity to your tuna and onion pasta, you can deglaze the sauce with lemon juice or garnish the dish with grated lemon zest.
