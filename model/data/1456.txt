name:
White hot chocolate with mint
ingredients:
Whole milk: 2cups(500 g)
Mint syrup: 3.3tbsp(50 g)
Mint: 30leaves
White chocolate: 1.25oz(35 g)
Cornstarch: 2 Â½tbsp(20 g)
Fresh liquid cream: 0.3cup(80 g)- (optional)
Mint: to taste- (optional)

info:
Difficulty: Easy
Prep time: 5 min
Cook time: 10 min
Serving: 2
Cost: Low

instructions:
To prepare the white chocolate with mint, chop the white chocolate (1-2). Pour the milk into a saucepan and turn on medium heat, then add the cornstarch and stir with the whisk.
Add the mint syrup , then the mint leaves , stirring continuously and bring to a boil (it will take about 4-5 minutes).
Turn off the heat, remove the mint leaves and add the chopped white chocolate . Stir again with the whisk to melt it. The white hot chocolate with mint is ready. Now whip the cream with an electric mixer and place it in a piping bag.
Pour the white hot chocolate with mint into the cups , add a dollop of cream and garnish with mint leaves . Your white hot chocolate with mint is ready to serve.
It is advisable to consume immediately.
You can garnish the white hot chocolate with mint with crumbled meringues and mint leaves!
