name:
Strawberry Salad with Balsamic
ingredients:
Valerian (songino): 4cups(100 g)
Arugula: 1 ½cup(100 g)
Strawberries: 1 ½cup(200 g)
Grana Padano PDO cheese: 3.5oz(100 g)
Balsamic vinegar: 2 ¾tbsp(40 g)
Extra virgin olive oil: 3 ½tbsp(50 g)
Fine salt: to taste
Black pepper: to taste

info:
Difficulty: Very easy
Prep time: 15 min
Serving: 4
Cost: Very low

To prepare the strawberry salad with balsamic, first wash the lamb’s lettuce under plenty of fresh running water, gently dry it with a clean cloth , then tear it into pieces with your fingers. Do the same with the arugula ,
washing and drying it carefully . Also wash the strawberries with cold water and dry them ,
then cut them in half and into quarters ; remove the green leaves and set them aside .
Take a rather large salad bowl and combine the salad and the strawberries ; then add the salt ,
ground pepper , and finely sliced parmesan . Dress with a tablespoon of balsamic vinegar
and a tablespoon of oil , then gently mix everything : your strawberry salad with balsamic is ready to be served !
If the salad is already dressed, it is advisable to eat it within the day to maintain its freshness. Freezing is not recommended.
You can mix different types of salad greens of your choice, such as endive, lamb’s lettuce, mixed greens, dandelion, or chervil.
