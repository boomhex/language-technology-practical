name:
Linguine with Tuna and Cherry Tomatoes
ingredients:
Linguine pasta: 11.3oz(320 g)
Datterino tomatoes: 2.2lbs(500 g)
Tuna in oil: 10.5oz(300 g)- (drained weight)
Green olives: ½cup(100 g)
White onions: ½
Oregano: to taste
Extra virgin olive oil: to taste
Fine salt: to taste
Black pepper: to taste

info:
Difficulty: Easy
Prep time: 10 min
Cook time: 15 min
Serving: 4
Cost: Low

instructions:
Even today dinner is saved with these delicious linguine with tuna and cherry tomatoes, a quick, easy, and tasty pasta dish, perfect for those who want to bring a tasty dish to the table but have little time to dedicate to cooking. The secret to the success of this delightful sauce is the presence of a flavorful mix of tuna and olives , tantalizing ingredients that are always available in the pantry, making them perfect for quick-fix recipes like this! Discover here how to prepare linguine with tuna and cherry tomatoes and don't miss other variations of this pasta :
To prepare linguine with tuna and tomatoes, first clean and chop the onion and the olives . Wash and halve the cherry tomatoes .
Heat a splash of oil in a pan, add the onion and the olives , then pepper .
Salt and let it sauté for a minute . Add the cherry tomatoes and stew them for a couple of minutes . Meanwhile, cook the linguine for half of their cooking time.
Add the tuna to the sauce and continue cooking for the time it takes to cook the pasta . Drain the linguine into the pan , making sure to keep some of the cooking water.
Finish cooking the pasta, adding the reserved cooking water as needed . When ready , serve the linguine with tuna and cherry tomatoes garnished with fresh oregano leaves .
We recommend consuming the linguine with tuna and cherry tomatoes immediately.
Here we used jarred tuna fillets, but you can also make the recipe with canned tuna.
