name:
Puff Pastries with Flavored Butter and Anchovies
ingredients:
Olive puff pastry: 12
Butter: 4tbsp(60 g)
Basil: 1 Â¼tsp(5 g)
Chives: 1tsp(5 g)
Rosemary: 1tsp(5 g)
Anchovies: 12

info:
Difficulty: Very easy
Prep time: 10 min
Makes: 12 pieces
Cost: Low

instructions:
The puff pastries with flavored butter and anchovies are a tasty and original finger food to present on your table as an appetizer or for a cocktail party. These tempting snacks are made with a delicious cream of butter flavored with chives, rosemary, and basil, which pairs well with the strong taste of anchovy.
To prepare the puff pastries with flavored butter and anchovies, start by finely chopping the chives and rosemary , then put them together in a small bowl. Also chop the basil leaves and add them to the butter, which should be at room temperature,
then also add the chopped aromatic herbs . Mix the butter well with the help of a fork until you obtain the consistency of a mousse. Place the mixture in a piping bag with a star nozzle and start composing the pastries: squeeze a dollop of butter cream on each pastry and place a rolled anchovy fillet .
The puff pastries with flavored butter and anchovies should be prepared and assembled at the moment. It is recommended to eat the puff pastries with flavored butter and anchovies within the day.
