name:
Penne alla vodka
ingredients:
Penne Rigate pasta: 11.3oz(320 g)
Tomato purée: 1cup(250 g)
Smoked pancetta: 7oz(200 g)
Fresh liquid cream: 0.67cup(150 g)
Vodka: 4.25oz(120 g)
White onions: 1
Parsley: to taste
Extra virgin olive oil: 2spoonfuls
Salt: to taste

info:
Difficulty: Easy
Prep time: 5 min
Cook time: 20 min
Serving: 4
Cost: Low

instructions:
To prepare the penne with vodka, first, peel an onion and finely chop it . Also, dice the smoked bacon . In a skillet, pour in the oil, add the onion , and sauté for 5 minutes.
Add the bacon and cook for another 5 minutes . Deglaze with the vodka and let all the alcohol evaporate over medium heat. Then pour in the tomato sauce and cook gently for 10 minutes. Meanwhile, cook the penne in salted boiling water.
Add the cream to the sauce , mix well , and let the sauce thicken for another 5-7 minutes. Drain the penne while still al dente and transfer them to the skillet .
Mix to combine the pasta well with the sauce . Finally, flavor with chopped parsley . Plate and garnish with a few parsley leaves. Your penne with vodka are ready to be enjoyed !
It is recommended to consume the penne with vodka immediately; if necessary, you can store them in the refrigerator in a sealed container for one day. When ready to eat, reheat them in a skillet with a splash of milk or cream.
For a more refined variant, you can replace the bacon with sautéed shrimp, adding them along with the chopped parsley.
If you love spice, sauté fresh chili with the onion!
