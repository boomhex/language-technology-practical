name:
Cocoa Shortcrust Roses
ingredients:
Type 00 flour: 2 ¼cups(290 g)
Potato starch: 2tbsp(30 g)
Unsweetened cocoa powder: 0.4cup(50 g)
Butter: 1.1cups(250 g)- soft
Powdered brown sugar: ½cup(100 g)
Egg whites: ½cup(115 g)- (at room temperature)
Orange peel: 1

info:
Difficulty: Easy
Prep time: 20 min
Cook time: 15 min
Makes: 25 pieces
Cost: Low

instructions:
To prepare the cocoa shortcrust roses, first sift the flour together with the potato starch and cocoa into a bowl, and mix to combine everything. In the bowl of a stand mixer fitted with a whisk attachment, add the softened butter and powdered brown sugar , start the mixer, and add the grated orange zest .
When the butter and sugar are creamy, pour in the egg whites and continue to work to incorporate them, using a spatula to gather the mixture from the sides of the bowl . Finally, add the sifted powders, one tablespoon at a time .
Continue working the dough to combine everything , then transfer it to a piping bag with a 1/4 inch star tip. Line a baking sheet with parchment paper and use the piping bag to form roses with a diameter of 2-2.5 inches . Bake in a preheated convection oven at 340°F for about 15 minutes .
Once cooked, remove the roses from the oven and transfer them to a wire rack to cool . Your cocoa shortcrust roses are ready to be enjoyed or gifted !
The cocoa shortcrust roses can be stored in a tin box for 4-5 days.
If you prefer, you can replace the orange zest with the seeds of a vanilla bean.
