name:
Cheesy Vegetable Pie
ingredients:
Mozzarella cheese: 7oz(200 g)
Type 00 flour: 1 ¼cup(150 g)
Eggs: 1.75oz(50 g)- whole
Egg yolks: 1 ⅛cup(15 g)
Whole milk: 6 ¾tbsp(100 ml)- at room temperature
Instant yeast for salted preparations: 1 ½tsp(5 g)
Fine salt: ½tsp(3 g)
Broccoli: 7.1oz(200 g)- cleaned
Carrots: 0.9cup(100 g)
Potatoes: 5.25oz(150 g)
Celery: 2ribs
Garlic: 1clove
Leeks: 2.8oz(80 g)
Extra virgin olive oil: to taste
Fine salt: to taste
Black pepper: to taste
Thyme: to taste
Marjoram: to taste

info:
Difficulty: Easy
Prep time: 20 min
Cook time: 40 min
Makes: 6 pieces

instructions:
To make the cheesy vegetable pie, first clean and cut the potatoes into cubes of about 1 cm . Cut the carrots and celery into cubes of about 1/2 cm.
Thinly slice the leek . Cut the broccoli into pieces . Pour a drizzle of oil into a large pan. Add the garlic clove and let it sauté over high heat for about 2 minutes.
Then add the cubed potatoes and the sliced leek. Let cook over low heat for about 5 minutes. Add all the chopped vegetables to the pan .
Stir, salt, and pepper . Cook over high heat for about 5 minutes. Turn off the heat and set aside. In a bowl, pour the milk, egg, yolk , aromatic herbs, salt .
Mix with a whisk . Incorporate the flour and the baking powder .
Mix well to obtain a smooth mixture . Add the now cooled vegetables to the mixture and mix everything .
Butter 6 aluminum molds. Fill the molds halfway with the mixture . Place a piece of mozzarella of about 1.5 inches at the center of each mold on top of the vegetable mixture . Cover the mozzarella with the vegetable mixture, filling the mold to the edge .
Place the molds on a tray and bake in a preheated static oven at 350°F for 30/35 minutes until the surface is golden. Remove from the oven and unmold the cheesy vegetable pie while still hot .
The cheesy vegetable pie can be stored in the fridge for 2 days. You can freeze it after cooking.
Have fun customizing the filling with various seasonal vegetables!
