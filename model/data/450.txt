name:
Creamy Pumpkin and Gorgonzola Spaghetti
ingredients:
Spaghetti: 11.3oz(320 g)
Pumpkin: 3.4cups(800 g)
Thyme: 1bunch
Garlic: 3cloves
Rosemary: 3sprigs
Fine salt: to taste
Extra virgin olive oil: to taste
Gorgonzola cheese: 5.3oz(150 g)- sweet
Whole milk: ¼cup(50 g)
Amaretti cookies: 4
Edible flowers: to taste
Thyme: to taste
Marjoram: to taste

info:
Difficulty: Easy
Prep time: 20 min
Cook time: 45 min
Serving: 4
Cost: Average

instructions:
To prepare the creamy spaghetti with pumpkin and gorgonzola, start by cleaning the pumpkin: cut it in half, remove the seeds , cut it into slices so it will be easier to remove the skin .
Cut it into small cubes of about 0.5 inches . Place the pumpkin on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper, season with garlic, rosemary, thyme, salt , and oil. Bake at 350°F for 30/35 minutes. Once cooked, remove the garlic and herbs .
Pour the cooked pumpkin into a tall glass, add a little hot water, adjust the amount based on the consistency . Blend with an immersion blender to obtain a velvety cream .
Boil the spaghetti for just 2-3 minutes . Meanwhile, prepare the fondue: in a saucepan, pour the gorgonzola and milk , melt the cheese over low heat, stirring with a whisk until you get a smooth cream.
Pour the pumpkin cream into a pan , thin it with some cooking water to dilute it . Drain the spaghetti here , cook them by adding hot water as needed .
Serve the pasta with the sauce , the fondue , edible flowers, crumbled amaretti, thyme, and marjoram to taste. The creamy pumpkin and gorgonzola spaghetti .
We recommend consuming the creamy pumpkin and gorgonzola spaghetti immediately.
You can substitute the gorgonzola with taleggio and the amaretti with toasted bread crumbs.
