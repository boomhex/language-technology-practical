name:
Classic puff pastries with pumpkin and ginger compote
ingredients:
Pumpkin: 0.8cup(200 g)- cleaned
Shallot: 1
Rosemary: 2sprigs
Fresh ginger: 1tsp(5 g)
Extra virgin olive oil: 2spoonfuls
Smoked pancetta: 2slices
Fine salt: to taste
White wine: 1.7oz(50 ml)
Crispy baked puff pastry: 12

info:
Difficulty: Easy
Prep time: 20 min
Cook time: 20 min
Makes: 12 pieces
Cost: Low

instructions:

To prepare the classic puff pastries with pumpkin and ginger compote, start by peeling the pumpkin with a knife and cut the pulp into small cubes . In a non-stick saucepan, heat the extra virgin olive oil and sauté the chopped shallots, then add the pumpkin pieces and brown them over medium heat for about ten minutes.
Add the peeled and sliced ginger and rosemary sprigs (4-5). Let it infuse for a few seconds, then deglaze with white wine and cook the pumpkin, adding a little hot water if needed.
Blend half of the pumpkin until you get a thick cream and transfer it to a piping bag. Meanwhile, cut the slices of smoked bacon into rectangles and fry them for a few seconds in a non-stick pan to make them crispy. Assemble the snack by placing some pumpkin compote on each pastry , a few pumpkin cubes, a strip of smoked bacon, and a few rosemary needles. Here are your classic puff pastries with pumpkin and ginger compote ready!
Consume the classic puff pastries with pumpkin and ginger compote as soon as they are made. If there is any leftover, the pumpkin compote can be stored in an airtight container for two days.
