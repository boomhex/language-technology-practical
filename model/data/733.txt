name:
Coconut Banana Bread
ingredients:
Coconut milk: 1cup(250 g)
Bananas: 0.4lb(200 g)- ripe
Type 00 flour: 1.67cups(200 g)
Brown sugar: ½cup(100 g)
Coconut oil: 4 ⅓tbsp(60 g)
Dark chocolate chips: 2.1oz(60 g)
Shredded coconut: 4tbsp(30 g)
Baking powder: 2 ½tsp(10 g)
Coconut oil: to taste
Type 00 flour: to taste
Dehydrated coconut: to taste- flakes
Mix of dried fruits: to taste- hazelnuts, walnuts, almonds

info:
Difficulty: Easy
Prep time: 20 min
Cook time: 55 min
Serving: 6

instructions:
To make the coconut banana bread, first put the ripe bananas in a large bowl and mash them with a fork , then add the brown sugar .
Pour in the coconut oil and whisk the ingredients together. Add the coconut milk and mix again to combine everything .
Now, add the flour , baking powder , and shredded coconut . Incorporate the dry ingredients with a whisk until you get a homogeneous mixture.
Finally, add the chocolate chips . Pour the batter into a 8x4 inch loaf pan that you have previously greased with coconut oil and floured , then garnish the top with coconut flakes .
Add the nuts and bake in a preheated static oven at 350°F for about 50-55 minutes . Once baked, remove from the oven and let your coconut banana bread cool before removing it from the pan and serving !
The coconut banana bread can be stored for 2-3 days at room temperature or in the refrigerator on warmer days.
Coconut oil can be replaced with vegetable oil.
If you like, you can flavor the dough with cinnamon or ginger powder!
