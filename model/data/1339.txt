name:
Tub gurnard in fish stew
ingredients:
Gurnard: 14.1oz(400 g)- (cleaned fillets)
Cherry tomatoes: 2cups(300 g)
Carrots: 3.5oz(100 g)
Celery: ¾cup(100 g)
Spring onions: 1.75oz(50 g)
White wine: 1 ½tbsp(20 g)
Water: 1 ¼cup(300 g)
Garlic: 1clove
Fresh chili pepper: 1
Thyme: to taste
Extra virgin olive oil: to taste
Fine salt: to taste
Black pepper: to taste

info:
Difficulty: Easy
Prep time: 10 min
Cook time: 30 min
Serving: 4
Cost: Average

instructions:
To make the tub gurnard in fish stew, first prepare the vegetables for the sauté: trim the spring onion , remove the outer leaves, and slice it finely . Wash and trim the celery and cut it into slices .
Peel the carrots and also cut them into thin slices . In a saucepan, pour a drizzle of olive oil , place it on the heat to warm, then add the sliced spring onion and the garlic clove. Let it brown over low heat.
Meanwhile, cut the chili pepper and remove the seeds . Add the chili pepper to the saucepan , let it flavor for a couple of minutes, then add the celery and carrots .
Also incorporate the washed and dried cherry tomatoes and cook for a few minutes. Now deglaze with the wine and let the alcohol evaporate. At this point, place the tub gurnard fillets in the saucepan .
Pour in the water , cover with the lid , and cook the fish for 20 minutes over medium heat. Halfway through cooking, adjust the salt .
Scent with thyme leaves . After 20 minutes, the tub gurnard will be tender and will have absorbed all the aromas of the sauce, so turn off the heat and add some freshly ground pepper . Serve the tub gurnard in fish stew hot with a drizzle of raw olive oil !
The tub gurnard in fish stew can be stored in the refrigerator for one day. You can freeze it if you used fresh fish.
Try adding some pitted black olives to the sauce to make the stew even tastier!
