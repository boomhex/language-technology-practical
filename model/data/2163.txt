name:
Pasta with swordfish and zucchini
ingredients:
Fusilli pasta: 11.3oz(320 g)
Swordfish: 0.8lb(350 g)
Zucchini: 3 Â¼cups(400 g)
White wine: 1.5oz(40 g)
Garlic: 1clove
Extra virgin olive oil: 2tbsp(30 g)
Green pepper: to taste- the black one
Lemon peel: 1
Thyme: 1sprig
Fine salt: to taste

info:
Difficulty: Very easy
Prep time: 15 min
Cook time: 16 min
Serving: 4
Cost: Average

instructions:
To make the pasta with swordfish and zucchini, bring plenty of water to a boil in a pot and when it boils, salt it; meanwhile wash and trim the zucchinis, then julienne them with a coarse grater , then clean the swordfish slices: remove the strip of lateral skin and cut the flesh first into strips
then into small cubes of about 1/2 inch . In a pan, heat the extra virgin olive oil with the whole garlic clove , then add the swordfish pieces
let them brown for 1 minute, stirring, then deglaze with the white wine , let it evaporate for a few moments, and then add the julienned zucchini , salt, pepper, and cook for 4-5 minutes, stirring occasionally. Then turn off the heat and scent with fresh thyme leaves ,
remove the garlic clove with kitchen tongs and scent with grated lemon zest. Boil the fusilli in the pot with salted water brought to a boil for about 11 minutes or as indicated on the package ; they should be al dente.
Drain them with a slotted spoon directly into the pan with the fish . Add a bit of pepper to taste and serve the pasta with swordfish and zucchini hot !
It is preferable to consume the pasta with swordfish and zucchini immediately, if there are leftovers you can store it in an airtight container in the refrigerator for a maximum of one day. Freezing is not recommended.
Seasonal variation: add a touch of extra color by adding delicious cherry tomatoes to your dish!
