name:
Flatbreads with basil cream
ingredients:
Baked mash potatoes: 12
Robiola cheese: 7oz(200 g)
Basil: 1 ½cup(30 g)
Milk: 1tsp
Tomatoes: - cherry tomato 12
Olive oil: to taste
Thyme: 2sprigs
Oregano: to taste
Pepper: - black as needed
Garlic: - 1/2 clove
Sugar: 1spoonful
Salt: to taste

info:
Difficulty: Easy
Prep time: 20 min
Serving: 4
Cost: Low
Note + cooking time for the cherry tomatoes

instructions:

The recipe for flatbreads with basil cream starts with the preparation of the confit cherry tomatoes: cut the cherry tomatoes in half and place them cut side up on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper. Salt, pepper , and sprinkle the cherry tomatoes with sugar, thyme, minced garlic, oregano , finally drizzle the cherry tomatoes with extra virgin olive oil and bake at 285°F (fan oven) for about an hour and a half.
Meanwhile, prepare the cream by placing the basil, robiola, and a teaspoon of milk in a mixer; when the mixture is well blended , salt, pepper, and with the help of a piping bag, pour the cream onto each flatbread , finally decorate with the confit cherry tomatoes and serve.
