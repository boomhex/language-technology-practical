name:
Pumpkin Peel Chips
ingredients:
Pumpkin: 1- the peel
Paprika: 1tsp
Garlic powder: to taste
Sesame seeds: to taste
Black sesame seeds: to taste
Pumpkin seeds: to taste
Sunflower seeds: to taste
Rosemary: to taste
Extra virgin olive oil: to taste
Fine salt: to taste

To make the pumpkin peel chips, first wash the pumpkin well, then remove the peel using a vegetable peeler . Transfer the peels to a bowl and season with garlic powder and paprika .
Add salt and oil and mix well .
Spread the seasoned peels on a baking tray lined with parchment paper, then garnish with white and black sesame seeds , pumpkin seeds , and sunflower seeds .
Sprinkle with some chopped rosemary needles and bake in a preheated static oven at 356°F for about 25-30 minutes. Take out of the oven and serve your pumpkin peel chips immediately !
It is recommended to consume the pumpkin peel chips immediately. You can store them at room temperature for 4-5 days.
You can flavor the pumpkin peel chips to your liking using other spices or herbs!
We recommend using the peel of Delica or Butternut squash for optimal results.
