name:
Ciffe e ciaffe
ingredients:
Pancetta: 0.75lb(400 g)
Pork loin: 0.9lb(400 g)
Pork jowl: 0.9lb(400 g)
Pork ribs: 0.9lb(400 g)
Sweet dried Peperoncinos: 6
Dry white wine: 3.5oz(100 g)
Vegetable broth: Â½cup(100 g)
Garlic: 6cloves
Bay leaves: 7leaves
Rosemary: 3sprigs
Extra virgin olive oil: to taste
Fine salt: to taste
Black pepper: to taste

info:
Difficulty: Easy
Prep time: 10 min
Cook time: 60 min
Serving: 4
Cost: Low

instructions:
To prepare Ciffe e Ciaffe, first cut the meat into rough pieces: the loin , the pancetta , and the cheeks .
In a large pan, heat the oil, add the bay leaves and rosemary sprigs for flavor, then add the meat: the whole ribs and the remaining roughly chopped pieces . Brown the meat for 5-7 minutes over high heat, then deglaze with wine . Let it evaporate.
Cover with a lid and let it cook over low heat, stirring often, for about 30 minutes. Check that the meat doesn't dry out, then add 7 oz of vegetable broth , stir and cover again with the lid . The Ciffe e Ciaffe should cook for a total of about 50 minutes, but times may vary depending on the size of the meat.
Meanwhile, break the dried peppers into a bowl, removing the seeds inside . When there are 10 minutes left, add the dried peppers and the unpeeled garlic .
Salt , pepper and finish cooking . Serve the Ciffe e Ciaffe hot .
Ciffe e Ciaffe can be stored in the refrigerator for 1-2 days, reheat well before serving. Freezing is not recommended.
Also try the variation of this traditional recipe that involves using fresh peppers instead of dried peppers!
