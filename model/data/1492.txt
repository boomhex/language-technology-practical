name:
Tomato and chickpea soup with bulgur
ingredients:
Bulgur: 1.1cups(200 g)
Peeled tomatoes: 1.6cups(380 g)
Precooked chickpeas: 0.6cup(140 g)
Carrots: 3
Shallot: ½
Tomato paste: 2tbsp(30 g)
Sweet Paprika: 2tsp(5 g)
Sage: 6leaves
Thyme: to taste
Marjoram: to taste
Extra virgin olive oil: to taste
Fine salt: to taste
Black pepper: to taste

info:
Difficulty: Very easy
Prep time: 5 min
Cook time: 55 min
Serving: 4
Cost: Low

instructions:
To prepare the tomato and chickpea soup with bulgur, first clean and chop the shallot , then peel and slice the carrots thinly . In a pot, sauté the shallot with a drizzle of oil for 2-3 minutes ,
then add the carrots and cook for another 3 minutes, adding a little water as needed. Now pour in the peeled tomatoes and mash them with a wooden spoon . Season with salt and pepper and cook over medium heat for about 5 minutes.
At this point, add the drained chickpeas and the aromatic herbs , mix well, cover with a lid , and cook for about 10 minutes, adding more water as needed to prevent burning.
After this time, pour the bulgur directly into the pot , mix well and add the paprika . Continue cooking for another 30 minutes, adding a little water at a time as needed .
Add the tomato paste . At the end of cooking, season with a drizzle of raw oil and a grind of black pepper. Your tomato and chickpea soup with bulgur is ready to be served hot !
The tomato and chickpea soup with bulgur can be stored in the refrigerator for up to one day. Freezing is not recommended.
If you wish, you can also add potatoes during the preparation by placing them in the pot along with the chickpeas!
