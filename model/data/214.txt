name:
Buckwheat pasta with pea cream and crispy speck
ingredients:
Buckwheat rigatoni: 11.3oz(320 g)
Speck: 3.5oz(100 g)- thin slices
Peas: 1.4cups(200 g)
Spinach: 3.3cups(100 g)
Extra virgin olive oil: 1.3tbsp(20 g)
Fine salt: to taste
Black pepper: to taste

info:
Difficulty: Easy
Prep time: 15 min
Cook time: 15 min
Serving: 4
Cost: Low

instructions:
To prepare buckwheat pasta with pea cream and crispy speck, first, blanch the peas in salted boiling water for 5 minutes . Drain them and plunge them into ice water . In the same water, blanch the spinach for 5 minutes .
Drain and also plunge the spinach into ice water . Drain the vegetables and transfer them to a jug, being careful to set aside about 1.8 oz of peas for plating. Season with oil , salt, and pepper .
Add a little cooking water and blend with an immersion blender until a smooth and homogeneous cream is obtained . Transfer the cream to a pan and set aside.
Cook the buckwheat pasta in the same water in which you blanched the vegetables . Meanwhile, roughly chop the speck and toast it briefly in a pan .
It should become crispy . Drain the pasta al dente and transfer it to the pan , then toss to combine .
Plate and top with the reserved peas and speck . Your buckwheat pasta with pea cream and crispy speck is ready to be served !
It's recommended to consume the buckwheat pasta with pea cream and crispy speck immediately; if necessary, you can store it in the refrigerator for one day.
As an alternative to speck, you can use guanciale or smoked bacon.
For a vegetarian version, you can replace the crispy speck with toasted nuts, such as almonds or hazelnuts!
