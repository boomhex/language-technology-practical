name:
Chocolate Banana Bread
ingredients:
Bananas: 1lb(250 g)- ripe (to peel)
Type 00 flour: 0.8cup(100 g)
Brown sugar: ½cup(85 g)
Unsweetened cocoa powder: 9 ¼tbsp(50 g)
Eggs: 2
Peanut butter: 5tbsp(75 g)
Chocolate drops: 3tbsp(30 g)
Milk: ⅛cup(30 g)
Baking powder: 1.4tsp(7 g)
Cinnamon powder: ½tsp
Salt: 1pinch
Milk Chocolate: 3.5oz(100 g)
Chocolate drops: to taste

info:
Difficulty: Average
Prep time: 20 min
Cook time: 30 min
Serving: 6
Cost: Low

instructions:
The chocolate banana bread is an irresistible dessert, perfect to start the day with energy or for a cozy and tasty snack break. Ripe bananas add softness and moisture to the batter of the classic banana bread , but in this case, the dark chocolate makes it even more delicious and will win you over with the first bite.
A simple recipe, within everyone's reach , that combines the natural sweetness of fruit with the pleasure of chocolate in a truly enveloping mix. Furthermore, it's a great anti-waste idea to use up the blackened bananas that no one wants to eat anymore!
Whether at breakfast or during the day, the chocolate banana bread will always be ready to give you a moment of genuine sweetness... a recipe that smells like home and will bring many smiles to the table!

To prepare the chocolate banana bread, peel the bananas and break them into a large bowl , then mash them with a fork .
When you have a creamy puree , add the eggs and peanut butter .
Add the salt , brown sugar , and cinnamon .
Mix with a whisk until you get a homogeneous mixture . At this point, sift the flour into the bowl .
Sift the cocoa and baking powder as well, then mix everything thoroughly .
You will get a thick batter , so dilute it with a bit of milk if needed .
Finally, add the chocolate chips and incorporate them uniformly into the mixture . Pour the batter into a loaf pan lined with parchment paper .
Bake in a preheated static oven at 356°F for about 30 minutes , always checking the cooking with a toothpick before removing from the oven. Once cooked, let the banana bread cool in the pan , then unmold it .
Melt the milk chocolate in the microwave or in a bain-marie and let it drizzle over the surface of the banana bread , then garnish with more chocolate chips. Your chocolate banana bread is ready to be enjoyed !
The chocolate banana bread can be stored at room temperature for about 2-3 days, wrapped in plastic wrap or kept in an airtight container.
It can be frozen sliced after baking and cooling.
You can further enrich the banana bread batter with walnuts, almonds, or hazelnuts for a crunchy note.
