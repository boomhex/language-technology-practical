name:
White Chocolate and Lemon Cake
ingredients:
White chocolate: 4.25oz(120 g)
Butter: 6tbsp(80 g)
Sugar: 4tbsp(50 g)
Type 00 flour: ⅓cup(40 g)
Potato starch: 3 ¾tbsp(30 g)
Lemon peel: ½
Lemon juice: ½
Eggs: 2- medium
Powdered sugar: to taste

info:
Difficulty: Average
Prep time: 20 min
Cook time: 12 min
Makes: 10 pieces
Cost: Average

instructions:
The white chocolate and lemon cake is a spoon dessert inspired by the chocolate cake with a melting heart , a must-have on the dessert menus of all restaurants, alongside the traditional and timeless tiramisu . No one can resist the soft heart of this cake, presented here in a version with captivating flavors, featuring an unusual combination: the sweetness of white chocolate alongside the fresh and aromatic taste of lemon. Come discover this original variant and try other cake recipes to make at home:

To prepare the white chocolate and lemon cake, first chop the chocolate . Place a saucepan over a bain-marie and pour in the chocolate and the butter .
Once the white chocolate is melted, set aside to cool . In a bowl, pour the two eggs and the sugar , and beat the mixture with electric whisks until it becomes fluffy.
Add the sifted cornstarch and flour . Incorporate the previously melted white chocolate .
Add the lemon zest and its juice . Stir to combine .
Butter and flour the aluminum molds with a capacity of 1/2 cup, pour the batter here . With these doses, you will get 10 cakes. Bake in a preheated static oven at 350°F for 12 minutes. Remove the cakes from the oven .
Unmold the hot cakes and cover with powdered sugar to taste . Serve the white chocolate and lemon cakes immediately .
It is recommended to consume the White Chocolate and Lemon Melting Heart Cakes immediately. You can store the cakes in the freezer for up to a month and bake them from frozen, increasing the baking time.
Not all ovens perform the same, so we recommend testing the cooking on one cake before baking them all.
