name:
Creamy prosciutto pasta
ingredients:
Penne Rigate pasta: 11.3oz(320 g)
Fresh liquid cream: 0.9cup(200 g)
Prosciutto cotto: 3.5oz(100 g)
Extra virgin olive oil: 3spoonfuls
Garlic: 1clove
Fine salt: to taste
Black pepper: to taste
Nutmeg: to taste
Grana Padano PDO cheese: ¾cup(75 g)- freshly grated

info:
Difficulty: Very easy
Prep time: 10 min
Cook time: 10 min
Serving: 4
Cost: Low

instructions:
The Creamy prosciutto pasta is a very appetizing dish, an excellent quick meal to prepare when time is short, but you do not want to give up a dish that tickles the palate. Less elaborate than the traditional tortellini with ham and cream or the Fusilli with cream, ham, and peas , these penne will delight the whole family, thanks to their irresistible creaminess. A pleasurable sauce to prepare in the pasta cooking time which, once ready, will be to be enjoyed very hot!
Try these creamy pastas too:
To make the Creamy prosciutto pasta , first place a pot full of water on the stove, salt it at boiling: it will be used to cook the pasta. Now take the slices of cooked ham and, with a knife, cut them into small pieces . Cook the pasta al dente and take care of the sauce.
Pour the extra virgin olive oil into a pan and lightly brown a clove of garlic . Add the cooked ham and let it sauté for just a minute, then remove the garlic .
Add the fresh cream , the grated Grana Padano cheese , black pepper
and nutmeg . Give it a good stir . In the meantime, the pasta will have cooked, drain it al dente and put it in the pan with the sauce .
Mix well all the ingredients and add a little cooking water from the pasta , just enough to make the dish creamy . Serve the Creamy prosciutto pasta very hot .
We recommend consuming the Creamy prosciutto pasta immediately.
We recommend consuming the Creamy prosciutto pasta right away, otherwise it tends to dry out, losing its typical creaminess.
