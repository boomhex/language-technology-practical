name:
Vegan custard
ingredients:
Rice milk: 1.7cups(400 g)
Sugar: Â½cup(90 g)
Cornstarch: 7tbsp(55 g)
Powdered turmeric: 1pinch
Vanilla extract: 1tsp
Lemon peel: to taste- organic

info:
Difficulty: Very easy
Prep time: 10 min
Cook time: 10 min
Makes: pieces
Cost: Low

instructions:
To prepare the vegan custard, pour the sugar , cornstarch , and a pinch of turmeric, just the tip of a teaspoon into a saucepan.
Also add the vanilla extract and mix the powders with a hand whisk . Gradually add the rice milk, stirring continuously to avoid lumps . Place the saucepan on the stove over low heat and continue stirring .
Add the zest of half an organic lemon , being careful not to include the white part, which is very bitter. Stir for 2 minutes , then remove the lemon peel with a strainer .
Continue cooking, stirring until the cream is almost boiling . At this point, turn off the stove and continue stirring during the first minutes of cooling . Transfer the custard to a bowl , and the vegan custard is ready. You can use it immediately or cover it with cling film and store it in the fridge.
Vegan custard can be stored in the refrigerator for up to 3 days.
By slightly increasing the amount of cornstarch, you can obtain a firmer cream suitable for filling.
