name:
Tomato pastina
ingredients:
Risoni pasta: 11.3oz(320 g)
Tomato purée: 8.8oz(250 g)
Vegetable broth: to taste
White onions: 1
Garlic: 1clove
Basil: to taste
Fine salt: to taste
Black pepper: to taste
Parmigiano Reggiano PDO cheese: 2tbsp
Butter: 0.7oz(20 g)- (chilled)
Green Peperoncino: to taste
Sugar: ¾tsp- (optional)

info:
Difficulty: Very easy
Prep time: 10 min
Cook time: 15 min
Serving: 4
Cost: Low

instructions:
"What should I prepare for dinner tonight?" In response to the timeless question, we have a classic suggestion: tomato pastina! A comforting dish that takes us back to our childhood years. This recipe draws inspiration from tomato soup but offers a delightful twist compared to traditional pastina in broth. Here, the pasta is sautéed directly in a simple sauce crafted with garlic and onions. A dash of chili pepper adds an unexpected and flavorful kick. Don't forget to serve this indulgent and creamy pasta dish with a generous dusting of Parmigiano Reggiano!
It's a quick and easy recipe, prepared in a single pan, making it a perfect time-saving solution for your winter dinner menus!
To prepare tomato pastina, peel and chop the onion . In a pan, heat the oil and add the onion , a pinch of salt, and sauté until golden brown.
Also, add the chili and the basil stems. As soon as the onion becomes translucent, add the grated garlic . Sauté it a little more to soften it. Pour in the tomato puree .
Season with salt and a pinch of sugar (optional) . Cook for 5 minutes with a lid on over low heat, stirring occasionally. Add the orzo , cover it with the hot broth , and bring it to a simmer while stirring occasionally, making sure the pasta cooks to perfection.
Once the cooking is complete, blend in the cheese and cold butter , flavor it with basil leaves , and, if needed, add a touch more broth just enough to make the dish creamy.
The tomato pastina is ready , season it with a sprinkle of grated Parmigiano Reggiano cheese to your liking, and serve it right at the table .
It is recommended to consume the tomato pastina immediately.
If you don't have time to prepare the broth, you can cook the pastina with hot water.
