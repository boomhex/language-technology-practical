name:
Risotto-style Spaghetti with Garlic, Oil, and Chili Pepper
ingredients:
Spaghettoni: 11.3oz(320 g)
Garlic: 3cloves
Fresh chili pepper: 2
Parsley: to taste
Extra virgin olive oil: 3 ½tbsp(50 g)

info:
Difficulty: Easy
Prep time: 10 min
Cook time: 15 min
Serving: 4
Cost: Very low

instructions:
To prepare the risotto-style garlic, oil, and chili spaghetti, first bring a pot of salted water to a boil to cook the pasta. Meanwhile, chop the garlic and chili pepper into pieces , then finely chop the parsley. Pour the oil, garlic, and chili pepper into a large pan .
Sauté over low heat for a couple of minutes, being very careful not to burn the garlic . When the water reaches a boil, cook the spaghetti for half the time indicated on the package . In the meantime, add a ladle of pasta cooking water to the sauté .
Drain the spaghetti directly into the pan and finish cooking them, adding more cooking water as needed and stirring often as if it were a risotto . Finally, season with the chopped parsley .
Plate the spaghetti with its sauce and garnish with additional parsley to taste . Serve your risotto-style garlic, oil, and chili spaghetti immediately !
It is recommended to consume the risotto-style garlic, oil, and chili spaghetti as soon as it is ready.
If you prefer a less spicy result, you can remove the seeds from the chili pepper before chopping it into pieces.
