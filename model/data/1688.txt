name:
Gluten-free shortcrust pastry
ingredients:
Fioretto corn flour: 1 ½cup(165 g)
Rice flour: 1 ½cup(200 g)
Butter: 4.6oz(130 g)- cold
Egg yolks: 4 ¼tbsp(60 g)- (about 4)
Eggs: 2oz(55 g)- whole (about 1)
Brown sugar: ½cup(120 g)
Lemon peel: ½
Fine salt: 1pinch

info:
Difficulty: Easy
Prep time: 10 min
Makes: pieces
Cost: Low
Note + 2 hours of resting the pastry in the refrigerator

instructions:
To make the gluten-free shortcrust pastry, place the cold butter in pieces, the rice flour , the corn flour , and the brown sugar into the bowl of a food processor with blades.
Flavor the dough with lemon zest then add the egg yolks and the whole egg .
Finally, add a pinch of salt , then start the mixer to obtain a homogeneous and soft mixture . Transfer it to a lightly floured work surface, knead with your hands to compact it, and form a dough ball . Wrap the dough in plastic wrap and place it in the refrigerator to firm up for at least 2 hours before rolling it out and using it in your recipes.
The gluten-free pastry can be stored in the refrigerator for 3-4 days. Alternatively, it can be frozen for up to 1 month.
As an alternative to lemon zest, you can use the seeds from a vanilla pod or its extract to flavor the dough.
