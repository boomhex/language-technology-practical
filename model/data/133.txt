name:
Walnut and Chocolate Cake
ingredients:
Type 00 flour: 2cups(250 g)
Potato starch: 4tbsp(35 g)
Sugar: 1cup(200 g)
Walnut kernels: 5oz(140 g)- (coarsely chopped)
Dark chocolate chips: 5oz(140 g)
Whole milk: 1 ¼cup(280 g)
Eggs: 5.8oz(165 g)- (about 3 medium)
Sunflower seed oil: ½cup(120 g)
Baking powder: 2tsp(10 g)
Vanilla extract: 1tsp(5 g)

info:
Difficulty: Easy
Prep time: 20 min
Cook time: 60 min
Serving: 8
Cost: Average

instructions:
To prepare the walnut and chocolate cake, first crack the eggs into a bowl, add the sugar , and beat with a whisk until the mixture is light and frothy ; this will take about 10 minutes.
At this point, add the vegetable oil , vanilla extract , and milk .
Mix well, then sift in the flour , cornstarch , and baking powder .
Add the coarsely chopped walnuts and chocolate chips , then mix everything well. Transfer the mixture into a 9-inch mold lined with parchment paper .
Level the surface and bake in a preheated static oven at 340°F for about 60 minutes . Check the baking with the toothpick test, then remove from the oven and let cool before dusting with powdered sugar. Your walnut and chocolate cake is ready to be enjoyed !
The walnut and chocolate cake can be stored at room temperature for 3-4 days, covered with a cake dome.
You can freeze it in slices, after baking and cooling it.
If desired, you can further flavor the batter with some grated orange zest or a pinch of ground cinnamon!
