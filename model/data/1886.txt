name:
Butter and sardine toast
ingredients:
Sardines in oil: 3.5oz(100 g)- spicy drained
Homemade bread: 4slices
Salted butter: 3oz(80 g)
Peach: 1
Parsley: to taste

info:
Difficulty: Easy
Prep time: 5 min
Cook time: 10 min
Serving: 4

instructions:
To prepare the butter and sardine toast, first chop the parsley , melt the butter in a small bowl , just enough to create a creamy texture, and add the chopped parsley .
and mix well . Set aside the butter sauce and work with the peach: wash it, divide it in half, remove the pit, then slice it and cut the slices into cubes . Collect them in a bowl.
Slice the rustic bread , heat a grill and toast the bread until it is golden and crispy. Everything is ready to assemble the toast: take a slice of toasted bread, spread the butter cream on top
Place a spoonful of peach cubes on top and finish with the sardine fillets . The butter and sardine toasts are ready to be enjoyed !
Consume the butter and sardine toasts immediately. Freezing is not recommended.
These summer toasts are so appetizing that you'll want to prepare them even out of season: simply replace the peaches with diced apples to enjoy them all year round!
