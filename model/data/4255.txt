name:
Zucchini and feta fritters with yogurt sauce
ingredients:
Zucchini: 1.1lbs(500 g)
Feta cheese: 7oz(200 g)
Eggs: 2
Onions: ½
Garlic: 1clove
Type 00 flour: ½cup(60 g)
Mint: 10leaves
Parsley: to taste
Fine salt: 1pinch
Black pepper: 1pinch
Peanut seed oil: 8 ½cups(2 l)
Greek yogurt: ¾cup(200 g)
Black olives: 0.3cup(50 g)- pitted
Lemon juice: 1spoonful
Mint: 1spoonful
Chives: 1spoonful
Wildflower honey: 1tsp
Extra virgin olive oil: 1spoonful
Orange peel: to taste

info:
Difficulty: Easy
Prep time: 30 min
Cook time: 10 min
Makes: 16 pieces
Cost: Low
Note + 15 min. of zucchini resting time

instructions:
The zucchini and feta fritters with yogurt sauce are a summer dish inspired by the traditional kolokythokeftedes , the zucchini fritters typical of Greek cuisine, enriched with a fresh and tantalizing sauce made from Greek yogurt .
This dish combines the delicacy of zucchini and the savory taste of feta with the aromatic touch of mint and parsley, creating a perfect balance of Mediterranean flavors. The sauce, prepared with Greek yogurt, black olives, a drizzle of honey for a slight sweet and sour note, and enriched with mint and chives, adds an irresistible aroma that enhances every bite.
Perfect for your summer menus, the zucchini and feta fritters with yogurt sauce combine taste and authenticity, bringing the freshness of Greek cuisine to the table, ideal for those looking for a fried treat full of authentic flavors!

To prepare the zucchini and feta fritters with yogurt sauce, start with the batter: wash and trim the zucchini, then grate them with a large-holed grater . Place the zucchini on a dish towel and salt them , then let them rest for 15 minutes: this way, they will release some of their water . Finally, squeeze them directly inside the dish towel.
Gather the grated zucchini in a bowl, add the crumbled feta , the chopped onion , and also the chopped garlic .
Flavor with chopped mint and parsley , then incorporate the eggs , salt, and pepper .
Mix and add all-purpose flour to adjust the consistency and obtain a homogeneous batter .
Meanwhile, prepare the sauce: in a bowl, pour the Greek yogurt and chopped pitted black olives . Scent with chopped mint and chives , then add the honey and lemon juice.
Finish with olive oil , salt , and pepper, then mix to combine . Finally, flavor with grated orange zest.
The sauce is ready, set it aside . Now heat the seed oil in a high-sided pan until it reaches about 340°F. With two spoons, scoop out small quenelles of batter and immerse them in the hot oil .
Fry for a couple of minutes or until golden brown , then drain the fritters on a tray lined with paper towels . Serve your zucchini and feta fritters with yogurt sauce immediately !
We recommend serving the zucchini and feta fritters with yogurt sauce as soon as they're ready!
To achieve perfect fritters, we recommend not skipping the resting time for the zucchini: this step is crucial to remove excess water, resulting in a more compact and crispy texture when fried.
