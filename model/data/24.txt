name:
Anchovies in scapece
ingredients:
Anchovies: 14.1oz(400 g)- (already gutted)
Type 00 flour: to taste
Fine salt: to taste
Peanut seed oil: to taste
Garlic: 2cloves
Mint: to taste
White wine vinegar: 13tbsp(200 ml)
Water: ½cup(100 ml)
Extra virgin olive oil: 3.4tbsp(50 ml)

info:
Difficulty: Easy
Prep time: 10 min
Cook time: 10 min
Serving: 4
Cost: Low
Note + the marinating time (about 12 h)

instructions:
To prepare the anchovies in scapece , first rinse the cleaned anchovies, drain them and pat them dry with paper towels . Then, flour them .
Remove the excess flour and set aside . Chop the garlic cloves and fresh mint . In a small pot, pour the aromatic mixture .
Add the vinegar , water , and extra virgin olive oil .
Heat the marinade on the stove without boiling for 10 minutes on low heat . Then proceed with frying: heat the peanut oil in a pan to 338°F and fry a few anchovies at a time for about 2 minutes .
Drain the anchovies on frying paper or paper towels , then lightly salt them .
Pour the marinade over the anchovies . Cover the anchovies in scapece with plastic wrap and place in the fridge overnight before serving them .
The anchovies in scapece can be stored in the fridge for 3-4 days in an airtight jar, covered with the marinade.
We do not recommend freezing.
To flavor the marinade, you can use oregano or parsley instead of mint.
If you prefer to buy whole anchovies, you will need about 1.3 lbs: you can then clean them following our guide: How to Clean Anchovies .
