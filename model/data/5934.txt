name:
Fluffy pancake
ingredients:
Oat flour: 5tbsp(25 g)
Egg whites: 2(70 g)- (room temperature)
Greek yogurt: 2tbsp(30 g)
Baking powder: 1tsp(4 g)
Agave syrup: 2tsp(15 g)
Sunflower seed oil: to taste
Agave syrup: to taste
Berries: to taste
Ground pistachio: to taste

info:
Difficulty: Easy
Prep time: 10 min
Cook time: 20 min
Makes: 1 pieces
Cost: Low

instructions:
To prepare fluffy pancakes , separate the yolks from the whites and place the latter in a bowl . Beat the whites with a whisk until frothy. Add the yogurt and beat some more with the whisk.
Add the oat flour , the agave syrup and the baking powder .
Stir just enough to obtain a homogeneous mixture . Grease a deep pan roughly 5" in diameter with seed (or coconut) oil and heat on the stove. Add the batter .

Cover with a lid and cook for around 10 minutes on a low flame, then delicately turn it over using a spatula or a fork, and finish cooking on the other side for another 10 minutes.Â  Place the fluffy pancake on a serving dish .

Garnish with wild berries , agave syrup and ground pistachios . Serve immediately .

Fluffy pancake is best enjoyed freshly made. If any is leftover, store in the refrigerator for 1-2 days a most.
Thoroughly crease the pan so that the batter does not stick to the sides. Oat flour can be replaced with rice flour, and greek yogurt with plant-based yogurt. The leftover yolks can be used to make an omelette, or to prepare a pastry cream.
