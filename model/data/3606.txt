name:
Broad bean pesto (Marò)
ingredients:
Fava beans: 1cup(150 g)- fresh
Pecorino cheese: 2.6oz(75 g)
Extra virgin olive oil: 4 ¾tbsp(70 ml)
Garlic: 1clove
Mint: 4leaves

info:
Difficulty: Easy
Prep time: 20 min
Serving: 4
Cost: Low

instructions:

To prepare the broad bean pesto, start by How to Clean Fava Beans them, then shell them and remove the tougher outer skin (1-2); make sure to remove the thin skin that covers them as well .
Place the broad beans in the mixer and add the whole peeled garlic clove , the grated cheese , and the mint leaves
and the oil in a stream . Turn on the mixer and blend everything intermittently, pausing at regular intervals after a few seconds (to avoid overheating the blades too much), until you get a creamy and homogeneous mixture ; transfer the obtained broad bean pesto into a small bowl and if you are not using it immediately, you can cover it with plastic wrap or extra virgin olive oil. Use it to dress delicious first courses!
Store the broad bean pesto in the refrigerator covered with plastic wrap or in a hermetically sealed jar for 2-3 days. You can freeze the pesto in small jars and thaw it when needed.
For perfect broad bean pesto, put the mixer blades in the refrigerator at least an hour before preparation to avoid overheating the sauce too much. Then proceed by blending the ingredients intermittently, meaning blend for a few seconds, then stop, blend again, and so on until you achieve the right consistency!
