name:
Penne with White Rabbit Ragù
ingredients:
Penne Rigate pasta: 11oz(320 g)
Rabbit: 1.75lbs(800 g)- ground
Carrots: 0.8cup(100 g)
Celery: 1.8oz(50 g)
White onions: 0.7cup(100 g)
White wine: 0.42cup(100 g)
Thyme: to taste
Garlic: 1clove
Bay leaves: 3leaves
Rosemary: 1sprig
Extra virgin olive oil: 2tbsp(30 g)
Fine salt: to taste
Black pepper: to taste
Carrots: 0.9cup(100 g)
Celery: 3oz(80 g)
White onions: 3.5oz(100 g)

info:
Difficulty: Easy
Prep time: 30 min
Cook time: 2 h
Serving: 4
Cost: Low

instructions:
To prepare penne with white rabbit ragù, first start with the Vegetable broth . Then move on to the How to make the soffritto : peel the celery , the carrot ,
cut the celery into strips, chop it , and repeat the same process for the carrot (5-6).
Also chop the onion and the rosemary , then in a pan with the oil, sauté the onion .
Then add the celery , the carrot , the garlic ,
the bay leaves and the rosemary, then add the ground meat , letting it cook for at least 10 minutes on low heat .
Then deglaze with the white wine , season with salt and pepper ,
pour in two ladles of hot vegetable broth you prepared , then cover with a lid and let it cook on very low heat for at least 2 hours. Once the time has passed, remove the bay leaves ,
remove the garlic and cook the pasta in plenty of salted water . Once ready, add it directly to the pan with the sauce ,
add the pepper , the chopped thyme , mix everything to flavor, and your penne with white rabbit ragù are ready to be enjoyed !
You can store penne with white rabbit ragù in the refrigerator in an airtight container for up to 1 day. Freezing is not recommended.
The ragù can be frozen if all fresh ingredients, not previously frozen, were used.
To add extra flavor to your penne with white rabbit ragù, you can add fresh or powdered chili pepper, and if you want to make a red version, you can use tomato puree.
