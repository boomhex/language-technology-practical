name:
Ricotta cream
ingredients:
Sheep's milk ricotta cheese: 1lb(400 g)
Sugar: Â½cup(100 g)
Vanilla extract: 8drops

info:
Difficulty: Very easy
Prep time: 10 min
Serving: 4
Cost: Very low
Note + 1 hour to drain the ricotta

instructions:
To make the ricotta cream, place the cheese in a sieve over a bowl. Let it drain for 1 hour . After the indicated time, discard the whey collected in the bowl and sieve the ricotta by pressing it with the back of a spoon to make it fluffier and eliminate any lumps . Add the sugar
and the vanilla extract , stir to mix the ingredients well . The ricotta cream is ready for your delicious fillings .
The ricotta cream can be stored in the refrigerator for a couple of days. We do not recommend freezing it.
If you want a cream with a more velvety texture, you can replace the granulated sugar with powdered sugar.
Sheep ricotta has a strong flavor, for a milder taste you can use cow's milk ricotta.
