name:
Steamed Sea Bass with Marinated Zucchini
ingredients:
Sea bass (sea bass): 2.6lbs(1.2 kg)- (4 of about 0.7 lbs each)
Lemon juice: 2oz(60 g)
Lemon peel: 1
Parsley: to taste
Fine salt: to taste
Black pepper: to taste
Extra virgin olive oil: to taste
Zucchini: 2.6lbs(1.2 kg)
Garlic: 1clove
White wine vinegar: 6 ¾tbsp(100 g)
Water: ¼cup(50 g)
Mint: to taste
Extra virgin olive oil: 0.7tbsp(10 g)
Marjoram: to taste
Lemon peel: to taste

info:
Difficulty: Average
Prep time: 20 min
Cook time: 10 min
Serving: 4
Cost: Average
Note + the marinating time for fish and vegetables (45 min. total)

instructions:
To prepare the steamed sea bass with marinated zucchini, first, focus on the How to clean the seabass : make an incision along the belly, reaching below the head, remove the innards, and rinse well under running water to remove all impurities. Trim the ventral fins . Scale with the appropriate tool, or with the back of a knife, under running water . Make an oblique cut just after the gills, starting from the back and connecting to the cut made on the belly .
Score the tail and the dorsal part of the fish following the line of the spine . This way you will have obtained the fillet . Remove the bones with kitchen tweezers .
Cut each fillet in half . In a bowl, marinate the fillets with the zest and juice of one lemon .
Flavor with chopped parsley , salt, pepper, and a drizzle of olive oil. Mix and let it marinate for about 15 minutes in the refrigerator covered with plastic wrap.
Meanwhile, wash the zucchini and cut them into slices about 1/4 inch thick . Grill the zucchini on both sides for a couple of minutes or until the typical grill marks appear . Transfer them to a bowl and pour the water and white wine vinegar over them .
Flavor with a sliced clove of garlic , salt, pepper, chopped mint , and a drizzle of extra virgin olive oil. Let the zucchini rest in the marinade for at least 30 minutes in the refrigerator covered with plastic wrap. Now prepare a pot for steaming: add water and salt, then bring to a boil. Place the sea bass fillets in the steamer basket .
Cover with a lid and cook for about 5-7 minutes, until they are tender and easily flake apart . Arrange the marinated zucchini on a serving plate .
Place the steamed sea bass fillets on top and finish the dish with some of the zucchini marinade, lemon zest , and marjoram leaves to taste. Your steamed sea bass with marinated zucchini is ready to be served !
We recommend consuming the steamed sea bass immediately.
You can store the marinated zucchini for 1-2 days in the refrigerator covered with plastic wrap.
To make your steamed sea bass even more fragrant, you can flavor the water in the steamer with some fresh herbs like parsley, thyme, marjoram, or a bay leaf.
