name:
Classic puff pastries with figs, parmesan, and honey
ingredients:
Figs: 0.8cup(120 g)
Grana Padano PDO cheese: 0.9oz(25 g)
Honey: - wildflower honey as needed
Crispy baked puff pastry: 12

info:
Difficulty: Easy
Prep time: 10 min
Makes: 12 pieces
Cost: Low

instructions:
Sfoglie classiche con fichi, grana e miele is one of those finger foods that just totally steals the show at gatherings, especially in Northern Italy . Seriously good stuff. Plus, they’re not just nice to look at—they're moist inside thanks to the fruit and honey, while the outside stays crispy and light. Pretty cool, right? Perfect for anyone who enjoys a fig and cheese puff pastry with a little twist.

Whether you call it a fig and cheese tartlet or just a fun little pastry, people end up reaching for seconds. To prepare the classic puff pastries with figs, parmesan, and honey, start by washing the figs well and cutting them into rather thin wedges . With the help of a peeler, make parmesan cheese flakes and place a couple on each pastry .
Then place two fig wedges on each pastry and garnish with a teaspoon of wildflower honey . The classic puff pastries with figs, parmesan, and honey are ready : serve and enjoy them quickly, to not lose their freshness!
It's preferable to assemble the classic puff pastries with figs at the moment.
If you want, you can replace the parmesan cheese with other strong-flavored cheeses, such as brie or pecorino.
