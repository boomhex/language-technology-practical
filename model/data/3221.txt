name:
Capocollo cone stuffed on classic sheets
ingredients:

Mint: 4leaves
Crispy baked puff pastry: 12
Dill: 2sprigs

Fine salt: to taste

Mint: 12leaves
Dill: 12tufts

Prep time: 20 min
Makes: 12 pieces

To prepare the capocollo cone stuffed on classic sheets, take the slices of capocollo (or coppa), fold them in half and cut them into two equal parts , then form cones with each piece by wrapping the capocollo around itself .
Separately, place the goat cheese in a bowl and finely chop the mint and dill with a knife , then add the aromatic herbs to the cheese (5-6). Mix well to blend all the ingredients, then salt and pepper to taste .
Transfer the mixture into a piping bag with a star tip, then fill each cone with the goat cheese herb cream . Place a cone on each sheet and garnish with a mint leaf and dill as shown in the photo . The capocollo cone stuffed on classic sheets is ready , we recommend serving and enjoying it immediately!
We recommend consuming the capocollo cone stuffed on classic sheets immediately, but if you wish, you can store it in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to one day.
If you like, you can replace the goat cheese with another cheese, as long as it has the same consistency (for example, Philadelphia), and you can flavor it with chives or parsley.
