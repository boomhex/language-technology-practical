name:
Veal escalopes pizzaiola style
ingredients:
Sliced veal: 1.8lbs(800 g)
Tomato purée: 2.1cups(500 g)
Mozzarella cheese: 2 ½cups(300 g)
Butter: 7tbsp(100 g)
White wine: 1.7oz(50 g)
Type 00 flour: to taste
Basil: to taste
Oregano: to taste- dry
Fine salt: to taste
Black pepper: to taste

info:
Difficulty: Easy
Prep time: 20 min
Cook time: 20 min
Serving: 4
Cost: Low

instructions:
To prepare veal escalopes pizzaiola style, first pound the meat to make the slices more tender and thin . Season with salt and pepper , then lightly flour them .
In a hot pan, melt the butter , then add the slices and brown over medium heat for about 2-3 minutes per side .
As they cook, transfer them to a baking dish or a plate and cover with a sheet of aluminum foil . Meanwhile, deglaze the pan with white wine and let all the alcohol evaporate.
Pour the tomato puree into the pan , add the basil and cover with a lid . Let it cook gently for about 10 minutes.
Meanwhile, slice the mozzarella and pat it dry well with paper towels . Once the sauce is ready, adjust the salt .
Place the escalopes back into the pan , cover them with the sauce and distribute the mozzarella slices over the meat .
Add the dried oregano , close with the lid and continue cooking for 2 minutes, just enough time to melt the mozzarella .
Plate and garnish with a few basil leaves . Your veal escalopes pizzaiola style are ready to be enjoyed !
It is recommended to consume veal escalopes pizzaiola style as soon as they are ready; if necessary, you can store them in the refrigerator in a closed container for 2 days. When serving, simply heat them gently in a pan with a splash of water or a drizzle of oil.
You can choose the meat you prefer: veal, pork, chicken, or turkey, based on your tastes or what you have at home.
For an extra flavor boost, you can enrich the sauce with Taggiasca olives or a teaspoon of desalted capers.
