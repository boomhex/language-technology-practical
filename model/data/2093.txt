name:
Beetroot Pesto
ingredients:
Precooked beets: 0.9lb(200 g)
Pine nuts: 2 ½tbsp(25 g)
Parmigiano Reggiano PDO cheese: 0.9oz(25 g)- to grate
Extra virgin olive oil: 5tbsp(65 g)
Lemon juice: - halfway
Basil: 5leaves
Fine salt: to taste

info:
Difficulty: Easy
Prep time: 20 min
Cook time: 2 min
Serving: 4
Cost: Very low

To prepare the beetroot pesto, start by toasting the pine nuts. So, pour them into a pan and continuously toss them over high heat, being careful not to burn them ; once golden, set them aside. Meanwhile, cut the beetroots into chunks ,
and also add the beetroot to the blender together with the pine nuts . Also add the grated Parmesan cheese ,
the basil leaves , a pinch of salt , and the juice of half a lemon .
Close the food processor and activate the blades, pouring the oil gradually , blend until you obtain a smooth and homogeneous cream . Transfer into a bowl, and here's your beetroot pesto , ready to be used as a pasta dressing or served on fragrant crostini!
You can store the beetroot pesto in the fridge for a couple of days at most, keep it in a resealable jar. If you prefer, you can also freeze it.
