name:
Orange and Chocolate Cupcake
ingredients:
Eggs: 3- medium
Type 00 flour: 2 ¼cups(270 g)
Sugar: 1cup(200 g)
Corn oil: ½cup(100 ml)
Unsweetened cocoa powder: 8tbsp(40 g)
Orange juice: 0.6cup(150 ml)- (from about 2 medium oranges)
Baking powder: 2 ½tsp(10 g)
Fine salt: 1pinch
Dark chocolate chips: 2.5oz(70 g)
Cream cheese: 7.1oz(200 g)
Fresh liquid cream: 1 ¼cup(300 ml)
Unsweetened cocoa powder: 3tbsp(30 g)
Powdered sugar: 1.67cups(200 g)
Orange flavoring: to taste
Orange peel: 1

info:
Difficulty: Easy
Prep time: 20 min
Cook time: 25 min
Makes: 12 pieces
Cost: Low

instructions:
To prepare the orange and chocolate cupcakes, crack the eggs into a bowl and add a pinch of salt , add the oil and whisk everything together by hand .
Add the sugar and mix everything again. Squeeze the oranges and add the juice to the egg mixture , mix again. Then add the flour ,
the unsweetened cocoa powder and the baking powder , mix well until everything is combined. Finally, add the chocolate chips .
Line a muffin tin with paper liners. Fill the various molds with the batter , reaching a little over halfway . Bake at 350°F for 25 minutes. Once cooked, remove from the oven and let cool .
Start on the frosting: pour the fresh cream into a bowl, add 150 g of powdered sugar from the total amount and beat with an electric mixer . Pour the cheese into another bowl, add the cocoa ,
and the remaining 50 g of powdered sugar . Add the orange flavoring and mix with a spatula until everything is combined .
Add the cheese mixture to the whipped cream and combine the 2 mixtures with an electric mixer at very low speed until smooth and homogeneous .
Fill a pastry bag with a star-shaped nozzle with the frosting and decorate the now cold cupcakes . Finish with grated orange zest . The orange and chocolate cupcakes are ready to be enjoyed .
Store the orange and chocolate cupcakes for 2-3 days in the fridge in an airtight container. They can be frozen without frosting once baked and cooled.
You can also decorate your cupcakes with chocolate decorations or candied orange peels!
