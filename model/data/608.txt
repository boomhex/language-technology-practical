name:
Cold Farro Salad
ingredients:
Spelt: 1 ¼cup(200 g)
Red peppers: 0.9lb(390 g)
Precooked chickpeas: 0.625cup(150 g)
Taggiasca olives: 1oz(28 g)
Greek yogurt: ¾cup(170 g)
Basil: 10leaves
Extra virgin olive oil: 3spoonfuls
Fine salt: to taste

info:
Difficulty: Very easy
Prep time: 20 min
Cook time: 10 min
Serving: 4
Cost: Low

instructions:
To prepare the cold farro salad, bring a pot of lightly salted water to a boil and cook the farro for the time indicated on the package. Wash and chop the basil leaves , pour the yogurt into a small bowl, add a pinch of salt and a tablespoon of oil .
Flavor with the torn basil leaves . Mix well, cover the bowl with plastic wrap , and place it in the refrigerator. Meanwhile, wash the pepper, cut it in half, and remove the stem, filaments, and seeds .
Cut it into cubes and place it in a bowl. Halve the olives and add them to the peppers. Drain the chickpeas and add them to the peppers.
Season everything with a pinch of salt and 2 tablespoons of oil and mix. Drain the farro , rinse it under running water to stop the cooking.
Add the farro to the vegetables , mix well. Cover the bowl with plastic wrap and then chill in the refrigerator for at least an hour. Serve the cold farro salad in individual bowls, accompanied by a spoonful of yogurt and basil sauce .
Cold farro can be stored in the refrigerator, covered, for 2-3 days.
You can replace the pepper with an equal weight of diced tomatoes and a sliced celery stalk. Instead of chickpeas, you can use tuna
