name:
Green tea salmon bites with vegetables
ingredients:
Salmon fillets: 1.8lbs(800 g)- Norwegian
Green Tea: 1 ¼tsp(6 g)- in powder or dried leaves
Baked croissant peppers: 3.5oz(100 g)
Carrots: 1 ½cup(200 g)
Celery: 0.66lb(300 g)
Extra virgin olive oil: 2tbsp(30 g)
Fine salt: 1pinch

info:
Difficulty: Easy
Prep time: 20 min
Cook time: 3 min
Serving: 4

instructions:

To prepare the green tea salmon bites with vegetables, start with the vegetables: wash them, trim and peel the carrot , then remove the stem from the sweet peppers and cut them in half , then remove the seeds inside using a small knife .
Julienne the peppers , carrots , and the celery stalk . Set the vegetables aside,
move on to the salmon: remove the skin from the salmon fillet if present, then cut it in half , then reduce it to cubes . In a steamer, pour the water and add the green tea
bring to a boil and insert the steamer basket , then add the salmon cubes and cover with the lid ; cook the salmon for 2-3 minutes.
Indeed, just a few minutes will be enough for it to be cooked and pink . Serve it on serving plates accompanied by the sliced vegetables, seasoned with pepper to taste , and your green tea salmon bites with vegetables will be ready to be enjoyed .
Store your green tea salmon bites with vegetables for no more than 1 day in the refrigerator, closed in an airtight container!
If you like, you can accompany the salmon with other seasonal vegetables: fennel, artichokes, or cucumbers and dress them with a balsamic vinegar vinaigrette. You can also cook the whole salmon; in this case, the cooking times will be extended by a few minutes!
