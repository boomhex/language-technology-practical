name:
Mushrooms and Radicchio in a Pan
ingredients:
Champignon mushrooms: 7 ¼cups(500 g)
Radicchio: 10.6oz(300 g)- (1 medium)
Extra virgin olive oil: 3spoonfuls
Garlic: 1clove
Fine salt: to taste
Black pepper: to taste
Red wine: ½cup(100 ml)

info:
Difficulty: Easy
Prep time: 5 min
Cook time: 10 min
Serving: 4
Cost: Low

instructions:
To prepare mushrooms and radicchio in a pan, first separate the radicchio leaves , rinse them under running water, and then cut them into small pieces .
Now clean the champignon mushrooms: cut the base and wipe off any soil residues with a cloth . Slice the mushrooms and set them aside . Pour the extra virgin olive oil into a pan, add the garlic clove , and let it brown.
Then remove the garlic and add the mushrooms , let them sauté for a couple of minutes. At this point, add the radicchio , season with salt and black pepper
and deglaze with red wine . Stir the mushrooms and radicchio in the pan and cover with a lid , let cook for two minutes. Mushrooms and radicchio in a pan are ready to be served .
Store the mushrooms and radicchio in the refrigerator for 2-3 days. It's possible to freeze the preparation after cooking.
Garnish the dish with fresh chopped parsley; it will add a note of flavor and color to the dish!
