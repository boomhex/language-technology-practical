name:
Vanilla muffins with sour cherry filling
ingredients:
Type 00 flour: 1 ½cup(190 g)
Butter: 0.4cup(90 g)- softened
Whole milk: ½cup(125 ml)
Sugar: ½cup(100 g)
Eggs: 1
Egg yolks: 1
Baking soda: 1pinch
Baking powder: 1 ¾tsp(8 g)
Black cherried in syrup: 8
Vanilla bean: ½
Fine salt: 1pinch

info:
Difficulty: Easy
Prep time: 20 min
Makes: 8 pieces
Cost: Low

To make the vanilla muffins with sour cherry heart: in a bowl, place the softened butter and beat it with an electric beater (or a stand mixer) together with the sugar (1-2), add the room temperature egg , continuing to beat,
then pour in the room temperature milk and continue mixing the ingredients until you get a smooth mixture . Separately, sift the flour with the baking powder, salt, and a pinch of baking soda
and add the dry ingredients to the mixture . At this point, flavor with the seeds from half a vanilla pod . The muffin batter is ready, distribute the batter into 8 silicone molds suitable for microwave cooking .
Insert a sour cherry into each muffin , place the muffins on a microwave tray and bake the muffins in the Samsung Smart Oven microwave by selecting the muffin cooking program 2-13 with a maximum weight of 400 gr which will automatically choose the cooking time and temperature based on the weight. Once cooked, remove the muffins from the oven and let them cool slightly . Your sour cherry and vanilla muffins are ready to be enjoyed!
You can store the vanilla muffins with sour cherry heart under a glass dome or in a tin box for 4-5 days. It's possible to freeze the muffins and thaw them at room temperature when needed.
If you don't like sour cherries, you can fill the muffins with dark or white chocolate.
