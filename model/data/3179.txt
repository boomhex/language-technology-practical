name:
Coconut Milk Cream
ingredients:
Coconut milk: 1.67cups(400 ml)
Sugar: 4tbsp(50 g)
Rice flour: 2tbsp(20 g)

info:
Difficulty: Very easy
Prep time: 5 min
Cook time: 10 min
Serving: 4
Cost: Low

instructions:
To prepare the coconut milk cream, start by pouring the sugar and the rice flour into a saucepan. Then gradually add the coconut milk, stirring continuously with a whisk .
Place on the heat and continue stirring until reaching the desired thickness, then remove from the heat and keep stirring (5-6).
Pour the mixture into a bowl . Cover with cling film touching the surface and let it cool before enjoying your coconut cream .
The coconut milk cream can be stored in the refrigerator for up to 3-4 days in an airtight container. Freezing is not recommended.
If lumps form during cooking, you can sift the cream through a sieve once cooked! It will become smooth and velvety again.
If you prefer, you can flavor the cream with vanilla or lime zest!
