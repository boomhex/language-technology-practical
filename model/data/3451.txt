name:
Pork chops with beer and aromatic herbs
ingredients:
Pork chop: 4
Red beer: 0.42cup(100 ml)
Extra virgin olive oil: 3tbsp(40 g)
Butter: 3tbsp(40 g)
Garlic: ½
Rosemary: 1sprig
Thyme: 2sprigs
Lemon peel: ½
Fine salt: to taste

info:
Difficulty: Very easy
Prep time: 15 min
Cook time: 10 min
Serving: 4
Cost: Average

To prepare pork chops with beer and aromatic herbs, start by cleaning and chopping the garlic, aromatic herbs, and lemon peel . Place the 4 chops on a cutting board and season them with salt and pepper on each side. Heat the oil in a pan and cook the meat for 5-6 minutes on each side .
Set the chops aside, and in the same pan add the butter , the mixture of aromatic herbs , and the beer . Let it thicken for a minute, stirring well, then turn off the heat. Place the chops on plates and drizzle each with the beer sauce. Serve them while still steaming.
