name:
Sardinian gnocchetti with sausage and asparagus
ingredients:
Gnocchetti Sardi pasta: 11.3oz(320 g)
Sausage: 9oz(250 g)
Asparagus: 5.3oz(150 g)- to clean
Fresh liquid cream: ½cup(120 g)
Shallot: 1
Mint: to taste
Extra virgin olive oil: to taste
Fine salt: to taste
Black pepper: to taste

info:
Difficulty: Easy
Prep time: 10 min
Cook time: 20 min
Serving: 4
Cost: Average

instructions:
To prepare the Sardinian gnocchetti with asparagus and sausage, first, peel the shallot and slice it thinly . Cut the sausage into small pieces . Now wash the asparagus and remove the woody part of the stem .
Cut the stem into rings and the tips in half . Heat the oil in a pan, add the sliced shallot , and sauté over moderate heat.
Add the sausage and brown it for a moment , then also add the asparagus . Let it cook for about 5 minutes and in the meantime, put a pot filled with salted water on the stove that will be used to cook the gnocchetti. When the water boils, cook the pasta al dente .
Thin the sauce with a ladle of cooking water . Continue to cook until it is dry, then salt and add the cream . Drain the gnocchetti into the sauce and toss them until a creamy consistency is reached.
Add pepper and fresh mint and serve the Sardinian gnocchetti with sausage and asparagus hot .
We recommend consuming the Sardinian gnocchetti with sausage and asparagus immediately; if there are leftovers, store them in the refrigerator in an airtight container for a couple of days.
The addition of lemon zest will make the dish even fresher and more aromatic!
