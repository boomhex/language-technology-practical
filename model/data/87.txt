name:
Baked Anchovies
ingredients:
Anchovies: 350- (to clean)
Breadcrumbs: 1.1cups(130 g)
Corn flour: ⅓cup(50 g)
Parsley: to taste
Extra virgin olive oil: to taste
Salt: to taste
Black pepper: to taste
Arugula: to taste
Lemons: to taste

To prepare the baked anchovies, first focus on the How to Clean Anchovies : remove the head and guts, then open them like a book and remove the spine . Rinse under running water and set aside. Now prepare the breading: finely chop the parsley . In a bowl, combine the breadcrumbs and about three-quarters of the chopped parsley .
Add the cornmeal , season with salt and pepper , then drizzle with a bit of olive oil . Mix to combine all the ingredients.
Spread half of the breading on the bottom of a baking dish and level it out . Neatly arrange the anchovies opened like a book and cover with the remaining breading .
Sprinkle the remaining parsley on the surface and a dusting of Parmesan , then drizzle with more olive oil .
Bake in a static oven at 392°F for 20 minutes . Remove from the oven and plate the anchovies on a bed of arugula, finishing with a few slices of lemon . Your baked anchovies are ready to be served !
It is recommended to consume the baked anchovies immediately. If necessary, you can store them in the refrigerator, in a closed container, for up to one day.
If you wish, you can flavor the breading with some grated lemon zest!
