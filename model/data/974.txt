name:
Rice and Chickpea Soup
ingredients:
Originario rice: 1cup(200 g)
Precooked chickpeas: 1 ½cup(350 g)
Carrots: 2.8oz(80 g)
White onions: ¾cup(120 g)
Garlic: 1clove
Vegetable broth: to taste
Curry: 1tsp
Rosemary: 1sprig
Black pepper: to taste
Fine salt: to taste
Extra virgin olive oil: to taste
Champignon mushrooms: 3 ½cups(250 g)

instructions:

To make the rice and chickpea soup, first, take care of cleaning the mushrooms: cut the base of the stem and remove the soil residues with a damp cloth. Slice the mushrooms and set them aside . Clean and chop the onion and carrots . Heat a dash of oil in the pan .
Sauté the carrot, onion, and garlic over medium-low heat . Add the mushrooms and cook for a couple of minutes. Add the chickpeas and mix.
Cover with the hot broth . Once it reaches boiling, add the rice , stir and season with salt, pepper, and curry .
Fragrance with chopped rosemary . Cook the rice, adding broth if necessary, stirring often . Serve the rice and chickpea soup hot .
The rice and chickpea soup can be stored in the refrigerator for up to one day. Freezing is not recommended.
If you use dried chickpeas, halve the indicated dosage, soak them for 12 hours in cold water, and then cook them for about 2 hours; they should be cooked but firm.
