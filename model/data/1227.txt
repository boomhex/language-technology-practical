name:
Cod with Orange
ingredients:
Desalted cod: 1.75lbs(800 g)
Orange juice: 1.1cups(250 g)
Orange peel: 2
Extra virgin olive oil: 2tbsp(30 g)
Fresh liquid cream: ½cup(120 g)
Garlic: 1clove
Type 0 flour: ⅓cup(40 g)
Rosemary: 1sprig
Raisins: 1.5oz(40 g)
Fine salt: to taste

info:
Difficulty: Easy
Prep time: 10 min
Cook time: 15 min
Serving: 4
Cost: Average

instructions:
To prepare the cod with orange, first squeeze the orange juice and grate the zest . Take the desalted cod and cut it into four parts .
Remove any bones with a kitchen tweezer . At this point, flour the cod on all sides . In a pan, heat the oil with a clove of garlic .
Place the pieces of cod and brown them for 3 minutes over medium heat, then turn them over and cook for another 3 minutes. Deglaze with the orange juice and flavor with the grated zest .
Season with a sprig of rosemary , salt, and add the raisins . Continue cooking to reduce the sauce over medium heat for about 3 minutes, then pour in the cream .
Cover with the lid and continue to cook for another 4-5 minutes over low heat . Once cooked, the sauce will have reduced and the fish will be cooked through . Serve the cod with orange hot .
The cod with orange can be stored in the fridge in an airtight container for one day. To reheat, simply add a few tablespoons of milk and warm it for a few minutes in a saucepan.
Add little salt because the cod is still quite salty.
