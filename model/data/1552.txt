name:
Onions with Raisins
ingredients:
Borettane onions: 1.75lbs(800 g)- to clean
Raisins: 6tbsp(50 g)
Apple cider vinegar: ¼cup(60 g)
Marsala wine: 1 ½tbsp(25 g)
Butter: 2 ¾tbsp(40 g)
Fine salt: 4pinches
Black pepper: to taste
Water: 0.42cup(100 g)

info:
Difficulty: Easy
Prep time: 10 min
Cook time: 20 min
Serving: 4
Cost: Low
Note + 20 minutes of soaking the raisins

instructions:
To prepare the recipe for onions with raisins, first soak the raisins for 20 minutes in a glass of lukewarm water . Meanwhile, peel the onions and gather them in a bowl .
Place a pot full of salted water on the stove and bring it to a boil , then add the onions and blanch them for 2 minutes . Drain them and set aside.
Now, add the butter to a pan and melt it over low heat. Then add the onions and sauté them gently for 3 minutes, stirring occasionally. Then deglaze with the vinegar and
pour in 100 ml of water . Squeeze the raisins well and add them to the onions .
At this point, salt and pepper, and continue cooking for 7 minutes with the pot covered . Then deglaze with the Marsala , let it evaporate, then cover the pan again and continue cooking for another 7 minutes. At the end, the onions will become tender, and the cooking liquid will have almost completely evaporated . Serve the onions with raisins hot.
The onions with raisins can be stored in the refrigerator covered with plastic wrap for up to 3 days. Freezing is not recommended.
Adding some pine nuts will give a pleasant crunch to the dish!
