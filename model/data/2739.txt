name:
Honey Roasted Shrimp
ingredients:
Shrimps: 36
Wildflower honey: ¼cup(100 g)
Fresh ginger: ½tsp(3 g)
Garlic: 1clove
Lime: 2
Peperoncino: 1
Fine salt: to taste
Black pepper: to taste
Sunflower seed oil: ½cup(100 ml)
Soy milk: 3 ½tbsp(50 ml)
Mint: 8leaves
Lime juice: 1tsp

info:
Difficulty: Easy
Prep time: 20 min
Cook time: 5 min
Makes: 12 pieces
Cost: Average

instructions:
To prepare the honey roasted shrimp, peel and grate the fresh ginger (1-2), crush a garlic clove ,
grate the zest of a lime and squeeze out the juice , then strain it through a sieve .
Pour the honey into a small saucepan and add the juice of a lime , the garlic, ginger, chopped chili pepper, lime zest, salt , and pepper. Heat and thicken by cooking over moderate heat for 5 minutes . Then let the sauce cool.
In the meantime, How to clean shrimp : remove the head and legs, remove the shell , and use a toothpick to extract the vein. Slice the other lime into very thin slices and then cut each into 4 wedges
assemble the skewers: skewer 3 shrimp on a wooden skewer alternating with a lime slice. Set aside and meanwhile prepare the Fake mayonnaise without eggs : in a tall and narrow bowl, pour the seed oil , the milk ,
add a teaspoon of lime juice and mint leaves . Salt, pepper, and blend with an immersion blender until smooth .
Now proceed with cooking, brush the skewers with the honey sauce and then cook on a hot grill on both sides for a couple of minutes . Serve the honey roasted skewers with the mint faux mayonnaise .
The honey roasted skewers are excellent when enjoyed hot. If there are leftovers, you can store them covered with plastic wrap in the refrigerator for one day.
Want a milder version? Replace the chili pepper with sweet paprika, it will give the sauce a more intense aroma.
