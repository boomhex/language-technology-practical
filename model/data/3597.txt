name:
Brazilian Quindim
ingredients:
Coconut flour: 1 ⅓cup(150 g)
Egg yolks: 6
Condensed milk: 1 ⅛cup(350 g)
Coconut milk: 3.3tbsp(50 ml)
Lime juice: 1.5oz(45 g)
Butter: to taste
Sugar: to taste

info:
Difficulty: Easy
Prep time: 25 min
Cook time: 40 min
Serving: 4
Cost: Low

instructions:
To make the Brazilian quindim, squeeze the lime juice , place the coconut flour in a bowl, add the condensed milk and mix with a spatula to combine the ingredients .
Separate the egg whites from the yolks and incorporate the yolks into the mix . Add the lime juice and the coconut milk. Mix the ingredients with a spatula to combine.
Then cover with plastic wrap and let it rest for 15 minutes. Meanwhile, butter a cupcake mold and sprinkle it with granulated sugar and place it inside a larger baking tray, this will be used for the bain-marie cooking method.
Take the mixture and lastly add the coconut milk , mix to combine the ingredients and pour the mixture into the mold using a ladle . Heat water in a saucepan
and pour it into the large tray containing the mold, the water should cover two-thirds of the molds. Bake in a preheated static oven at 350°F for 40 minutes (or 320°F fan oven for 30 minutes). Once cooked, take your quindim out of the oven and let them cool before unmolding, turning the mold over onto a tray. The Brazilian quindim are ready to be enjoyed .
Store the Brazilian quindim in the refrigerator for a maximum of a couple of days.
With the leftover egg whites, you can make delicious meringues or an egg white omelet!
