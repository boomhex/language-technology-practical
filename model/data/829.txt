name:
Fettuccine with Peppers
ingredients:
Egg fettuccine pasta: 14.1oz(400 g)
Red peppers: 2 â…“cups(350 g)
Yellow peppers: 0.8lb(350 g)
Onions: 3.5oz(100 g)
Fresh liquid cream: 1.1cups(250 g)
Parsley: 1tuft
Fine salt: to taste
Black pepper: to taste
Extra virgin olive oil: to taste

info:
Difficulty: Very easy
Prep time: 15 min
Cook time: 20 min
Serving: 4
Cost: Low

instructions:
To prepare fettuccine with peppers, first chop the onion , then clean the peppers: remove the cap , remove the core with the stem, seeds, and white ribs .
Then cut the peppers into sticks and chop the parsley . Heat a pan on the stove with a swirl of oil .
Add the onion and let it soften . When it is well softened, add the peppers and cook over medium heat for 10-15 minutes . Season with salt, pour in the cream , and finish cooking the sauce over low heat.
Put a pot with plenty of salted water on the stove where you will cook the fettuccine al dente . As soon as the fettuccine are almost cooked, drain them and stir directly in the pan with the vegetable sauce .
Finish with fresh parsley and a grind of pepper . Serve your fettuccine with peppers immediately !
We recommend consuming the fettuccine with peppers immediately.
As an alternative to parsley, you can use fresh basil or thyme.
