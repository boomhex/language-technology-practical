name:
Cherry Tomato Hummus
ingredients:
Cherry tomatoes: 2 ¼cups(350 g)
Precooked chickpeas: 2cups(470 g)
Tahini: 2tbsp(30 g)
Sesame seeds: 2 ½tsp(10 g)
Garlic: 2cloves- poached
Sugar: 1tsp(5 g)
Extra virgin olive oil: to taste
Oregano: to taste- dry
Salt: to taste
Black pepper: to taste

instructions:

To prepare cherry tomato hummus, first thoroughly wash the cherry tomatoes. In a skillet, pour a drizzle of oil and add the garlic cloves with their skins on . Season with salt and sugar .
Add dried oregano and cook for 5-6 minutes , then remove the garlic and set aside some cherry tomatoes for the final decoration.
In a blender, place the pre-cooked chickpeas , cherry tomatoes , and tahini .
Season with salt and pepper . Add the oil and sesame seeds .
Finally, add the dried oregano and blend until you obtain a smooth and homogeneous cream . Transfer the cherry tomato hummus into a small bowl .
Garnish with a drizzle of oil , sesame seeds, and some whole cherry tomatoes . The cherry tomato hummus is ready to be served !
Cherry tomato hummus can be stored in the refrigerator for 3-4 days, in an airtight container, covered with a layer of oil.
Freezing is not recommended.
If you don't have tahini available, you can add a larger amount of sesame seeds.
The consistency may vary depending on how watery the cherry tomatoes are.
You can customize the cherry tomato hummus by adding other spices and aromatic herbs!
