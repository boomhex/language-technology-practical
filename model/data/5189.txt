name:
Sweet Potato Chips with Grana Padano Fondue
ingredients:
Sweet potatoes (or yams): 10.6oz(300 g)
Grana Padano PDO cheese: 6.3oz(180 g)
Fresh liquid cream: 1cup(250 g)
Fine salt: to taste

info:
Difficulty: Easy
Prep time: 15 min
Cook time: 30 min
Serving: 4

instructions:
Sweet potato chips with Grana Padano fondue are a tempting appetizer, freely inspired by the traditional cheese fondue , a warm and enveloping dish with timeless flavors. Here you will find a velvety and tasty sauce, as the classic recipe requires, but with the special taste of Grana Padano PDO, accompanied not by the typical boiled potatoes but by crunchy and delicious chips of sweet potatoes, which will add an extra touch to this convivial and indulgent dish. Let yourself be tempted by this refined dish, you will be surprised to discover how easy it is to make!
To make Sweet Potato Chips with Grana Padano Fondue, wash the potatoes and slice them with a mandolin on the short side, keeping a thickness of about 1/2 mm . Spread them out on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper and bake in a preheated convection oven at 284Â°F for 20 minutes.
When cooking is complete, remove them from the oven and salt to taste . Now take care of the fondue: pour the cream into a small pot and bring it to a simmer, then turn off the heat and incorporate the grated Grana Padano PDO .
Stir well with a spoon until you get a smooth, lump-free sauce, then set aside . Enjoy Sweet Potato Chips with Grana Padano Fondue immediately .
We recommend consuming the Grana Padano fondue immediately. The chips can be stored at room temperature in a closed container for about 4-5 days.
If you love strong flavors, you can season the chips with paprika or pepper.
