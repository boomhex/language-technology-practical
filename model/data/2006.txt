name:
Tuna fillets with tahini and hummus
ingredients:
Tuna filet: 8.1oz(230 g)- (2 medium)
Extra virgin olive oil: 2tsp(10 g)
Fine salt: to taste
Black pepper: to taste
Precooked chickpeas: 1 ¼cup(300 g)
Tahini: 2tbsp(30 g)
Parsley: 1sprig
Smoked paprika: 1tsp
Lemon juice: 4tbsp(60 g)
Extra virgin olive oil: 3.7tbsp(50 g)
Lemon juice: 2tbsp(30 g)
Water: 3.1tsp(15 g)
Smoked paprika: ½tsp

info:
Difficulty: Easy
Prep time: 15 min
Cook time: 6 min
Serving: 2

instructions:
To prepare the tuna fillets with tahini and hummus, start by making the hummus: squeeze the lemon and strain the juice. Pour the pre-cooked chickpeas into the bowl of a mixer with blades, add 2 tbsp of tahini , and season with parsley
and paprika , then pour in 1/4 cup of lemon juice and 1/4 cup of olive oil .
Blend until you obtain a smooth and lump-free mixture that you will set aside. Now take care of the accompanying sauce: pour 2 tbsp of tahini, water , and 2 tbsp of lemon juice into a bowl,
stir with a spoon to mix well and flavor with half a teaspoon of paprika ; stir to taste and set aside. Now take care of the tuna: season the fillets with oil, salt, and pepper ,
then place them on a hot pan and cook for about 3 minutes per side , the time may vary depending on the thickness of the fillet. Once ready, serve the tuna immediately, accompanied by the tahini sauce and hummus .
Tuna fillets with tahini and hummus should be consumed immediately, and any form of storage is not recommended. Tahini sauce and hummus can be stored in the refrigerator in an airtight container for 2-3 days. Freezing is not recommended.
Love spicy notes? You can add a pinch of chili pepper to the hummus. If you can't find tahini, you can make it at home following the instructions in our Hummus guide.
