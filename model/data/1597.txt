name:
Almond milk cake without eggs
ingredients:
Almond milk: 0.8cup(200 g)
Sugar: 1cup(200 g)
Type 00 flour: 2cups(250 g)
Vegetable oil: ½cup(100 g)
Lemon peel: 1
Baking powder: 1 ¼tsp(6 g)
Powdered sugar: to taste
Almond slices: 1 ½tbsp(10 g)

info:
Difficulty: Easy
Prep time: 15 min
Cook time: 40 min
Serving: 4
Cost: Low

instructions:
To prepare the almond milk cake without eggs, start by grating the zest of an untreated lemon (1-2) and set it aside for a moment. In a bowl, pour in the flour and sugar ,
the baking powder , and the previously grated lemon zest. Then pour in the almond milk . Also add the vegetable oil , stir with a spatula to start,
then continue with a hand whisk until you obtain a homogeneous mixture. Grease a 9-inch diameter mold and pour in the batter . Bake the cake in a static oven preheated to 350°F for about 40 minutes (perform the toothpick test before taking it out).
Once cooked, remove the cake from the oven and let it cool. Decorate the cooled cake with powdered sugar and almond flakes . Your almond milk cake is ready to be served.
Store the cake under a glass dome for 2-3 days.
You can freeze it already portioned.
You can use citrus zest instead of lemon zest.
