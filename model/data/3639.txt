name:
Vegetable risotto
ingredients:
Carnaroli rice: 1.6cups(320 g)
Carrots: 3.5oz(100 g)
Yellow peppers: 1.8oz(50 g)- already cleaned
Red peppers: 1.8oz(50 g)- cleaned
Eggplant: 1 ¼cup(100 g)
Zucchini: ½cup(100 g)
Peas: 0.3cup(50 g)
Pomodori di Pachino (cherry tomatoes): 5.3oz(150 g)
Celery: 1rib
Yellow onions: 1- medium
Butter: 1 ½tbsp(20 g)
Extra virgin olive oil: 6spoonfuls
Vegetable broth: 4 ¼cups(1 l)
Parsley: 2spoonfuls- minced
Parmigiano Reggiano PDO cheese: 4spoonfuls
White wine: 2.7tbsp(40 ml)
Fine salt: to taste
Black pepper: to taste

info:
Difficulty: Easy
Prep time: 15 min
Cook time: 30 min
Serving: 4
Cost: Low

instructions:

To prepare the vegetable risotto, first clean and trim all the vegetables: cut them into brunoise (very small cubes) (1-2). Make sure to cut all the vegetables to the same size so that they cook evenly. Once the operation is completed, set the vegetables aside .
Cut the cherry tomatoes into wedges, place them in a bowl, and add a teaspoon of sugar , so that they lose their acidity. Chop the onion , and let half of it soften over very low heat in a large pan along with three tablespoons of oil and the butter; to prevent the onion from burning at this stage, you can add a ladle of broth .
When the onion becomes translucent (it will take about 15 minutes), add all the diced vegetables to the pan: the zucchini, half a carrot , the diced eggplant, peas , and bell peppers . Salt and pepper and let the vegetables cook for about 15 minutes, they should be soft but not mushy.
In another pan, How to make the soffritto in the remaining oil a mince prepared with the celery stalk, the other half of the onion, and the carrot . Again, let it go over low heat for about 10 minutes; to avoid burning the soffritto, you can add a ladle of broth here as well. Then add the rice and toast it together with the soffritto . Add the white wine and let it evaporate.
When the wine has evaporated, add a ladle of broth and let it cook, adding a ladle of broth from time to time , stirring constantly with a wooden spoon. When the rice is halfway cooked, salt and add the vegetables, which should now be cooked , and continue cooking the rice, always adding a ladle of broth from time to time. When the rice is cooked, also add the cherry tomatoes , mix everything well, and turn off the heat.
At this point, you can proceed to stir the risotto, turn off the heat, and add the butter , Parmesan cheese , chopped parsley , and add a grind of freshly ground pepper, give it a final stir and your vegetable risotto is ready for your table!
Vegetable risotto can be stored in the refrigerator for one day in an airtight container. When serving, heat it in a pan, adding a knob of butter or a ladle of hot water.
This risotto can be prepared with any type of vegetables of your choice, depending on the season in which it is prepared. You can also use frozen vegetables, such as peas, which can be added to the pan along with the vegetables.
