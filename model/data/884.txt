name:
Mini muffins with spinach and goat cheese
ingredients:
Type 00 flour: 2cups(250 g)
Instant yeast for salted preparations: 1tsp(3 g)
Grana Padano PDO cheese: 2.5oz(70 g)
Spinach: 3.3cups(100 g)
Garlic: 1clove
Eggs: 2oz(55 g)
Whole milk: 0.8cup(200 g)- at room temperature
Butter: 7tbsp(100 g)- soft
Fine salt: to taste
Black pepper: to taste
Goat: 0.44lb(200 g)
Fresh liquid cream: 0.8cup(200 g)
Chives: to taste

info:
Difficulty: Average
Prep time: 15 min
Cook time: 25 min
Makes: 36 pieces

instructions:
To make the mini muffins, pour a drizzle of oil and a clove of garlic into a saucepan. Let it sauté for a couple of minutes. Add the previously washed spinach , season with salt and pepper.
Cook for 5 minutes over high heat, stirring until wilted. Remove the garlic , transfer the cooked spinach to a tall glass, and blend with an immersion blender . Set aside the obtained cream .
In a bowl, add the softened butter and grated cheese . Work the ingredients with electric whisks for about 2 minutes. Add the egg to the mixture , and mix until fully absorbed.
Also add the blended spinach to the bowl , work quickly with the whisks and add the flour, instant yeast, and milk .
Work with the whisks until you get a homogeneous mixture . Transfer the mixture into a piping bag without a nozzle and fill a 12 mini muffin mold , with these doses you will obtain 36 muffins. Bake in a preheated static oven at 356°F for 20 minutes.
Once cooked, take the muffins out of the oven and let them cool . In the meantime, prepare the goat cheese cream by pouring the cheese, a drizzle of oil , salt, and pepper into a bowl.
Mix with a hand whisk and also add the fresh cream . Work the ingredients with the whisk until you obtain a smooth cream. Take the mini muffins out of the oven and let them cool completely, then top them with the goat cheese cream on top . Season with pepper and chives. The muffins are ready to be enjoyed .
Store the mini muffins in the refrigerator for one day.
You can add a spicy note to the cream by adding a few drops of Tabasco, dried chili, or hot paprika powder.
