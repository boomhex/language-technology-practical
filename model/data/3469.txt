name:
Banana milkshake
ingredients:
Bananas: 0.7lb(300 g)
Ice: 2.1oz(60 g)
Cinnamon sticks: Â½tsp(2 g)
Whole milk: 0.625cup(150 g)

info:
Difficulty: Very easy
Prep time: 10 min
Serving: 4
Cost: Very low

instructions:
To prepare the banana milkshake, start by peeling the bananas and cutting them into chunks . Then place the freshly cut banana pieces into the blender .
Add the cinnamon , ice cubes , and cold milk .
Blend until you get a thick and creamy mixture . Then pour it into the glasses and decorate with a few pieces of cinnamon . Serve the banana milkshake immediately and enjoy it well chilled!
If you want to sweeten your milkshake, add a teaspoon of honey, it will make it sweeter... Alternatively, you can flavor the milkshake with a teaspoon of cocoa instead of cinnamon!
It is advisable to consume the banana milkshake immediately to prevent the fruit from oxidizing. Freezing is not recommended!
