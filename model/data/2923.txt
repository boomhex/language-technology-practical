name:
Jerusalem artichoke flan
ingredients:
Jerusalem artichoke: 2.2lbs(1 kg)
Potatoes: 0.75lb(350 g)
Eggs: 3- medium
Garlic: 1clove
Rosemary: 1sprig
Water: to taste
Fine salt: to taste
Black pepper: to taste
Chives: to taste
Extra virgin olive oil: to taste

info:
Difficulty: Easy
Prep time: 15 min
Cook time: 60 min
Makes: 7 pieces
Cost: Low

instructions:
To prepare the Jerusalem artichoke flan, you can start by cleaning the tuber: remove the outer skin with a peeler , then rinse it under running water and cut it into cubes ; in the end, you should obtain about 1.5 lbs of cleaned Jerusalem artichokes. In a large pan, pour a drizzle of oil and a clove of garlic, peeled and sliced thinly .
Slightly brown the garlic in the pan , then add the Jerusalem artichoke cubes, stir, and season with salt and pepper to taste. Let the Jerusalem artichokes cook for about 10 minutes. Meanwhile, peel the potatoes with a vegetable peeler ,
and cut them into small cubes to speed up the cooking; in a large pot, pour the oil and add the potatoes .
Add a sprig of rosemary , salt, and pepper to taste, and let them cook for 10 minutes. Stir occasionally to brown the potato cubes well. When they are nicely browned, turn off the heat . In a blender, place the cooked Jerusalem artichokes .
Add the potatoes as well , then blend everything. Next, add the eggs and the chopped chives .
Run the blender until you get a smooth and creamy mixture . Then prepare the 1/2 cup aluminum molds, brush them with some oil , and line them with parchment paper: cut out a circle for the bottom and strips for the sides to fully cover the molds .
Then, with a ladle, pour the Jerusalem artichoke and potato mixture into each mold , leaving half an inch from the edge. Place the molds in a high-edged baking tray and pour in water until it reaches the base of the flans . Bake in a preheated static oven at 350Â°F for 40-45 minutes. Then remove the flans, place them on a baking sheet, and grill them for 5 minutes. Then take them out of the oven .
Remove the Jerusalem artichoke flans, let them cool slightly, then unmold them by turning them out onto a cutting board (22-23). You can serve them decorated with a sprig of chives and enjoy them warm or even cold as an appetizer or a tasty side dish!
You can store the Jerusalem artichoke flans in the refrigerator for up to 2-3 days in an airtight container or covered with plastic wrap. They can be frozen if fresh ingredients have been used.
