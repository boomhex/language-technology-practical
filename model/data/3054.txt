name:
Classic puff pastries with spinach raisins and pine nuts
ingredients:
Crispy baked puff pastry: 12
Spinach: 2 ¾cups(80 g)
Raisins: 1 ½tbsp(15 g)
Pine nuts: 1tsp(5 g)
Garlic: 1clove
Fine salt: to taste
Black pepper: to taste
Extra virgin olive oil: to taste

info:
Difficulty: Very easy
Prep time: 10 min
Cook time: 5 min
Makes: 12 pieces
Cost: Low

To prepare the classic puff pastries with spinach, raisins, and pine nuts, start by soaking the raisins . Meanwhile, carefully wash the spinach and dry them. Then take a non-stick pan and heat a drizzle of extra virgin olive oil and a peeled clove of garlic . At this point, add the spinach and quickly sauté over medium heat for 2 minutes until they are slightly wilted .
Now add the pine nuts and the previously soaked and drained raisins . Salt and pepper to taste and cook for another 3 minutes. Place the classic puff pastries on a cutting board and lay the mixture on top . Your classic puff pastries with spinach, raisins, and pine nuts are ready to be served as a delightful appetizer!
The classic puff pastries with spinach, raisins, and pine nuts can be stored in the fridge in an airtight container or well covered with plastic wrap for up to 1 day.
If you want to give a stronger note to your classic puff pastries with spinach, raisins, and pine nuts, you can melt two anchovy fillets together with the garlic.
