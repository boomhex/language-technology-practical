name:
Squid with Peas
ingredients:
Calamari (squid): 1.8lbs(800 g)- (to clean)
Frozen peas: 2 ½cups(400 g)- (or fresh)
Fresh scallion: 1
Dry white wine: ½cup(100 g)
Garlic: 1clove
Extra virgin olive oil: to taste
Fine salt: to taste

info:
Difficulty: Easy
Prep time: 20 min
Cook time: 25 min
Serving: 4
Cost: Average

instructions:
To prepare the squid with peas, first clean the squid: rinse them, separate the head from the mantle, and gently remove the innards and the quill of cartilage inside . Rinse them well, inside and out, and remove the skin , including the beak. Rinse multiple times and finally cut them into rings about 3/4 inch wide .
Clean and finely chop the scallion . Heat a splash of oil in a pan, add the scallion , and let it wilt, then add the peas , salt, and cook for about 10 minutes over medium-low heat.
Meanwhile, pour the squid into another pan with another splash of oil and the garlic clove . Deglaze with white wine and cook for about 10 minutes over medium-low heat , then remove the garlic from the pan .
At this point, add the peas , add a drizzle of oil, and cook for another 10 minutes . Adjust the salt, if necessary, and serve your squid with peas accompanied by slices of toasted bread !
Squid with peas can be stored in the refrigerator for a maximum of one day. Freezing is not recommended.
Squid with peas is also excellent for dressing pasta!
You can use fresh peas if in season; in this case, extend the cooking time by about 5 minutes.
