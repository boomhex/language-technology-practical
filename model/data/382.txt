name:
Baked Mackerel
ingredients:
Mackerel fillets: 0.8lb(380 g)
Cluster tomatoes: 2
Black olives: 1.5oz(40 g)
Capers: 2 ½tbsp(35 g)
Extra virgin olive oil: 1 ½tbsp(20 g)
Salt: to taste
Thyme: to taste- fresh

info:
Difficulty: Very easy
Prep time: 15 min
Cook time: 15 min
Serving: 4
Cost: Low

instructions:
To prepare the baked mackerel, first wash and dry the tomatoes, then cut them into very small cubes . Transfer them to a bowl and add the capers , sliced black olives, and extra virgin olive oil .
Mix everything together . Wash and dry the mackerel fillets, place them neatly on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper, and season with salt . Cover each fillet with the dressing, distributing it with a teaspoon .
Add a few sprigs of fresh thyme and bake in a static oven at 356°F for 15 minutes . Here is your baked mackerel , serve it with fresh vegetables!
Baked mackerel can be stored in the refrigerator, in a closed container, for up to one day.
You can use dried thyme instead of fresh.
The mackerel's dressing lends itself to numerous variations: for example, you could use onions, thinly sliced potatoes, and rosemary!
