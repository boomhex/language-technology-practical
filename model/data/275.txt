name:
Gluten-free apple cake
ingredients:
Golden delicious apples: 1- weight to clean
Gluten free flour mix: 2.4cups(300 g)
Eggs: 4
Brown sugar: ¼cup(50 g)
Lemon peel: 1
Honey: 1 ½tsp(10 g)
Cinnamon powder: to taste
Sunflower seed oil: ½cup(100 g)
Greek yogurt: 0.6cup(150 g)
Potato starch: 6tbsp(50 g)
Baking powder: 2tsp(10 g)
Powdered sugar: to taste

To prepare the gluten-free apple cake, start by pouring the eggs into a bowl . Add the brown sugar and turn on the electric mixer at medium speed .
After about 4 minutes, you'll get a frothy mixture. Flavor the batter with the zest of one lemon . Add the honey and ground cinnamon .
Pour in the sunflower oil , Greek yogurt and restart the electric beaters .
Once you've reached a frothy consistency, add the sifted dry ingredients. First, the gluten-free flour mix , then the potato starch , and finally the baking powder .
With the help of a spatula, fold in the powders from the bottom upwards with delicate movements , until all the ingredients are combined . Pour the mixture into a 9-inch round pan lined with parchment paper .
Take the apple and peel it , cut it into 1/2-inch slices and arrange them in a fan shape over the batter .
Bake the gluten-free apple cake in a static oven at 340 degrees Fahrenheit for 45 minutes . Once cooled, sprinkle the surface with powdered sugar . Your cake is ready!
Once cooled, the gluten-free apple cake can be frozen in portions.
It keeps in the fridge for 2-3 days.
Instead of potato starch, you can use rice starch.
You can use other types of apples suitable for baking, like Fuji, Gala, and Renette.
