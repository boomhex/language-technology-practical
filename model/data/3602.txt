name:
Slices with tomato, thyme, and marjoram
ingredients:
Sliced beef: 8- for a total of 600 g
Canned tomatoes: 8.8oz(250 g)
Extra virgin olive oil: 3spoonfuls
Butter: 2tbsp(30 g)
Thyme: 2sprigs
Marjoram: 1sprig
Garlic: 1clove
Shallot: 1
Fine salt: to taste
Black pepper: to taste

info:
Difficulty: Easy
Prep time: 5 min
Cook time: 10 min
Serving: 4
Cost: Low

In a large pan, heat the oil and butter, add the crushed garlic and chopped shallot , let them soften (it will take about 15 minutes) and then add the slices . Brown the meat, first on one side then on the other ,
then add the thyme and marjoram . Let it flavor and pour in the tomato pulp . Cook everything for 5 minutes on high heat, and when the tomato has thickened, adjust with salt and pepper and then turn off. Serve the slices with tomato, thyme, and marjoram well drizzled with their cooking sauce .
