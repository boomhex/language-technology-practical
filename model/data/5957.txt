name:
Pan-fried carrots
ingredients:
Carrots: 8oz(250 g)
Garlic: 2cloves- (unpeeled)
Thyme: 2sprigs
Paprika: ½tsp
Extra virgin olive oil: to taste
Fine salt: to taste
Black pepper: to taste

info:
Difficulty: Very easy
Prep time: 5 min
Cook time: 15 min
Serving: 2
Cost: Very low

instructions:
To prepare pan-fried carrots , start by peeling the carrots and cutting them into 4 pieces lengthwise . Heat a pan, add a drizzle of oil , then add the carrots, the unpeeled garlic clove and the thyme sprigs .
Add salt , pepper and season with the paprika. Sauté the carrots on a high flame until brown and slightly soft . Serve your pan-fried carrots hot or warm !
Pan-fried carrots can be stored in the refrigerator for 2 days, in a sealed container.
If you prefer, you can replace the thyme with rosemary.
You can use whatever kind of paprika you like, or leave it out altogether!
