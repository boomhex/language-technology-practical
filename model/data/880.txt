name:
Delicious coffee with cream
ingredients:
Instant coffee: 1tsp
Sugar: 2tsp
Water: to taste- hot
Condensed milk: 1tsp
Spreadable hazelnut cream: 1tsp
Unsweetened cocoa powder: to taste
Fresh liquid cream: to taste

To prepare the indulgent coffee with cream, heat the water and dissolve a teaspoon of instant coffee and two of sugar in it.
Blend with a milk frother until you create a foam . Separately, pour the condensed milk into a cup .
Add the hazelnut cream as well . Pour the coffee over .
Whip the liquid cream with electric beaters until it is very firm , then transfer it to a piping bag with a fluted nozzle. Squeeze the cream over the coffee .
You should create a nice dollop that covers the entire surface . Now dust with unsweetened cocoa powder and serve your indulgent coffee with cream immediately .
We recommend consuming the indulgent coffee with cream right away.
Garnish the coffee with hazelnut crumbs to make it even more inviting!
