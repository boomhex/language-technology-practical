name:
Classic vanilla pudding
ingredients:
Whole milk: 1 ½cup(350 g)
Fresh liquid cream: 0.8cup(200 g)
Sugar: ½cup(100 g)
Egg yolks: 2.6oz(75 g)- (approximately 4)
Cornstarch: 0.7cup(75 g)
Vanilla bean: 1
Raspberries: 36

To prepare the vanilla pudding, pour the cream and the milk into a saucepan , then add the vanilla bean along with the seeds you scraped out with the tip of a knife . Bring the mixture to a boil over low heat.
In a separate bowl, add the egg yolks and sugar , mix with a whisk, and also add the cornstarch . Continue mixing with the whisk until you obtain a smooth and homogeneous mixture .
When the milk and cream are about to boil, remove the vanilla pod and pour in the yolk mixture while whisking . Continue stirring over low heat for a few minutes until the cream thickens . Take 4 glasses with a capacity of 6 oz each and fill them halfway with the obtained cream .
Add 4 raspberries to each glass , then cover with the rest of the mixture and decorate as desired with the remaining raspberries . Cover with plastic wrap and place in the fridge to set for at least 6 hours: your vanilla pudding is ready to be served!
The vanilla pudding can be stored in the fridge for 3-4 days, covered with plastic wrap. Freezing is not recommended.
You can use other fresh fruits instead of raspberries, or replace them with a How to prepare coulis !
