name:
Flat beans with tomato
ingredients:
Flat beans: 0.9lb(400 g)
Cluster tomatoes: 2- ripe and firm
Red onions: ½
Tomato purée: 0.3cup(70 g)
Extra virgin olive oil: to taste
Fine salt: to taste
Black pepper: to taste

info:
Difficulty: Easy
Prep time: 10 min
Cook time: 20 min
Serving: 4
Cost: Low

instructions:

To prepare flat beans with tomato, cut a cross at the base of the tomatoes with a knife , then blanch them in hot water for about 1 minute . Drain them
and cool them by immersing them in a bowl of cold water . Wash the flat beans under running water , then trim them
and cut them into diamond shapes . Retrieve the tomatoes and peel them by pulling the skin where the incisions were made . Cut the tomatoes into wedges
and remove the internal part with the seeds . Clean and slice the red onion , heat the oil in a pan ,
add the onion , let it stew over low heat for 5 minutes, then add the tomatoes , let them flavor for a couple of minutes, then add the flat beans .
Add a little hot water, about 2 ladles , salt and pepper, cover with the lid
Cook the flat beans for about 10 minutes, then add the tomato purée and continue cooking for about 5 minutes or as long as needed to thicken the sauce . Serve your flat beans with tomato hot, drizzled with extra virgin olive oil !
Flat beans with tomato can be stored in the refrigerator for up to 3 days in an airtight container. It is possible to freeze the dish if fresh ingredients have been used.
You can scent the dish with fresh basil leaves or add a spicy note by adding chili pepper to the sauce.
