name:
Winter Rice Salad
ingredients:
Rice: 1.67cups(320 g)- Ribe
Cabbage: 3oz(80 g)- red
Cauliflower: 2.8oz(80 g)
Carrots: 2.8oz(80 g)
Walnut kernels: ½cup(50 g)
Thyme: 3sprigs
Extra virgin olive oil: to taste
Fine salt: to taste
Lemon juice: 2 ¾tbsp(40 g)
Black pepper: to taste

To prepare the winter rice salad, bring water to a boil in a pot, then salt it and cook the rice for the time indicated on the package . While the rice is cooking, wash and prepare the vegetables: julienne the cabbage and cut the cauliflower florets into small pieces .
Now, peel the carrots with a vegetable peeler and julienne them . In a pan, heat oil and thyme .
Pepper and toast the walnuts for a few minutes . Set aside the toasted walnuts and prepare the citronette: squeeze the lemon juice .
Strain the juice and pour it into a bowl , then salt, pepper, and mix . Transfer the citronette to a kitchen squeeze bottle.
In a large bowl, pour in the carrots and cabbage and the cauliflower, then dress with the citronette . Drain the rice, which should be cooked by now .
Dress the rice with the oil and mix. Now combine the rice with the vegetables . Serve the warm winter rice salad garnished with the toasted walnuts !
The winter rice salad can be stored in the fridge without dressing for a couple of days. We do not recommend freezing it.
If you don't like raw vegetables, you can sauté them in a pan with oil before adding them to the dish.
