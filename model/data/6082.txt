name:
Baked tomatoes
ingredients:
San Marzano tomatoes: 6
Garlic: 3cloves
Extra virgin olive oil: to taste
Fine salt: to taste
Black pepper: to taste
Oregano: to taste

info:
Difficulty: Very easy
Prep time: 10 min
Cook time: 40 min
Serving: 6
Cost: Very low

instructions:
In summer we pay homage to the season's most important fresh produce: tomatoes . Enjoy them stuffed, Apulian-style gratin, simply au gratin confit, or in the most simple and genuine way, just like we show you in this baked tomato recipe. Discover how to prepare these delicious tomatoes with just a few simple ingredients: garlic, salt, pepper and a handful of aromatic oregano is all you need to serve a healthy and delicious side dish that exalts the unique flavor of fresh tomatoes.
To make baked tomatoes , wash and cut the tomatoes in half lengthwise . Peel and thinly slice the garlic cloves . Place the tomatoes, with the cut side down, on a tray lined with parchment paper .
Cut some slices into the back of the tomatoes and fill with the garlic slices . Drizzle with a dash of oil .
Sprinkle with salt and bake in an oven preheated to 355 °F ( 180 °C) for 20 minutes. Remove the tomatoes from the oven and turn them over one by one .
Drizzle with a little more oil, season with salt, pepper and lots of oregano . Baked in the oven for another 20-25 minutes. The baked tomatoes are ready to be served at your table .
Store baked tomatoes in the refrigerator for a couple of days. Freezing is not recommended.
