name:
Yogurt Smoothie
ingredients:
Yogurt: 1cup(250 g)
Lime juice: 0.9tbsp(13 g)- (about 1)
Melon: 420- pulp
Peach: 2.3cups(350 g)- pulp

info:
Difficulty: Easy
Prep time: 10 min
Serving: 4
Cost: Low

instructions:
When the weather warms up, there's nothing like grabbing a yogurt smoothie . Really good stuff. Originally from the U.S., this drink has gone global, with endless variations to enjoy. And the fresh fruit? Seriously good. Imagine summer peaches, ripe melon, or any fruit that looks juicy and sweet at the market. Some folks even throw in veggies like carrots or zucchini—sounds odd, right?—but it actually makes for a more creamy and balanced texture. The mix of yogurt and fruit creates this smooth, almost silky texture that's perfect when you want something cool and light.
These easy smoothie recipes are forgiving. You can pretty much mix whatever you have and end up with something healthy and refreshing. Peaches and melon are fantastic starting points, but hey, go wild. Try blueberries, strawberries, or even pineapple—just toss in what you like or what's in season.
Yogurt, especially Greek yogurt, adds a bit of tang and makes the drink filling enough for a breakfast smoothie or an afternoon snack. And look, if you're after more protein or a low-calorie option, swap in your favorite yogurt and maybe add a scoop of protein powder. You'll find tons of healthy smoothie recipes out there, but honestly, this version is as simple as it gets and still feels a bit special because of the fresh fruit.
Making your own homemade smoothie is energizing—it encourages you to try new combos. Whether you keep it classic with just fruit and yogurt or get creative with veggie add-ins, the result is always cool and satisfying. It's the kind of treat that makes hot days enjoyable, knowing you can blend something that's good for you and tastes really really great, too.
To prepare the yogurt smoothie, first wash the peaches (you'll need about 3), peel them and remove the pits, then roughly chop them and set them aside . Now handle the melon: cut it in half, remove the seeds and the rind, then cut it into pieces .
Cut the lime in half and squeeze it using a juicer . Collect the juice in a glass and set it aside. Now take a blender and place the melon
and the peaches . Add the yogurt and the lime juice , close the blender, and turn it on. Blend until you obtain a creamy mixture. Here is your fresh yogurt smoothie ready!
The smoothie should be consumed the same day.
If you desire a sweeter taste, you can add a tablespoon of sugar or honey. If you prefer a more liquid texture, you can add half a glass of cold milk.
