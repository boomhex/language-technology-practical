name:
Risotto with potato cream and cuttlefish ragout
ingredients:
Carnaroli rice: 0.9cup(180 g)
Octopi: 0.5lb(250 g)- (to clean)
Potatoes: 3.5oz(100 g)
Garlic: 1clove
Parsley: 1bunch- small
Lemon peel: to taste- (to grate)
Water: to taste- hot
White wine: to taste
Extra virgin olive oil: to taste
Fine salt: to taste
Spinach: 1.7cups(50 g)
Cuttlefish ink: to taste

info:
Difficulty: Average
Prep time: 30 min
Cook time: 50 min
Serving: 2
Cost: Low

instructions:
To prepare the risotto with potato cream and cuttlefish ragout, first take care of the How to clean cuttlefish : under running water, remove the bone , then pull off the tentacles and entrails. Remove the skin , the eye, and the beak . Finally, separate the fins from the body and the siphon.
Cut the cuttlefish first into strips and then into small cubes . Also cut the tentacles into cubes . Bring a pot of water to a boil and keep it warm: you will need it to cook the different preparations.
In a pan, flavor a swirl of oil with the crushed garlic clove and the parsley stalks , then add the cuttlefish and brown them, stirring .
Deglaze with white wine and cook over moderate heat for about twenty minutes, adding a ladle of hot water if needed . At the end of cooking, remove the garlic and parsley stalks from the pan .
While the cuttlefish are cooking, peel the potatoes and cut them into cubes . Pour the potatoes into another pan with a drizzle of hot oil and brown them until golden with a pinch of salt .
Moisten with a ladle of hot water and cook for a few more minutes. Transfer the potatoes to a jug and blend with an immersion mixer to obtain a cream .
Move on to the rice: dry-toast the rice in a saucepan with a pinch of salt , then cook it for about 12 minutes, adding a ladle of hot water at a time . During the last few minutes of cooking, add the potato cream .
Mix well and finish cooking the risotto . Meanwhile, put the baby spinach in the same pan as the potatoes with a drizzle of oil and saut√© briefly, just enough time to wilt it . Lightly salt.
When the risotto is cooked, remove it from the heat and stir in a drizzle of oil , then season with chopped parsley leaves and grated lemon zest . If necessary, you can also add a little hot water to make it creamier.
You are ready to plate: brush the plate with cuttlefish ink , then add a couple of spoonfuls of cuttlefish ragout and cover with the risotto .
Garnish with more lemon zest and a few baby spinach leaves . Your risotto with potato cream and cuttlefish ragout is ready to be served !
It is recommended to consume the risotto with potato cream and cuttlefish ragout immediately.
If you prefer, you can replace the hot water with vegetable broth.
