name:
Apple and Spinach Salad
ingredients:
Red Delicious apples: 0.5lb(250 g)
Spinach: 3 ⅓cups(100 g)
Peeled almonds: ½cup(60 g)
Primosale cheese: 5.3oz(150 g)
Extra virgin olive oil: to taste
Fine salt: to taste
Wildflower honey: to taste

info:
Difficulty: Very easy
Prep time: 10 min
Serving: 2
Cost: Low

instructions:
To make the apple and spinach salad, first rinse the spinach leaves under running water and let them drain in a colander . Cut the primosale cheese into chunks , then thoroughly wash the apple and cut it in half .
Remove the core and then slice it to obtain thin wedges . Pour the spinach into a bowl, season with salt
and a drizzle of olive oil . Mix and then add the primosale cheese and apple slices .
Add the almonds last , season with the wildflower honey , and serve the apple and spinach salad immediately .
We recommend consuming the apple and spinach salad right away.
As an alternative to apples, you can use seasonal crunchy pears. The honey will give a sweet and sour note; if you do not like it, you can use balsamic vinegar for flavoring.
