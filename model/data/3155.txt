name:
Orange Cream
ingredients:
Whole milk: 2cups(500 g)
Sugar: 1cup(200 g)
Orange juice: 1.7oz(50 g)
Orange peel: 1
Egg yolks: 3.2oz(90 g)- (about 6)
Potato starch: 5tbsp(40 g)

info:
Difficulty: Easy
Prep time: 20 min
Cook time: 10 min
Serving: 4
Cost: Low

instructions:
To prepare the orange cream, first grate the zest , squeeze the orange and extract the right amount of juice. Pour the milk into a saucepan, add the orange zest and bring to a boil over low heat.
Meanwhile, in another pot, pour the egg yolks and sugar . Mix well with a spatula , then add the cornstarch
and stir again . Then add the orange juice and mix again until you get a smooth and lump-free mixture .
As soon as the milk starts to boil, pour some into the egg yolk mixture , to temper it. Then stir and add the remaining milk. Bring the cream to the heat and cook it for about 5 minutes,
stirring with a whisk, until it becomes smooth and thick . Transfer the cream into a bowl , cover with plastic wrap in contact and let it cool first at room temperature and then in the refrigerator.
The orange custard keeps in the refrigerator for a maximum of 3 days.
Freezing is not recommended.
It is possible to add 10 g of orange liqueur to the egg yolk and sugar mixture.
To quickly lower the temperature of the cream while keeping it shiny, you can pour it into a bowl immersed in another larger one with ice and stir with a whisk until it is lukewarm. Then always cover with plastic wrap in contact and store in the fridge.
