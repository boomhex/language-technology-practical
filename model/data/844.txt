name:
Crispy Baked Chickpeas
ingredients:
Precooked chickpeas: 1 ½cup(250 g)- (drained weight)
Type 00 flour: 1cup(10 g)
Sweet Paprika: 1tsp
Thyme: 1tsp- dry
Rosemary: 1tsp- dry
Oregano: 1spoonful- dry
Fine salt: to taste
Extra virgin olive oil: to taste

instructions:
You know, if you’re on the hunt for a snack that's both tasty and satisfying, these baked crispy chickpeas are a must-try. Really, they come straight from the Middle Eastern snack playbook. The charm? It's all in the super crunchy texture. You get this by thoroughly drying the chickpeas and tossing them in flour. Pretty simple. This easy step turns them from just another roasted chickpeas recipe into a snack you'll want around all the time. Paprika, rosemary, oregano, and thyme are great seasonings to start with, but, you know, mix in any spices you're craving. It's minimal ingredients, minimal fuss, and super super tasty.

Toss these bites into salads, sprinkle them over rice bowls, or mix them into pasta—like whole wheat spaghetti with avocado cream and some of these tender, spiced chickpeas. Seriously good. They soak up flavors while keeping that crunch, so every bite is a surprise. Perfect for a healthy snack during hangouts or while chilling with a movie. And here's the thing—you don't need to be a kitchen wizard. Just a few ingredients and you’ve got that crispy exterior and yummy taste. The whole vibe is about sharing, relaxing and making it your own—which is great. Try swapping herbs or adding more spice to make your own oven-roasted chickpeas or crispy roasted chickpeas . Pour the well-drained chickpeas into a bowl, add the flour , and mix . Add the paprika .
Add the rosemary , thyme , and oregano .
Adjust the salt and add a drizzle of oil . Mix to evenly coat all the chickpeas .
Spread the chickpeas on a baking tray lined with parchment paper so that they do not overlap and are spaced apart . Add another drizzle of oil and bake in a preheated oven at 392°F (convection mode) for 30 minutes, shaking the tray a couple of times during cooking to turn them. If they are not golden enough, turn on the grill for 5 minutes. Let your crispy baked chickpeas cool before serving !
We recommend consuming the crispy chickpeas as soon as they are ready. Alternatively, you can store them at room temperature for up to one day in an airtight jar.
You can use hot paprika and flavor the crispy baked chickpeas with other herbs or spices.
Don't throw away the chickpea water, known as aquafaba: you can use it to prepare other vegan recipes like Eggless Frittata or Vegan meringues !
