name:
Pasta with pumpkin cream and taleggio
ingredients:
Spaghetti: 11.3oz(320 g)
Italian kale (cavolo nero): 1 ½cup(100 g)- (to clean)
Extra virgin olive oil: to taste
Black pepper: to taste
Delica pumpkin: 1 ¾cup(400 g)
Taleggio cheese: 3.5oz(100 g)
Water: ¾cup(200 g)
Fine salt: to taste
Leeks: 2 ¼cups(200 g)
Peanut seed oil: to taste

To prepare the pasta with pumpkin cream and taleggio, start with the kale: separate the leaves from the stalks , then place them on a baking tray lined with parchment paper, spacing them slightly apart . Bake in a preheated convection oven at 340°F for 20 minutes. Meanwhile, take care of the pumpkin: clean it from seeds and internal filaments .
Cut it into 4 parts , then put it in a bowl and cover with plastic wrap . Microwave for 5 minutes at the highest temperature. Once cooked, scoop out the flesh with a spoon .
Transfer the flesh into a pitcher, then add the water and salt . Blend with an immersion blender to obtain a smooth and homogeneous cream and set aside.
At this point, the kale will be cooked , and the leaves should be dry and crispy. Place them in a spice grinder and grind to obtain a powder .
Move on to the leek: clean and finely slice the leek . Heat the vegetable oil to a temperature of 355°F and fry the leek rings for about a minute, until golden. Drain and transfer to absorbent paper or paper towels for fried foods.
Now cook the spaghetti in salted boiling water . Meanwhile, heat the pumpkin cream in a saucepan, then add the diced taleggio and stir over low heat to melt it completely .
Drain the pasta one minute before the end of the cooking time and transfer it to a large pan with a splash of cooking water, then add the pumpkin and taleggio cream and mix well to combine. Finally, toss with a drizzle of olive oil and a pinch of pepper .
Plate and garnish with the kale powder, using a sieve , and the fried leek . Your pasta with pumpkin cream and taleggio is ready to be served !
It is advisable to consume the pasta with pumpkin cream and taleggio immediately. If necessary, it can be stored in the refrigerator for 1-2 days.
If you don't have a spice grinder, you can crumble the kale leaves by hand.
Alternatively to the microwave, you can bake the pumpkin: cut it in half, clean it, and bake at 390°F for about 45 minutes or until tender.
The discarded kale stalks can be used to enrich a vegetable broth.
