name:
Scialatielli with radicchio and Montasio fondue
ingredients:
Scialatielli pasta: 14.1oz(400 g)- (fresh)
Pancetta: 5.3oz(150 g)
Radicchio: 5.3oz(150 g)- red
Fine salt: to taste
Black pepper: to taste
Butter: 2tbsp(30 g)
Montasio cheese: 7oz(200 g)
Fresh liquid cream: 0.8cup(200 g)
Extra virgin olive oil: to taste

info:
Difficulty: Easy
Prep time: 30 min
Cook time: 25 min
Serving: 4
Cost: Low

instructions:

To prepare scialatielli with radicchio and Montasio fondue, start by making the fondue: bring the cream to a boil in a non-stick pan , adding salt and pepper to taste. Only when it has reached a boil, add the grated Montasio cheese and, while continuing to stir, let it melt completely : the cream should thicken.
Clean, wash, and cut the red radicchio into julienne strips and set it aside, then cut the smoked bacon into fine strips and let it brown in a non-stick pan with a little oil, over low heat for a few minutes .
When the bacon has browned, add the butter , let it melt completely, and add the radicchio . Increase the heat and let the radicchio wilt over high heat for a couple of minutes. In a large pot of salted boiling water, cook the fresh scialatielli and drain them al dente .
Add the pasta to the pot with the radicchio and sauté for a couple of minutes over medium heat , then add the Montasio fondue, still warm, and mix well to combine all the ingredients . Continue cooking until a thick cream forms . The scialatielli with radicchio and Montasio fondue are ready, serve them hot.
You can store scialatielli with radicchio and Montasio fondue for a couple of days, sealed in an airtight container and kept in the refrigerator.
