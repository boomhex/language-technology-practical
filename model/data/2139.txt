name:
Pasta with Broad Beans and Peas
ingredients:
Gluten-free Spaghetti pasta: 11.3oz(320 g)
Leeks: 1cup(100 g)
Fava beans: 1.1cups(250 g)
Peas: 1 ¼cup(150 g)- shelled
Pecorino cheese: 1.1oz(30 g)- grated
Fine salt: to taste
Black pepper: to taste
Thyme: 1sprig
Extra virgin olive oil: 2tbsp(30 g)-

info:
Difficulty: Very easy
Prep time: 20 min
Cook time: 30 min
Serving: 4
Cost: Low

instructions:
To make the pasta with broad beans and peas, start by placing the water for the pasta on the stove: when it boils, salt it. Then clean the broad beans: trim and split the pod in half to extract them , then remove the outer skin of each broad bean and collect them all in a bowl , to obtain 6.3 oz. Then take the leek and remove the roots and the green part
Remove the tougher outer leaves of the leek , slice it thinly , then heat the olive oil in a large saucepan and add the sliced leek , sauté for 1 minute.
Then cover with a ladle of cooking water , let the leek wilt for about 10 minutes then add the broad beans and shelled peas
Cook for 10 minutes (if you are using frozen legumes, cook for 5 minutes). Once cooked, salt and pepper . Cook the spaghetti for about 9 minutes, they should be al dente (follow the cooking time on the package) . Once cooked, drain the gluten-free spaghetti directly into the pan with the legumes
Season with the grated cheese and flavor with fresh thyme leaves . Mix well to combine the ingredients and serve the pasta with broad beans and peas piping hot .
It is recommended to consume the pasta with broad beans and peas immediately. If there are leftovers, you can store it for one day in the refrigerator in an airtight container or covered with plastic wrap. Freezing is not recommended.
For a fresh taste, replace the pecorino with grated lemon zest, creating a tasty and appetizing vegan version!
