name:
Fried Green Tomatoes
ingredients:
Green tomatoes: 4- medium
Eggs: 2
Type 00 flour: 2cups(80 g)
Fioretto corn flour: ½cup(80 g)
Sugar: 0.8tbsp(10 g)
Fine salt: to taste
Black pepper: to taste
Peanut seed oil: 1.6cups(350 g)

info:
Difficulty: Easy
Prep time: 15 min
Cook time: 15 min
Serving: 4
Cost: Low
Note + 30 minutes of resting

instructions:

To prepare the fried green tomatoes, start by washing the tomatoes under running water , then cut each one crosswise into slices about 0.5 inches thick . At this point, place them on a rack, salt them, and leave them this way for about 30 minutes to allow them to lose some of their moisture .
In a bowl, sift the cornmeal and then sift the all-purpose flour into the same bowl . Then add the sugar and mix with a spoon until you have a uniform mixture.
In another container, beat the eggs with salt and pepper . After 30 minutes, take your tomato slices and dip them first in the egg and then in the flour mix .
Repeat the same operation once more, then dip the newly breaded tomatoes again in the egg (10-11), and finish by coating them in the flours again. Then fry them, a few pieces at a time, in plenty of hot oil .
Once they are well golden on both sides, drain them of excess oil and place them on a tray lined with paper towels, without overlapping them . Serve your fried green tomatoes piping hot !
To enjoy the crispiness of fried green tomatoes, it's advisable to consume them immediately after preparation, alternatively, you can store them for a day in the refrigerator. Freezing is not recommended.
Before breading your tomato slices, try dabbing them with a sheet of paper towel to allow the breading to adhere perfectly!
In the market, you can find very small green tomatoes (same size as cherry tomatoes); in this case, simply cut the tomato in half. Salt it, let it lose excess moisture, then proceed in the same way as indicated in the recipe.
