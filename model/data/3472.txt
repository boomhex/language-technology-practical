name:
Hot dog and fries sandwich
ingredients:
Bread: 1- type "francesino"
Frankfurter: 1
Fried potatoes: to taste- frozen
Scamorza (provola) cheese: 3- thin slices
Fine salt: to taste
Mayonnaise: to taste- (or ketchup)

info:
Difficulty: Very easy
Prep time: 10 min
Cook time: 15 min
Serving: 1
Cost: Very low

instructions:
Golden fries piled right into a hot dog sandwich bring a really nice mix of classic American flavors and family-friendly comfort—definitely a favorite for anyone looking for a casual, shareable meal. Everything about this American hot dog sandwich works so well for busy families, whether it’s lunch with kids or quick group dinners. Kids love how the crunch from those fresh fries gives every bite a mix of salty and soft (and parents usually like the simple, extra-satisfying bite just as much). Stacked together, you get a filling, hearty meal, packed with all the colorful toppings that make this a stand-out Italian hot dog or even a quick nod to the New Jersey hot dog tradition.
If you’re after a fries sandwich that’s more about great eating than fussy rules, this pick delivers...usually everyone at the table can make it their own, swapping out toppings or sauces, going classic one day, then mixing it up the next (think: deli mustard or even ketchup if that’s your style). This simple meal is a smart choice for nights you need something DELICIOUS that doesn’t require a lot of fuss—just show up, load your roll, and dig in. Picnic, weeknight, lazy Saturday—really any family meal works. Crowd-pleasers like this often get requested again since the classic flavors in every hot dog and fry combo please both picky kids and adventurous adults. Honestly, the great thing about the hot dog sandwich with fries is how it feels extra special without ever being complicated—nice color, fun to eat, plenty of satisfying bites. Families love having a reliable go-to that still offers a bit of a twist, and this one never disappoints. (It’s pretty much always AMAZING when served fresh from the counter!)
To prepare the hot dog and fries sandwich, start by lining a baking sheet with parchment paper, place the fries on it , and bake them for about 15 minutes at 392°F, when they are ready, salt them. Meanwhile, cut the scamorza cheese into very thin slices and cut the roll in half lengthwise ,
also cut the hot dog lengthwise . Then heat a non-stick pan and place the roll (with the cut side facing down) and the hot dog . Cook for about 5 minutes, turning the hot dog halfway through. Then begin assembling the sandwich: cover the roll with slices of scamorza cheese, which will melt when in contact with the hot bread, add the hot dog and place the fries on top , sprinkle with ketchup or mayonnaise (whichever you prefer) and close with the other half of the roll.
