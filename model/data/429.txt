name:
Basque cheesecake
ingredients:
Cream cheese: 2.2lbs(500 g)- at room temperature
Fresh liquid cream: 1 ½cup(350 g)- at room temperature
Sugar: 0.8cup(150 g)
Eggs: 4- medium (at room temperature)
Cornstarch: 3.1tbsp(25 g)
Vanilla extract: 1tsp
Milk Chocolate: to taste- melted

info:
Difficulty: Very easy
Prep time: 20 min
Cook time: 50 min
Serving: 6
Cost: Low
Note + the setting time in the fridge (8-12 h)

instructions:
To make the Basque cheesecake, first pour the cream cheese and sugar into a large bowl and mix well with a hand whisk , then add one egg at a time .
Continue to work with the whisk to gradually incorporate the eggs into the mixture . Sift the cornstarch and mix again, then also pour in the cream .
Finally, add the vanilla extract and blend it into the mixture: it should be smooth, thick, and homogeneous . Line the base and sides of a 9-inch springform pan with parchment paper .
Pour the mixture into the pan and bake in a preheated static oven at 392°F for about 50 minutes . When the surface is golden and almost burnt, remove from the oven and let cool in the pan, then place the cheesecake in the refrigerator overnight . Serve your Basque cheesecake with melted milk chocolate!
The Basque cheesecake can be stored in the refrigerator for 2 days in an airtight container.
If you prefer, you can flavor the mixture with cinnamon or orange or lemon zest as an alternative to vanilla.
You can serve the Basque cheesecake with melted chocolate or with other sauces of your choice, fresh fruit, or whipped cream.
