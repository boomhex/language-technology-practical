name:
Fusilloni with peas, shrimp, and cherry tomatoes
ingredients:
Fusilloni pasta: 11.3oz(320 g)
Shrimps: 10.6oz(300 g)- shrimps
Tomatoes: ¾cup(150 g)
Peas: 1 ¼cup(200 g)- shelled
Onions: 1
Garlic: 1clove
Extra virgin olive oil: 4spoonfuls
Vegetable broth: 1mestolo
Thyme: to taste
Basil: 1tuft
Parsley: 1tuft
Fine salt: to taste
Black pepper: to taste

info:
Difficulty: Easy
Prep time: 10 min
Cook time: 30 min
Serving: 4
Cost: Low

instructions:
Fusilloni with peas, shrimp, and cherry tomatoes is a simple and tasty first course, dressed with a flavorful sauce that is prepared in just a few minutes. Great both hot and warm, fusilloni with peas, shrimp, and cherry tomatoes can be enjoyed both in summer and during the cold season.
Finely chop the onion and garlic, then heat the four tablespoons of oil in a non-stick pan and then sauté the garlic and onion . When the onion is golden, add the shelled peas , let them cook for a few minutes, and then add a ladle of broth .
Meanwhile, as the broth evaporates, chop the tomatoes into pieces, then add them to the pan and also pour in the shrimp and let everything cook for about ten minutes. Turn off the heat, adjust the salt and pepper, and add the chopped herbs .
In the meantime, bring salted water to a boil, and add the fusilloni . Drain the pasta when it's al dente , then pour it into the pan, mix everything well, and serve. Enjoy your meal!
You can prepare this dish with any pasta shape you prefer. You can replace the peas with fava beans.
