name:
Barley and Salmon Salad
ingredients:
Pearl barley: 1 Â¼cup(240 g)
Salmon steaks: 10.6oz(300 g)
Avocado: 5.3oz(150 g)
Fennel: 3.2oz(90 g)- with fronds
Spinach: 0.8cup(25 g)
Extra virgin olive oil: to taste
Fine salt: to taste
Black pepper: to taste
Greek yogurt: 1cup(250 g)
Extra virgin olive oil: 0.7tbsp(10 g)
Oranges: 1

info:
Difficulty: Easy
Prep time: 15 min
Cook time: 25 min
Serving: 4
Cost: Average

instructions:
To prepare the barley and salmon salad, first rinse the barley under running water , then cook it in boiling water for 20 minutes or as indicated on the package. While the barley is cooking, prepare the accompanying sauce: place the yogurt in a bowl, season with the grated zest of an orange
and add the juice as well , salt , and pepper. Remove the fronds from the fennel
and chop them finely , then slice the fennel and set aside. Flavor the yogurt sauce with the fennel fronds
and olive oil , mix and set aside. Meanwhile, the barley will have finished cooking, drain it and let it cool in a bowl . Once the barley is cool, add the washed and dried baby spinach and the sliced fennel .
Now prepare the salmon: remove the bones with tweezers , then heat the olive oil in a pan , place the salmon fillets, salt, pepper , and cook them for 2 minutes on one side and 1 minute on the other.
Once cooked , transfer the salmon to a plate and cut it into small pieces . Add the salmon pieces to the barley salad .
Now handle the avocado: cut it in half (19-20), remove the pit, and score the flesh by making horizontal and vertical cuts :
this way you'll obtain squares to add to the preparation . One last touch of flavor is missing: the orange sauce prepared earlier . The barley and salmon salad is ready to be enjoyed .
The barley and salmon salad keeps for a maximum of one day in the refrigerator. Freezing is not recommended.
Want to add an extra touch of color and flavor to the dish? Flavor the cooking water with turmeric, and you'll get a pleasantly golden dish!
