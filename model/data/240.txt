name:
Very soft carrot cake
ingredients:
Carrots: 1 ½cup(250 g)- (to clean)
Type 00 flour: 1 ¼cup(150 g)
Potato starch: 0.4cup(50 g)
Brown sugar: ½cup(100 g)
Eggs: 2
Sunflower seed oil: 0.4cup(80 g)
Baking powder: 1.1tbsp(16 g)
Powdered turmeric: to taste
Greek yogurt: 0.6cup(150 g)
Powdered sugar: ¼cup(50 g)
Vanilla extract: 1tsp

info:
Difficulty: Easy
Prep time: 30 min
Cook time: 40 min
Serving: 6
Cost: Low

instructions:
To prepare the very soft carrot cake, first peel and grate the carrots using a coarse grater . In a bowl, place the eggs and brown sugar , then beat with an electric mixer .
When you have obtained a light and frothy mixture, add the grated carrots and turmeric , then mix again with the mixer .
Pour in the vegetable oil and mix. At this point, add the baking powder , corn starch, and flour that you have previously sifted .
Incorporate the powders with the mixer until you obtain a homogeneous mixture . Pour the batter into a 9-inch round pan lined with parchment paper .
Bake in a preheated static oven at 350°F for about 35-40 minutes . Check the baking with a toothpick, if it comes out clean, remove the cake from the oven and let it cool completely in the pan . Meanwhile, prepare the frosting: pour the powdered sugar into a bowl .
Add the Greek yogurt and vanilla extract , then mix with the electric mixer .
You should obtain a smooth and homogeneous cream . Once the cake has completely cooled, spread the frosting over the entire surface with the help of a spatula . Your very soft carrot cake is ready to be enjoyed !
The very soft carrot cake without frosting can be stored at room temperature for 2-3 days, covered with a dome. It can be frozen, whole or in portions.
The yogurt frosting can be stored separately in the refrigerator for 1-2 days.
Turmeric gives a nice golden color to the very soft carrot cake, but if you prefer, you can omit it.
