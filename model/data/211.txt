name:
Easy Tomato Soup
ingredients:
Filini pasta: 1 ½cup(250 g)
Pizzutelli tomatoes: 1 ¾cup(300 g)
Red spring onions: 2.8oz(80 g)- from Tropea
Oregano: to taste- fresh
Basil: to taste
Extra virgin olive oil: 2spoonfuls
Fine salt: to taste
Black pepper: to taste
Water: 3 ¼cups(750 g)

instructions:

To prepare the tomato soup, first wash the tomatoes. Then cut them into quarters and then into rather small cubes . Set aside and move on to the Tropea onion; remove the outermost leaves
and slice it . Heat a drizzle of oil in a saucepan, add the onion and 1/4 cup of water . Let the onions stew over moderate heat for about ten minutes,
then add the tomatoes , the fresh oregano leaves , the basil leaves and season with salt and pepper.
Add 7 oz of water and cook for about 15 minutes. Then pour in another 17.5 oz of water and bring to a boil, then add the pasta directly into the tomato soup and finish cooking the pasta. If you choose a shape like ours, 2 minutes of cooking will suffice. Scent with a few fresh oregano leaves ,
a few basil leaves , adjust the salt and mix everything . The tomato soup is ready to serve .
Consume the tomato soup immediately; otherwise, the pasta will become overcooked.
Freezing is not recommended.
Make the tomato soup using a mix of tomatoes from your land. Add Parmesan cheese and a chili pepper for a bolder taste!
