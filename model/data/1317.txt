name:
Cucumber and hummus finger food
ingredients:
Cucumbers: 1 ½cup(165 g)
Flatbread: 2- large
Fresh chili pepper: 0.35oz(10 g)
Wild fennel: to taste
Precooked chickpeas: ½cup(100 g)- (drained weight)
Tahini: 2tbsp(30 g)
Lemon juice: 2tsp(10 g)
Extra virgin olive oil: 2tsp(10 g)
Fine salt: to taste

info:
Difficulty: Very easy
Prep time: 15 min
Cook time: 5 min
Makes: 16 pieces
Cost: Low

instructions:
To prepare the cucumber and hummus finger foods, first cut out 16 discs from the flatbreads using a pastry cutter with a diameter of 2 inches . Heat a grill pan well and grill the discs for 2 minutes on one side and 1 minute on the other , then set them aside .
Cut the cucumber into slices 0.4 inches thick, leaving the skin on . Now move on to the preparation of the hummus: put the chickpeas in a blender with the tahini , then add the salt ,
the lemon juice , and the oil . Blend until you get a smooth and homogeneous cream .
Finally, finely chop the fresh chili pepper after removing the seeds . You are ready to assemble the finger foods: lay a cucumber slice on each flatbread disc , then add a teaspoon of hummus .
Lastly, garnish with the fresh chili pepper and a sprig of wild fennel . Your cucumber and hummus finger foods are ready to be enjoyed !
It is recommended to consume the cucumber and hummus finger foods immediately.
An appetizer as simple as it is versatile: vary the base as desired for a gluten-free version, or prepare a cream of robiola mixed with lemon zest and paprika as an alternative to hummus!
