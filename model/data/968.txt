name:
Ruby chocolate ganache
ingredients:
Ruby chocolate: 14.1oz(400 g)- (in pearls)
Fresh liquid cream: ¾cup(200 g)

instructions:
Ruby chocolate ganache is one of those desserts that instantly grabs your attention with its vivid pink color and creamy texture. These beans give the chocolate a natural tangy flavor that’s kind of like a mix between white chocolate and berries. The result? To prepare the ruby chocolate ganache, start by heating the cream until it almost boils . Meanwhile, place the ruby chocolate pearls or chopped chocolate in a large bowl . As soon as the cream is hot, pour it over the chocolate .
Stir immediately to melt the ruby chocolate , and when the ingredients are completely combined, your ruby chocolate ganache is ready to use . If you prefer a whipped effect, simply cover the ganache with plastic wrap in contact with the surface and, after at least 2 hours, stir vigorously with a spoon before using .
Ruby chocolate ganache can be stored for one day outside the refrigerator if it's not too hot, or in the refrigerator well covered for 2-3 days.
You can also whip the ruby chocolate ganache with an electric mixer, but only for a few moments.
Once ready, it can be used to fill tartlets or pastry shells by pouring it warm inside: let it rest and whip it for your preferred decorations!
