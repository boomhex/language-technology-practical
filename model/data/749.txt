name:
Congee
ingredients:
Glutinous rice: ½cup(100 g)
Water: 3.3cups(800 ml)
Sugar: to taste
Honey: to taste
Strawberries: to taste
Goji berries: to taste
Black sesame seeds: to taste
Soy sauce: to taste
Fresh scallion: to taste
Peanuts: to taste- chopped

To prepare the congee, start with the glutinous rice , rinse it under running water twice. Pour the water into a pot, place on the heat, and bring to a boil, then add the rice and let it cook over low heat for about 45-50 minutes .
Stir occasionally to prevent sticking to the bottom . At the end of cooking, when the rice is thick and creamy , pour it into two separate bowls .
In one bowl, prepare the sweet congee: season with honey , sugar, black sesame seeds , and goji berries .
Finish with sliced strawberries , the sweet congee is ready . Take care of the savory version: season with soy sauce .
Garnish with crushed peanuts and slices of fresh scallion . The savory congee is also ready to be served .
We recommend consuming the congee immediately.
Accompany the congee with steamed buns for a rich and hearty breakfast.
