name:
Beetroot gnocchetti with cherry tomatoes and stracciatella
ingredients:
Precooked beets: 1 ½cup(200 g)
Type 00 flour: 2.4cups(300 g)
Grana Padano PDO cheese: 1.8oz(50 g)
Eggs: 1
Stracciatella cheese: to taste
Fine salt: to taste
Basil: to taste
Cherry tomatoes: 1.3cups(200 g)
Sugar: to taste
Oregano: to taste
Extra virgin olive oil: to taste
Black pepper: to taste

info:
Difficulty: Difficult
Prep time: 1 h 30 min
Cook time: 10 min
Serving: 4
Cost: Average
Note + 1 hour of cooking for the confit cherry tomatoes

instructions:
To prepare the Beetroot gnocchetti with cherry tomatoes and stracciatella, wash the cherry tomatoes, dry them, and cut them in half . Place them on a baking sheet covered with parchment paper, sprinkle with sugar , add salt, pepper, and oil .
Fragrance with oregano and bake in a preheated static oven for about 1 hour at 320°F. Put the pre-cooked beets in a mixer and add salt .
Blend until you get a smooth mixture . Pour it into a bowl and add part of the all-purpose flour and the Grana Padano PDO .
Finally, add the egg . Work the dough for 5 minutes, adding the remaining flour , and then form a loaf .
Divide the loaf in two , create small rolls , and with the help of a sharp knife, cut pieces of about 3/4 inch .
Place them on a floured tray and bring a pot filled with water to a boil. Salt and cook the gnocchetti . Once they rise to the surface, drain them into a bowl .

Meanwhile, the confit tomatoes will be ready . Dress the gnocchetti with oil and add the confit cherry tomatoes .
Fragrance with fresh basil and salt. Finally, add the stracciatella and serve the Beetroot gnocchetti with cherry tomatoes and stracciatella hot .
The Beetroot gnocchetti with cherry tomatoes and stracciatella can be stored for one day in the fridge. You can freeze the uncooked gnocchi and then cook them directly from frozen.
The flour is added in two stages to better control the dough's consistency.
