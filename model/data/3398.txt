name:
Galician-style Octopus (pulpo a la gallega)
ingredients:
Octopus: 2.6lbs(1.2 kg)- already cleaned
Potatoes: 1.3lbs(600 g)
Onions: 1
Celery: 1rib
Carrots: 1
Bay leaves: 2leaves
Parsley: 1oz(30 g)- to chop
Coarse salt: to taste
Extra virgin olive oil: to taste
Smoked paprika: 1tsp

info:
Difficulty: Easy
Prep time: 15 min
Cook time: 60 min
Serving: 4
Cost: Low

instructions:
To prepare Galician-style octopus, first cut the celery and carrots into pieces. Cut the onion in half .
Thoroughly wash the octopus, then clean it by emptying the head and removing the eyes and the central beak . In a large pot, pour water, add the carrots , celery, and onion .
Add two bay leaves and bring to a boil. Then dip the octopus and lift it 3-4 times or until the tentacles curl. Then immerse it completely; cover with a lid and cook for 40-50 minutes until the octopus is tender.
Drain the octopus and vegetables and let them cool completely. Meanwhile, peel the potatoes and cut them into half-inch slices .
Boil the potatoes in the octopus cooking water until they become soft but firm. Meanwhile, cut the octopus into chunks . Drain the potatoes .
Plate the dish by placing the potatoes as the base , add a pinch of salt, then place the octopus pieces on the potatoes . Add chopped parsley .
Finish with a drizzle of oil and sprinkle with sweet paprika . The Galician-style octopus is ready to be enjoyed .
Consume the Galician-style octopus immediately. If there are leftovers, you can store them in the refrigerator in a closed container for up to 2 days.
If you want to add a citrus note, you can add some grated lemon or orange zest. If you love spiciness, you can replace the sweet paprika with hot paprika.
