name:
Chickpea Soup in the Microwave
ingredients:
Dried chickpeas: 1.67cups(300 g)
Leeks: 1
Carrots: 1
Celery: 1rib
Rosemary: 1sprig
Thyme: 1sprig
Bay leaves: 1foglio
Vegetable broth: 4 ¼cups(1 l)
Extra virgin olive oil: 3spoonfuls
Salt: to taste
Black pepper: to taste

To prepare the chickpea soup in the microwave, first soak the chickpeas with a pinch of baking soda for 12 hours. Remove the outer leaves of the leek and slice it finely into rings .
Peel a carrot and first cut it into sticks and then into cubes .
Peel the celery to remove the strings , and also cut it into cubes . Drain the chickpeas and transfer them to a large microwave-safe bowl.
Add the leeks, carrot, celery, bay leaf , and an aromatic bundle , made of a sprig of rosemary and thyme tied together with kitchen twine. Season with salt .
Add the pepper , the oil , and the vegetable broth .
Stir everything together and cover with plastic wrap . Cook in the microwave at 650 W for 45 minutes. Remove the bay leaf and the aromatic bundle and serve. Your chickpea soup in the microwave is ready to be served !
You can store the chickpea soup in the refrigerator, in a closed container, for up to 2 days. If there's any leftover, you can freeze the soup after cooking.
If you prefer a creamier soup, you can blend part of the chickpeas before serving the dish.
You can add your preferred spices, for example, a pinch of curry, a tablespoon of paprika, or if you like it spicy, a chili pepper.
You can garnish the dish with chopped parsley to add a touch of freshness.
Serve the soup accompanied by croutons or lightly toasted bread.
