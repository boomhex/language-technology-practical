Baked Breadsticks with Ham and Emmental

To prepare the baked breadsticks with ham and Emmental, start by cutting 12 thin slices of Emmental , then place a slice of cooked ham on a cutting board and cut it in half widthwise . Then take a tomato-flavored breadstick and wrap the Emmental slice around it , positioning it in the center of the tomato-flavored breadstick, leaving both ends free.
Roll up the cooked ham slice in the same way . Arrange the decorated breadsticks in a baking dish lined with parchment paper . Bake in a preheated static oven at 375°F (320°F in a convection oven) for five minutes until the cheese melts .
The baked breadsticks with ham and Emmental should be consumed immediately to enjoy them warm and crispy.
You can replace the cooked ham with speck or prosciutto, for example, creating the combinations you like best!
