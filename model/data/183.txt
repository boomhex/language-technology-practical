name:
Cream Cake
ingredients:
Fresh liquid cream: ¾cup(200 g)
Eggs: 3
Type 00 flour: 1.6cups(200 g)
Sugar: 0.6cup(120 g)
Lemon peel: 1
Baking powder: 3 ½tsp(16 g)
Salt: 1pinch
Powdered sugar: to taste

info:
Difficulty: Easy
Prep time: 15 min
Cook time: 45 min
Serving: 6
Cost: Low

instructions:
To prepare the cream cake, start by pouring the whole eggs and sugar into a large bowl . Then beat with electric whisks until the eggs are pale, fluffy, and frothy .
Next, add the fresh cream and continue to beat with the electric whisks to combine the mixture .
Continue by adding the grated zest of one lemon , keep mixing , and add the pinch of salt .
Now you can add the flour, making sure to sift it with a sieve . Once well combined , the last step is to add the sifted baking powder .
At this point, continue to mix with the electric whisks until you obtain a creamy and homogeneous mixture . Pour the mixture into a springform pan of 7-8 inches lined with parchment paper or greased and floured . Bake in a preheated static oven at 350°F in the middle rack for about 45 minutes. Times may vary depending on the oven, check the baking with a toothpick.
The cream cake is ready, take it out of the oven and let it cool down . Once cool, transfer it to a plate and serve it with a dusting of powdered sugar .
The cream cake can be stored for 3 or 4 days at room temperature.
It can also be frozen in portions.

The cream cake can be prepared with the addition of chocolate chips or chunks, and the lemon zest can be replaced with the zest of an orange.
The cream cake can be served with dollops of whipped cream or ice cream balls of choice.
