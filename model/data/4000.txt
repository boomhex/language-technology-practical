name:
Pappardelle with Truffle and Mascarpone Cream
ingredients:
Egg pappardelle pasta: 8.8oz(250 g)
Black truffle: 1.5oz(40 g)
Mascarpone cheese: 1cup(250 g)
Anchovies: 5- fillets in oil
Extra virgin olive oil: 2spoonfuls
Fine salt: to taste
Garlic: 1clove

info:
Difficulty: Easy
Prep time: 10 min
Cook time: 15 min
Serving: 4
Cost: High

instructions:
Pappardelle with truffle and mascarpone cream is a delicious pasta dish, perfect for special occasions. The pappardelle are enhanced by the king of mushrooms: truffle, which is sliced into thin flakes and added to the mascarpone cream. The fruity and spicy aroma of the truffle pairs well with the creamy and delicate flavor of the mascarpone, creating a refined yet simple and quick dish to prepare.
Also try:

To prepare the pappardelle with truffle and mascarpone cream, gently saut√© a clove of garlic in a little extra virgin olive oil over low heat, and when it is golden, remove it (1-2). Add the anchovy fillets
and let them dissolve well , then add the mascarpone and stir to combine all the ingredients . Simmer over low heat for a few minutes and adjust the salt. In a large pot with plenty of salted water, cook the pappardelle
and drain them al dente . Add the pasta to the mascarpone cream and toss for a few moments in the pan . Garnish with thin slices of black truffle and mix well to combine everything . Serve the pappardelle with truffle and mascarpone cream hot, garnishing the plates with freshly grated truffle flakes.
It is recommended to eat the pappardelle with truffle and mascarpone cream immediately.
If your truffle is not already cleaned, simply brush it with a semi-hard brush to remove dirt and impurities from the surface. If you wish, you can use another type of pasta; maltagliati, fettuccine, or linguine are particularly suitable.
