name:
Pasta with robiola cream with shrimp and zucchini
ingredients:
Linguine pasta: 11.3oz(320 g)
Zucchini: 2 ½cups(300 g)
Shrimps: 5.3oz(150 g)
Robiola cheese: 7.1oz(200 g)
Extra virgin olive oil: 2tbsp(30 g)
Marjoram: to taste
Fine salt: to taste
Black pepper: to taste
Robiola cheese: to taste

To prepare the pasta with robiola cream with shrimp and zucchini, wash, trim, and peel the zucchini, recovering only the green outer part . Place the strips on a baking sheet and season with oil and salt , bake in a preheated static oven at 392°F for 5 minutes .
Cut the remaining part of the zucchini into pieces , place in a mixer, and add 2 tablespoons of oil and salt , blend until you get a smooth cream and set aside.
Shell the shrimp and cut them into pieces about 3/4 inch . Heat a pot of water and salt it when it boils, it will be used to cook the pasta. In a pan, pour a drizzle of extra virgin olive oil and add the shrimp . Cook for 2 minutes over high heat and salt. Turn off the heat , transfer the shrimp to a bowl, and set aside.
Pour the zucchini cream into the pan used to cook the shrimp . Cook the linguine in boiling water for 10 minutes . Pour 3.5 oz of robiola into a bowl, season with oil .
Stir to combine and obtain a cream . Drain the pasta into the pan with the zucchini cream . Add a ladle of cooking water to combine and toss for a couple of minutes.
Add the shrimp and stir with the heat off. Pour a layer of robiola cream on the plate and then distribute a portion of pasta on top, garnish with the zucchini and the remaining fresh robiola. The Pasta with robiola cream with shrimp and zucchini is ready !

We recommend consuming the Pasta with robiola cream with shrimp and zucchini immediately.
Instead of marjoram, you can use mint to give the dish a fresh note.
