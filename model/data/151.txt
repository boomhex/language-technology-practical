name:
Clafoutis with figs
ingredients:
Figs: 1.8lbs(800 g)- ripe
Sugar: 1 ½cup(300 g)
Type 00 flour: 1.6cups(200 g)
Almond flour: ½cup(50 g)
Potato starch: 6tbsp(50 g)
Eggs: 5.8oz(165 g)- (about 3 medium)
Fresh liquid cream: 1 ¼cup(300 g)
Rum: 2oz(60 g)
Vanilla extract: 1tsp
Powdered sugar: to taste

info:
Difficulty: Easy
Prep time: 25 min
Cook time: 55 min
Serving: 8
Cost: Low

instructions:
To prepare the fig clafoutis, first crack the eggs into a large bowl, then add the vanilla extract and sugar . Beat with an electric mixer until the mixture is light and frothy .
Pour in the rum and cream and mix with a hand whisk .
Finally, add the cornstarch , almond flour , and all-purpose flour .
Incorporate the dry ingredients with a whisk and set the batter aside for the moment. Move on to the figs: peel them and chop them coarsely .
Line an 11-inch mold with parchment paper, then distribute the figs on the base and level them with the back of a spoon . Finally, pour the batter over the layer of figs .
Bake in a preheated static oven at 356°F for about 55 minutes . Once golden on the surface, remove the clafoutis from the oven and let it cool slightly, then dust with powdered sugar to taste . Your fig clafoutis is ready to be enjoyed while still warm !
The fig clafoutis can be stored in the refrigerator for 2 days.
You can freeze it and let it thaw at room temperature before enjoying.
Instead of rum, you can use cognac or brandy.
If desired, you can flavor the batter with a pinch of cinnamon!
