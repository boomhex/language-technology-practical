name:
Seared Tuna with Winter Ratatouille
ingredients:
Tuna: 1.8lbs(800 g)- steak
Extra virgin olive oil: to taste
Salt: to taste
Purple cabbage: 2 ½cups(200 g)- (to be cleaned)
Thyme: 2sprigs
Water: to taste
Pumpkin: 1lb(200 g)- (to clean)
Italian kale (cavolo nero): 2 ½cups(200 g)- (to be cleaned)
Garlic: 1clove
Thyme: 1sprig
Cauliflower: 3 ½cups(350 g)- (to clean)
Black pepper: to taste

info:
Difficulty: Average
Prep time: 30 min
Cook time: 60 min
Serving: 4
Cost: Average

instructions:
Seared tuna with winter ratatouille is a refined second course of fish, ideal for impressing your guests with an explosion of colors and flavors. The fresh tuna, briefly seared to preserve its juiciness, pairs perfectly with the richness of the special version ratatouille , prepared with seasonal vegetables such as pumpkin, kale, and purple cabbage. The cauliflower cream, soft and velvety, contrasts with the other textures while the final sprinkle of bottarga adds a salty touch and a unique aroma. Seared tuna with winter ratatouille is a dish to be served on a special occasion, like an elegant dinner or an important event, perfect for delighting gourmet cuisine lovers.

To prepare the seared tuna with winter ratatouille, start with the purple cabbage: julienne the cabbage and place it in a pot. Add the thyme and cover with water .
Let the cabbage boil for 30 minutes , then drain and set aside. Reduce the cooking liquid until you obtain a thick sauce , which will take about 30 minutes at a steady boil.
Clean the kale by removing the central rib , then julienne it . Cut the pumpkin into cubes .
In a pan, heat the oil with the garlic clove and thyme , then add the pumpkin and cook for 8 minutes. Add the kale .
Adjust with salt and sauté everything for 5 minutes. Finally, add the drained purple cabbage , mix the ingredients and set aside. Now prepare the cauliflower cream: clean the cauliflower, leaving only the florets .
In a pan, heat the oil, then add the cauliflower and brown for a few minutes. Season with salt and pepper , then pour in a ladle of water .
Cover with a lid and cook for about 20 minutes; if it dries too much, you can add more water. Transfer everything to a jug and blend with an immersion blender until you obtain a smooth and homogeneous cream . Set aside.
Finally, focus on the tuna: salt the fillet on both sides . In a pan, heat a drizzle of oil, add the tuna , and sear it for 2 minutes on each side .
Slice the seared tuna . You are ready to plate: distribute the cauliflower cream on the bottom of the plate , then add the vegetables .
Place the tuna slices on the vegetables , then garnish with the purple cabbage sauce. Finish with a drizzle of oil and a sprinkle of bottarga . The seared tuna with winter ratatouille is ready to be served !
It is recommended to consume the seared tuna with winter ratatouille as soon as it is ready.
You can prepare the winter ratatouille and the cauliflower cream in advance and store them in the refrigerator for up to 2 days.
If you cannot find kale or pumpkin, you can use other winter vegetables such as fennel, artichokes, or Jerusalem artichokes, maintaining the seasonality concept.
The cauliflower cream can be replaced with potato or parsnip cream for a sweeter and more delicate flavor.
For an even more intense flavor, you can marinate the tuna for 15-20 minutes with oil, lemon, ginger, and a pinch of pepper.
