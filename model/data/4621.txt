name:
Air Fryer Baked Apples
ingredients:
Golden delicious apples: 4
Raisins: ½cup(60 g)
Peeled almonds: 1.5oz(40 g)
Pecans: 1.5oz(40 g)
Sugar: 5tsp(20 g)
Lemon juice: 1
Cinnamon powder: 1tsp

info:
Difficulty: Very easy
Prep time: 10 min
Cook time: 20 min
Serving: 4
Cost: Low

instructions:
Air fryer baked apples are a quick and smart version of the classic grandma's recipe, a great alternative to stovetop baked apples and microwave baked apples . In this version, we have enhanced the apples with nuts, lemon juice, sugar, and cinnamon: a fragrant and enveloping mix that will give you a rustic and genuine dessert, perfect for the whole family. You can enjoy the air fryer baked apples with a scoop of ice cream or use them as a tasty filling for strudels, tarts, and puff pastries!
Discover how simple it can be to bring this apple dessert to the table and many other delicious recipes with the help of the air fryer:

To prepare the air fryer apples, first peel the apples , then cut them in half and remove the core. Cut the apple halves into more wedges , then cut them in half crosswise .
Coarsely chop the pecans and almonds . In a bowl, combine the apples and the chopped nuts .
Add the sugar , raisins , and ground cinnamon .
Drizzle with lemon juice and mix well to season .
Transfer the apples to the air fryer basket and bake at 392°F (200°C) for 20 minutes . Let cool slightly and serve your air fryer baked apples !
You can store the air fryer baked apples in the refrigerator for a couple of days in an airtight container.
Air fryer baked apples can be prepared in many ways, depending on taste: you can cook them with water, apple juice, or wine for a richer flavor, replacing the sugar with honey or maple syrup. Spices like ginger, vanilla, or citrus zest make them even more fragrant!
