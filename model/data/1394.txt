name:
Chocolate chip loaf cake
ingredients:
Type 00 flour: 2cups(250 g)
Eggs: 5.8oz(165 g)- (about 3 medium) at room temperature
Brown sugar: ¾cup(160 g)
Butter: 3.5oz(100 g)
Fresh liquid cream: ½cup(100 g)- at room temperature
Dark chocolate chips: ¾cup(150 g)
Baking powder: 2.7tsp(12 g)
Orange peel: ½
Fine salt: 1pinch
Butter: to taste
Type 00 flour: to taste

info:
Difficulty: Very easy
Prep time: 10 min
Cook time: 50 min
Serving: 6
Cost: Low

instructions:
To prepare the chocolate chip loaf cake, first melt the butter in a small saucepan and let it cool. Meanwhile, pour the eggs into a large bowl, add the sugar , salt , and the grated zest of half an orange .
Whisk to combine the ingredients , then add the cooled melted butter and the cream , and continue to mix.
Now sift the flour and baking powder into the bowl and incorporate them with the whisk until you have a smooth and homogeneous mixture . Finally, add the chocolate chips and stir to incorporate the powders evenly.
Butter and flour a loaf pan measuring 10x4.3 inches and pour the batter inside . Bake in a preheated static oven at 356°F for 50 minutes, positioning the pan on the middle rack. Before taking it out, do the toothpick test, then let it cool. Your chocolate chip loaf cake is ready to be enjoyed !
The chocolate chip loaf cake can be stored at room temperature for about 2 days, covered with a glass dome. It can be frozen after baking and cooling, possibly already portioned.
You can flavor your loaf cake as you like, replacing the orange zest with lemon zest or with the seeds of a vanilla bean.
