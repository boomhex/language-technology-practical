name:
Aromatic Fried Rabbit
ingredients:
Rabbit: 1.5lbs(700 g)- in pieces
Eggs: 3- medium
Lemons: 3
Sage: 2sprigs
Rosemary: 2sprigs
Oregano: 1spoonful
Breadcrumbs: 1.67cups(200 g)
Type 00 flour: 1.6cups(200 g)
Fine salt: to taste
Black pepper: to taste
Peanut seed oil: to taste

info:
Difficulty: Easy
Prep time: 15 min
Cook time: 25 min
Serving: 4
Cost: Average

instructions:

To prepare the aromatic fried rabbit, start by chopping the rosemary and sage . Add the chop into the bowl with the rabbit pieces and also add the oregano , then season with salt .
Squeeze the lemons and pour the juice over the rabbit . Stir to flavor and cover with plastic wrap ; refrigerate for a couple of hours.
Crack the eggs into a large bowl and beat them with a fork. Salt . In another bowl, pour breadcrumbs and flour , then mix the powders .
Take the rabbit pieces and dip them in the eggs, drained from the marinade . Coat each piece of rabbit in the mixture of breadcrumbs and flour (11-12).
Heat plenty of oil in a large pan and fry the rabbit pieces until golden on all sides (14-15). Drain them and place them on a plate with kitchen paper to absorb excess oil . Serve the rabbit pieces with herbs while still hot, accompanied by lemon slices or sauces of your choice!
You can use panko instead of breadcrumbs for an even more golden and crispy breading!
