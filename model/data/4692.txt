name:
Baked Carrots
ingredients:
Carrots: 4cups(500 g)
Rosemary: to taste
Extra virgin olive oil: to taste
Salt: to taste
Black pepper: to taste

info:
Difficulty: Very easy
Prep time: 10 min
Cook time: 40 min
Serving: 4
Cost: Very low

instructions:
Baked carrots are a simple and versatile side dish that can complement a wide variety of main courses! They are a great choice when you want to serve something light yet tasty without spending too much time in the kitchen. Carrots are great to eat raw, boiled, or cooked in a pan , but oven baking gives them a perfect texture: slightly crispy outside and soft inside. You can also customize the recipe to your liking by playing with spices and herbs to create different accompaniments for your dishes each time. If you're looking for a side dish that requires little effort, baked carrots are definitely for you!

To prepare the baked carrots, first wash them thoroughly, then dry them with a clean dish towel . Trim the ends and slice them in half lengthwise .
Shorten the length by cutting them in half and gather them in a bowl. Season with oil , salt, and pepper .
Massage the sticks to coat them evenly. Place the carrots on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper, making sure not to overlap them, add some sprigs of rosemary , and bake in a preheated static oven at 430Â°F for 40 minutes. Once cooked, garnish with more fresh rosemary. Your baked carrots are ready to be served !
Baked carrots can be stored in the refrigerator, in a sealed container, for 3 days. Before serving, you can reheat them in the oven at a low temperature or in the microwave to maintain their texture.
They can be frozen.
For an impressive side dish use colored carrots: orange, white, yellow, and purple will make this basic recipe more refined and original!
To make sweet and sour carrots , you can add a drizzle of honey or maple syrup at the end of cooking.
