name:
Black olive pâté
ingredients:
Black olives: 1.67cups(200 g)
Extra virgin olive oil: 3 ¼tbsp(45 g)

info:
Difficulty: Easy
Prep time: 15 min
Serving: 6
Cost: Low

instructions:
To prepare the olive pâté, start by pitting the olives one by one , place them in a blender , and blend until you get a smooth mixture .
Add the extra virgin olive oil in a thin stream until you obtain a smooth and sufficiently compact cream . Your pâté is ready to be served. You can enjoy it on crunchy crostini: to prepare them, simply slice the bread, place it on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper, drizzle with a little oil, and toast it in the oven on grill mode for 2 minutes, turning it halfway through to toast it evenly. You can spread the black olive pâté on hot bread with a drizzle of oil cooked in the oven until it turns golden brown .
It can be stored in the refrigerator for 14 days covered with oil. Freezing is not recommended.
Depending on your taste, you can add anchovies in oil and/or capers to this delicious black olive pâté.
