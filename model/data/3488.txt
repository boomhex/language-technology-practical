name:
Mint-scented grains
ingredients:
Wheat: 2 ⅓cups(280 g)
Zucchini: 1 ½cup(250 g)
Tomatoes: 2cups(350 g)- (3 medium)
Mint: to taste-
Anchovies in oil: 5-
Extra virgin olive oil: 3spoonfuls-
Fine salt: to taste
Black pepper: to taste
Shrimp paste: 7oz(200 g)

info:
Difficulty: Very easy
Prep time: 15 min
Cook time: 20 min
Serving: 4
Cost: Low

To prepare the mint-scented grains, start by washing the zucchini, removing the ends, and cutting them into small cubes; also wash the tomatoes, cut them in half, remove the seeds with a teaspoon, and cut them into small cubes as well. Finely chop the anchovy fillets and let them dissolve in 2 tablespoons of oil in a non-stick pan . Add the zucchini and cook them over high heat for a few minutes, then add the shrimp that you have boiled in hot water and let them flavor with the zucchini.
Meanwhile, cook the grains in plenty of salted boiling water, following the package cooking instructions (usually for 8/10 minutes). Drain the grains, let them cool slightly, add the mix of zucchini and shrimp, the diced tomatoes , the remaining tablespoon of oil, adjust salt and pepper, and mix well to flavor the ingredients. Finally, incorporate plenty of finely chopped mint . Serve the mint-scented grains cold or still warm, garnished with whole mint leaves.
