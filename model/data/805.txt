name:
Raspberry and Chocolate Popsicles
ingredients:
Vanilla-flavored plant-based yogurt: 4cups(1 kg)
Raspberries: 1cup(125 g)
Vegan white chocolate: 14oz(400 g)
Powdered sugar: 2spoonfuls
Dried raspberries: to taste

instructions:

To prepare the raspberry and chocolate popsicles, pour the yogurt into a clean cloth and let it drain for about a night or at least 6-7 hours. After this time , transfer the yogurt to a bowl .
Add the powdered sugar to sweeten and stir . Separately, coarsely mash the raspberries .
Add the raspberries to the yogurt and mix . Pour the mixture into the appropriate ice cream molds .
Insert the stick and let it set in the freezer for at least 3 hours . Once the ice creams are set , prepare the coating: melt the white chocolate in a bain-marie .
Once melted , transfer the chocolate to a tall and narrow cup . Dip the ice creams .
Drain them and cover them with the dehydrated raspberries . Place them on a tray lined with parchment paper and wait for the coating to fully solidify. The raspberry and chocolate popsicles are ready to be enjoyed .
The raspberry and chocolate popsicles can be stored in the freezer for 5 days.
As an alternative to raspberries, you can use other fresh fruits like strawberries or peaches.
If you can't find dehydrated raspberries, you can replace them with chopped hazelnuts or pistachios.
Adjust the amount of sugar based on the sweetness of the yogurt you are using: if you wish, you can also omit it or replace it with agave syrup.
