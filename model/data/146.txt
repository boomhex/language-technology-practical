name:
Marinated croaker
ingredients:
Croaker: 1fillet- chilled
Lemons: 1
Lime peel: 1
Orange peel: ½
Extra virgin olive oil: to taste
Wild fennel: to taste
Green apples: 8.8oz(250 g)
Celery: 2ribs
Ice: to taste
Fennel: 2 ¼cups(200 g)
Fresh ginger: 1.1oz(30 g)
Fine salt: to taste
Sunflower seed oil: 1cup(200 g)
Chives: 2tbsp(30 g)
Celery: to taste- leaves
Leeks: 1oz(30 g)- leaves
Dill: 6tbsp(30 g)
Red onions: to taste- in sweet and sour
Dill: to taste
Maldon salt: to taste
Black pepper: to taste

To prepare the marinated croaker with citrus and green apple extract, start by cutting the croaker fillet into strips and then into small cubes . Transfer the fish to a bowl and season with lemon zest .
Orange zest and lime . Scent with fennel fronds and season with oil .
Mix, cover with plastic wrap and place in the fridge while you prepare the rest. Prepare the green oil: blanch the dill with a small bunch of chives for 15 seconds. Drain .
Then immediately immerse in ice water to keep the color vibrant , set aside. Do the same with the leaves of the two celery stalks, blanch in the same water , drain ,
immerse in the bowl with ice water , then set aside. Lastly, peel the leek and blanch the leaves for 1 minute. Again, drain
Pass them in ice water too . Gather all the blanched herbs, squeeze them, and place them in a pitcher , add the seed oil .
Blend with an immersion blender , then transfer the sauce to a saucepan and heat it for 2-3 minutes, until a film forms on the surface, then strain it through a sieve and a paper towel .
Collect the oil and set it aside . Now for the extract: clean and cut the fennel into pieces , do the same with the celery .
Cut the apple in half, remove the core, and cut it into chunks with the peel . Lastly, peel and slice the fresh ginger . Prepare the pitcher with ice to prevent oxidation.
Place the cut fruit and vegetables in the extractor and collect the juice in the pitcher . Season with salt and set aside.
Take the marinated croaker, season it with lime juice and salt, then plate it garnishing with Maldon salt flakes, pepper, dill , and sweet and sour red onions .
Complete by pouring the apple extract and the green oil . Serve your seafood appetizer immediately .
The marinated croaker with citrus and green apple extract should be consumed immediately.
You can prepare the green oil in advance and store it in the fridge.
If you don't have an extractor, you can blend the apple and vegetables and then strain them through a sieve.
Before consuming raw, marinated, or not perfectly cooked fish, it is recommended to freeze it for at least 96 hours at -0.4 degrees in a domestic freezer marked with 3 or more stars, according to the health ministry guidelines .
