name:
Chocolate and tahini treats
ingredients:
Dark chocolate: 7.1oz(200 g)
Tahini: 8tbsp(120 g)
Honey: 2spoonfuls
Green Peperoncino: to taste
Unsweetened cocoa powder: to taste

info:
Difficulty: Easy
Prep time: 10 min
Cook time: 10 min
Makes: 24 pieces
Cost: Low
Note + chilling time in the fridge (2 h)

instructions:
To prepare the chocolate and tahini treats, first chop the dark chocolate and melt it in a double boiler with the tahini and honey . Stir until all the chocolate is melted and you have a homogeneous mixture.
Add the chili powder and stir again . Pour everything into an 8x6 inch baking pan lined with parchment paper . Level it and let it set in the refrigerator until it becomes solid; it will take about 2 hours.
After the setting time, cut the slab into regular cubes , then roll them in cocoa powder . Your chocolate and tahini treats are ready to be served !
Chocolate and tahini treats can be stored in the refrigerator, in an airtight container, for up to 2 weeks.
If you wish, you can replace the dark chocolate with white chocolate for a sweeter and creamier result.
In addition to chili, you can add cinnamon, ginger, or cardamom for an exotic touch!
