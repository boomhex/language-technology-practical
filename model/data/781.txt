name:
Risotto with Zucchini and Stracchino
ingredients:
Carnaroli rice: 1 ½cup(320 g)
Zucchini: 4 ¼cups(500 g)
Stracchino cheese: 3.5oz(100 g)
Butter: 2.8tbsp(40 g)
Vegetable broth: to taste
Fine salt: to taste
Black pepper: to taste

info:
Difficulty: Very easy
Prep time: 25 min
Cook time: 20 min
Serving: 4
Cost: Low

instructions:
To prepare the zucchini and stracchino risotto, prepare the classic vegetable broth and keep it warm. Trim the zucchini and grate all except one. From this last one, extract the green part to cut into sticks . Cut the white parts into pieces and, together with the trimmed parts, pour them into the broth .
In the meantime, melt the butter in a saucepan , add the rice and let it toast for a couple of minutes, stirring often . Start cooking the rice by adding the hot broth gradually: the rice should always be covered with broth .
After a few minutes, add the grated zucchini and continue to cook the rice, adding ladles of broth as needed . Let them absorb, stirring occasionally. When you're almost done cooking, adjust the salt in the rice and add the zucchini skin cut into matchsticks .
At the end of cooking, turn off the heat and stir in the stracchino until it becomes creamy . Plate and garnish with a pinch of black pepper . Your zucchini and stracchino risotto is ready, enjoy your meal .
It is recommended to consume the zucchini and stracchino risotto immediately to maintain its texture, but you can store it in the fridge for up to 1-2 days.
Freezing is not recommended.
Do you want to add a nice touch of freshness to your zucchini and stracchino risotto? Grate a tiny amount of lemon zest directly onto the plates!
