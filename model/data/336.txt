name:
Pasta potatoes and scampi
ingredients:
Mixed Pasta: 11.3oz(320 g)
Vegetable broth: 2cups(500 g)
Parsley: to taste
Lemon peel: to taste
White pepper: to taste
Extra virgin olive oil: to taste
Potatoes: 0.7lb(300 g)
Shallot: 1
Lemon juice: 2tbsp(30 g)
Extra virgin olive oil: 1 ½tbsp(20 g)
Fine salt: to taste
Fresh liquid cream: ¼cup(60 g)
Caciocavallo cheese: 1oz(25 g)- (to grate)
Colatura di alici (fishsauce): 1tsp
Black pepper: to taste
Scampi: 6
Colatura di alici (fishsauce): to taste
Lemon juice: to taste
Lime peel: to taste

instructions:

To prepare pasta with potatoes and scampi, first make the Vegetable broth following our recipe and keep it warm. Proceed to How to clean shrimp : remove the head , eliminate the shell and the vein, then finely chop the flesh . Transfer them to a bowl and let them rest in the refrigerator until needed.
Now focus on the cheese, pepper, and anchovy sauce: pour the cream into a small saucepan, then add the black pepper , grated caciocavallo cheese , and anchovy sauce .
Heat gently while stirring with a whisk until you obtain a smooth and velvety mixture . Set aside and work on the potato cream: peel and chop the shallot .
Peel the potatoes and cut them first into slices, then into sticks, and finally into small cubes . In a pot, heat a drizzle of oil, add the shallot , and sauté for a few minutes. Add the potatoes .
Cook on high heat for about 5 minutes, stirring constantly to prevent sticking . Once the potatoes are uniformly golden, cover with the hot vegetable broth and continue cooking for another 6 minutes. Once cooked, off the heat, scent with grated lemon zest .
Season with salt and transfer everything to a jug. Add lemon juice and oil, then blend with an immersion blender until smooth and velvety . Set aside.
At this point, cook the pasta in plenty of salted water ; cook 3-4 minutes less than the time indicated on the package. Meanwhile, prepare the base for the pasta: in a saucepan, pour a few ladles of vegetable broth , then flavor with grated lemon zest .
Add the oil and bring to a boil. Drain the pasta and transfer it to the saucepan , then add the potato cream .
Stir and finish cooking the pasta . At the end of cooking, on low heat, add the lemon zest and a dash of white pepper .
Finally, gloss with a drizzle of oil . Turn off the heat and let the pasta rest. Meanwhile, season the scampi tartare with lemon juice and anchovy sauce .
Scent with lemon and lime zest . Add a pinch of salt and white pepper . Gently mix to blend the ingredients well.
Everything is ready for plating: place a generous spoonful of scampi tartare in the center of the plate , then add the pasta and potatoes and the cheese and pepper sauce .
Place another teaspoon of scampi tartare on the pasta and finish with chopped parsley . Your pasta with potatoes and scampi is ready to be served !
It is recommended to consume the pasta with potatoes and scampi as soon as it's ready.
Do not discard the scampi scraps, but use them to make a stock or a bisque to flavor other delicious first courses.
