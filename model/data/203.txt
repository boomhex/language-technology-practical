name:
Buckwheat penne with wok mushrooms
ingredients:
Buckwheat penne: 11.3oz(320 g)
Champignon mushrooms: 40
Carrots: 1
Onions: 1
Garlic: 1clove
Gluten-free soy sauce: 4tsp
Extra virgin olive oil: 4spoonfuls
Parmigiano Reggiano PDO cheese: 4spoonfuls

info:
Difficulty: Average
Prep time: 20 min
Cook time: 10 min
Serving: 4
Cost: Low

instructions:
To prepare the buckwheat penne with wok mushrooms, start by cleaning the vegetables. Slice the onion thinly and carrot . Preheat a wok pan and pour in half of the oil .
Add to the wok the peeled and finely chopped garlic , sliced onion , and chopped carrots .
Keep the temperature medium-high and stir frequently with a spoon . How to Clean Porcini Mushrooms and cut them into chunks , then transfer them into the pan with the rest of the ingredients .
Continue to cook over medium-high heat, stirring often to prevent the ingredients at the bottom of the wok from burning . Boil the buckwheat penne in salted boiling water . Once ready, drain them and transfer them to the wok .
To finish, pour in the other half of the oil , the gluten-free soy sauce , and the grated Parmesan cheese .
Now all you have to do is stir well to mix all the ingredients , scent with sage leaves , and your dish is ready to be served! .
Buckwheat penne with wok mushrooms can be stored for one day in an airtight container. Freezing is not recommended.
For those who love a stronger mushroom flavor, instead of champignons, you can rehydrate dried porcini and add them to the wok last, perhaps finely sliced. If you don't have a wok pan, you can use a non-stick pan with a thick base.
