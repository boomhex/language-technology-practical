name:
7 jar zucchini cake
ingredients:
Zucchini: 12oz(340 g)- (about 2)
Type 00 flour: 2vasetti
Nut flour: 1vasetto
Natural plain yogurt: 1vasetto
Sunflower seed oil: 1vasetto
Pecorino cheese: 1vasetto- to be grated
Fontina cheese: 1vasetto- diced
Eggs: 3
Instant yeast for salted preparations: 4tsp(16 g)- (1 packet)
Thyme: to taste
Fine salt: to taste
Black pepper: to taste

info:
Difficulty: Easy
Prep time: 20 min
Cook time: 60 min
Serving: 6
Cost: Low

instructions:
To prepare the 7 jar zucchini cake, first grate the zucchini with a large-hole grater . Now pour the yogurt into a large bowl so you can use the jar as a unit of measure. Add the eggs and start mixing with a whisk.
While mixing, slowly pour in the seed oil , then add the grated pecorino cheese and hazelnut flour .
Also add the all-purpose flour , then mix well to combine everything , salt and pepper .
Add the baking powder and thyme leaves , then add the grated zucchini and mix with a spatula to incorporate them into the mixture.
Lastly, add the cubes of fontina cheese and mix again to obtain a homogeneous mixture . Line an 8-inch diameter mold with parchment paper and pour the obtained mixture inside .
Level the surface and bake in a preheated static oven at 356Â°F for about 60 minutes. Once cooked, remove from the oven and let it cool slightly . Your 7 jar zucchini cake is ready to be enjoyed warm or at room temperature !
The 7 jar zucchini cake can be stored in the refrigerator for 2 days.
Freezing is not recommended.
You can substitute the fontina with another cheese of the same consistency such as edam, provolone, or caciocavallo.
As an alternative to hazelnut flour, you can use the same amount of almond or pistachio flour.
Make sure to buy whole plain yogurt, not sweetened!
