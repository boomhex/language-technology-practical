name:
Gourmet fried octopus
ingredients:
Octopus: 1.75lbs(800 g)- already cleaned
Rice flour: to taste
Peanut seed oil: 4 ¼cups(1 l)
Yellow cherry tomatoes: 0.67cup(100 g)
Datterino tomatoes: ½cup(100 g)
Zucchini: 4- medium
Garlic: 1clove
Extra virgin olive oil: to taste
Salt: to taste
Black pepper: to taste
Marjoram: to taste
Basil: to taste
Sugar: to taste

info:
Difficulty: Difficult
Prep time: 40 min
Cook time: 2 h 10 min
Serving: 4
Cost: Average

instructions:
Gourmet fried octopus is a main dish that amazes with its variety of textures, flavors, and colors. The octopus, through a double cooking method, in a pot and fried, is tender on the inside and crispy on the outside. The zucchini cream, velvety and delicately flavored, is the perfect complement. The confit tomatoes add color and depth to the dish with their sweet and sour note. To finish, a mayonnaise-inspired sauce, prepared from the octopus cooking liquid, gives the dish an authentic concentration of sea flavor. Whether it's for a romantic evening, a family party, or with friends, this recipe is perfect for special occasions. All that's left is to roll up your sleeves and discover how satisfying it is to create refined dishes and leave your guests speechless.

Place the cleaned octopus in a pot and cook dry, covered, for 40 minutes . Meanwhile, halve the cherry tomatoes and place them on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper .
Add a drizzle of oil and the sugar. Season with salt and pepper . Add marjoram, basil , and a crushed garlic clove. Bake at 320°F for 90 minutes .
Slice the zucchinis into rounds and place them in a pan with a drizzle of oil , season with salt and pepper , and cook until they are golden .
Transfer them to a blender, add a little water , a drizzle of oil, and blend until smooth and homogeneous , set aside.
Once the octopus is cooked, remove it from the pot and let it cool completely. Meanwhile, keep the cooking liquid on the heat and reduce it by more than half .
Transfer it to a blender, add the oil , and blend until you get a sauce that is not too thick . To gauge the right proportions, note that the oil quantity should be double the amount of the reduced liquid.
Once cold, cut the octopus into pieces, coat them in rice flour , and fry them in peanut oil at 356°F for a few minutes until crispy . Drain on paper towels to remove excess oil .
Now plate: place the zucchini cream at the bottom of the plate , continue with the fried octopus and cherry tomatoes .
Finish with a spoonful of sauce and some basil leaves . The gourmet fried octopus is ready to be enjoyed .
It is advisable to consume the gourmet fried octopus immediately. If there are leftovers, they can be stored in the fridge, in a closed container, for up to one day.
You can prepare the zucchini cream and confit tomatoes in advance, which will allow you to focus on frying the octopus and assembling the dish. For frying, it is important that the oil is at the correct temperature; oil that is too cold will make the octopus greasy and not very crispy, while oil that is too hot could burn it. The ideal temperature is around 356°F.
