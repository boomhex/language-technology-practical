name:
Turkey curry with crispy vegetables
ingredients:
Turkey breast: 1.8lbs(800 g)
Curry: 6tsp(15 g)
Carrots: 8.8oz(250 g)
Zucchini: 2cups(250 g)
Green beans: 8.8oz(250 g)
Leeks: 5.3oz(150 g)
Garlic: 1clove
Thyme: 4sprigs
Acacia honey: 2tbsp(40 g)
Apple cider vinegar: 1 ½tbsp(20 ml)
Fine salt: 1pinch
Black pepper: to taste
Vegetable broth: ¾cup(200 ml)
Brown sugar: 1tsp

info:
Difficulty: Easy
Prep time: 35 min
Cook time: 30 min
Serving: 4
Cost: Low

To prepare the turkey curry with crispy vegetables, start by preparing the meat: cut the turkey meat into small pieces , then wash all the vegetables; clean and thinly slice the leek , cut the carrots into small sticks
trim and cut the zucchini into small pieces, the same size as the carrots , then trim and do the same operation with the green beans . Set the vegetables aside and proceed with cooking the meat: in a pan, sauté a garlic clove over a low heat with a drizzle of oil , when it is well browned, you can remove it
and pour in the turkey . Brown it for a few moments, stirring; then salt and pepper , add the acacia honey ,
deglaze with the apple cider vinegar , then add the brown sugar the curry and mix the ingredients so that the meat is flavored. Cook covered for about 15 minutes, stirring occasionally.
Once ready, drain the turkey, setting aside the cooking juices in a bowl . Then keep the turkey covered with plastic wrap while you continue preparing: in the same pan where you cooked the chicken, pour the cooking juices
pour in the leek , let it wilt over low heat by gradually adding Vegetable broth to aid cooking . When it is wilted, add the carrots and cook for 5-6 minutes.
then add the green beans and cook for another 3-4 minutes . Add the zucchini last and cook for another 2-3 minutes. Then flavor everything with chopped thyme .
Mix and let it flavor , then add the turkey you had set aside , mix it with the vegetables and turn off the heat. Serve the turkey curry with crispy vegetables very hot , if you prefer accompanied by Pilaf Rice !
You can store turkey curry with crispy vegetables in the refrigerator in an airtight container for 2-3 days. You can freeze it if you used fresh, non-defrosted ingredients.
You can also add a bit of chili pepper to the turkey curry and choose seasonal vegetables you prefer as a condiment!
