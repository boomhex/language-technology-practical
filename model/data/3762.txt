name:
Avocado Spreadable Cheese
ingredients:
Avocado: 1- ripe
Greek yogurt: 0.8cup(200 g)
Fresh chili pepper: - 1/2
Fine salt: to taste
Black pepper: to taste

info:
Difficulty: Easy
Prep time: 20 min
Serving: 10
Cost: Low
Note + 24 hours of resting

Peel the avocado, remove the central pit, and cut it into pieces. Wash the chili pepper and slice half of it; place the avocado, chili pepper, and yogurt in a bowl, then blend the ingredients until they become a smooth, lump-free cream, finally season with salt and pepper.
Place the cream on a dry and clean cloth; close the cloth and put it inside a strainer placed over a bowl that will collect the yogurt whey.
Let the cheese rest in the refrigerator for at least 24 hours. After this time, the product will have lost most of its whey and will have become a spreadable cream. Store the avocado cheese in the refrigerator in a dry, clean glass jar with an airtight seal. The avocado cheese keeps in the refrigerator for 5 days.
If you like, you can add as much chili pepper to the avocado cheese as you like, to make it more or less spicy according to your taste. If you want an even thicker cheese, let it rest in the fridge for a few more hours; the loss of whey will make it very manageable; to present it at the table, you can form balls that you then season with a little oil, chili powder, and some herbs of your choice.
