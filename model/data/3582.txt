name:
Potato and Speck Croquettes
ingredients:
Potatoes: 1.8lbs(800 g)
Speck: 3.5oz(100 g)
Eggs: 2- medium
Grana Padano PDO cheese: 3.5oz(100 g)- to grate
Parsley: to taste
Nutmeg: to taste
Fine salt: to taste
Black pepper: to taste
Eggs: 1- medium
Breadcrumbs: to taste
Peanut seed oil: to taste

info:
Difficulty: Easy
Prep time: 20 min
Cook time: 25 min
Makes: 15 pieces
Cost: Low

instructions:
In a saucepan, bring salted water to a boil, then cook the potatoes with the skins on . Once cooked, peel them and mash them with a potato masher into a bowl . Then add the parsley, salt, and pepper to the mashed potatoes.
Next, chop the speck and add it to the mixture , then add the eggs and grated Grana Padano cheese . Mix all the ingredients well
and take a portion of the mixture (about 1.8 oz) and shape it to form croquettes . Then dip the croquettes in beaten egg , and then in breadcrumbs .
Finally, fry them in hot oil and let them brown well, turning them from one side to the other. (10-11) Drain them on a paper towel and serve hot .
