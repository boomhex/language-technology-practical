name:
Risotto with Peppers and Arugula
ingredients:
Carnaroli rice: 1.6cups(320 g)
Quartirolo Lombardo cheese: 2.8oz(80 g)
Water: 1.7cups(400 g)
White wine: 2 ½tbsp(40 g)
Extra virgin olive oil: to taste
Salt: to taste
Yellow peppers: 1.3lbs(600 g)
Yellow onions: 1
Water: to taste
Arugula: 2 ½cups(50 g)
Extra virgin olive oil: 1.7tbsp(25 g)
Water: 1 ½tbsp(20 g)

info:
Difficulty: Easy
Prep time: 30 min
Cook time: 35 min
Serving: 4
Cost: Low

instructions:
Risotto with peppers and arugula is a first course that blends the sweet taste of peppers with the intense freshness of arugula. This recipe not only highlights the vibrant colors of Mediterranean cuisine but also superbly combines the sauces derived from these two main ingredients to achieve a creamy and velvety texture. The pepper, with its sweet flavor, adds warmth and depth to the dish, while the arugula, with its characteristic spicy and slightly bitter taste, balances the sweetness of the pepper cream , creating an appealing contrast of flavors. Risotto with peppers and arugula is a dish that suits multiple occasions; whether for an elegant dinner, a family lunch, or a special event, it will always know how to surprise and delight your guests!

To prepare the risotto with peppers and arugula, first clean the peppers: remove the ends , seeds, and inner filaments , then cut them into strips .
Slice the onion julienne style and place it in a pan with a drizzle of oil and a pinch of salt . Cook over medium heat for 10 minutes, stirring frequently. Then add the peppers and sauté for another 10 minutes.
Set aside a few strips of pepper for the final garnish . Transfer everything to a blender, add some water , and puree until you get a smooth and homogeneous sauce . Keep warm.
Now prepare the arugula sauce: in a blender, put the arugula (save a few leaves for the garnish), add the oil , water , a pinch of salt, and blend. The result will be a smooth and homogeneous sauce . Set aside.
In a pot, pour a drizzle of oil, add the rice , and toast for about 7 minutes. Deglaze with white wine , and once all the alcohol has evaporated, add 2 ladles of boiling water .
Adjust the salt , and when there are 5 minutes left to the end of cooking, add the hot pepper sauce . When there are 2 minutes left to the end of cooking the rice, turn off the heat and season with pepper .
Manteca with oil , 2 oz of crumbled quartirolo cheese , and mix well .
Plate the risotto and place drops of arugula sauce on the surface, the reserved pepper strips , the remaining quartirolo , and the fresh arugula leaves. Your risotto with peppers and arugula is ready to be enjoyed !
It is advisable to consume the risotto with peppers and arugula immediately, but if you have leftovers, you can store it in the refrigerator in a sealed container for 2 days.
The pepper cream and arugula sauce can be prepared in advance and stored in the refrigerator for 2 days, or even frozen for longer storage.
To enrich the dish, you can add shrimp or diced chicken breast, or other seasonal vegetables like asparagus, fresh peas, or mushrooms, to make the dish even more interesting and nutritious!
