name:
Pasta with ricotta cheese and basil
ingredients:
Whole grain egg fettuccine pasta: 8.8oz(250 g)
Cow's milk ricotta cheese: 7oz(200 g)
Basil: 1tbsp(15 g)
Almond flakes: 2 ½tbsp(20 g)
Extra virgin olive oil: to taste
Fine salt: to taste

info:
Difficulty: Very easy
Prep time: 5 min
Cook time: 8 min
Serving: 4
Cost: Very low

instructions:
A first course that combines the delicacy of ricotta made with cow’s milk with the freshness of basil leaves. For a quick lunch when you have very few ingredients at home, we recommend our recipe for pasta with ricotta cheese and basil . It is a union that confirms the winning combination of creamy or less creamy cheeses and aromatic herbs. For those who love pasta with ricotta cheese, it will be a confirmation with an extra note that brings spring and summer to mind!
To prepare pasta with ricotta cheese and basil , put a pot of salted water to boil. Clean the basil leaves with a cloth, then chop them coarsely with a knife . Toast the almond slices over low heat in a large pan . Brown them lightly. In the meantime, boil the pasta for the time indicated on the package .
Pour the ricotta into a saucepan and add a ladle of water from the cooking water of the pasta . Drain the pasta directly into the saucepan, keeping the water , stir, then add a ladle of hot cooking water ,
stir again, then add the basil . Season with oil , and almost all of the previously toasted almond slices :
mix , then serve the pasta with ricotta cheese and basil by adding the remaining almonds on serving plates (11-12) and add a drizzle of oil to taste.
You can keep the pasta in the fridge for a day.
Freezing is not recommended.
Add lemon zest to give it even more freshness or flavor the ricotta with saffron powder to enrich your pasta.
