name:
Bruschetta with Broccoli Cream
ingredients:
Broccoli stalks: 5.3oz(150 g)- and leaves
Walnut kernels: 0.6cup(70 g)
Pecorino cheese: 1.4oz(40 g)- to grate
Garlic: 1clove- (optional)
Extra virgin olive oil: to taste
Fine salt: to taste
Black pepper: to taste
Bread: 8slices
Sun-dried tomatoes in oil: to taste
Anchovies in oil: to taste

info:
Difficulty: Very easy
Prep time: 15 min
Cook time: 15 min
Serving: 4
Cost: Average

instructions:
To make the broccoli cream bruschetta, first peel the broccoli stalk with a peeler to remove the tougher outer part , then chop it roughly . Also chop the leaves into pieces, if present. Steam the stalks and leaves for 10 minutes .
Transfer everything to a blender and add the peeled garlic clove , grated Pecorino cheese , and walnut kernels .
Blend for a few seconds, then add the oil gradually and continue blending until you reach a creamy and smooth consistency . Adjust the broccoli cream with salt and pepper. At this point, slice the bread, season with oil and pepper, and toast in the hot oven for a few minutes.
Spread the broccoli cream on the toasted bread slices and garnish as desired with sun-dried tomatoes or anchovies. Your broccoli cream bruschetta is ready to be enjoyed !
The broccoli cream can be stored in the refrigerator for a couple of days, covered with plastic wrap.
You can also freeze it.
For this recipe, you can use either the broccoli stalks or leaves, or a mix of both!
If you prefer, you can replace the walnuts with cashews.
To use the broccoli cream as a pasta dressing, dilute it with a bit of cooking water until you reach the ideal consistency.
