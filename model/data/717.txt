name:
Cocoa cake without butter
ingredients:
Cow's milk ricotta cheese: 2 ½cups(600 g)- well drained
Eggs: 7.1oz(200 g)- (4 small)
Sugar: 1cup(200 g)
Type 00 flour: 0.8cup(100 g)
Unsweetened cocoa powder: ½cup(50 g)
Baking powder: 1 ½tsp(8 g)
Rum: 0.5oz(15 ml)
Fine salt: 1pinch
Powdered sugar: to taste

info:
Difficulty: Easy
Prep time: 20 min
Cook time: 45 min
Makes: 28 pieces
Cost: Low

instructions:
To prepare the cocoa cake without butter, start with the batter. Pour the ricotta and sugar into a bowl . Mix well with a fork until the ingredients are combined. Now add the rum .
Also add the 4 previously cracked eggs and continue working with the fork until a uniform cream is obtained . Now add the powders: start with the flour .
Also add the baking powder , cocoa , and a pinch of salt .
Using a hand whisk, mix the batter well, it should be thick, homogeneous, and free of lumps . Get a 8x12 inch pan, line it with parchment paper and pour in the batter. Level the surface with the back of a spoon and bake in a preheated fan oven at 340°F for 45 minutes, on the middle rack. Take the cake out of the oven and let it cool. Now, gently lifting the parchment paper, transfer the cake to a work surface .
Dust with powdered sugar and cut the cake into 28 cubes, making 7 vertical strips and 4 horizontal ones . The cocoa cake without butter is ready to serve .
The cocoa cake without butter keeps in the refrigerator for up to 3 days.
Enrich the cocoa cake without butter with orange zest and serve it with dollops of whipped cream!
