name:
Gluten-free pumpkin gnocchi
ingredients:
Pumpkin: 1.5lbs(700 g)- (to clean)
Rice flour: 1 ¼cup(160 g)
Cornstarch: 0.625cup(80 g)
Eggs: 1
Fine salt: to taste
Butter: 3tbsp(40 g)
Sage: 1sprig

To prepare the gluten-free pumpkin gnocchi, first clean the pumpkin from seeds and internal filaments , then cut it in half and place it in a bowl covered with plastic wrap. Microwave for 10 minutes at 800 Watts .
Once cooked, scoop out the pumpkin pulp with a spoon and let it cool. At this point, mash the pumpkin with a fork , then add the egg .
Stir well until the egg is completely absorbed , then add the rice flour and corn starch .
Mix again to thoroughly incorporate the powders . You should obtain a homogeneous and non-sticky mixture . Now, lightly dust the work surface with rice flour .
Divide the dough into smaller portions using a dough scraper and form logs , then cut into 1/2 inch pieces .
After forming all the gnocchi, proceed with cooking: melt the butter in a pan with sage leaves . Boil the gnocchi in salted boiling water .
As soon as they float to the surface, drain the gnocchi into the pan with the dressing . Plate and serve immediately your gluten-free pumpkin gnocchi !
Cooked gluten-free pumpkin gnocchi can be stored in the refrigerator for a couple of days in an airtight container.
It is advisable to cook the gnocchi immediately and not store them raw.
If desired, you can freeze them raw and then cook them directly from frozen.
If the dough turns out too moist, let it rest under a cloth before deciding to add more flour.
