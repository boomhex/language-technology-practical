name:
Rabbit stew with olives and sun-dried tomatoes
ingredients:
Rabbit: 2.2lbs(1 kg)
Sun-dried tomatoes in oil: 3.5oz(100 g)
Black olives: ½cup(100 g)- pitted
White onions: ¼cup(40 g)
Garlic: 2cloves
Rosemary: 1sprig
Vegetable broth: 1.3cups(300 g)
Marsala wine: 1.7oz(50 g)
Fine salt: to taste
Black pepper: to taste
Extra virgin olive oil: 1.7tsp(8 g)

info:
Difficulty: Easy
Prep time: 20 min
Cook time: 50 min
Serving: 4
Cost: Low

instructions:
To prepare rabbit stew with olives and sun-dried tomatoes, first drain the sun-dried tomatoes from the oil and prepare the Vegetable broth following our recipe. Slice both the onion and the garlic . Instead, cut the sun-dried tomatoes into strips .
In a pot, heat the oil and add the rabbit and brown on all sides over medium heat until golden, it will take about 5-10 minutes. Then add the onion and garlic .
Add the rosemary and deglaze with Marsala . Stir and let all the alcohol evaporate. At this point, add the sun-dried tomatoes .
Pour the broth into the pot , season with salt and pepper .
Stir, cover with a lid , and cook over low heat for about 40 minutes or until the rabbit is tender. If necessary, you can add more broth during cooking. In the last 5 minutes of cooking, add the olives and stir one last time. Serve .
Complete with a bit of cooking juice and some chopped rosemary . Rabbit stew with sun-dried tomatoes is ready to be served hot .
Rabbit stew with olives and sun-dried tomatoes can be stored in the fridge, in a closed container, for 2 days. Before enjoying it again, we recommend gently reheating it in a pan, adding a splash of water or broth.
As an alternative to Marsala, you can deglaze the rabbit with a good dry white wine. If you like it spicy, you can add a fresh chili during browning or a pinch of smoked paprika.
