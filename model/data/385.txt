name:
Vegetable stock in the microwave
ingredients:
Yellow onions: 1
Carrots: 1 ½cup(200 g)
Celery: 7.1oz(200 g)
Leeks: 1
Shallot: 2oz(50 g)
Zucchini: ¾cup(100 g)
Cluster tomatoes: 7oz(200 g)
Water: 4glasses(1 l)
Coarse salt: to taste
Ground black pepper: to taste

info:
Difficulty: Very easy
Prep time: 10 min
Cook time: 25 min
Serving: 4
Cost: Very low

instructions:
To prepare the vegetable stock in the microwave, first, focus on cleaning the vegetables. Clean a yellow onion, cut it in half , and then slice it into wedges . Clean a shallot and cut it into wedges .
Remove the ends of the leek, cut it in half, and slice it into pieces . Cut the celery in half and then into chunks .
Wash and cut the carrot and zucchini into chunks. Wash and cut the tomatoes into wedges .
Place all the vegetables in a pyrex bowl, add coarse salt and whole peppercorns . Cover with cold water and cook in the microwave at 650 W for 25 minutes.
Once cooking is done , strain the vegetables using a colander . The microwave vegetable stock is ready to be used in your recipes.
You can store the vegetable stock in the refrigerator in a closed container for up to a week, or you can freeze it in ice cube trays, so you'll have the amount you need whenever necessary.
To prepare the vegetable stock, you can use any type of vegetables you like, preferably in season, maintaining the base of the stock, which is: onion, celery, and carrot. The filtered vegetables can be repurposed, for example by blending them for use in other preparations.
