name:
Pan-Seared Salmon Trout
ingredients:
Salmon trout: 1.8lbs(800 g)- (4 fillets)
Cherry tomatoes: 2cups(300 g)
Taggiasca olives: 0.4cup(80 g)- pitted
Dry white wine: ⅓cup(80 g)
Extra virgin olive oil: to taste
Fine salt: to taste
Black pepper: to taste
Fennel: 0.9lb(400 g)
Oranges: 1.1lbs(500 g)
Lamb's lettuce: 4cups(80 g)
Parsley: to taste

instructions:

To prepare the pan-seared salmon trout, start with the cherry tomatoes: cut them in half and place them in a bowl along with Taggiasca olives . Season with salt, oil, and a garlic clove , mix and set aside for the moment.
Heat a drizzle of oil in a large non-stick pan and lay the trout fillets skin-side down . Cook over medium heat for 2-3 minutes, then slightly increase the heat and deglaze with white wine . Once the alcohol has completely evaporated, add the cherry tomatoes and olives mix .
Cover with a lid and continue cooking for another 5-7 minutes without turning the fillets. Meanwhile, prepare the fennel and orange salad: thinly slice the fennel , then peel the oranges and section them by cutting between the pulp and the white membrane with a sharp knife .
Combine the fennel and oranges in a bowl , then season with salt, pepper, and oil . Mix gently .
At this point, the trout will be cooked, so adjust the seasoning with salt and pepper. Turn off the heat and let it rest for a minute before plating . Distribute the lamb's lettuce at the base of the plate .
Place a trout fillet on top with its cherry tomato and olive sauce , then add the fennel and orange salad to the side . Finally, garnish with chopped parsley. Your pan-seared salmon trout is ready to be served !
The pan-seared salmon trout can be stored in the refrigerator for a maximum of one day in an airtight container.
If you wish, you can also add capers to the cherry tomatoes and olives mix for an extra kick of flavor!
