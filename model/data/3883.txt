name:
Pear and Cinnamon Sauce
ingredients:
Pears: 2 ½cups(500 g)- (the pulp)
Cinnamon powder: ½tsp
Sugar: ½cup(100 g)
White wine: ½glass
Lemon juice: 1spoonful

info:
Difficulty: Easy
Prep time: 10 min
Cook time: 30 min
Serving: 4
Cost: Low

instructions:

Wash, peel, quarter, and core the pears. Cut the flesh into small pieces and place them in a pot where you will add the wine, sugar, cinnamon, and lemon juice. Slowly bring everything to a boil, and, keeping the heat low, cook and thicken the mixture (about 20-25 minutes). Turn off the heat and let it cool, then pass the mixture through a sieve (or blender).
For this recipe, I recommend buying pears that are not too ripe and have a fine (not too grainy) and aromatic flesh, such as Decana, William, or Abate.
