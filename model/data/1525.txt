name:
Chicken with Orange and Almonds
ingredients:
Chicken drumsticks: 0.9lb(400 g)- (4 pieces)
Orange juice: 1glass(250 g)- (about 2 oranges)
Dry white wine: 2.5oz(70 g)
Extra virgin olive oil: 2tbsp(30 g)
Almond slices: 2tbsp(15 g)
Rosemary: 1tsp- dry
Onions: Â½
Wildflower honey: 1spoonful

info:
Difficulty: Easy
Prep time: 10 min
Cook time: 25 min
Serving: 2
Cost: Low

instructions:
To prepare the chicken with orange and almonds, first, squeeze the oranges . Then strain the juice and add 1 tablespoon of honey , stirring to dissolve it. Pour the sliced almonds into a pan
and toast them for a few minutes over medium heat . Transfer to a small bowl and set aside. In the same pan, place the chicken drumsticks, the onion cut into wedges, and the rosemary .
Then also pour in the oil and brown the chicken for a couple of minutes. Then deglaze with the white wine and wait for it to completely evaporate before adding the orange juice .
Let it cook this way for about 25 minutes with the lid on , raising the heat in the last minutes of cooking to brown them (11-12).
When the drumsticks are ready, transfer them to a plate , garnish with the almond slices , and serve .
It is recommended to consume the chicken with orange and almonds immediately. Alternatively, it can be stored in the refrigerator for up to 1 day.
Instead of onion, you can use a clove of garlic. You can also flavor the drumsticks with parsley, sage, or bay leaf.
