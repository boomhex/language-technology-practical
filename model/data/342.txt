name:
Cold pasta with roasted carrot cream
ingredients:
Tortiglioni pasta: 11.3oz(320 g)
Basil: to taste
Carrots: 1 ½cup(200 g)
Extra virgin olive oil: to taste
Fine salt: to taste
Water: 0.4cup(100 g)
Cucumbers: 1 ¼cup(150 g)
Extra virgin olive oil: 1 ½tbsp(20 g)
Black pepper: to taste
Lemon peel: 1
Lemon juice: 1

To prepare the cold pasta with roasted carrot cream, first, trim and peel the carrots , then roughly chop them into cubes . In a skillet, heat a drizzle of oil, then add the carrots and roast them on low heat for about 10 minutes.
Transfer them to a container , add water , salt, and blend with an immersion blender until a smooth and homogeneous cream is obtained and set aside.
Clean the cucumbers and cut them first into sticks and then into small cubes of 0.2 inches. Transfer them to a bowl and season with oil , salt, and pepper .
Continue with the lemon zest and juice , mix , and let it rest until use.
Cook the pasta in boiling salted water . Drain it into a bowl, add the carrot cream , and mix. Also, add the cucumbers , keeping some aside for plating.
Mix again and plate, add the cucumbers set aside and a few basil leaves . Your cold pasta with roasted carrot cream is ready to be enjoyed !
The cold pasta with roasted carrot cream can be perfectly stored in the refrigerator, in a sealed container, for 1-2 days.
You can add a few tablespoons of ricotta or robiola to the carrot cream for a silkier texture. If you like experimenting, you can flavor the carrot cream with curry, cumin, or freshly grated ginger.
