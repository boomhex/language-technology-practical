name:
Sautéed Champignon Mushrooms
ingredients:
Champignon mushrooms: 2.2lbs(1 kg)
Garlic: 1clove
Parsley: to taste
Extra virgin olive oil: 1.4tbsp(20 g)

info:
Difficulty: Very easy
Prep time: 10 min
Cook time: 10 min
Serving: 4
Cost: Very low

instructions:
To prepare sautéed champignon mushrooms, first trim the end part of the stems, which is usually very dirty with soil, then clean the mushroom caps with a damp cloth . First, cut them in half and then into wedges . Set aside.
Crush the garlic cloves with their skins on and sauté them in a pan with a drizzle of oil and the parsley stems . Remove the garlic .
Add the mushrooms and brown them over high heat. Season with salt and pepper. Lower the heat and cook for 10 minutes, stirring occasionally .
Remove the parsley stems as well , add the chopped fresh parsley , and plate. Your sautéed champignon mushrooms are ready to be served .
Sautéed champignon mushrooms can be stored in the refrigerator, in a closed container, for 2 days.
You can add a sprinkle of Parmesan for a tastier touch, or incorporate them into a cream sauce for a creamier version. If you love spicy food, add a fresh chili pepper cut into slices to the pan along with the garlic and celery stems.
