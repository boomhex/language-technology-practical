name:
Sautéed Broccoli Rabe
ingredients:
Broccoli rabe: 2.2lbs(1 kg)- (cleaned 360 g)
Garlic: 2cloves
Fresh chili pepper: 1
Extra virgin olive oil: to taste
Fine salt: to taste

To prepare the sautéed broccoli rabe, first, clean the vegetables. Remove the toughest stems and outer leaves . Once cleaned, you will have about 12.7 oz of broccoli rabe , wash them several times with cold water and drain them.
Place a large pot full of water on the stove, and when it comes to a boil, add the broccoli rabe and let them cook for 15 minutes. When there are 2-3 minutes left, prepare a sauté in a pan with oil, 2 whole garlic cloves, and a chopped chili pepper . As soon as the broccoli rabe is well wilted, drain them .
When the garlic is golden, add the broccoli rabe , season with salt , and sauté over medium-high heat for about ten minutes . Turn off the heat and serve.
Broccoli rabe can be stored in the refrigerator, well sealed in an airtight container, for 2 days. You can freeze the broccoli rabe after boiling them in food bags. Before sautéing them, just let them thaw.
You can add some anchovy fillets to the garlic and chili pepper sauté if desired.
