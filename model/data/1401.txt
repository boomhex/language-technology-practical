name:
Fava Pod Omelette
ingredients:
Fava bean peels: 6cups(300 g)
Mint: to taste
Type 00 flour: 0.6cup(75 g)
Pecorino cheese: 1oz(30 g)- grated
Dried chili pepper: 1
Garlic: 1clove
Extra virgin olive oil: to taste
Fine salt: to taste

info:
Difficulty: Easy
Prep time: 10 min
Cook time: 20 min
Serving: 4
Cost: Very low

instructions:
To prepare the fava pod omelette, first remove the string from the fava pods . Then blanch them in boiling salted water for about 15 minutes after it starts boiling. Then drain them and let them cool slightly.
Cut them into coarse pieces with a knife and transfer them to a bowl. Add the flour , plenty of mint leaves ,
and grated Pecorino cheese . Season with salt and mix everything together . Heat a drizzle of oil in a pan, add a clove of garlic and the crumbled chili pepper . Let it fry, then remove the garlic clove
and pour the mixture into the pan . Using the back of a spoon or a spatula, spread it over the entire surface of the pan, about 9 1/2 inches in diameter. Wait about ten minutes ,
then using a plate, flip the omelette and finish cooking for another 10 minutes on the other side . Transfer to a serving plate and serve the fava pod omelette hot !
The fava pod omelette can be stored in the refrigerator for a couple of days.
Flavor with lemon zest and accompany your omelette with pilaf rice. If you prefer, you can leave the previously blanched pods whole.
To obtain 10 1/2 oz of fava pods, you will need about 14 3/4 oz of fava beans.
