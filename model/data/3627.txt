name:
Berry Swirl Cup
ingredients:
Mascarpone cheese: 1cup(250 g)
Powdered sugar: 1cup(120 g)
Fresh liquid cream: 6 ¾tbsp(100 ml)
Vanilla extract: a few- drop
Lemons: 1
Sugar: ¼cup(50 g)
Whole milk: 3spoonfuls
Blueberries: ½cup(100 g)
Red currants: ¾cup(100 g)
Raspberries: 0.8cup(100 g)

info:
Difficulty: Easy
Prep time: 10 min
Serving: 4
Cost: Low
Note plus the resting time in the refrigerator

instructions:

In a bowl, soften the mascarpone by adding 2-3 tablespoons of milk (1-2), then add the powdered sugar , vanilla extract, and mix all the ingredients well .
In another bowl, whip the cream and add it to the mascarpone mixture . Then place the mixture into the cups and let them set in the refrigerator . Mix the berries with 1/4 cup of sugar, the juice, and the grated zest of 1 lemon: mix gently and let them macerate . Remove the cups from the refrigerator and garnish them with the berries, swirling them with the juice from the berries.
