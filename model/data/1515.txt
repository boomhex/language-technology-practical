name:
Spaghetti with red onions, walnuts, and gorgonzola
ingredients:
Spaghetti: 11.3oz(320 g)
Gorgonzola cheese: 12.3oz(350 g)
Walnut kernels: ½cup(50 g)
Red onions: 12.3oz(350 g)
Whole milk: ½cup(100 g)
Butter: 2tbsp(30 g)
Extra virgin olive oil: 1 ½tbsp(20 g)
Dry white wine: 3oz(80 g)
Fine salt: 1pinch
Black pepper: 1pinch

info:
Difficulty: Easy
Prep time: 15 min
Cook time: 30 min
Serving: 4
Cost: Average

instructions:
To prepare spaghetti with red onions, walnuts, and gorgonzola, first clean the onions and then slice them . In a pan, heat the olive oil over low heat and then add the butter ; once the butter is melted, add the onions . Sauté for 5 minutes.
Meanwhile, cut the gorgonzola into cubes and chop the walnuts . When the onions are softer, deglaze with white wine , let the alcohol evaporate, and then turn off the heat.
In a small pot, pour the milk and heat it, add the cubed gorgonzola , and let it melt completely. At this point, add salt , pepper, and the chopped walnuts . Stir and cook for another 10 minutes. In the meantime, place a pot full of salted water on the heat and bring to a boil; this will be used for cooking the pasta.
When the sauce is ready, pour it over the onions and stir to combine . Cook the spaghetti until al dente, adjusting according to the time indicated on the package .
Drain the pasta directly into the pan with the sauce, off the heat , stir to flavor , and serve the spaghetti with red onions, walnuts, and gorgonzola hot .
We recommend consuming the spaghetti with red onions, walnuts, and gorgonzola immediately to preserve its creaminess.
For a more flavorful variant, you can add some crispy sautéed speck strips to the dish at the end.
