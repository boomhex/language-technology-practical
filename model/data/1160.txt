name:
Guinea fowl stew
ingredients:
Guinea fowl: 1.8lbs(800 g)- (in pieces)
Peeled tomatoes: 1 ¾cup(400 g)- (not drained)
Sweet Pancetta: 3.2oz(90 g)
Celery: 2ribs
Carrots: 1
Yellow onions: ½
Water: ½cup(120 g)
Marjoram: to taste
Thyme: to taste
Fine salt: to taste
Black pepper: to taste

info:
Difficulty: Easy
Prep time: 10 min
Cook time: 60 min
Serving: 4
Cost: Average

instructions:
To prepare the stewed guinea fowl, first clean and chop the celery , the carrot , and the onion .
In a pan, add the onion and diced bacon and sauté for about 3-4 minutes, then add the celery and carrot and cook for another 5 minutes .
At this point, add the guinea fowl pieces and brown them for about 10 minutes over medium heat, covering the pan with a lid. Then pour in the peeled tomatoes and water .
Season with marjoram and thyme leaves , cover again with the lid , and let cook for about 30 minutes over medium-low heat.
After this time, adjust the salt and pepper and cook for another 15 minutes over medium heat without the lid, until the sauce has thickened . Turn off the heat and serve your stewed guinea fowl immediately !
The stewed guinea fowl can be stored in the refrigerator for up to 4 days. Freezing is not recommended.
When in season, you can replace the peeled tomatoes with cherry tomatoes. If you have leftover meat, you can shred it and use it with the sauce to dress pasta!
