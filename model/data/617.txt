name:
Garganelli with asparagus and gorgonzola
ingredients:
Egg Garganelli pasta: 11.3oz(320 g)
Shallot: 1
Asparagus: 0.7lb(300 g)
Gorgonzola cheese: 7.1oz(200 g)
Marjoram: to taste
Fine salt: to taste
Black pepper: to taste

info:
Difficulty: Average
Prep time: 15 min
Cook time: 30 min
Serving: 4

instructions:

To prepare garganelli with asparagus and gorgonzola, dice the gorgonzola and keep it at room temperature . Clean and slice the shallot . Heat a tablespoon of oil in a pan .
Add the shallot and let it soften . Slice the asparagus into rounds .
Add the asparagus to the pan and let them cook over medium heat for about 10 minutes, or until they are softened, salting them halfway through cooking . Cook the garganelli in boiling salted water , undercooking them by a couple of minutes.
Add a ladle of pasta water to the asparagus , drain the garganelli into the pan , and finish cooking. Turn off the heat and add the gorgonzola .
Mix well and serve the garganelli with asparagus and gorgonzola hot, with pepper to taste and marjoram leaves .
Store the garganelli with asparagus and gorgonzola in the refrigerator for up to one day. We do not recommend freezing.
Make sure to keep the gorgonzola at room temperature so it blends better.
