name:
Farro Salad with Vegetables and Tuna
ingredients:
Pearled farro: 1 ¼cup(250 g)
Datterino tomatoes: 0.9lb(400 g)
Carrots: 2
Celery: 2ribs
Tuna: 7.1oz(200 g)
Basil: to taste
Thyme: to taste
Marjoram: to taste
Oregano: to taste
Extra virgin olive oil: to taste
Fine salt: to taste
Grana Padano PDO cheese: 1.8oz(50 g)

info:
Difficulty: Easy
Prep time: 30 min
Cook time: 20 min
Serving: 4
Cost: Average

instructions:
Farro salad with vegetables and tuna: here's the quick and easy idea to bring to the table a complete summer meal, genuine and with simple flavors. The farro with its versatility lends itself to countless combinations, and here we offer you a colorful mix of seasonal vegetables and tender, tasty fresh tuna bites. To enhance the taste of this Spelt salad with vegetables we added a burst of aromatic herbs that will give a pleasant fragrant touch.
Don't miss other attractive ideas to make with farro:
To prepare farro salad with vegetables and tuna, rinse the farro well to remove impurities . Boil the farro in salted water for 30-40 minutes (check the times on the package) . Wash and cut the cherry tomatoes in half .
Peel the carrots and cut them into small cubes , do the same with the celery . Take the tuna and take only the heart of the fillet, cut it into slices .
Then from these, cut strips and reduce them to cubes . In a bowl, combine the tomatoes, celery , and carrots.
Add the tuna as well and mix. Meanwhile, drain the farro and place it in a dish. Add the vegetables and tuna .
Season with the aromatic herbs , salt, and raw oil . Mix and garnish the farro with vegetables and tuna as desired with Grana Padano DOP flakes.
We recommend consuming the farro with vegetables and tuna immediately. This dish is not suitable for freezing.
Farro doesn't always require soaking, check the instructions on the package. The leftover part of the tuna, which has some stripes, is perfect for cooking; for example, you can use it to make an excellent pasta with tuna.
