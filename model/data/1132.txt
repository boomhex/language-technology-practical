name:
Potato and Lentil Curry Soup
ingredients:
Potatoes: 1.1lbs(500 g)- floury
Dried lentils: ¾cup(150 g)
Carrots: 1
Celery: 1rib
White onions: 2oz(50 g)
Curry: 1tsp- satin
Vegetable broth: 2.1cups(500 g)
Extra virgin olive oil: 2tbsp(30 g)
Fine salt: to taste

info:
Difficulty: Very easy
Prep time: 10 min
Cook time: 40 min
Serving: 4
Cost: Low
Note + preparation time for the vegetable broth

instructions:
To prepare the potato and lentil curry soup, first make the Vegetable broth , then peel and slice the potatoes . Clean the celery, carrot, and onion and roughly chop them, then transfer them to a food processor and finely chop .
Pour the chopped mixture into a saucepan with the oil and sauté for 2 minutes , then add the potatoes and season for about 3 minutes, stirring occasionally . At this point, add the lentils, which you have previously rinsed under running water .
Season with curry , salt, and cover just above the level with the vegetable broth . Cook over medium-low heat for about 30-35 minutes, covering with a lid and stirring occasionally .
After this time , blend everything with an immersion blender . Your potato and lentil curry soup is ready to be served piping hot , drizzled with a splash of raw oil!
The potato and lentil curry soup can be stored in the refrigerator for 2-3 days. You can freeze it after it has completely cooled down.
To obtain a thicker or thinner soup, adjust the cooking time according to the desired consistency. We recommend using medium spicy curry so as not to overpower the flavor of the potato and lentil soup!
