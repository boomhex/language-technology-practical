name:
Crostoni with savoy cabbage, casera cheese and speck
ingredients:
Multigrain whole wheat bread: 6slices- (50 g each)
Verza (savoy cabbage): 0.7lb(320 g)
Speck: 1.5oz(40 g)
Valtellina Casera cheese: 1.75oz(50 g)- aged
Raisins: 1.75oz(50 g)
Pine nuts: 3tbsp(30 g)
Garlic: 1clove
Fresh chili pepper: 1
Extra virgin olive oil: to taste
Fine salt: to taste

info:
Difficulty: Easy
Prep time: 30 min
Cook time: 20 min
Makes: 6 pieces
Cost: Average

instructions:
To prepare the crostoni with savoy cabbage, casera cheese, and speck, start by cleaning the savoy cabbage. Leaf it to obtain 200 g of leaves and cut them into thin strips about 3/4 inch . Then finely chop the chili pepper and move to the stove .
In a saucepan, heat the chili pepper and garlic in the oil, then add the savoy cabbage , season with salt, and cover with a lid, letting it cook on low heat for 10 minutes . Remove the lid and continue cooking for a few more minutes, allowing any excess liquid to evaporate; add the pine nuts ,
and the raisins . At the end of cooking, remove the garlic and let it cool for a moment .
Meanwhile, preheat the oven and cut 6 slices from the bread, each about 5/8 inch thick . Then grate the casera cheese using a coarse grater and finally cut the speck into strips .
Proceed with the composition. Arrange the bread on a baking sheet with parchment paper and garnish with the savoy cabbage , then with the cheese and the speck .
Drizzle a few drops of oil over all the crostoni and bake them in a static oven at 428Â°F for about 8 minutes . Here are your piping hot crostoni with savoy cabbage, casera cheese, and speck ready: enjoy your meal .
It is better not to store or freeze the crostoni with savoy cabbage, casera cheese, and speck because the texture will be lost.
Add sun-dried tomatoes in oil or almond slivers to enrich your crostoni with savoy cabbage, casera cheese, and speck!
