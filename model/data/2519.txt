name:
Mini strudel with berries and ricotta
ingredients:
Pasta disks: 16
Berries: 2.1cups(300 g)- (blackberries, raspberries, blueberries)
Ricotta cheese: 1 ¼cup(300 g)
Powdered sugar: 0.8cup(100 g)
Vanilla bean: 1
Eggs: 1
Ground pistachio: 1.1oz(30 g)

info:
Difficulty: Easy
Prep time: 15 min
Cook time: 10 min
Makes: 16 pieces

instructions:

To prepare the mini strudels with berries and ricotta, start by sifting the ricotta, placing it in a fine mesh strainer over a bowl . Once ready, add the powdered sugar , then cut the vanilla bean lengthwise
and extract the seeds by scraping it with a small knife . Add them to the ricotta and mix everything with a spatula; wash the berries under plenty of fresh running water. Take one pastry disk at a time
and fill with a teaspoon of ricotta and distribute the berries . Brush the edges with beaten egg
and close the sides (10-11), then roll to form a roll shape . Continue until you have used all the filling.
As you prepare them, place the rolls on a baking tray lined with a sheet of parchment paper , then brush them with the remaining beaten egg . Sprinkle the surface with chopped pistachios ;
once finished , bake in a preheated static oven at 392°F for 10 minutes (or at 356°F for 8 minutes if using a convection oven). When they are ready , turn off the oven and plate your hot mini strudels with berries and ricotta .
You can store the mini strudels with berries and ricotta for 2-3 days covered in the fridge. Freezing is not recommended.
The right accompaniment for these delicious mini strudels? Serve them with a lemon custard, and the pairing will be perfect!
