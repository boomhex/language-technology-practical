name:
Raita
ingredients:
Natural plain yogurt: 1cup(250 g)- creamy and thick
Cucumbers: 1 Â¾cup(220 g)
Cumin: 1tsp
Coriander: 1spoonful- minced
Fine salt: to taste

info:
Difficulty: Very easy
Prep time: 10 min
Serving: 4
Cost: Very low

instructions:
To make the raita, start by peeling the cucumber with a vegetable peeler or a knife, then cut the cucumber in half , slice the two halves lengthwise into thin slices , and cut these into sticks
and finally reduce the sticks into small cubes . For more details, check out our Cooking School How to Cut Vegetables. Wash the coriander and pat it dry with a towel, then finely chop it with a knife and set it aside. Now take a bowl and pour the yogurt inside ,
add the ground cumin , the diced cucumber , the chopped coriander ,
salt and mix the sauce with a spoon so that all the ingredients blend and flavor each other. Once this operation is complete, your homemade raita is ready , store it in the refrigerator until it's time to serve and enjoy it cold.
Store the raita in the refrigerator in an airtight container for a couple of days. As it is a very quick preparation, it is recommended to consume it fresh and to avoid freezing it.
Coriander is not a flavor for everyone, so if you don't like it, no regrets, just replace it with some mint leaves: your raita will be fresh and balsamic, unforgettable.
