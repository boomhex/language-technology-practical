name:
Guinness chocolate cake
ingredients:
Butter: 1cup(225 g)- at room temperature
Brown sugar: 1.6cups(350 g)
Eggs: 4
Type 00 flour: 1 ¾cup(225 g)
Unsweetened cocoa powder: 0.8cup(100 g)
Guinness Stout: 1.67cups(400 ml)
Baking powder: 3 ¼tsp(16 g)
Mascarpone cheese: ¾cup(200 g)
Cream cheese: 7oz(200 g)
Powdered sugar: 2cups(250 g)

info:
Difficulty: Easy
Prep time: 30 min
Cook time: 1 h 10 min
Serving: 12
Cost: Low

instructions:
To prepare the Guinness chocolate cake, sift the flour together with the baking powder and cocoa powder into a bowl (1-2). Then place the softened butter and dark brown sugar in a bowl and beat the ingredients together with a stand mixer or an electric mixer to cream them.
Add the eggs one at a time and continue mixing until they are well incorporated into the mixture. Add the dry ingredients one tablespoon at a time , until you obtain a smooth and creamy batter. Finally, add the Guinness beer in a thin stream and stir.
Butter and line a springform pan with a diameter of 9-10 inches with parchment paper ( click here for more instructions), and pour the cake batter evenly inside . Bake in a preheated oven at 350°F for 1 hour and 10 minutes, then remove the Guinness cake and let it cool.
Meanwhile, prepare the topping cream: mix the mascarpone with the Philadelphia and powdered sugar , working the ingredients with a whisk to obtain a soft and dense cream . Spread the cream on the cake and create swirls that resemble the foam of Guinness beer. Your Guinness chocolate cake is ready to be served!
Store the Guinness chocolate cake covered in the refrigerator for 3 days.
You can prepare the topping for the Guinness cake with 7 oz of mascarpone and 7 oz of whipped cream or with Chantilly cream ( Chantilly cream ).
