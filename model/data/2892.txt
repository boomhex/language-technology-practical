name:
American fried chicken
ingredients:
Chicken legs: 1.1lbs(500 g)
Chicken wings: 1.1lbs(500 g)
Buttermilk: 1cup(250 g)
Type 00 flour: to taste
Green Peperoncino: to taste
Paprika: to taste
Garlic powder: to taste
Peanut seed oil: to taste

info:
Difficulty: Very easy
Prep time: 10 min
Cook time: 20 min
Serving: 4
Cost: Low

instructions:
To make American fried chicken, start by preparing the spice mix: place all the spices and flour in a bowl and then transfer them to a freezer bag to mix them . Place the chicken thighs and wings in a bowl and pour the buttermilk inside . To make it at home, follow our guide How to make buttermilk .
Turn the chicken pieces so they become well moistened then place them in the freezer bag along with the spices , hold the bag tightly closed with one hand and shake it to flavor the chicken pieces, which should be well floured . Let the chicken rest for a few minutes.
Meanwhile, place a pot on the stove with seed oil, heat the oil; the ideal temperature is about 340-355Â°F, if you don't have a thermometer, dip a pinch of breadcrumbs in the oil and if it sizzles then it's ready for frying. The chicken pieces should be well immersed in the oil . Cook the chicken for 10 minutes on each side. Once cooked, use kitchen tongs to remove the chicken and place it on a tray lined with paper towels so it can absorb the excess oil . Your American fried chicken is ready to enjoy!
It is preferable to consume American fried chicken immediately and freezing is not recommended.
Choose the spice mix according to your preferences: some versions include curry, for example, and also the use of different finely chopped aromatic herbs such as bay leaf, marjoram, oregano, or sage.
