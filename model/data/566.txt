name:
Butterless Margherita Cake
ingredients:
Type 00 flour: 2.3cups(300 g)
Potato starch: 1.1cups(130 g)
Sugar: 1cup(190 g)
Soy milk: 1cup(250 g)
Sunflower seed oil: ½cup(100 g)
Lemon peel: 1
Baking powder: 3 ½tsp(16 g)
Fine salt: 1pinch
Powdered sugar: to taste- (for dusting)

info:
Difficulty: Easy
Prep time: 15 min
Cook time: 40 min
Serving: 6
Cost: Average

instructions:
To prepare the butterless Margherita cake, in a bowl pour in the sifted flour and cornstarch . Then add sugar , a pinch of salt .
You can grate the lemon zest and mix . Pour in the seed oil .
Add the plant-based milk , we used soy milk. Mix and then finally sift in the baking powder . Mix again to absorb.
Oil and flour a 9-inch diameter pan, pour the batter inside , bake in a preheated static oven for about 35-40 minutes at 356°F. Do the toothpick test, then remove from the oven . Let cool and then with plenty of powdered sugar !
You can store the butterless Margherita cake for 2-3 days under a glass dome.
We do not recommend freezing.
You can flavor with vanilla instead of lemon zest.
You can use the plant-based milk of your choice.
