name:
Flatbread with ham and scamorza cheese
ingredients:
Flatbread: 2- Very Flaky
Prosciutto cotto: 5.3oz(150 g)- (roasted)
Scamorza (provola) cheese: 8.1oz(230 g)
Mustard: to taste- ancient

info:
Difficulty: Very easy
Prep time: 5 min
Cook time: 2 min
Serving: 2
Cost: Low

instructions:
To prepare the flatbread with ham and scamorza, first slice the latter into 1/4 inch thick slices . Place the flatbread in a pan and heat it for 30 seconds , then flip it and place half of the scamorza slices on one side .
Cover with a lid and wait another 30 seconds until the cheese has melted . Then transfer the flatbread to a cutting board ,
place half of the ham slices , add a bit of whole grain mustard , and fold the flatbread . Prepare the second flatbread the same way and serve them.
We recommend consuming the flatbread with ham and scamorza immediately.
Enrich the flatbread with grilled zucchini!
