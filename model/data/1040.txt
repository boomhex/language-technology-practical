name:
Chicken with Zucchini Cream
ingredients:
Chicken breast: 1.1lbs(500 g)
White zucchini: 200
Leeks: 1
Vegetable broth: ¾cup(200 g)
Fresh liquid cream: 2 ¾tbsp(40 g)
Chives: to taste
Basil: to taste
Dry white wine: ¾cup(200 g)
Extra virgin olive oil: to taste
Type 00 flour: to taste
Fine salt: to taste
Black pepper: to taste

info:
Difficulty: Average
Prep time: 15 min
Cook time: 30 min
Serving: 4
Cost: Low

instructions:
To make the chicken with zucchini cream, first prepare the broth following our Vegetable broth recipe. Wash the zucchinis, then trim them and slice them into thin rounds . Remove the outer leaves of the leek and slice it thinly as well . Heat a drizzle of olive oil in a pan, then add the leek .
Add the zucchinis as well , salt and pepper, and sauté over moderate heat for 4-5 minutes. Then pour in the broth and continue cooking for 10 minutes over medium-low heat.
Turn off the heat , season with a few basil leaves and some chive stems
Now incorporate the cream , transfer the zucchinis to a tall glass and blend them with an immersion mixer to obtain a smooth cream . Set aside.
Cut the chicken breast into strips , coat it with flour carefully . Heat a drizzle of olive oil in a pan, then add the chicken strips .
Sauté them on all sides, then salt, pepper , and deglaze with white wine . Continue cooking for another 10 minutes over medium-low heat .
Add the zucchini cream , toss in the pan for about 5-8 minutes to blend and flavor. Serve the chicken with zucchini cream hot with a drizzle of raw olive oil .
Chicken with cream can be stored in the refrigerator for a couple of days.
It can also be frozen if you used fresh ingredients.
Accompany the chicken with zucchini cream with some Pilaf Rice or couscous.
