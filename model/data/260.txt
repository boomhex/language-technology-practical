name:
Pasta with sausage and black cabbage
ingredients:
Mezze Maniche Rigate pasta: 11.3oz(320 g)
Sausage: 10.6oz(300 g)
Italian kale (cavolo nero): 7.1oz(200 g)- (to clean)
White onions: 1
Garlic: 1clove
Parmigiano Reggiano PDO cheese: 3.5oz(100 g)- (to grate)
White wine: 3.4oz(100 g)
Extra virgin olive oil: to taste
Salt: to taste
Black pepper: to taste

info:
Difficulty: Easy
Prep time: 10 min
Cook time: 25 min
Serving: 4
Cost: Low

instructions:
To prepare the pasta with sausage and black cabbage, first wash the cabbage and remove the central rib , then cut it into pieces . Cook in salted boiling water for 5 minutes .
Drain and set aside both the cabbage and the cooking water. Clean and finely chop the onion , then remove the casing from the sausage and cut it into pieces.
In a pan, heat the oil, add the onion and a clove of garlic, then fry gently for 2 minutes. Add the sausage and brown over medium-high heat until golden. Now deglaze with white wine and scrape the pan, allowing all the alcohol to evaporate.
Meanwhile, cook the pasta in the reserved cabbage cooking water that you brought back to a boil . Remove the garlic from the pan and add the cabbage , then season with salt and pepper.
Stir to combine the ingredients and turn off the heat. Drain the pasta al dente and transfer it to the pan , then toss with a ladle of cooking water and grated Parmesan .
Plate , then finish with more grated Parmesan to taste and a grind of pepper. The pasta with sausage and black cabbage is ready to be enjoyed !
Pasta with sausage and black cabbage can be stored in the refrigerator, in an airtight container, for 1-2 days. When ready to eat, reheat it in a pan with a little water or oil to restore the creaminess of the sauce.
For a creamier version, you can add a tablespoon of robiola cheese at the end of cooking.
