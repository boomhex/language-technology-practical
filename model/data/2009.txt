name:
Chia pudding
ingredients:
Chia seeds: 2.1tbsp(30 g)
Almond milk: ¾cup(200 g)
Unsweetened cocoa powder: ¼tbsp(3 g)
Heather honey: 2 ½tsp(10 g)
Greek yogurt: ¾cup(200 g)
Black cherried in syrup: ⅓cup(80 g)
Mint: to taste- (for decoration)

info:
Difficulty: Easy
Prep time: 10 min
Serving: 2
Cost: Average
Note + a night of rest in the fridge

instructions:
To prepare chia pudding, start by pouring the chia seeds into a large bowl , then add the unsweetened cocoa powder , mix with a whisk and gradually pour in the almond milk ,
stirring continuously to create a homogeneous mixture. Also add the heather honey , stir again and cover with plastic wrap . Leave the mixture in the fridge overnight.
Once ready, you can assemble the chia pudding in the glass: take the chia mixture with a spoon , create a layer at the bottom of the glass , then add a layer of Greek yogurt
and pour the sour cherries with some syrup (10-11). Then continue with another layer of chia
and a layer of Greek yogurt reaching the rim of the glass . Finally, decorate with 2-3 sour cherries and some syrup and a mint leaf . Your chia pudding is ready to be served.
Chia seeds soaked with milk can be stored in the fridge for 1-2 days covered with plastic wrap.
Freezing is not recommended.
For chia pudding, you can also use rice milk. You can flavor the chia mixture with ground cinnamon. You can enrich the chia pudding with nuts of your choice.
