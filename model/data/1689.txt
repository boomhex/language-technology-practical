name:
Omelette with tomatoes and basil
ingredients:
Eggs: 10.8oz(305 g)- (about 6)
Datterino tomatoes: 1.1cups(200 g)
Basil: 2 ½tbsp(6 g)
Red onions: 1.5oz(40 g)
Parmigiano Reggiano PDO cheese: 0.2lb(50 g)- to grate
Sweet Paprika: ¾tsp
Fine salt: to taste
Extra virgin olive oil: to taste

info:
Difficulty: Very easy
Prep time: 10 min
Cook time: 15 min
Serving: 4
Cost: Low

instructions:
To make the omelette with tomatoes and basil, clean the onion and slice it thinly . Wash and dry the cherry tomatoes and then cut them into quarters . In a bowl, pour the eggs and beat them with a whisk .
Season with grated Parmesan cheese , salt , and sweet paprika .
Beat again with the whisk to combine the ingredients, then add the cherry tomatoes , the sliced onion , and the basil leaves previously cleaned with a cloth .
Mix again to evenly distribute the ingredients in the mixture . Now start cooking: heat the oil in a non-stick pan, and when it's hot, pour the mixture ,
cover with the lid and let the omelette cook for 10 minutes on moderate heat. After the 10 minutes , flip the omelette: to do this, use the lid or a plate, place the plate on the pan
and with a quick movement, flip the pan and transfer the omelette onto the plate . Now let the omelette slide back into the pan to cook the other side for 5 minutes . When fully cooked, serve the omelette with tomatoes and basil hot .
The omelette with tomatoes and basil can be stored in the fridge for one day. We do not recommend freezing it.
The omelette with tomatoes and basil is a recipe with simple flavors, but if you want to enrich it, you can try adding other tantalizing ingredients such as olives or desalted capers.
