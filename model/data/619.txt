name:
Cardamom Rice with Eggplants
ingredients:
Basmati rice: 1cup(320 g)
Eggplant: 11.3oz(320 g)- long
Cherry tomatoes: 1.67cups(250 g)
Fresh scallion: 1
Celery: 1rib
Fresh chili pepper: 1
Green olives: 1.8oz(50 g)
Caper berries: 2tsp(10 g)
Bay leaves: 2leaves
Cardamon: to taste- seeds
Ground black pepper: to taste
Fine salt: to taste

info:
Prep time: 15 min
Cook time: 45 min
Serving: 4

To prepare cardamom rice with eggplants, first, prepare the vegetables: trim the eggplants and cut them into 3/4 inch cubes , then cut the cherry tomatoes in half . Clean the spring onion and slice it thinly .
Do the same with the celery and finally, slice the chili lengthwise . In a pan, flavor the oil with the chili for a couple of minutes over low heat .
Add the spring onion and sauté for a few minutes, then add the celery and, shortly after, the eggplants .
Finally, add the cherry tomatoes and a little water , cover with a lid, and cook over medium heat for 5 minutes .
In the meantime, halve the larger caper berries and coarsely chop the green olives, removing the pits . After 5 minutes, add the caper berries and olives , then stir and adjust the salt. Cook for another 5 minutes without the lid, then remove the vegetables from the heat and keep them warm .
Meanwhile, bring a pot of water to a boil to cook the rice flavored with cardamom seeds , bay leaves, and peppercorns . Pour the basmati rice into the boiling water and cook for about 10 minutes.
After this time, drain the rice and plate, garnishing with a few spoonfuls of spicy vegetables . Finish with a drizzle of raw oil and serve your cardamom rice with eggplants !
Cardamom rice with eggplants can be stored for 2 days in the refrigerator.
You can prepare the vegetables in advance and store them in the refrigerator for 2 days.
It is preferable not to salt the rice cooking water because the vegetables are already quite salty.
