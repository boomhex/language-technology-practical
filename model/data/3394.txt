name:
Tex-mex puff pastry
ingredients:
Crispy baked puff pastry: 12
Green spicy Peperoncino: 1
Gruyere cheese: 2oz(60 g)

info:
Difficulty: Very easy
Prep time: 10 min
Cook time: 1 min
Makes: 12 pieces
Cost: Low

To prepare the tex-mex puff pastry: wash the chili pepper well , cut it into small pieces , and grate the Gruyère cheese .
Place the 12 pastries on a baking sheet and fill each one with a heap of cheese and 3-4 pieces of chili pepper . Put the filled pastries in a hot oven at 350°F just for a minute, the time it takes for the cheese to melt . Serve the tex-mex pastries immediately!
The tex-mex pastries should be eaten immediately: their simple preparation allows them to be assembled at the last moment and served just after baking!
