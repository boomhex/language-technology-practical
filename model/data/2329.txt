name:
Half sleeves with peppers and zucchinis
ingredients:
Mezze Maniche Rigate pasta: 11.3oz(320 g)
Zucchini: 2 ½cups(300 g)
Red peppers: 5.3oz(150 g)
Yellow peppers: 5.3oz(150 g)
Fine salt: to taste
Black pepper: to taste
Rosemary: to taste
Extra virgin olive oil: 4 ½tbsp(60 g)
Garlic: 1clove

info:
Difficulty: Very easy
Prep time: 10 min
Cook time: 60 min
Serving: 4
Cost: Very low

instructions:

To prepare the half sleeves with peppers and zucchinis, first wash and cook 2 whole peppers in a static oven preheated to 482°F, for about 30-40 minutes . When they are halfway through cooking, turn them with the help of kitchen tongs . Once cooked, peel them using a knife ,
then cut them into pieces and set aside. Then, focus on the zucchinis: wash them, trim them , then slice them about 1/8 inch thick using a mandoline .
Place them on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper, then salt them , pepper them, add the oil , and cook at 482°F for about 15-20 minutes. Once cooked, cut them like the peppers and set aside.
Meanwhile, cook the pasta , then in a pan, pour some oil and fry a clove of garlic . Chop the rosemary with a knife
and, when the garlic is well browned, remove it from the pan and add the chopped rosemary . Then add the peppers and zucchinis and stir occasionally to mix the flavors well.
Once cooked, combine the pasta with the vegetables , mix so that it blends well with all the ingredients , and your half sleeves with peppers and zucchinis are ready to be served!
Store the half sleeves with peppers and zucchinis for a day at most in the refrigerator, in an airtight container.
To add an extra touch of flavor to your dish, you can add anchovies melted in the pan and also some fillets to nicely garnish your dish!
