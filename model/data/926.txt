name:
Sautéed Spinach
ingredients:
Spinach: 2.2lbs(1 kg)
Extra virgin olive oil: 3.7tbsp(50 g)
Garlic: 2cloves
Fine salt: to taste

info:
Difficulty: Very easy
Prep time: 15 min
Cook time: 15 min
Serving: 4
Cost: Very low

instructions:
To prepare sautéed spinach, first wash the spinach leaves . Remove the tougher part of the spinach stalk .
Peel the garlic cloves, cut them in half and remove the inner core . Pour the extra virgin olive oil into a large pan together with the garlic cloves and heat over moderate heat . Now add the washed spinach .
Add them all to the pan even if they seem very bulky ; they will wilt quickly. Cover with the lid and cook for 5 minutes on high heat. Remove the lid, season with salt and pepper, and stir with a spatula . Continue cooking over medium heat for another 10 minutes until the sautéed spinach is cooked .
Sautéed spinach can be stored in the refrigerator for up to 4 days. If desired, they can be frozen.
For a light cooking, you can add just a drizzle of oil. Make sure to add the damp spinach, not dried, so they will cook better and not stick.
