name:
Savory Rosemary Castagnole
ingredients:
Type 00 flour: 1.6cups(200 g)
Butter: 3tbsp(40 g)- softened
Parmigiano Reggiano PDO cheese: 1oz(30 g)- to be grated
Provola cheese: 1.8oz(50 g)
Dry white wine: 1 ½tbsp(20 g)
Eggs: 1
Instant yeast for salted preparations: 2tsp(6 g)
Rosemary: to taste
Fine salt: 2pinches
Vegetable oil: to taste

info:
Difficulty: Easy
Prep time: 30 min
Cook time: 20 min
Makes: 30 pieces
Cost: Low
Note + 30 min resting of the dough in the refrigerator

instructions:
To prepare the savory rosemary castagnole, pour the flour into a large bowl, add the instant yeast , the grated Parmesan cheese , and the egg ,
then also add the softened butter , the rosemary , the white wine , and the salt.
Start mixing the ingredients with a fork , then transfer it to a lightly floured work surface and knead by hand for about 5 minutes until you get a homogeneous dough . Place the dough back into the bowl, cover with plastic wrap , and let it rest in the refrigerator for 30 minutes. Meanwhile, cut the provola cheese into cubes: you'll need about thirty.
After the resting time, divide the dough into small portions of about 12 grams each . Flatten each piece of dough on the palm of your hand and place a cube of provola cheese in the center , then close the dough and form a ball .
Heat the seed oil in a saucepan until it reaches a temperature of 320°F-340°F and fry a few castagnole at a time for a few minutes . When they are well golden, drain them with a slotted spoon and transfer them onto a sheet of paper towels. Finish frying and serve your savory rosemary castagnole !
It is recommended to consume the savory rosemary castagnole as soon as they are ready. Freezing is not recommended.
You can vary the flavor of the savory castagnole by using other spices and herbs such as parsley, thyme, paprika, or nutmeg.
