name:
Soft anchovy buns
ingredients:
Type 0 flour: 4cups(500 g)
Water: 1cup(250 g)
Anchovies in oil: 1.1oz(30 g)
Fresh brewer's yeast: 0.5oz(12 g)
Sugar: 1tsp
Fine salt: 1tsp
Vegetable oil: to taste

info:
Difficulty: Average
Prep time: 30 min
Cook time: 20 min
Makes: 13 pieces
Cost: Low
Note + 2 hours of rising

instructions:
To prepare the soft anchovy buns, start by crumbling the yeast into a bowl, pour in the room temperature water and the sugar . Mix everything well with a fork .
Transfer everything into the mixer bowl. Then add the flour , salt , and anchovy fillets, or anchovies, drained from the oil .
Attach the hook and run the mixer for about 5 minutes or until all the ingredients are well combined . Transfer to the work surface and knead for a few moments until you obtain a smooth ball .
Place the dough back into the mixer bowl and cover with plastic wrap , let it rise for about 2 hours, until it doubles in volume . Then start taking portions of 60 g each, in total you will get about 13 pieces .
Form a log about 8 inches and then twist it tightly on itself to form a snail shape .
Place the buns on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper and brush with some vegetable oil . Cover and let rest for another 15 minutes . Meanwhile, heat the oven to 350Â°F and bake the sea buns for about 20 minutes .
The sea buns can be stored at room temperature, in a plastic bag, for up to 3-4 days. If desired, they can be frozen once fully cooked and cooled.
Want to make your sea buns more special? Try adding a sprinkle of black pepper. If you like strong flavors, add a bit of black olive tapenade!
