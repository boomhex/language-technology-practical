name:
Vegan dove cake
ingredients:
Type 00 flour: 2.3cups(300 g)
Potato starch: ½cup(75 g)
Sugar: ⅓cup(75 g)
Baking powder: 3tsp(16 g)
Sunflower seed oil: ⅓cup(75 ml)
Oat beverage: 1 ¼cup(300 ml)
Unsweetened soy yogurt: 0.375cup(90 g)
Candied orange: 2.5oz(70 g)
Lemon peel: 1
Granulated sugar: to taste
Almonds: to taste- whole with the peel

instructions:

To prepare the vegan dove cake, mix flour and potato starch in a bowl , then sift them together . Do the same with the baking powder .
Incorporate the sugar and pour in the oat milk , seed oil, and soy yogurt .
Flavor the batter with grated lemon zest and diced candied oranges . Mix with a whisk .
The batter should be smooth and homogeneous . Pour it into a 1 lb dove mold and level the surface with a spatula . Garnish with sugar sprinkles .
Finally, decorate with whole almonds and bake in a preheated static oven at 375°F for 45 minutes. After this time, quickly open the oven, cover the dove with parchment paper on top, and continue cooking in ventilated mode for another 10 minutes. Do the toothpick test, and when the vegan dove cake is cooked, turn off the oven and leave it inside for 5 minutes with the oven open. After this time, take the cake out and wait for it to cool completely before cutting and serving it .
The vegan dove cake can be stored under a glass dome for a couple of days.

You can replace the candied fruit with the same amount of chocolate chips.
