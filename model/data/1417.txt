name:
Small apples filled with cream or fruit perfect for a snack
ingredients:
Gala apples: 5- baby
Whole grain flour: 0.4cup(50 g)
Nut flour: ¼cup(25 g)
Almond milk: ¼cup(40 g)
Butter: 2tbsp(30 g)- melted
Cinnamon powder: 1tsp
Hazelnuts: 0.75oz(20 g)
Orange juice: 0.42cup(100 g)
Natural plain yogurt: ½cup(125 g)
Vanilla bean: 1

info:
Difficulty: Average
Prep time: 20 min
Cook time: 45 min
Makes: 5 pieces
Cost: Low

instructions:
To make the filled small apples, first cut off the top of the apple and scoop out the pulp with a melon baller , making sure to remove the core. You should have an apple shell with its "cap" for closing .
Transfer the apple pulp to a food processor and blend it . In a bowl, pour the whole wheat flour and hazelnut flour .
Flavor with a teaspoon of cinnamon , pour in the milk , and the blended apples .
Incorporate with a spatula and finally add the hazelnuts (10-11). Place the emptied small apples in a baking dish and stuff them with the prepared mixture .
Close with the top part of the apples , drizzle with orange juice and melted butter .
Bake in a preheated static oven at 350°F for 45 minutes. Once ready, remove the filled apples from the oven and let them cool slightly . Meanwhile, pour the yogurt into a bowl and flavor it with the seeds from the vanilla pod . Pour the sauce over the apple filling .
Close with the top part and drizzle with the cooking juices . Serve the filled small apples immediately .
It is recommended to consume the filled small apples immediately.
If you like an alcoholic note, you can add rum to the filling of the filled small apples.
