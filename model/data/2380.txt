name:
Vermicelli with meat and spicy sauce
ingredients:
Vermicelli pasta: 0.7lb(320 g)
Beef: 1.1lbs(500 g)
Carrots: ¾cup(100 g)
Celery: 2.1oz(60 g)
White onions: ½cup(100 g)
Red wine: 0.4cup(100 g)
Fresh chili pepper: 2- (small)
Bay leaves: 3leaves
Rosemary: 1sprig
Peeled tomatoes: 3cups(800 g)
Fine salt: to taste
Black pepper: to taste
Garlic: 1clove

info:
Difficulty: Average
Prep time: 30 min
Cook time: 2 h
Serving: 4
Cost: Low

instructions:
To prepare vermicelli with meat and spicy sauce, prepare the How to make the soffritto . Wash all the vegetables under plenty of fresh running water, then trim them and peel the carrots with a peeler . Finely chop the celery, carrot, and onion, and cut the garlic in half .
Cut the chili pepper lengthwise with a small knife , remove the internal seeds and chop it . Take the meat and remove any fatty parts ,
cut it into slices and then into chunks . Put a non-stick pot with oil on the stove, and when it is hot, add the onion ,
the chopped carrot, celery , and garlic ; cook for 10 minutes, then add the diced meat .
Add the bay leaf, chopped rosemary , chili pepper , and deglaze with red wine .
Add the peeled tomatoes and stir with a kitchen spatula or wooden spoon ; adjust with salt and
pepper and cook for at least 2 hours, stirring occasionally. After the required time, remove the garlic and bay leaf .
Put a pot with salted water on the stove and cook the pasta . Once ready, drain it and add it directly to the sauce . Stir to flavor and finally serve your vermicelli with meat and chili sauce while hot !
You can store the vermicelli with meat and spicy sauce for a maximum of 2-3 days in an airtight container or covered with plastic wrap. Freezing is not recommended.
If you have leftovers, you can freeze the meat sauce, only if you have used fresh, non-defrosted ingredients.
To prepare your sauce, if you prefer, you can use tomato passata instead of whole peeled tomatoes.
