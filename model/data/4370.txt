name:
Chicken strips in air fryer
ingredients:
Chicken breast: 10.5oz(300 g)
Eggs: 1
Breadcrumbs: 2cups(200 g)
Parmigiano Reggiano PDO cheese: 1.8oz(50 g)- (to grate)
Paprika: to taste
Fine salt: to taste
Black pepper: to taste
Mayonnaise: to taste

info:
Difficulty: Very easy
Prep time: 15 min
Cook time: 15 min
Serving: 4
Cost: Low

instructions:
Yes to fried chicken with mayonnaise , but without the guilt! How? With the recipe for chicken strips in an air fryer, an easy and quick cooking method that avoids the use of oil, resulting in a lighter but equally crispy and golden outcome. All you have to do is coat the chicken strips with an egg and seasoned breadcrumb coating, then the air fryer will do the rest! Perfect for a homemade appetizer or a last-minute dinner, the chicken strips in the air fryer are even better when served with sauces of your choice... a tantalizing and wholesome idea for preparing chicken breast, ideal to serve as a main course to children or your health-conscious friends.

To prepare the chicken strips in the air fryer, first cut the chicken breast into strips about 0.6-0.8 inches wide and transfer them to a bowl. Put the breadcrumbs in a baking dish, then season with paprika and grated Parmesan cheese .
Add salt and pepper , then mix everything together . Pour the beaten egg into the bowl with the chicken .
Mix well , then pass the strips into the baking dish with the breadcrumbs and coat them evenly .
After breading them, place the chicken strips in the air fryer (we used a 6-liter capacity fryer) and cook at 356Â°F for 15 minutes, until they are nicely golden and crispy . Serve your air fryer chicken strips immediately with a bowl of mayonnaise or other sauces of your choice !
It is recommended to consume the chicken strips in the air fryer right away.
You can vary the coating by using the spices or herbs you prefer!
