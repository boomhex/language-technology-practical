name:
Spaghetti alla Turiddu
ingredients:
Spaghetti: 5.6oz(160 g)
Cluster tomatoes: 1 Â¼cup(250 g)
Green olives: 2.3oz(65 g)
Fine salt: to taste
Black pepper: to taste
Oregano: 1tsp- dry
Pecorino cheese: to taste- Sicilian
Oregano: to taste- fresh

info:
Difficulty: Easy
Prep time: 10 min
Cook time: 15 min
Serving: 2
Cost: Low

instructions:
To prepare Turiddu Spaghetti, cut the chili pepper in half , wash and dice the tomatoes . In a pan, heat some oil with garlic and chili pepper .
Add the tomato , salt , pepper, and dried oregano .
Cover with a lid and cook for 20 minutes on low heat . Halfway through the sauce cooking, boil the spaghetti for the time indicated on the package . When the spaghetti is al dente, remove the garlic and chili pepper from the sauce .
Add the olives , a ladle of the cooking water , and more dried oregano to taste .
Drain the spaghetti here , fragrance with fresh oregano . Mix the spaghetti with the grated Pecorino cheese with the heat off .
Mix to combine , then serve the Turiddu Spaghetti immediately with more Pecorino to taste and fresh oregano leaves .
We recommend consuming Turiddu Spaghetti immediately.
There are several variations of this pasta; some add anchovies, capers, and even toasted breadcrumbs.
These spaghetti take their name from one of the protagonists of Cavalleria rusticana , the famous theatrical play by Pietro Mascagni, inspired by the novella of the same name by Giovanni Verga.
