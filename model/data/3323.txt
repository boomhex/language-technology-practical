name:
Turnip tops with sausage
ingredients:
Broccoli rabe: 2.2lbs(1 kg)
Sausage: 1.1lbs(500 g)- spicy
Extra virgin olive oil: 3spoonfuls
Fresh chili pepper: 1
Garlic: 1clove
Fine salt: to taste

info:
Difficulty: Easy
Prep time: 30 min
Cook time: 30 min
Serving: 4
Cost: Low

instructions:
To prepare turnip tops with sausage, start by cleaning the vegetables: wash the turnip tops well and remove the tougher stems and any damaged leaves, cut the leaves into pieces while keeping the tops whole (1-2). Blanch them for 5 minutes in salted boiling water and drain well.
Meanwhile, cut the sausage into small pieces and put it to brown in a non-stick pan , adding a couple of tablespoons of the turnip tops' cooking water after a few minutes .
Cook the sausages . Peel the garlic and slice the chili pepper . In a pan, place the extra virgin olive oil, add the halved garlic clove and sliced chili pepper. Brown the garlic and then add the boiled turnip tops, sauté for a few minutes , and finally, season with salt. Serve the turnip tops with the crispy, hot sausage!
If you prefer, you can sauté the sausage in smaller pieces with garlic and chili pepper and then cook the turnip tops in the same pan!
