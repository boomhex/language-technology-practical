name:
Mini saffron and salmon wheels
ingredients:
Mini Wheels pasta: 1 ½cup(200 g)
Peas: 1 ½cup(200 g)- to shell
Salmon steaks: 6oz(170 g)
Saffron: 0.042oz(0.125 g)- (one packet)
Parsley: to taste
Fine salt: to taste
Extra virgin olive oil: to taste
Bean sprouts: to taste

info:
Difficulty: Easy
Prep time: 15 min
Cook time: 20 min
Serving: 4
Cost: Average

instructions:

To prepare the mini saffron and salmon wheels, start by placing a pot full of water on the stove for cooking the pasta, color the water with saffron . Take care of the seasoning: shell the peas (2-3),
then cook them in slightly salted boiling water for about 10 minutes so they remain crispy. Set aside the cooked peas and place a pot full of water on the stove to cook the pasta, color the water with saffron . Take care of the salmon: first cut the fillet into strips
and then into cubes . Heat the oil in a pan, add the salmon cubes , salt them, season with parsley , and sear them for just a few moments,
just until they become pink . Meanwhile, cook the pasta in the saffron-flavored water for about 6 minutes or the time indicated on the package. Once ready, drain the pasta directly into the pan with the salmon .
Add the peas , mix to season, and then serve on plates garnishing with beet sprouts . Your mini saffron and salmon wheels are ready to be enjoyed .
The mini wheels are a dish to be consumed immediately, freezing is not recommended.
As an alternative to peas, you can use zucchini, and if you can't find beet sprouts, you can replace them with soybean sprouts.
