name:
Meat Ravioli
ingredients:
Type 00 flour: 3 ¼cups(400 g)
Eggs: 4
Ground beef: 5.3oz(150 g)
Ground pork: 5.3oz(150 g)
Parmigiano Reggiano PDO cheese: ½cup(50 g)- grated
Stale bread: ¾cup(50 g)
Milk: to taste
Eggs: 1
Nutmeg: to taste
Salt: to taste
Black pepper: to taste
Butter: ½cup(100 g)
Sage: to taste
Parmigiano Reggiano PDO cheese: to taste- grated

To prepare the meat ravioli, first place the flour in a mound and put the eggs in its center. Gradually incorporate the flour with a fork 1 and then knead 2 until you obtain a smooth and homogeneous dough 3 . Wrap it with plastic wrap and let it rest in a cool, dry place for 30 minutes.
Meanwhile, focus on the filling: soak the stale bread in milk 4 . In another bowl, combine the ground beef and pork. Add the Parmesan 5 and the egg 6 .
Also add the well-squeezed bread 7 , nutmeg 8 , salt, and pepper 9 .
Mix with your hands to obtain a homogeneous mixture 10 , then transfer it to a piping bag 11 . Take the dough and dust it with flour 12 .
Roll out the dough with a pasta maker 13 or a rolling pin until it has a thickness of about 1/16-1/8 inch. Divide it in half 14 , brush one with water, then place small heaps of filling at a distance of 1-1.5 inches from each other 15 .
Place the other sheet on top, matching the edges, then use your hands to remove the air around the filling 16 and press the two sheets together well. Trim the edges with a pasta cutter, and cut to create square ravioli about 1.5 inches on each side 17 . Cook the ravioli in boiling salted water 18 for 3-4 minutes or until they float to the surface.
In a pan, melt the butter 19 over medium heat, add the sage 20 , and let it flavor until the butter becomes golden and the sage crispy. Drain the ravioli and transfer them to the pan 21 . Sauté gently for 1 minute.
Plate the ravioli, add a tablespoon of melted butter 22 , and finish with a sprinkle of grated Parmesan 23 . Your meat ravioli are ready to be enjoyed 24 !
Meat ravioli should be consumed as soon as they are ready, but if there are leftovers, you can store them in the refrigerator in a closed container for 1 day. Raw ravioli can be stored in the refrigerator covered with plastic wrap on a floured tray, making sure they do not stick together, for 1 day. If you wish to freeze them, transfer them to the freezer directly on the tray. Once frozen, you can collect them in a food bag and store them for up to 2 months.
Besides the classic butter and sage, you can serve them with a simple tomato sauce or a delicate cheese cream.
