name:
Fregola with eggplants
ingredients:
Fregola pasta: 2cups(200 g)
Eggplant: 0.9lb(400 g)
Cluster tomatoes: 0.9lb(400 g)
Garlic: 1clove
Salted capers: 2tsp(10 g)- desalted
Green olives: 1.1oz(30 g)- pitted
Extra virgin olive oil: 3spoonfuls
Fine salt: to taste
Black pepper: to taste

info:
Difficulty: Very easy
Prep time: 15 min
Cook time: 30 min
Serving: 4
Cost: Low

instructions:
To prepare fregola with eggplants, first wash and dry the eggplants, then remove the stem and cut them into cubes of 1 inch . Also, dice the tomatoes and set aside.
In a pan, pour a drizzle of oil and add the garlic , saut√© it for a few minutes, then add the eggplants . Cook for 15 minutes over a moderate heat. Add the tomatoes .
Season with salt and pepper and continue cooking. Meanwhile, slice the olives into rings . Once the eggplants have softened, add the olives .
Add the capers , stir and let it flavor for a few minutes. Cook the fregola in plenty of salted boiling water . Once cooked, drain and add it to the pan and cook just enough to let it absorb the flavors.
Plate , finish with a drizzle of oil and your fregola with eggplants is ready to be served .
Consume fregola with eggplants as soon as it's ready. If there are leftovers, you can store them in the refrigerator, in a closed container, for up to 2 days.
For a stronger flavor, you can season the fregola with a handful of Sardinian pecorino or with cubes of feta. Adding chili pepper can give a spicy note that enlivens the flavors, while fresh herbs like basil or parsley can add an aromatic freshness that completes the dish.
