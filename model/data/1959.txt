name:
Chocolate glaze
ingredients:
Dark chocolate: 5.25oz(150 g)
Whole milk: 0.42cup(100 g)
Glucose: 2 ½tbsp(30 g)

info:
Difficulty: Easy
Prep time: 10 min
Cook time: 5 min
Serving: 8
Cost: Low
Note plus cooling time (15 minutes)

instructions:
To prepare the chocolate glaze, first finely chop the dark chocolate . Then pour the milk into a saucepan , and add the glucose .
Stir with a spatula to dissolve it . Then heat everything and as soon as it reaches the boiling point, while keeping the saucepan on the heat but at a very low flame, add the chocolate and stir until it is completely melted .
You need to achieve a smooth and fluid consistency ; then transfer it into a small bowl , cover with plastic wrap , and let it cool for about 15 minutes before using it. The ideal temperature is 91°F.
The chocolate glaze can be stored in the refrigerator for up to a week. When ready to use, just heat it for a few seconds in the microwave.
If you can't find glucose, you can substitute it with honey in the same quantities.
With this chocolate glaze, you will be able to cover a 9-inch cake.
