name:
Crispy salmon on flatbreads
ingredients:
Cracker: 12
Salmon fillets: 4.2oz(120 g)
Homemade bread: 0.75oz(20 g)- stale
Lemon peel: ½
Parsley: 3tbsp(10 g)
Mint: 1sprig
Thyme: 4sprigs
Marjoram: 4sprigs
Extra virgin olive oil: 2spoonfuls
Black pepper: to taste
Fine salt: to taste

info:
Difficulty: Easy
Prep time: 25 min
Cook time: 10 min
Serving: 12
Cost: Very low

instructions:

To prepare crispy salmon on flatbreads, first take care of the bread by cutting the slices into small cubes . Put the reduced bread in a blender , then add the chopped parsley
add the zest of half a lemon and add the mint leaves , thyme ,
and marjoram leaves to the blender. Then adjust with salt and pepper and also add the two tablespoons of extra virgin olive oil .
Coarsely chop the mixture , not too finely, and transfer it to a bowl. At this point, take care of the salmon, pat the fillets dry, and cut 12 slices the same size as the flatbreads on which they will be placed . Coat the salmon fillets in the breadcrumb mixture , making sure it adheres well on all sides.
Place the breaded salmon on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper . Bake at 375°F for ten minutes in a preheated static oven (in a convection oven at 320°F for 10 minutes). Once the salmon is cooked and golden brown , remove it from the oven, let it cool slightly, and then place each fillet on a flatbread and arrange a few herb leaves on each fillet. Serve your crispy salmon on flatbreads!
To best enjoy the crispiness of the salmon, this snack should be eaten as soon as it's ready. If you really want to prepare it in advance, we recommend storing the already cooked salmon in the refrigerator inside an airtight container for up to one day. When it's time to serve, warm it up for a few minutes and place it on the flatbreads.
