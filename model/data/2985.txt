name:
Flatbreads with Asiago Potato Salad
ingredients:
Cracker: 12
Potatoes: 0.3lb(150 g)
Asiago cheese: 1.1oz(30 g)
Curry: 1pinch
Parsley: to taste
Fine salt: to taste
Black pepper: to taste
Extra virgin olive oil: to taste

info:
Difficulty: Easy
Prep time: 20 min
Cook time: 10 min
Makes: 12 pieces
Cost: Low

To prepare the flatbreads with Asiago potato salad, start by cooking the potatoes in a pressure cooker for 10 minutes. Once cooked, drain them with a colander and peel them , then place them on a cutting board and cut them in half. Then cut them into small cubes , transfer them into a bowl and season with extra virgin olive oil .
Salt and pepper the potato salad to taste and also add some chopped parsley . Now place the Asiago cheese on the cutting board and after forming sticks, cut into cubes .
Add the Asiago cubes to the potatoes and then arrange the crispy flatbreads on a surface. Dress them by spooning the Asiago potato salad on top and finish with a sprinkle of curry . Your flatbreads with Asiago potato salad are ready to be served!
You can store the flatbreads with Asiago potato salad in the fridge in an airtight container or well covered with plastic wrap for up to 1 day.
If you want to enrich your flatbreads with Asiago potato salad, top with a sprinkle of grated Parmesan cheese!
