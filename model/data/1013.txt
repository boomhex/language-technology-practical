name:
Salmon carpaccio with lime guacamole
ingredients:
Salmon fillets: 0.9lb(400 g)- already frozen
Farro rolls: 2
Lemon juice: ½
Extra virgin olive oil: to taste
Fine salt: to taste
Red pepper: to taste
Avocado: 1
Fresh scallion: 1
Lime juice: ½
Tabasco® sauce: 3drops

info:
Difficulty: Easy
Prep time: 25 min
Serving: 4
Cost: Average

instructions:
To prepare the salmon carpaccio with guacamole, let's start with the latter: after splitting the avocado by cutting it lengthwise , remove the pit by hitting it with the part near the handle of the knife and twist to detach the seed . Then slide a spoon between the pulp and the skin ,
extract the fruit and mash it with a fork in a bowl . Squeeze the lime juice . Clean the spring onion, chop it with a knife
and add it to the bowl . Also add salt and Tabasco . Mix and keep cool .
Now slice the rolls into rounds and cut the slices in half to obtain half-moons . Place them on a baking sheet with parchment paper
and toast them for a few minutes under the oven grill . Cut the salmon into slices diagonally , place on the serving plate
Drizzle the salmon with the citronette , serve with bread and guacamole and the salmon carpaccio with guacamole is ready .
