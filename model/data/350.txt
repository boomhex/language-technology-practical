name:
Catalonia in the pan
ingredients:
Chicory: 4bunches
Anchovies in oil: 3fillets
Garlic: 3cloves
Fresh chili pepper: 3
Black olives: 10- pitted
Extra virgin olive oil: to taste

info:
Difficulty: Very easy
Prep time: 5 min
Cook time: 15 min
Serving: 4
Cost: Low

instructions:
To prepare Catalonia in the pan, remove the leaves from the central stem and cut off the hardest part of each with a knife . Then plunge them into abundant salted boiling water and blanch for 3 minutes.
Drain them into a bowl and set aside. Clean the garlic, cut it in half , and slice the chili pepper .
In a pan, pour a drizzle of olive oil, add the garlic, the chili pepper , and sauté for 2 minutes. Then add the anchovy fillets and let them melt well . Add the whole olives .
Immediately add the Catalonia as well and sauté for 5 minutes . Plate up and serve your Catalonia in the pan hot .
Catalonia in the pan can be stored in the refrigerator, in a sealed container, for 2-3 days. Before serving it again, it is advisable to heat it in the pan for a few minutes, adding a drizzle of olive oil if necessary.
If you don't like anchovies, you can replace them with capers. You can enrich the initial sauté with chopped shallots. For a greener color, pass the drained vegetable in ice water and then drain it again!
