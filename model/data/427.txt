name:
Roasted Pumpkin Seeds
ingredients:
Pumpkin seeds: to taste
Paprika: to taste
Extra virgin olive oil: to taste
Fine salt: to taste

info:
Difficulty: Very easy
Prep time: 20 min
Cook time: 15 min
Makes: pieces
Cost: Very low

instructions:
To make roasted pumpkin seeds, first cut the pumpkin in half , then scoop out the seeds with a spoon and separate them from the fibers .
Rinse the seeds in a bowl with water , then drain them and transfer them to a clean cloth . Pat the seeds dry.
Spread the seeds on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper and season with paprika , salt , and oil .
Mix and bake in a preheated static oven at 392Â°F for about 10-15 minutes. Remove from the oven and serve your crunchy roasted pumpkin seeds !
Roasted pumpkin seeds can be stored at room temperature for a week, in an airtight container.
You can season your roasted pumpkin seeds with the spices you prefer!
They can be enjoyed both with and without the shell.
