name:
Potato, Speck, and Taleggio Pie
ingredients:
Potatoes: 2.2lbs(1 kg)
Speck: 5.25oz(150 g)- sliced
Taleggio cheese: 7oz(200 g)
Grana Padano PDO cheese: 2.1oz(60 g)
Fine salt: to taste
Extra virgin olive oil: to taste
Breadcrumbs: to taste

info:
Difficulty: Easy
Prep time: 20 min
Cook time: 60 min
Serving: 4
Cost: Low

instructions:
To prepare the potato, speck, and taleggio pie, boil the potatoes in plenty of water , drain them, and peel them while still hot. Slice them about 1/4 inch thick . Also, slice the taleggio and then cut it into pieces .
Grease the bottom of a 12x8 inch baking dish , then sprinkle with breadcrumbs . Lay the potato slices forming a compact layer .
Salt and sprinkle with grated Grana Padano . Lay the speck slices , then the taleggio .
Continue with another layer of potatoes, salt, season with Grana Padano , speck , and more taleggio.
Finally, on the last layer of potatoes , add Grana Padano and breadcrumbs . Cook in a preheated oven with a fan at 400Â°F for about 25 minutes, finishing with the grill function for the last few minutes to brown the surface and make the crust crispy. The potato, speck, and taleggio pie is ready to be served .
The potato, speck, and taleggio pie can be stored in the refrigerator for 2-3 days. Freezing is not recommended.
If you don't like the strong flavor of taleggio, you can replace it with a mild provolone.
