name:
Cuba libre
ingredients:
White Rum: 3.5oz(100 ml)
Coca Cola: 1cup(240 ml)
Lime juice: 4tsp(20 ml)
Lime: 4cloves
Ice: to taste

instructions:
From the heart of Cuba comes this drink that's more than just a simple mix—it's really, really a whole vibe. The Cuba Libre recipe dates back to the early 1900s, during Cuba’s war for independence. And the story? American soldiers on the island mixed local rum with Coca-Cola from the States, adding a squeeze of lime. And you know what? As they toasted, they'd shout, “Por Cuba libre!” meaning “For a free Cuba!” That's how the name stuck. Pretty simple, right? Now, when you enjoy a classic rum and Coke with lime, you're tasting a bit of Caribbean history.
What's cool is how it blends the tangy kick of fresh lime, the sweet fizz from the cola, and that smooth, moist feel from good Cuban rum. Folks say a real traditional Cuban cocktail brings out those laid-back, party vibes with just a few basic ingredients.
In Havana—where else?—you’ll see people sipping this at street corners and rooftop bars, where the sound of salsa and the smell of summer fill the air. The Cuba Libre ingredients are simple—rum, cola, and lime—but together they create something that feels like freedom in a glass. Seriously good. Some locals prefer the lighter Cuban rums for that extra crispy finish, while others like a bit more bite.
The fresh lime wedge isn't just for show—it brightens the whole drink and makes it tender and refreshing, especially on hot nights. The how to make Cuba Libre story is all about keeping things easy and celebrating with friends, much like those toasts from a century ago. For real, people worldwide love this easy rum cocktail not just for the taste but for the golden memories and good times it brings. Whether you’re by the beach or chilling at home, this tangy and sweet mix brings a little Cuban spirit right to your glass.

To prepare the Cuba Libre, start with the lime. Squeeze its juice using a citrus juicer and pass the obtained liquid through a strainer . Cut the lime into wedges to use for garnishing the cocktail.
In a highball glass, add ice cubes , pour in half of the lime juice , and also add half of the white rum .
Now, you only need to pour the Coca-Cola . With our quantities, you will get 2 cocktails. Garnish and scent your cocktail with lime wedges , and you can already enjoy your refreshing Cuba Libre !
The Cuba Libre should be consumed immediately.
Many non-alcoholic products are available on the market, allowing everyone to enjoy the taste of cocktails. Look for rum made with 0% alcohol and enjoy your Virgin Cuba Libre.
