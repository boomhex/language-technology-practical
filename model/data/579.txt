name:
Zucchini omelette in the microwave
ingredients:
Eggs: 3
Zucchini: 1 ⅓cup(170 g)
Grana Padano PDO cheese: 0.9oz(25 g)- grated
Thyme: to taste
Extra virgin olive oil: to taste
Salt: to taste
Black pepper: to taste

To prepare the zucchini omelette in the microwave, first thinly slice the zucchini into rounds about 1/8 inch thick . Transfer the zucchini to a 20 cm (about 8 inches) round baking dish suitable for microwave cooking .
Season with oil , salt , and pepper .
Add the thyme and cook in the microwave at 650 W for 3 minutes. Meanwhile, crack the eggs into a bowl, then season with salt and pepper .
Beat vigorously with a hand whisk or a fork , then add the grated Grana Padano DOP and mix one last time .
Pour the eggs into the baking dish with the zucchini and cook at 650 W for 3 minutes, then switch to grill mode and cook for another 3 minutes . The zucchini omelette in the microwave is ready to be served !
The zucchini omelette in the microwave can be stored in the fridge, in a closed container, for 2 days.
You can enrich the omelette using the spices you like best.
If you want, you can add finely chopped onion or chives for a stronger flavor!
