name:
Mango and passion fruit yogurt muffins
ingredients:
Type 00 flour: 2 ½cups(300 g)
Eggs: 6.4oz(180 g)- (about 3 medium)
Sugar: ¾cup(170 g)
Yogurt with fruit: (150 g)
Baking powder: 3 ¼tsp(16 g)
Butter: 9tbsp(130 g)
Lemon peel: 1

info:
Difficulty: Easy
Prep time: 15 min
Cook time: 30 min
Makes: 12 pieces

instructions:
To prepare the mango and passion fruit yogurt muffins, let the butter sit out of the fridge for about 30 minutes to soften. Then, place it in a large bowl and beat it with the sugar until you get a creamy mixture. Gradually add the eggs .
Continue beating . Once incorporated, add the hay milk yogurt with mango and passion fruit , and continue mixing to blend everything, then flavor with lemon zest .
Sift and gradually add the flour and baking powder , always mixing with the beaters until you get a nice thick batter . Use a spatula to transfer it into a piping bag .
Distribute 2.8 oz (approx. 1/3 cup) in the muffin cups placed in a muffin tray : with these quantities, you will get 12 muffins. Bake in a preheated static oven at 350°F for about 30 minutes. Once they are golden, take them out of the oven and let them cool at room temperature, before enjoying your mango and passion fruit yogurt muffins.
The mango and passion fruit yogurt muffins can be stored for 2-3 days under a glass dome.
They can also be frozen once completely cooled.


You can add mango and passion fruit pieces into the batter for an even more exotic and fresh effect!
