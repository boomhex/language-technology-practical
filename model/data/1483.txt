name:
Buckwheat penne with yogurt and olives
ingredients:
Buckwhat penne di grano saraceno: 11.3oz(320 g)
Natural plain yogurt: 1cup(250 g)
Black olives: 1.4oz(40 g)- pitted
Extra virgin olive oil: 2tbsp(30 g)
Garlic: 1clove
Sweet Paprika: 1spoonful
Fine salt: to taste

info:
Difficulty: Easy
Prep time: 10 min
Cook time: 15 min
Serving: 4
Cost: Average

To prepare the buckwheat penne with yogurt and olives, first put a pot full of salted water on the stove and bring it to a boil , it will be used for cooking the pasta. Then, take care of the seasoning: cut the olives into thin slices , peel the garlic and chop it with a knife .
Meanwhile, cook the pasta for the time indicated on the package . Pour the oil and chopped garlic into a pan and sauté for a few minutes over low heat to add flavor , then add the yogurt
and the olives , also add the paprika and cook for about a minute stirring with a wooden spoon, then remove the sauce from the heat. Pour a few tablespoons of sauce onto the serving dish
Add the drained pasta as well and cover with more sauce . The buckwheat penne is ready to be served .
We recommend consuming the buckwheat penne with yogurt and olives immediately.
If you do not like chopped garlic, you can add it whole and then remove it after browning it in oil.
