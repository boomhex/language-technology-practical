name:
Ricotta ring cake with honey and pine nuts
ingredients:
Cow's milk ricotta cheese: 1cup(250 g)
Wildflower honey: ½cup(150 g)
Pine nuts: 8tbsp(80 g)
Sunflower seed oil: 2 ½tbsp(40 g)
Eggs: 4- medium
Type 00 flour: 2cups(280 g)
Baking powder: 3 ½tsp(16 g)
Powdered sugar: to taste

info:
Difficulty: Easy
Prep time: 15 min
Cook time: 35 min
Serving: 8
Cost: Low

instructions:
To make the ricotta ring cake with honey and pine nuts, first soften the ricotta with honey in a bowl making it creamy, add the vegetable oil and mix the mixture with a spatula .
Add the whole eggs to the ricotta mixture and mix with electric beaters for about 5 minutes , the eggs should be completely absorbed .
At this point, incorporate the sifted flour and baking powder , using the electric beaters for a few more seconds .
Continue mixing with the spatula to make the mixture homogeneous . Butter and flour a 8-inch ring mold, pour the batter here , tap the mold to distribute it evenly .
Distribute the pine nuts on top and bake the cake in a preheated static oven at 356°F for about 35 minutes. Once cooked, remove the ricotta ring cake with honey and pine nuts from the oven , let it cool slightly before unmolding and serving it .
Store the ricotta ring cake with honey and pine nuts under a glass dome for 3-4 days. It can be frozen after baking.
To give a more pronounced aroma, you can add lemon zest or the seeds of a vanilla pod to the batter.
