name:
Arracanato Cod
ingredients:
Desalted cod: 1.8lbs(800 g)
Stale bread: 2 ½cups(150 g)- (only crumb)
Garlic: 1clove
Pine nuts: 2tbsp(20 g)
Lemon juice: 1
Raisins: 3tbsp(30 g)
Oregano: to taste
Breadcrumbs: 1spoonful
Extra virgin olive oil: to taste

info:
Difficulty: Easy
Prep time: 10 min
Cook time: 35 min
Serving: 4
Cost: Low

instructions:
To prepare arracanato cod, soak the raisins in cold water after rinsing them . Then take the already desalted cod, rinse it carefully under running water, pat dry with paper towels, then cut it into pieces and proceed to the seasoning: Crumble the stale bread into small pieces, gather it in a bowl with the pine nuts
the previously soaked and then squeezed raisins , the oregano , grated garlic ,
a good drizzle of oil and a bit of salt to taste . Mix well with a spoon to blend the flavors. On the bottom of the baking dish, squeeze the juice of a lemon
and also add about two tablespoons of water . Place the cod pieces and season them with the previously prepared breadcrumb mixture ,
sprinkle with some breadcrumbs and a drizzle of oil . Bake in a static oven at 350°F for about 35 minutes. Once ready, take out your arracanato cod and enjoy it while still hot!
You can store arracanato cod for 2-3 days.
Freezing is not recommended.
Instead of water, you could drizzle the bottom with white wine.
