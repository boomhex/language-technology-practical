name:
Stuffed Sardines
ingredients:
Sardine: 16- about 500-600g
Eggs: 1
Breadcrumbs: to taste
Genoese pesto (Basil sauce): 3 ½tbsp(50 g)
Peanut seed oil: to taste

info:
Difficulty: Easy
Prep time: 30 min
Cook time: 10 min
Serving: 4
Cost: Low

To start, you will need to How to Clean Anchovies . Remove the heads and guts of the sardines, then open them like a book and remove the central bone .
Rinse them quickly and place them on some kitchen paper to absorb excess water, then pat them dry . Next, proceed to prepare the homemade Genovese pesto. Take one sardine and place it with the skin facing down; place a bit of pesto in the center then place the second sardine on top with the skin facing outward .
In a shallow plate, beat an egg with a fork and, in another, pour some breadcrumbs. Dip the "sardine sandwiches" in the beaten egg and then in the breadcrumbs , then fry them in hot but not boiling oil until they are golden on both sides. Once golden, place the fried sardines on some kitchen paper to drain, salt them, and serve them hot.
If you wish, instead of sardines, you can also make this recipe with anchovies. As an alternative to pesto, you can use a mixture of parsley, garlic, and pepper, and to make the dish even richer, add a piece of caciocavallo, provola, or mozzarella.
