name:
Mini savory muffins with ricotta and speck
ingredients:
Cow's milk ricotta cheese: 5.3oz(150 g)
Speck: 4.25oz(120 g)- (12 slices)
Type 00 flour: 1 ½cup(170 g)
Eggs: 2
Grana Padano PDO cheese: 2spoonfuls- to grate
Instant yeast for salted preparations: 2tsp(10 g)
Chives: to taste
Vegetable oil: ¼cup(50 g)
Black pepper: to taste
Fine salt: to taste
Extra virgin olive oil: to taste

info:
Difficulty: Easy
Prep time: 15 min
Cook time: 25 min
Makes: 24 pieces
Cost: Low

instructions:
To make the mini savory ricotta and speck muffins, place the two slightly beaten eggs in a bowl , add the well-drained cow's milk ricotta and incorporate it by mixing with a hand whisk, then lightly salt ;
season with ground pepper and also add the baking powder for savory preparations . Flavor the dough with chives by cutting the greenest part of the stems with scissors .
Now incorporate the grated Grana cheese and, gradually, add the flour little by little, alternating with the seed oil (8-9).
Mix the mixture well . Once ready, transfer the batter into a disposable pastry bag (11-12).
Grease a 24 mini muffin mold with olive oil (a 12 mini muffin mold will also work, and you can then prepare a second batch) , brush thoroughly , take the slices of speck and cut them in half to line each cavity with each half ; squeeze the batter into each space up to 3/4 full .
squeeze the batter into each space up to 3/4 full . Once the tray is filled , bake the mini muffins in a preheated static oven at 350°F for 25 minutes (before taking them out, check that they are well browned on the surface). At the end of cooking, remove the mini savory ricotta and speck muffins from the oven and let them cool slightly before serving .
Store the ricotta and speck muffins under a glass dome for 1-2 days. Alternatively, you can freeze them once fully cooled.
Want to make your mini savory ricotta and speck muffins even more tantalizing? Add sun-dried tomatoes, black olives, or capers to the dough!
