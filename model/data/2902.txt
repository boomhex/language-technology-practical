name:
Vanilla ice cream with crunchy nuts
ingredients:
Fresh liquid cream: 2.1cups(500 ml)
Condensed milk: 1cup(297 g)
Sugar: 1cup(200 g)
Walnut kernels: 3.5oz(100 g)
Vanilla bean: 1

info:
Difficulty: Easy
Prep time: 10 min
Cook time: 10 min
Serving: 5
Cost: Low

instructions:

To make vanilla ice cream with crunchy nuts, start by placing the condensed milk in a large bowl , then take the vanilla pod, cut it lengthwise with a sharp knife , and extract the seeds
and add them to the condensed milk , mix with a whisk , and set aside. In another bowl, pour the fresh liquid cream
and whip it to stiff peaks , incorporate the vanilla-flavored condensed milk into the cream , and gently mix with a spatula to avoid deflating the cream until you obtain a homogeneous mixture.
Butter a high-edged mold or baking pan, a loaf pan is perfect for this preparation, line the edges and bottom with parchment paper , and pour the vanilla ice cream inside . Place the mixture in the freezer to set for 6-8 hours .
After the indicated time, your ice cream will be ready, focus on garnishing with the nuts. Place a small pot on the stove, pour in the granulated sugar , and melt over low heat until the sugar becomes dark and liquid. At this point, turn off the heat and dip the nuts for a few moments to coat them with the caramel ,
remove the nuts with kitchen tongs and place them on a tray covered with parchment paper . Now you can compose your ice cream cups: take a glass ice cream bowl and put a first layer of caramelized nuts ,
then add a scoop of ice cream and continue layering, alternating the ice cream with the nuts and caramel . Your vanilla ice cream with crunchy nuts is ready to be enjoyed! .
You can store the vanilla ice cream in the freezer in an airtight container for up to 1 week.
As an alternative to nuts, you can use almonds or Hazelnut Ice Cream . To make the ice cream even more delicious, you can add dark chocolate chips to the preparation.
