name:
Calabrian Stroncatura with Cherry Tomatoes and Anchovies
ingredients:
Stroncatura: 11.3oz(320 g)
Cherry tomatoes: 4cups(600 g)
Anchovies: 10- in oil
Taggiasca olives: 3tbsp(40 g)
Breadcrumbs: ½cup(50 g)
Garlic: 3cloves
Fresh chili pepper: 1
Extra virgin olive oil: to taste
Salt: to taste
Basil: to taste

info:
Difficulty: Very easy
Prep time: 10 min
Cook time: 30 min
Serving: 4
Cost: Very low

instructions:
To prepare the Calabrian stroncatura with cherry tomatoes and anchovies, first slice the chili pepper and quarter the cherry tomatoes . In a pan, pour a drizzle of olive oil and add 2 cloves of garlic in their skins, crushed .
Add the chili pepper and sauté for a few minutes. Add the anchovies and let them melt over low heat .
Add the cherry tomatoes , cover with a lid , and cook over medium heat for 10 minutes. Then add the Taggiasca olives , some fresh basil, and cook for another 10 minutes.
Meanwhile, in another pan, pour a drizzle of olive oil and add the other 2 cloves of garlic, still in their skins, crushed. Sauté for a few minutes, then add the breadcrumbs , cook until golden , then remove the garlic and set aside.
Cook the stroncatura in plenty of boiling salted water , drain it, transfer it to the pan with the sauce , and toss . If necessary, you can add some pasta cooking water.
Plate it, add the breadcrumbs , finish with oil-packed anchovy fillets, cut into diamond shapes of about 0.8 inches and fresh basil . Your Calabrian stroncatura with cherry tomatoes and anchovies is ready to be served .
The Calabrian stroncatura with cherry tomatoes and anchovies should be consumed immediately, but if any is left over, you can store it in the refrigerator, in a sealed container, for 1-2 days.
To intensify the Mediterranean flavors, you can add capers. If you prefer a fresher and citrusy flavor, you can add the grated zest of a lemon after plating.
