name:
Pasta with ricotta and cherry tomatoes
ingredients:
Cellentani pasta: 11.3oz(320 g)
Cherry tomatoes: 3.3cups(500 g)
Datterino tomatoes: 2cups(300 g)
Cow's milk ricotta cheese: 1cup(200 g)
Spring onions: 2.1oz(60 g)
Pecorino Romano PDO cheese: 1.8oz(50 g)- to grate
Basil: to taste
Extra virgin olive oil: to taste
Fine salt: to taste
Black pepper: to taste

To prepare the pasta with ricotta and cherry tomatoes, first cut the scallion into slices . Halve both the cherry tomatoes and the datterini .
In a saucepan, pour the oil and the scallion and let it stew for a couple of minutes, then add the cherry tomatoes and the datterini .
Salt and pepper , stir , and cook over medium heat with the lid on for about 10 minutes . In the meantime, cook the pasta in boiling salted water.
After the cooking time for the cherry tomatoes, turn off the heat and set aside some whole cherry tomatoes for plating . Blend the rest of the cherry tomatoes with an immersion blender , then sift the mixture through a fine-mesh sieve .
Pour the sauce obtained into the pan with the heat off and add the cow's milk ricotta . Scent with fresh basil and give it a good stir to mix everything .
Drain the pasta al dente and transfer it directly into the sauce . Mix with Pecorino Romano , a sprinkle of pepper, and your pasta with ricotta and cherry tomatoes is ready to be served !
Pasta with ricotta and cherry tomatoes can be stored in the refrigerator for one day.
Passing the sauce through the sieve is essential to remove the cherry tomato skins and obtain a smooth and silky cream.
