name:
Belgian Carrot Stoemp
ingredients:
Potatoes: 1.3lbs(600 g)
Carrots: 2.3cups(300 g)
Smoked pancetta: 3.5oz(100 g)- diced
Butter: 3 Â½tbsp(50 g)
Fresh liquid cream: 3.3tbsp(50 ml)
Fine salt: to taste
Black pepper: to taste
Nutmeg: to taste

info:
Difficulty: Easy
Prep time: 15 min
Cook time: 20 min
Serving: 4
Cost: Low

instructions:
Peel the carrots and potatoes ; cut the potatoes into pieces and the carrots into slices , then boil them in salted water .
In a pan, fry the diced bacon until crispy , then drain it and set it aside.
When the vegetables are ready to be mashed, drain them well and place them in a bowl; with the help of a fork, mash them until they become a puree. Add the butter, mix, and then add the crispy bacon , grated nutmeg, pepper, and finally add the cream ; after mixing all the ingredients, serve the carrot stoemp immediately.
Instead of bacon, you can add crumbled sausage or diced ham to the stoemp.
