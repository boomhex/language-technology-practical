name:
Dessert with Annurca Apples and Zabaglione
ingredients:
Annurca apples: 6
Type 00 flour: 150
Water: ¾cup(200 g)
Rolled oats: ⅓cup(30 g)
Cookies: 2.1oz(60 g)- crumbled
Almond slices: to taste
Powdered sugar: to taste
Egg yolks: 2
Sugar: 3 ¼tbsp(40 g)
Marsala wine: 1.4tbsp(20 g)
Sugar: ¾cup(150 g)
Whole milk: ½cup(120 g)
Fine salt: ½tsp
Butter: 3 ½tbsp(50 g)

To prepare the dessert with Annurca apples and zabaglione, peel the apples and use a knife to remove the core, leaving a hole in the center . In a bowl, mix the crumbled cookies with oats. In another bowl, gradually mix flour and water .
Stir well with a whisk to create a smooth, lump-free batter . Dip the apples first in the flour and water batter .
Coat the apples with the cookie and oat crumble . Place the apples on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper .

Decorate the surface with almond flakes . Bake in a static oven at 350°F for 45-50 minutes, checking occasionally, as the cooking time depends on how juicy or not the apple is. Meanwhile, prepare the zabaglione by placing the eggs and sugar in a bowl , mix to combine. Gradually add the Marsala wine .
Place in a bain-marie, continuing to stir until it thickens . If you have a thermometer, it should reach a temperature of 181°F . When the cream has thickened, remove from heat and let cool. Prepare the salted caramel: heat the sugar in a saucepan and the milk in another .
When the caramel has a golden color, add the milk and stir. Turn off the heat and continue stirring to thicken, add the salt and butter .
Mix and set aside to cool . Remove the baked apples from the oven and place them on a serving plate, fill them with the zabaglione .
Decorate with salted caramel and a dusting of powdered sugar . The dessert with Annurca apples and zabaglione is ready to be enjoyed .
We recommend consuming the dessert with Annurca apples and zabaglione immediately. Freezing is not recommended.
To flavor the zabaglione, you can use another fortified wine such as Vin Santo.
