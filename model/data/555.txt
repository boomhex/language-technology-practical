name:
Spicy Shrimp
ingredients:
Shrimps: 8
Red peppers: ⅓cup(50 g)
Green peppers: ⅓cup(50 g)
Onions: ½cup(50 g)
Tomato purée: 3spoonfuls
Hot sauce: 2tsp
Potato starch: 1tsp
Roasted salted peanuts: ⅓cup(50 g)
Sunflower seed oil: to taste
Fine salt: to taste

info:
Difficulty: Easy
Prep time: 10 min
Cook time: 10 min
Serving: 2
Cost: Average

To prepare spicy shrimp, first clean the shrimp by removing the head, shell, and extracting the intestine with a toothpick. In the wok, heat the vegetable oil , when it's hot enough add the shrimp and fry them .
Once golden , drain them on a tray lined with absorbent paper and set aside. Clean the green bell peppers and cut them into pieces about 0.5 inch .
Do the same with the red bell peppers and the onions .
Heat a little more oil in the clean wok , add the vegetables and let them cook for a few minutes. Add the tomato sauce .
Flavor with the spicy sauce and also add the shrimp . In a small bowl, mix the cornstarch with 2 tablespoons of water .
Stir and pour the sauce into the wok , mix and add a drizzle of vegetable oil , let it season for a few more moments until it thickens.
Finally, add the peanuts and adjust the salt . The spicy shrimp is ready to be enjoyed .
It is recommended to serve the spicy shrimp immediately or store them in the refrigerator, in an airtight container, for up to one day. Freezing is not recommended.
Accompany the spicy shrimp with steamed white rice for a tasty one-dish meal.
The spicy sauce is available in Asian grocery stores.
