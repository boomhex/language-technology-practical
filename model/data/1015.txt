name:
Flatbread with Parma ham and mascarpone
ingredients:
Flatbread: 2- Romagnole PGI Rimini-style
Prosciutto crudo: 5.3oz(150 g)
Mascarpone cheese: 1cup(250 g)
Almonds: 1.8oz(50 g)

info:
Difficulty: Easy
Prep time: 10 min
Cook time: 10 min
Serving: 2
Cost: Low

To prepare the flatbread with Parma ham and mascarpone, toast the almonds, which will be the crunchy note of the filling. Toast them in a pan without adding fats, over medium-low heat . Chop them with a knife . Heat one of the two flatbreads in a preheated pan, wait 30 seconds, then flip it to warm the other side.
Now, spread half of the mascarpone on half of the flatbread , cover with a lid, and wait another minute . Transfer the flatbread onto a cutting board ,
add a few slices of Parma ham and half of the toasted almonds . Fold it in half , prepare the other flatbread in the same way, and enjoy them while still warm!
It is recommended to consume the flatbread with Parma ham and mascarpone immediately.
If you prefer, you can enrich the flatbread with sun-dried tomatoes, drained and cut into strips!
