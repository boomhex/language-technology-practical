name:
Pumpkin cream with curry and coconut milk
ingredients:
Delica pumpkin: 1.3lbs(600 g)- (to be cleaned)
Potatoes: 7oz(200 g)
Carrots: 5.3oz(150 g)- (to be cleaned)
Leeks: 7oz(200 g)- (to clean)
Onions: 5.3oz(150 g)
Coconut milk: 0.4cup(100 g)
Garlic: 2cloves
Curry: to taste
Rosemary: to taste
Sage: to taste
Bay leaves: to taste
Vegetable broth: to taste
Extra virgin olive oil: to taste
Fine salt: to taste
Black pepper: to taste
Homemade bread: 8slices
Provola cheese: to taste

info:
Difficulty: Easy
Prep time: 30 min
Cook time: 40 min
Serving: 4
Cost: Low

instructions:
To make the pumpkin cream with curry and coconut milk, first prepare the vegetable broth, then clean the vegetables: cut the pumpkin in half, remove the seeds and inner strings, and slice it without peeling . Peel and chop the carrots into chunks , then do the same with the potatoes .
Clean and roughly chop the leek and onion . Arrange all the vegetables on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper, then add the garlic cloves .
Season with rosemary, sage, and bay leaf , then drizzle with olive oil , salt, and pepper . Bake in a preheated static oven at 356Â°F for 30 minutes.
Once cooked, remove the vegetables from the oven and peel the pumpkin . Transfer all the vegetables to a large pot .
Pour in the broth and cook for about ten minutes . Meanwhile, cut the bread into slices .
Heat a pan with a drizzle of oil, then place the slices of bread and toast on both sides . Add some slices of provolone on half of the bread slices , then cover with the others and let the cheese melt .
After the cooking time, turn off the heat and blend the vegetables with an immersion blender to obtain a smooth and homogeneous cream. Add the coconut milk and mix well .
Finally, add the curry and stir again. Plate and garnish the pumpkin cream with coconut milk . Your pumpkin cream with curry and coconut milk is ready to be enjoyed with the cheese bread !
The pumpkin cream with curry and coconut milk can be stored in the refrigerator for one day in an airtight container.
It can be frozen.
If you like spices, you can further flavor the pumpkin cream and coconut milk with cinnamon, turmeric, or ginger!
