name:
Gingerbread latte
ingredients:
Whole milk: 1 ¾cup(400 g)
Coffee: 5.6oz(160 g)- (from the moka pot)
Brown sugar: 0.8cup(160 g)
Water: 7tbsp(100 g)
Cinnamon powder: to taste
Powdered ginger: to taste
Unsweetened cocoa powder: to taste

To prepare the gingerbread latte, first make the syrup: pour the brown sugar into a saucepan , then add the cinnamon and ginger .
Add the water and mix the ingredients with a whisk . Place the saucepan on the stove and heat over medium-low heat until it boils, then cook for another 3 minutes until the mixture reaches a syrupy consistency . Turn off the heat and set aside.
Meanwhile, froth the milk using a frother until you get a soft and creamy foam . Finally, prepare the coffee. To assemble the drink, first pour the syrup into the glasses , then add the coffee .
Finally, garnish with the frothed milk and a sprinkle of unsweetened cocoa . Your gingerbread latte is ready to be enjoyed !
The gingerbread latte should be consumed immediately.
For an even more indulgent touch, you can decorate the gingerbread latte with a dollop of whipped cream on top!
