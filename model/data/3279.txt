name:
Spinach Roman Style
ingredients:
Spinach: 10cups(1 kg)
Butter: 3.5oz(100 g)
Pine nuts: ½cup(80 g)
Raisins: 0.67cup(100 g)

info:
Difficulty: Easy
Prep time: 15 min
Cook time: 10 min
Serving: 4
Cost: Low
Note plus the time needed to soften the raisins

instructions:

To prepare Roman-style spinach, soak the raisins in a small bowl of warm water for at least half an hour ; in the meantime, clean the spinach and wash it under fresh running water, then drain it well . Melt the butter in a non-stick pan ,
add the spinach , salt it , and let it soften for a few minutes over moderate heat, covered with a lid .
At this point, drain the raisins well, squeeze them , and add them to the spinach , add the pine nuts , stir, adjust the salt. Sauté everything for a couple of minutes, and your Roman-style spinach is ready!
Store the Roman-style spinach in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to one day.
If you can't find fresh spinach, you can use pre-packaged frozen spinach in the same amount.
