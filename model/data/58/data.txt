name:
Very soft carrot cake
ingredients:
Carrots: 1 ½cup(250 g)- (to clean)
Type 00 flour: 1 ¼cup(150 g)
Potato starch: 0.4cup(50 g)
Brown sugar: ½cup(100 g)
Eggs: 2
Sunflower seed oil: 0.4cup(80 g)
Baking powder: 1.1tbsp(16 g)
Powdered turmeric: to taste
Greek yogurt: 0.6cup(150 g)
Powdered sugar: ¼cup(50 g)
Vanilla extract: 1tsp

To prepare the very soft carrot cake, first peel and grate the carrots using a coarse grater 1 . In a bowl, place the eggs and brown sugar 2 , then beat with an electric mixer 3 .
When you have obtained a light and frothy mixture, add the grated carrots 4 and turmeric 5 , then mix again with the mixer 6 .
Pour in the vegetable oil 7 and mix. At this point, add the baking powder 8 , corn starch, and flour that you have previously sifted 9 .
Incorporate the powders with the mixer 10 until you obtain a homogeneous mixture 11 . Pour the batter into a 9-inch round pan lined with parchment paper 12 .
Bake in a preheated static oven at 350°F for about 35-40 minutes 13 . Check the baking with a toothpick, if it comes out clean, remove the cake from the oven and let it cool completely in the pan 14 . Meanwhile, prepare the frosting: pour the powdered sugar into a bowl 15 .
Add the Greek yogurt 16 and vanilla extract 17 , then mix with the electric mixer 18 .
You should obtain a smooth and homogeneous cream 19 . Once the cake has completely cooled, spread the frosting over the entire surface with the help of a spatula 20 . Your very soft carrot cake is ready to be enjoyed 21 !
The very soft carrot cake without frosting can be stored at room temperature for 2-3 days, covered with a dome. It can be frozen, whole or in portions.
The yogurt frosting can be stored separately in the refrigerator for 1-2 days.
Turmeric gives a nice golden color to the very soft carrot cake, but if you prefer, you can omit it.
