name:
Vegetable Peel Chips
ingredients:
Potato peels: to taste
Beetroot peels: to taste
Carrot peels: to taste
Sweet potato peels: to taste
Extra virgin olive oil: to taste
Fine salt: to taste
Sweet Paprika: to taste
Rosemary: to taste
Black pepper: to taste

info:
Difficulty: Very easy
Prep time: 15 min
Cook time: 15 min
Makes: pieces
Cost: Very low

instructions:
To prepare vegetable peel chips, first, wash the vegetables thoroughly, then dry them and peel with a vegetable peeler . Dry the peels thoroughly again, transfer them to a bowl, and season with paprika and pepper .
Salt , season with oil , and flavor with rosemary . Mix very well to distribute the flavors.
Place the peels on a baking sheet and bake in a preheated static oven at 392°F for at least 15 minutes or until they become crunchy . The times may vary from oven to oven. Remove from the oven and let the vegetable peel chips cool before serving .
Vegetable peel chips can be stored at room temperature for 4-5 days.
The thinner the peels, the crunchier the chips will be!
