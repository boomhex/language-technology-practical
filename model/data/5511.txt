name:
Pan-Seared Prawns
ingredients:
Shrimp: 1.3lbs(600 g)
Garlic: 2cloves
Lemon peel: 1
Lemon juice: 2tbsp(40 g)
Brandy: 1oz(30 ml)
Fresh chili pepper: 2
Parsley: 1tuft
Extra virgin olive oil: 2tbsp(30 g)
Fine salt: to taste

info:
Difficulty: Easy
Prep time: 15 min
Cook time: 5 min
Serving: 4
Cost: High

instructions:
Pan-seared prawns are an easy and quick fish main course, ideal for seafood lovers! Prawns, in fact, require a short cooking time to keep their meat tender, and this quick recipe will bring out their delicate flavor. How? With a spicy touch of chili pepper, the aroma of lemon, and a splash of brandy for a spectacular flambé effect! So fresh and light, prawns prepared this way are perfect for a summer lunch, perhaps accompanied by a side of vegetables also cooked in a pan.

To prepare pan-seared prawns, start by cleaning the prawns: cut the legs with scissors , then gently remove the shell, leaving the tail and head attached. Use a toothpick to remove the black vein, the intestine . Wash and chop a bunch of parsley with a knife and set aside .
Slice the chili pepper into rings , you can decide whether to keep the seeds or not. Squeeze the juice of one lemon . Now heat the oil in a pan, then sauté the peeled garlic and chili pepper .
After a few moments, place the prawns in the pan and cook them for 2 minutes on each side over high heat. Deglaze with the brandy , slightly tilt the pan and let it flambé until the flame extinguishes on its own. Add the grated lemon zest .
Season with a pinch of salt, the chopped parsley , and the lemon juice , cook for another couple of minutes. Serve the pan-seared prawns hot .
We recommend consuming the pan-seared prawns immediately.
You can substitute brandy with rum, marsala, or cognac.
