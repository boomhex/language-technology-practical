name:
Negroni cocktail
ingredients:
Campari: 1oz(30 ml)
Gin: 1oz(30 ml)
Vermouth: 1oz(30 ml)- red
Oranges: ½slice
Ice: to taste

info:
Difficulty: Very easy
Prep time: 5 min
Serving: 1

instructions:
To prepare the Negroni cocktail, start by pouring ice into a low tumbler and fill the glass up 3/4 full . Measure the gin and pour .
Do the same with the red vermouth by measuring it and pouring . Finally measure and pour the Campari .
Stir and garnish with half a slice of orange . Your Negroni cocktail is ready !
We recommend drinking the Negroni cocktail immediately.
The secret to the Negroni is its perfect balance of ingredients: thanks to the 3 equal parts rule, you can’t go wrong !
