name:
Quinoa eggplant and mint
ingredients:
Quinoa: 1 ¼cup(200 g)
Eggplant: 9oz(250 g)
Cherry tomatoes: 1 ¾cup(250 g)
Red onions: 1.8oz(50 g)
Water: 3tbsp(40 g)
Extra virgin olive oil: 0.7tbsp(10 g)
Arugula: 1cup(20 g)
Feta cheese: 2oz(60 g)
Mint: to taste
Fine salt: to taste
Black pepper: to taste

info:
Difficulty: Very easy
Prep time: 15 min
Cook time: 10 min
Serving: 4

instructions:
To prepare the quinoa eggplant and mint, start with the eggplants: wash and trim the eggplants, slice them , then dice them . Also, wash the cherry tomatoes and cut them into 4 parts ;
Clean the red onion and slice it ; in a pan, pour a drizzle of oil and add the onion, cook over medium-high heat; pour water to facilitate cooking and prevent burning, stirring occasionally. After 5-6 minutes, add the eggplants
and the cherry tomatoes, keeping the heat medium-high; salt and pepper to taste . It will take about 5-6 minutes on high heat, stirring occasionally. When the seasoning is ready, flavor with mint leaves with the heat off . Meanwhile, rinse the quinoa, then cook it in boiling water ; follow the times indicated on the package.
Once ready, drain it using a sieve and cool it under running cold water . Assemble the quinoa eggplant and mint recipe in a jar with a capacity of 70 cl by first pouring a layer of quinoa , then the eggplants and cherry tomatoes .
Continue with the feta and the arugula previously washed and patted dry . Your quinoa eggplant and mint recipe in a jar is ready to be served , also transported as a lunch break meal!
Store the quinoa eggplant and mint in the refrigerator for 1 day.
Freezing is not recommended.
Add a crunchy note with sliced almonds or pistachios, flavor with basil if you prefer, and raw cherry tomatoes along with grated carrots and cucumbers for a decidedly light and even fresher version!
