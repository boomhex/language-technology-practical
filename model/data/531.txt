name:
Pasta and beans with black cabbage
ingredients:
Ditalini: 11.3oz(320 g)
Onions: 1
Carrots: 1
Celery: 1rib
Garlic: 1clove
Fresh chili pepper: 1
Precooked Cannellini beans: 1cup(250 g)
White wine: ½glass
Italian kale (cavolo nero): 0.8lb(350 g)
Rosemary: 2sprigs
Vegetable broth: to taste
Parmigiano Reggiano PDO cheese: 2spoonfuls- grated
Parmigiano Reggiano PDO cheese: to taste- a crust

info:
Difficulty: Easy
Prep time: 15 min
Cook time: 20 min
Serving: 4
Cost: Low

instructions:
To prepare pasta and beans with black cabbage, start with the vegetables for the sauté: clean and chop the carrot , celery , and onion .
Also chop the tip of a chili pepper and clean the black cabbage, removing the leaves and chopping the stems .
Prepare the classic sauté with oil, chopped vegetables, chili pepper , and black cabbage stems . Sauté and add chopped rosemary and grated garlic .
Add the cannellini beans and deglaze with wine . Then add a ladle of broth and the Parmesan rind, let it simmer for 10 minutes. Remove the rind once it has released its flavor. Take out two ladles of soup .
Pour the ladles of soup into a bowl with a ladle of broth , blend with an immersion blender .
Pour the obtained purée back into the pot , add more broth , and the cabbage leaves .
Now add the pasta and cook, adding broth as needed . Finally, stir in a drizzle of oil .
Grated Parmigiano Reggiano DOP and pepper to taste. Stir and serve the pasta and beans with black cabbage hot .
We recommend consuming the pasta and beans with black cabbage within the day.
If using dried cannellini beans, you must soak them for 12 hours before boiling.
