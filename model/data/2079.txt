name:
Pasta with pumpkins
ingredients:
Mini Penne Rigate pasta: 2 ½cups(320 g)
Pumpkin: 0.9lb(200 g)
Carrots: 1 ⅓cup(150 g)
Zucchini: 1cup(150 g)
Rosemary: 1sprig
Extra virgin olive oil: 2tbsp(30 g)
Fine salt: to taste
Garlic: 1clove
Parmigiano Reggiano PDO cheese: 1.8oz(50 g)- to grate

info:
Difficulty: Easy
Prep time: 20 min
Cook time: 16 min
Serving: 4
Cost: Low

To prepare pasta with pumpkins, start by cutting the pumpkin into slices 0.4 inches thick , then remove the skin with a knife and cut each slice into sticks 0.8 inches thick from which you will make triangles .
Wash and trim the zucchini, then cut them into slices 0.2 inches thick , take each slice and carve two spaced triangles (5-6),
extract the triangles to get the shape of a cute pumpkin . Repeat the same procedure with the carrots: peel them, trim, then cut into slices 0.2 inches thick , carve the triangles with a knife
extract them to have your orange pumpkins . In the meantime, place a pot full of salted water on the stove that will be used for cooking the pasta. In a pan, heat the olive oil, then add the pumpkin triangles
flavor with the whole garlic clove (you can omit it if you don't prefer) and rosemary , let it sauté for 2-3 minutes, then also add the carrots
and zucchini , salt to taste then deglaze with a ladleful of pasta cooking water and cook for about 8 minutes on low heat, at the end of cooking remove the garlic with kitchen tongs. When the water for cooking boils, pour in the mini ridged penne
Cook the pasta for 6 minutes or the time indicated on the package, then drain it directly into the pan with the sauce , season with grated Parmesan cheese and serve the pasta with pumpkins immediately !
The pasta with pumpkins should be consumed as soon as it's ready. You can store only the sauce in the refrigerator for a couple of days in an airtight container. Freezing is not recommended.
To make the sauce even richer, you can add some Genovese pesto.
