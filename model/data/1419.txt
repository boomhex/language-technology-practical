name:
One-pot chicken biryani
ingredients:
Chicken legs: 4- (about 700 g)
Basmati rice: 1 ⅓cup(250 g)
Flat beans: 7oz(200 g)
Garlic: 3cloves
Fresh chili pepper: 1- long
Fresh ginger: 1.8oz(50 g)
Red onions: 7oz(200 g)
Clarified butter: 3 ½tbsp(50 g)
Chicken broth: 2.1cups(500 g)
Raisins: ⅓cup(50 g)
Almond slices: ½cup(40 g)
Fine salt: to taste
Black pepper: to taste
Powdered turmeric: 1spoonful
Powdered cumin: 1spoonful
Paprika: 1spoonful- strong
Coriander: to taste

info:
Difficulty: Very easy
Prep time: 10 min
Cook time: 30 min
Serving: 4
Cost: Average

instructions:
To prepare the one-pot chicken biryani, first, prepare the Chicken broth . Then peel the onion and slice it . Wash the snow peas and cut them into pieces . Take the ginger, peel it, and cut it into small pieces ; move on to the chili, remove the stem and cut it into pieces.
Place the ginger, chili, and garlic cloves in a mixer with blades, and blend everything . Now take a pan, add the clarified butter and let it melt .
Add the chicken thighs , the freshly prepared mixture , the onions ,
the cumin, paprika , and turmeric . Brown the thighs for about ten minutes, then add the flat beans
and a ladle of hot broth . Cover with a lid and let it cook for about 5-10 minutes over medium heat. Then remove the lid, add the rice
and cover it with the broth . Add the raisins , stir and cover again with the lid .
Let it cook for another 10 minutes at most, until the rice is cooked and the broth is absorbed . At this point, add the sliced almonds and coriander leaves before serving the one-pot chicken biryani .
We recommend consuming your one-pot chicken biryani immediately.
