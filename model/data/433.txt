name:
Venere rice with broccoli and seafood
ingredients:
Black Venus rice: 1 ½cup(320 g)
Mussels: 1.1lbs(500 g)- (already cleaned)
Clams: 1.1lbs(500 g)- (cleaned)
Prawns: 12
Calamari (squid): 3
Broccoli: 3.3cups(300 g)
Leeks: 1
Extra virgin olive oil: to taste
Fine salt: to taste

info:
Difficulty: Average
Prep time: 20 min
Cook time: 40 min
Serving: 4
Cost: Average

instructions:
To prepare Venere rice with broccoli and seafood, heat a drizzle of oil in a pan with the garlic clove . Add the cleaned mussels and clams , cover with a lid , and cook for 3-4 minutes or until they open.
Once ready , strain them through a sieve and save the cooking liquid. Separate the shellfish from the shells .
Set the seafood aside . Cook the Venere rice in plenty of salted water for 20 minutes . Clean the shrimp by removing the head and shell .
Cut the shrimp meat into chunks . Clean the calamari: remove the head and internal cartilage , then remove the skin .
Cut the body into rings . Heat a drizzle of oil in a pan and add the calamari rings and shrimp .
Sauté the calamari rings over high heat for 3 minutes , then drain everything on a paper towel . Clean the leek and cut it into rings .
Separate the broccoli florets and halve them . Now in the same pan, heat a drizzle of oil, add the sliced leek , and quickly sauté it, then add the broccoli .
Moisten with the shellfish liquid , cover with a lid , and cook for 5 minutes. Remove the lid and add the drained Venere rice .
Finish with the calamari, sautéed shrimp, mussels , clams, a drizzle of raw oil , and immediately serve the Venere rice with broccoli and seafood !
Venere rice with broccoli and seafood can be stored in the refrigerator for one day.
Freezing is not recommended.
Vary the vegetables according to seasonal availability, using for example zucchini, cherry tomatoes, or bell peppers, to enjoy a delicious dish all year round!
