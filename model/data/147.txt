name:
Confit cod on chickpea cream
ingredients:
Desalted cod: 1.1lbs(480 g)- cleaned
Extra virgin olive oil: 4 ¼cups(1 l)
Bay leaves: 2leaves
Precooked chickpeas: 2 ¼cups(520 g)
White onions: 1cup(150 g)
Rosemary: 2sprigs
Vegetable broth: to taste
Extra virgin olive oil: 4 ½tbsp(60 g)
Salt: to taste
Precooked chickpeas: ¾cup(180 g)
Rosemary: 1sprig
Sage: 3leaves
Extra virgin olive oil: to taste
Black pepper: to taste
Marjoram: to taste
Dried tomatoes: to taste

instructions:

To prepare the confit cod on chickpea cream, first finely chop the onions . In a pot, pour a drizzle of oil, add the rosemary , the onions , and sauté for 2 minutes.
Add 520 g of chickpeas and cook for 10 minutes, add a bit of vegetable broth . Season with salt .
Remove the rosemary and blend with an immersion blender, pouring in 60 g of oil in a thin stream, until you obtain a smooth cream .
In a pan, pour a drizzle of oil, add sage, rosemary, and chickpeas . Season with salt and pepper . Sauté until crispy. Cut the cod into evenly sized pieces .
In a pot, pour the oil, add the bay leaf, and heat to a temperature of 136°F (58°C); use a thermometer to monitor it. Immerse the cod and cook for 10-12 minutes . After this time, let the cod drain on a rack .
Cut the sun-dried tomato into strips . Plate by placing the chickpea cream at the bottom of the serving dish, lay two pieces of cod on top, add a spoonful of crispy chickpeas , and the sun-dried tomatoes .
Finish with fresh marjoram and a tablespoon of oil . The confit cod on chickpea cream is ready to be enjoyed .
The confit cod on chickpea cream should be consumed as soon as it is ready, but if there are leftovers, you can store them in the refrigerator, in a sealed container, for up to 1 day.
You can add your favorite spices to the chickpea cream; try, for example, a pinch of turmeric or cumin.
If you love spicy flavors, add a chili pepper or smoked paprika.
