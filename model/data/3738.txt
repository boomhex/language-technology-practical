name:
Celery Root Puree with Cream
ingredients:
White celery: 4cups(500 g)
Butter: 1.4tbsp(20 g)
White pepper: to taste
Fresh liquid cream: 1cup(250 g)
Fine salt: to taste
Grana Padano PDO cheese: 1.5oz(40 g)

info:
Difficulty: Easy
Prep time: 20 min
Cook time: 20 min
Serving: 4
Cost: Low

To prepare the celery root puree with cream, brush the celery root with a brush to remove any excess dirt on the outer surface ; peel it with a knife , then cut it into chunks .
Place a large pot with plenty of salted water on the stove, and when the water reaches a simmer, blanch the celery root for 10 minutes ; when it is ready , drain it well using a fine mesh strainer .
Place the blanched celery root chunks in a food mill or potato masher and process them to obtain a puree; add salt , white pepper
the butter cut into pieces , then mix with a spatula to combine all the ingredients well . Place a small saucepan with the cream on the stove ;
add salt, pepper , and grated cheese . Bring to a simmer, stirring well with a spatula or a wooden spoon .
As soon as the cream reaches a simmer, add it to the processed celery root and mix everything to obtain a thick, homogeneous puree: finally, you can serve and enjoy your celery root puree with cream piping hot .
Store the celery root puree with cream in the refrigerator in an airtight container for up to 3 days. When it's time to reheat it, just stir it a bit with a splash of milk, and your celery root puree will seem freshly made! Freezing is not recommended.
Want to give the puree more flavor? Simple: you can add chopped parsley or thyme or present your dish by completing it with chives: it will add an extra touch of color and goodness!
