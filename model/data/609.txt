To prepare perfect boiled potatoes, there's a small trick: use potatoes of the same size, this way you will ensure even cooking. Wash the potatoes thoroughly, then transfer them to a pot , pour cold water over them until they are completely covered , and add coarse salt .
Place the pot on the stove and, once it reaches a boil, let them cook for 20 minutes . To check if they are done, use the toothpick test. If it enters the potatoes without resistance, they are cooked . Otherwise, extend the cooking time as necessary. Remove the potatoes from the pot using a slotted spoon. Peel them immediately, it will be easier .
Cut the potatoes as you like . The boiled potatoes are ready, use them for your recipes or as a healthy and light side dish .
Boiled potatoes can be stored in the refrigerator, in a sealed container, for one or two days.

The cooking time depends greatly on the size of the potatoes, the important thing is to choose potatoes of similar size to achieve even cooking. For a healthy and light side dish, you can season the boiled potatoes with salt, extra virgin olive oil, vinegar, and some chopped parsley or chives. If you're in the mood for something special, cut the potatoes into chunks and sauté them for a few minutes with a pat of butter; they'll be ready when they have formed a delicious crust.
