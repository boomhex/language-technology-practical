name:
Chestnut and Bacon Bites
ingredients:
Chestnuts: 12
Bacon: 12slices

info:
Difficulty: Very easy
Prep time: 20 min
Cook time: 55 min
Makes: 12 pieces
Cost: Low

instructions:
To prepare the chestnut and bacon bites, start by boiling the chestnuts. Place them in a pot of boiling water and boil for about 40 minutes, depending on the size of the chestnuts . Then drain them .
Cool them down by placing them in a bowl full of cold water . At this point, use a small knife to peel the chestnuts , making sure to clean them thoroughly and completely .
Place a slice of bacon on the work surface and place a chestnut at the end facing you . Then roll the chestnut around the bacon and repeat the same operation for the others .
Transfer them onto a baking sheet lined with parchment paper and bake in a preheated static oven at 356Â°F for about 15 minutes. When the bacon is crisp, remove the chestnut bacon bites from the oven and serve them hot .
It is recommended to consume the chestnut bacon bites immediately. Freezing is not recommended.
Accompany these bites with a dipping sauce. For example, you can mix ketchup, mayonnaise, and Tabasco!
