name:
Mayonnaise with Hard-Boiled Eggs
ingredients:
Eggs: 2
Vegetable oil: 6tbsp(80 g)
Lemon juice: 1.3tbsp(20 g)
Salt: 1pinch
Pepper: 1pinch
Mustard: 1tsp

info:
Difficulty: Easy
Prep time: 10 min
Cook time: 9 min
Serving: 4
Cost: Low

instructions:
To prepare mayonnaise with hard-boiled eggs, start by cooking the eggs. Place the eggs in a small pot and cover them with plenty of water, put the pot on the stove, bring to a boil and cook the eggs for 9 minutes, counting the time from when it starts boiling . After the cooking time has passed, let the eggs cool at room temperature or, to speed it up, under running water. Gently tap the eggs to crack the shell .

At this point, gently remove the shell from both eggs and cut them in half .
Then place the eggs in the cup of an immersion blender, add the lemon juice , a pinch of salt , and a pinch of pepper .

Add the vegetable oil , then blend everything with the immersion blender until you get a fluffy, soft, and uniform mixture .
The mayonnaise with hard-boiled eggs is ready; you can add a teaspoon of mustard to taste or leave it plain, all that's left is to transfer it to a bowl and serve it as you prefer .

Mayonnaise with hard-boiled eggs can be stored in an airtight jar in the refrigerator for two days.
Freezing is not recommended.

Mayonnaise with hard-boiled eggs can be prepared in the same way using apple cider vinegar instead of lemon juice.
You can prepare mayonnaise with hard-boiled eggs using only the yolks.
