name:
Flatbread with prosciutto, fava bean pesto, and endive
ingredients:
Whole wheat flatbreads: 2
Belgian endive: 2
Pecorino Romano PDO cheese: 1.1oz(30 g)- in flakes
Prosciutto crudo: 2.5oz(70 g)
Fine salt: to taste
Black pepper: to taste
Extra virgin olive oil: to taste
Fava beans: 0.6cup(100 g)
Pecorino Romano PDO cheese: 3tbsp(15 g)- to grate
Extra virgin olive oil: 3tbsp(40 g)
Almonds: 1 ¼tbsp(15 g)
Water: 1 ½tbsp(20 g)

info:
Difficulty: Easy
Prep time: 20 min
Cook time: 20 min
Makes: 2 pieces

instructions:
To make the flatbread with prosciutto, fava bean pesto, and endive, first prepare the fava bean pesto: bring water to a boil in a small pot and then cook the shelled fava beans for 5 minutes . Drain them into a bowl and remove the skin .
Place the fava beans in the tall glass of a blender, add the olive oil , the grated pecorino , and the whole almonds .
Also add the water and blend everything with an immersion blender until you obtain a smooth cream. Add salt and pepper .
Slice the pecorino into flakes and set aside. Wash and cut the endive into wedges about 0.4 inches . Transfer the endive to a bowl, season with oil .
Season also with salt and pepper, then grill for 5 minutes on a hot grill (14-15).
Heat the flatbreads for a couple of minutes and fill each flatbread with half of the filling ingredients: first place the grilled endive , then the slices of prosciutto .
Season with the fava bean pesto and pecorino flakes. Close the flatbread with prosciutto, fava bean pesto, and endive and serve immediately .
We recommend consuming the flatbread with prosciutto, fava bean pesto, and endive immediately.
You can make a more delicate flavored fava bean cream using only oil, lemon juice, and half a clove of garlic.
