name:
Pasta salad with agretti and prawns
ingredients:
Whole wheat bow tie pasta: 11.3oz(320 g)
Peas: 1 ½cup(170 g)- (fresh or frozen)
Friar's beard (agretti): 10.5oz(300 g)
Red onions: 3.5oz(100 g)
Shrimp: 5.3oz(150 g)- (without head)
Lime peel: 1
Pink peppercorn: ¼tsp(1 g)
Mint: 2sprigs
Extra virgin olive oil: to taste
Fine salt: to taste
Black pepper: to taste

info:
Difficulty: Easy
Prep time: 10 min
Cook time: 25 min
Serving: 4
Cost: Low
Note plus the cooling time for the pasta

instructions:
To prepare the pasta salad with agretti and prawns, first chop the onion . Wash the agretti, then remove the bottom part and cut them into pieces .
Pour a drizzle of oil into a saucepan, add the chopped onion and let it soften. Add the peas , the agretti , and season with salt and pepper.
Add a couple of tablespoons of water and let them cook for about 10/15 minutes at most, stirring often, until well softened . Then cook the pasta in plenty of boiling water, salted to taste ; drain it al dente and let it cool slightly.
Shell the prawns and if necessary, remove the black thread found on the back. In a pan, heat a drizzle of oil and use it to sear the prawns over high heat. It will take about a couple of minutes, and you should turn them halfway through cooking . Allow the prawns to cool slightly, then cut them in half. Pour the agretti-based dressing into a bowl, add the now lukewarm pasta .
and the prawns . Mix everything, then add the pink peppercorns and the grated zest of a lime .
Add another drizzle of oil and the torn mint leaves . Mix and refrigerate until ready to serve your pasta salad with agretti and prawns .
Consume the pasta salad with agretti and prawns within one day in the refrigerator.
Add some crumbles of feta or semi-aged fresh cheese!
