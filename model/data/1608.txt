name:
Plum and Bacon Bites
ingredients:
Dried prunes: 8- pitted
Bacon: 8slices
Sage: 8leaves

info:
Difficulty: Easy
Prep time: 5 min
Cook time: 25 min
Serving: 4
Cost: Low

instructions:
To prepare the plum and bacon bites, first lay the bacon slices on a cutting board , place a pitted dried plum on one end , and roll the bacon to completely wrap the fruit.
Place the rolls on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper so that they are well spaced and bake in a static oven at 350Â°F for about 25 minutes to brown the bacon. Once ready, remove the plum and bacon bites from the oven , let them cool slightly, and then serve them garnished with sage leaves.
It is recommended to consume the plum and bacon bites immediately.
Add a few cheese cubes inside for a tasty gooey effect!
