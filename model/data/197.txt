name:
No-bake mint and chocolate crumble
ingredients:
Dry chocolate cookies: 2 ½cups(300 g)
Butter: ½cup(120 g)
Fresh liquid cream: 1cup(250 g)- cold
Mint syrup: ½cup(160 g)
Dark chocolate: 3.5oz(100 g)
Gelatin in sheets: 1tsp(5 g)
Dry chocolate cookies: 1 ¼cup(150 g)
Butter: 4tbsp(60 g)
Powdered sugar: to taste
Mint: to taste

info:
Difficulty: Average
Prep time: 30 min
Cook time: 10 min
Serving: 8
Cost: Low
Note + the resting time in the refrigerator (about 140 min.)

instructions:
To prepare the no-bake mint and chocolate crumble, first soak the gelatin sheets . Meanwhile, melt the butter in a saucepan and let it cool. Place the cookies in a food processor and blend until you get a grainy mixture. Transfer everything to a bowl and add the melted butter .
Mix well . Pour two-thirds of the mixture into a 9-inch diameter removable-bottom pan. Use this amount to make the edge , then add the remaining part for the base . Place in the refrigerator to set for 10-15 minutes.
In the meantime, prepare the filling: take 1/4 cup from the total mint syrup and heat it in a saucepan, then add the well-squeezed gelatin sheets . Stir and let it melt completely . Pour the syrup into a bowl .
Add the rest of the syrup at room temperature , stir and let it cool. Meanwhile, whip the cream .
Incorporate the whipped cream into the cold syrup and mix to combine the ingredients . Chop the chocolate with a knife and add it to the cream , then mix gently .
Take the base from the refrigerator and pour the filling into the pan , then return it to the refrigerator. Meanwhile, prepare the topping: melt the butter in a saucepan, chop the cookies in a food processor and mix both in a bowl .
Mix and cover the surface of the filling with the mixture obtained, which will still be soft . Let it firm up in the fridge for at least 2 hours .
Unmold the cake onto a serving plate and garnish with powdered sugar and mint leaves . Your no-bake mint and chocolate crumble is ready to be enjoyed !
The no-bake mint and chocolate crumble can be stored in the refrigerator for 2-3 days, in a closed container or covered with plastic wrap.
Try varying the flavor of your no-bake crumble by replacing the mint syrup with raspberry or strawberry syrup, for example!
