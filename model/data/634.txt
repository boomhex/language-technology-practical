name:
Giant American Pancakes
ingredients:
Type 00 flour: 1cup(130 g)
Eggs: 3
Whole milk: ¼cup(70 g)
Baking powder: 1tsp(5 g)
Vanilla bean: 1
Sugar: 1pinch
Fine salt: to taste
Extra virgin olive oil: to taste
Maple syrup: to taste
Blueberries: to taste
Greek yogurt: to taste
Apricots: to taste

instructions:

To prepare the giant American pancakes, pour the eggs and milk into a bowl, beat with the whisk to mix the liquids, then add the flour .
The baking powder , salt , a pinch of sugar, and the seeds from the vanilla pod . Mix to obtain a smooth and lump-free batter.
Now brush an 8-inch diameter non-stick pan with oil . Pour a ladle of batter and rotate the pan to distribute it on the bottom .
Cover with the lid and heat over moderate heat until bubbles form on the surface, it will take about 2 minutes. Flip the pancake and cook on the other side for a few minutes, until the bottom starts to brown .
Once ready, place the pancakes on a serving plate, spread them with Greek yogurt , garnish with blueberries and slices of apricots, then pour maple syrup to taste on top . The giant American pancakes are ready, serve them immediately .
It is recommended to consume the giant American pancakes immediately.
Alternatively, you can opt for a savory filling with ham and robiola cheese.
