name:
Baked Cauliflower
ingredients:
Cauliflower: 1.5lbs(700 g)
Smoked paprika: 1tsp
Garlic: 1clove
Thyme: 2sprigs
Extra virgin olive oil: 2.7tbsp(40 g)
Water: 2spoonfuls
Fine salt: to taste

info:
Difficulty: Very easy
Prep time: 20 min
Cook time: 35 min
Serving: 4
Cost: Low

instructions:
To prepare the baked cauliflower, first clean the cauliflower by removing the outer leaves and the stem, then separate the florets and rinse them. Cut the florets into slices about 0.4 inches thick and place them on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper .
Pour the oil and water into a small bowl , then add the paprika and the crushed or finely chopped garlic .
Mix well with a fork . Lightly salt the cauliflower slices and dress them with the emulsion .
Flavor with thyme leaves and proceed with cooking : bake in a preheated static oven at 375Â°F for 30 minutes, then turn on the grill for about 5 minutes until golden. Your baked cauliflower is ready to be enjoyed !
The baked cauliflower can be stored in the refrigerator for 1-2 days, in an airtight container.
Freezing is not recommended.
Instead of thyme, you can use rosemary or sage.
Those who love spicy can also add a pinch of chili pepper.
