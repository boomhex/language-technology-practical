name:
Pancotto with turnip tops
ingredients:
Homemade bread: 7.1oz(200 g)- 4 slices
Broccoli rabe: 4.4lbs(2 kg)- to clean
Leeks: 1
Garlic: 1clove
Green Peperoncino: to taste
Extra virgin olive oil: 8spoonfuls
Fine salt: to taste

info:
Difficulty: Very easy
Prep time: 10 min
Cook time: 30 min
Serving: 4
Cost: Low

instructions:
To prepare pancotto with turnip tops, first clean the tops, selecting the most tender tops and leaves . Immerse them in boiling salted water , add the garlic , and simmer on low heat for about 20 minutes until they are tender.
Once cooked, remove the garlic and add the broken pieces of bread . Stir and continue cooking for another 5 minutes, just enough time to soften the bread.
Meanwhile, clean and finely chop the leek , then distribute it on the bottom of the plates . Drain the vegetables and bread and transfer them to the plates .
Season with olive oil and a sprinkle of chili powder . Your pancotto with turnip tops is ready to be enjoyed !
Pancotto with turnip tops can be stored in the refrigerator, in an airtight container, for 3 days.
Like any traditional dish, pancotto with turnip tops lends itself to various interpretations. One of the most common variations includes the addition of an egg: you can cook it directly in the soup or add it poached to make the recipe even richer and more substantial.
