name:
Piselli a cecamariti
ingredients:
Frozen peas: 2cups(300 g)
Broccoli rabe: 1.5lbs(700 g)
Vegetable broth: 2.1cups(500 ml)
Garlic: 1clove
Fresh chili pepper: 2- small
Extra virgin olive oil: 3spoonfuls
Stale bread: 4.25oz(120 g)
Fine salt: to taste

info:
Difficulty: Easy
Prep time: 30 min
Cook time: 22 min
Serving: 4
Cost: Low

instructions:
To prepare the husbands' blinder peas, trim the turnip greens by removing the fibrous part of the stalk . Coarsely chop and wash them , then drain well. Bring lightly salted water to a boil in a pot, toss in the turnip greens , and blanch them for 5 minutes.
Meanwhile, peel the garlic and transfer it to a pot, add the chili peppers and 2 tablespoons of oil. Saut√© and then turn off the heat. Drain the turnip greens and transfer them to the pot with the garlic and chili peppers . Stir, cover, and cook for 10 minutes, continuing to stir occasionally.
Add the peas to the turnip greens , mix, and let them absorb the flavors. Add the broth and adjust with salt and pepper .
Cover and cook over moderate heat for 15 minutes. Slice the stale bread and cut it into cubes. Heat 4 tablespoons of olive oil in a small pan and toast the bread cubes . Transfer the turnip greens with peas to a plate and serve it together with the croutons .
You can store the cooked vegetables in an airtight container for 24 hours.
For a stronger flavor, you can cut the chili peppers into small pieces.
