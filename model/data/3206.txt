name:
Rose of Prosciutto on classic puff pastry
ingredients:
Prosciutto crudo: 3slices
Goat: 3.5oz(100 g)
Mint: 4leaves
Dill: 2sprigs
Black pepper: to taste
Fine salt: to taste
Extra virgin olive oil: to taste
Crispy baked puff pastry: 12
Mint: 12leaves
Dill: 1sprig

info:
Difficulty: Easy
Prep time: 15 min
Makes: 12 pieces
Cost: Low

To prepare the ham roses on classic puff pastries, start by making the goat cheese cream: in a bowl, place the goat cheese and work it into a cream with a fork . Chop the aromatic herbs and add them to the goat cheese cream
Mix well to combine all the ingredients, add a drizzle of extra virgin olive oil to soften the cheese , then salt and pepper to taste. Then transfer the mixture to a pastry bag fitted with a star tip and squeeze a tuft of goat cheese cream onto each pastry . Then cut the slices of ham into strips about 0.4 inches wide
Roll the slices of ham onto themselves and place them on the tuft of goat cheese cream . Complete the pastries with a mint leaf and a sprig of dill . The ham roses on classic puff pastries are ready to be served!
You can store the ham roses on classic puff pastries in the refrigerator for a couple of days in an airtight container.
You can replace the ham with any type of deli meat, such as speck or coppa. If you don't like goat cheese, you can use any other spreadable cheese, soften it with a tablespoon of milk.
