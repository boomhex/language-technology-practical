name:
Risotto with pesto and potatoes
ingredients:
Carnaroli rice: 1 ½cup(300 g)
Potatoes: 3- small
Extra virgin olive oil: 2spoonfuls
Garlic: 2cloves
Vegetable broth: 4 ¼cups(1 l)
Basil: 0.9oz(25 g)
Parmigiano Reggiano PDO cheese: 1.75oz(50 g)- to grate
Pine nuts: 2tsp(10 g)
Extra virgin olive oil: 3tbsp(50 g)
Fine salt: to taste

info:
Difficulty: Easy
Prep time: 10 min
Cook time: 15 min
Serving: 4
Cost: Low

instructions:
To prepare the potato and pesto risotto, make the pesto: clean the basil leaves with some kitchen paper , place two peeled garlic cloves without the germ inside the mortar , add a pinch of salt
and, using the pestle, crush well until the garlic is reduced to a paste . Then add the basil, add another pinch of salt , and crush the basil well against the sides of the mortar to break it down thoroughly .
Add the pine nuts , crush them, and then add the grated Parmesan . Finally, pour in the oil
and the pesto is ready . Peel the potatoes and cut them into cubes .
In a pan, add a whole peeled garlic clove, the oil , and brown the clove. Remove the garlic and add the potatoes , let them brown for a few seconds, and then add the rice ,
toast it quickly, and gradually add some vegetable broth , bring the risotto to cooking by sprinkling with some pepper , it will take about 12 minutes to reach al dente.
Once cooked, season the rice with the pesto and stir . Plate the potato and pesto risotto and enjoy it hot!
Consume the potato and pesto risotto immediately and hot. If there's any left, you can store it in the fridge where it will keep for a couple of days.
