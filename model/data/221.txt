name:
Sichuan Pepper Beef
ingredients:
Beef filet: 1.25lbs(600 g)
White onions: 1
Green peppers: 1
Peanut seed oil: 3spoonfuls
Water: 5.4tbsp(80 g)
Sichuan peppers: 2tsp
Fresh ginger: to taste
Garlic: 1clove
Potato starch: 1spoonful
Soy sauce: 1spoonful
Rice wine: 1spoonful
Hoisin sauce: 1spoonful
Water: ½cup(100 g)
Fine salt: to taste
Sesame seeds: to taste
Black sesame seeds: to taste

info:
Difficulty: Easy
Prep time: 20 min
Cook time: 12 min
Serving: 4
Cost: Average
Note + marinating time of the meat (30 min.)

instructions:
To prepare Sichuan pepper beef, first peel the ginger and finely chop it with a knife . Peel and slice the garlic .
In a bowl, place the ginger and garlic , then add the rice wine and the soy sauce .
Also add the hoisin sauce , the Sichuan pepper , and mix everything .
Cut the beef tenderloin first into thin medallions , then into strips , then transfer the meat to the bowl .
Mix well so that the meat is evenly coated by the marinade. Finally, pour in the water , the potato starch , and mix again.
Season with salt and mix one last time . Cover with plastic wrap and let it rest for 30 minutes.
Meanwhile, clean and slice the onion and pepper into strips . In the wok, pour the peanut oil and heat it well. Then add the onion and sauté for 3-4 minutes over high heat.
Add the peppers and cook for another 3-4 minutes, then add the strips of meat with their marinade and the water .
Cook everything for 3-4 minutes, still over high heat and stirring often . Plate and finish with white and black sesame seeds . Your Sichuan pepper beef is ready to be served !
It is recommended to consume Sichuan pepper beef as soon as it is ready. If necessary, you can store it in the refrigerator, in an airtight container, for up to 2 days.
If you prefer, you can replace the peppers with zucchini, carrots, or mushrooms.
