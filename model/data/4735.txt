name:
Water Apple Cake
ingredients:
Gala apples: 15.2oz(430 g)
Type 00 flour: 2 ½cups(300 g)
Sugar: 1cup(200 g)
Water: 0.9cup(210 g)
Orange juice: ½cup(120 g)
Orange peel: 2- to be grated
Sunflower seed oil: 6tbsp(90 g)
Baking powder: 1tbsp(16 g)

info:
Difficulty: Easy
Prep time: 20 min
Cook time: 60 min
Serving: 8
Cost: Low

instructions:
When you ask us for a light dessert, without butter, without eggs, easy and quick to prepare, the first thing that comes to mind is: it doesn't exist! But today we surprise you with a different answer… the water apple cake. The qualities of the water cake meet the rustic delight of the apple cake , and the result is a simple and soft dessert for the whole family's breakfast and snack time. Alternate the water apple cake with other variations like the Water and Cocoa Cake , the Coffee Water Cake , or the Chocolate Water Cake to carve out a moment of sweetness every day without too much guilt!
Try other delicious apple cake variations:

To prepare the water apple cake, first sift the flour into a bowl , then add the baking powder and sugar . Mix the dry ingredients with a hand whisk.
While continuing to mix, slowly pour in the water , the filtered orange juice , and the vegetable oil .
Finally, add the grated orange zest and mix again. At this point, wash the apples and cut them first into wedges and then into irregular cubes, without peeling them .
Also add the apple cubes to the mixture and mix with a spatula to incorporate them evenly . Pour the obtained batter into a 9-inch baking pan lined with parchment paper .
Bake in a preheated static oven at 350°F for about 60 minutes, checking the doneness with a toothpick . Once cooked, take it out of the oven and let it cool before serving your water apple cake !
The water apple cake can be stored at room temperature for 2-3 days, covered with a cake dome.
If you wish, you can flavor the water apple cake batter with some ground cinnamon!
