name:
Gratinated radicchio, hazelnuts, and scamorza salad
ingredients:
Radicchio: 3 ½cups(300 g)- baby quality
Hazelnuts: ½cup(80 g)- peeled
Smoked scamorza cheese: 2.8oz(80 g)
Extra virgin olive oil: to taste
Sage: to taste
Nutmeg: to taste
Fine salt: to taste
Black pepper: to taste

info:
Difficulty: Easy
Prep time: 10 min
Cook time: 5 min
Serving: 4
Cost: Low

instructions:
To prepare the gratinated radicchio, hazelnuts, and scamorza salad, start by chopping the hazelnuts and grating the scamorza . Wash, remove the base and outer leaves, then cut the radicchio in half ;
then divide it into 4-6 wedges, depending on the size . Transfer to a bowl and season with oil , salt , and pepper to taste.
Season with nutmeg then mix everything together . Take 4 oven-safe dishes, brush the base with oil .
Place the radicchio wedges inside them . Sprinkle the surface with grated scamorza , and distribute the hazelnuts
and a few sage leaves . Oil the surface again , then place in a preheated oven on grill mode at 430°F for 5 minutes, just enough time to gratinate the cheese and toast the hazelnuts . Enjoy your gratinated radicchio, hazelnuts, and scamorza salad while it's hot.
The gratinated radicchio, hazelnuts, and scamorza salad can be stored covered in the refrigerator for a maximum of 1-2 days, making sure to reheat it in the oven before consuming.
You can substitute the hazelnuts with other nuts. The scamorza can also be replaced with another stringy cheese.
