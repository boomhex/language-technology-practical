name:
Bread patties in a pan
ingredients:
Stale bread: 5cups(300 g)
Whole milk: 1.1cups(260 g)
Parmigiano Reggiano PDO cheese: 2.5oz(70 g)
Eggs: 1
Garlic: 1clove
Parsley: to taste
Breadcrumbs: to taste
Fine salt: to taste
Black pepper: to taste
Extra virgin olive oil: to taste
Fontina cheese: 1.5oz(40 g)

info:
Difficulty: Easy
Prep time: 20 min
Cook time: 10 min
Makes: 10 pieces
Cost: Low

instructions:
To prepare the bread patties in a pan, cut the bread into slices and remove the crust , then break it into small pieces . Transfer the bread into a bowl, pour the milk and let it soften for a few minutes .
Add the egg , grated cheese , and parsley .
Salt , pepper, and season with a crushed garlic clove . Knead the mixture with your hands .
Add breadcrumbs only if the mixture is too soft . Set the mixture aside and cut the fontina cheese into small cubes . Now form the patties by taking a portion of the mixture .
Flatten them in the center and insert a cube of fontina cheese . Continue until all the mixture is used .
Then coat each patty in breadcrumbs . Heat a drizzle of oil in a pan .
Let them brown on one side , then turn them over and finish cooking on the other side. Serve the bread patties in a pan hot , accompanied by your favorite sauces!
You can store the bread patties in a pan in the refrigerator for 2 days, in an airtight container. If you want to prepare them the day before, you can store the raw and breaded patties in the refrigerator for one day.
Freezing is not recommended.
With the leftover bread crusts, you can make excellent oven-baked croutons, flavored with herbs of your choice.
Fontina cheese can be replaced with provolone or caciocavallo.
