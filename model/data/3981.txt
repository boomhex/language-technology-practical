name:
Morzello (Morzeddhu alla Catanzarisi)
ingredients:
Tripe: 1.3lbs(600 g)- (honeycomb, rumen and omasum "hundredfold")
Oregano: 1sprig
Fresh chili pepper: 1
Extra virgin olive oil: 1spoonful
Fine salt: to taste
Veal: 6.4oz(180 g)- trachea
Veal: 5.5oz(160 g)- fatty
Veal: 0.9lb(400 g)- heart
Veal: 0.9lb(400 g)- lungs
Veal: 0.5lb(230 g)- belly
Veal: 0.7lb(300 g)- spleen
Tomato paste: 1.67cups(400 g)
Water: 21.1cups(5 l)
Bay leaves: 1leaf

info:
Difficulty: Average
Prep time: 30 min
Cook time: 5 h
Serving: 8
Cost: Low

instructions:

To prepare Morzello, pour water into a large pot and dissolve the tomato paste in it , add salt, olive oil , and let the sauce reduce for about 3 hours over medium heat, or until it thickens well. This cooking process will allow you to obtain an intensely flavored sauce to best season the offal.
Meanwhile, focus on cleaning the offal: rinse thoroughly under running water, or alternatively, you can ask your trusted butcher for pre-cleaned offal. Boil the tripe for 25 minutes , or until the meat is tender, then drain and cut into strips .
Place the cooked tripe in a bowl and set aside . Continue by cooking the remaining offal, place all the remaining pieces of veal in a pot , cover with water, and boil for about 40 minutes, until tender as well, testing the doneness by piercing the meat with a fork .
Once cooked, remove the offal, drain well , and cut into strips . Sauté all the offal in a pan to dry them .
When the tomato sauce is well reduced , add the tripe and the rest of the offal . Continue cooking for 1 and a half hours. Chop the chili pepper with a knife .
20 minutes before the end of cooking, season the Morzello with the herbs: bay leaf, oregano, and chopped chili pepper . After the indicated time, the Morzello will be ready to serve (17-18).
Store Morzello in an airtight container for a couple of days in the refrigerator. It can be frozen if fresh ingredients were used.
There are several variations of the recipe, a common alternative is to cook the tripe and offal together with the sauce and not separately. To best enjoy Morzello, accompany it with Calabrian pitta, and it will become an irresistible street food.
