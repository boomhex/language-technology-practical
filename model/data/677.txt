name:
Baked chicken wings
ingredients:
Chicken wings: 2.4lbs(1.1 kg)
Natural plain yogurt: 2cups(500 g)
Lemon juice: 2tbsp(30 g)
Type 00 flour: 1cup(120 g)
Smoked paprika: 4tsp
Extra virgin olive oil: to taste
Chives: to taste
Fine salt: to taste
Black pepper: to taste

info:
Difficulty: Easy
Prep time: 15 min
Cook time: 35 min
Serving: 4
Cost: Low
Note + 3 hours of marinating

instructions:
To prepare baked chicken wings, pour the yogurt into a large bowl, season with salt and pepper, add the lemon juice , and mix. Immerse the chicken wings .
Cover with plastic wrap and let marinate for 3 hours in the refrigerator. After the marinating time, drain the wings coarsely, so as not to completely remove the yogurt. In a bowl, pour the flour and paprika , coat the wings .
Then place the seasoned wings in a baking tray lined with parchment paper, then sprinkle with more paprika and finish with a drizzle of oil . Bake in a preheated oven for about 35 minutes (cooking time may vary depending on the size of the wings, if necessary continue for another 10 minutes) at 356°F in convection mode, or 392°F in static mode. At the end of cooking, sprinkle with a light chive chop and serve the baked chicken wings hot .
You can store baked chicken wings for one day in the refrigerator in an airtight container and reheat them before serving. Freezing is not recommended.
Accompany the baked chicken wings with sautéed potatoes or gratinated vegetables.
