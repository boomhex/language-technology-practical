Omelette with cherry tomatoes and pecorino
ingredients:
Cherry tomatoes: 2cups(300 g)
Datterino tomatoes: 2cups(300 g)
Eggs: 6- medium
Pecorino Romano PDO cheese: 3.5oz(100 g)- to grate
Fine salt: to taste
Black pepper: to taste
Extra virgin olive oil: to taste
Oregano: to taste- dry

To prepare the omelette with cherry tomatoes and pecorino, start by washing the cherry tomatoes . Cut them in half . In a bowl, pour the tomatoes and season with dried oregano .
Extra virgin olive oil , salt, pepper, and mix. Now in a pan, which measures 9.5 inches at the base and 11 inches at the surface, pour a drizzle of oil . Add the cherry tomatoes .
Cook over medium heat for about 5 minutes . While the cherry tomatoes are on the stove, take care of the eggs. Beat the eggs with a whisk with salt and pepper .
Incorporate the pecorino . After 5 minutes, the cherry tomatoes should not be cooked but just slightly wilted, pour in the eggs .
Cover with a lid and let it cook over medium-low heat for about 10 minutes . When the surface is slightly set but still moist , place a plate on top of the omelette .
With a decisive movement, flip the pan and slide the omelette to cook it on the other side. Cook for about 5 minutes . The omelette with cherry tomatoes and Pecorino is ready, serve immediately .
The omelette with cherry tomatoes and Pecorino can be stored in the fridge for 1-2 days.
We do not recommend freezing as the tomatoes may release water once the omelette is thawed.
You can vary the type of cherry tomatoes and replace oregano with fresh basil.
