name:
Flatbread with 'nduja, sausage and cherry tomatoes
ingredients:
Flatbread: 2- Very Flaky
Sausage: 0.9lb(400 g)
Nduja: 6.3oz(180 g)
Cherry tomatoes: 1.3cups(200 g)
Extra virgin olive oil: to taste

info:
Difficulty: Very easy
Prep time: 10 min
Cook time: 10 min
Serving: 2

To prepare the flatbread with 'nduja, sausage, and cherry tomatoes, first cut the cherry tomatoes in half , then remove the casing from the sausage and crumble it. Heat a drizzle of oil in a pan and add the crumbled sausage .
Brown the sausage, stirring with a ladle, until it changes color . Set the sausage aside and heat the first flatbread in another pan for 30 seconds , then flip it over to heat the other side for another 30 seconds .
Spread half of the 'nduja on one side and leave it on the heat for another minute . At this point, transfer the flatbread to a cutting board and add half of the browned sausage .
Distribute half of the cherry tomatoes and fold the flatbread in half . Proceed in the same way to prepare the second one and enjoy your flatbread with 'nduja, sausage, and cherry tomatoes .
It is recommended to consume the flatbread with 'nduja, sausage, and cherry tomatoes immediately or at most within a few hours.
You can make your flatbread even richer by adding some stracciatella!
