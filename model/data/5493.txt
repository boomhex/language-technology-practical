name:
Pork Loin with Milk
ingredients:
Pork loin: 1.3lbs(600 g)- sliced
Whole milk: 0.9cup(200 g)
Type 00 flour: 0.8cup(100 g)
Butter: 5.6tbsp(80 g)
Chives: to taste
Fine salt: to taste
Black pepper: to taste

info:
Difficulty: Easy
Prep time: 5 min
Cook time: 10 min
Serving: 4
Cost: Low

instructions:
The loin is a lean cut of pork, ideal for roasting or braising whole. If you have pork slices instead, you can use them for a quick and easy recipe like pork loin with milk! In just 10 minutes, you'll have a tender and flavorful main course that's perfect for the whole family. The pork slices are briefly seared in a pan before being immersed in a creamy milk sauce that guarantees a soft and succulent result. Serve the pork loin with milk with a side salad or seasonal vegetables for a tasty and complete dinner that you'll definitely want to repeat!
Don't miss these recipes with pork loin :
To make pork loin with milk, pour the flour into a baking dish, add salt and pepper. Dredge the pork slices in the flour, coating both sides and shaking off the excess .
Melt the butter in a pan , then place the pork loin slices in the pan and sear them over medium-high heat for a couple of minutes on each side .
Transfer the meat to a plate and keep it warm . Pour the milk into the same pan , add salt and pepper , and let it thicken for 4 minutes.
At this point, return the pork slices to the pan and continue cooking for another minute over low heat. Turn off the heat and garnish with chopped chives . Your pork loin with milk is ready to be served !
It is recommended to consume the pork loin with milk immediately. If necessary, you can store it in the refrigerator for 1-2 days in an airtight container.
If you prefer, you can substitute chives with parsley!
