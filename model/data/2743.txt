name:
Bertolda Cake
ingredients:
Farina di mais fumetto (corn flour for pastries): 1 ¼cup(150 g)
Type 00 flour: 1 ¼cup(150 g)
Butter: 8 ½tbsp(120 g)- at room temperature
Sugar: ½cup(120 g)
Whole milk: 0.8cup(200 ml)
Eggs: 1- medium
Egg yolks: 1
Baking powder: 3.3tsp(16 g)
Lemon peel: 1- untreated
Vanilla bean: 1- the seeds
Fine salt: to taste

info:
Difficulty: Easy
Prep time: 15 min
Cook time: 40 min
Serving: 10
Cost: Low

instructions:
To make the Bertolda cake, start with the aromas: slice the vanilla bean in half lengthwise and scrape out the seeds with a knife . Grate the zest of an untreated lemon and collect it in a small bowl . Now sift the corn 'fumetto' ,
the all-purpose flour and the baking powder and set the powders aside. In another bowl, place the room temperature butter cut into cubes, add the granulated sugar , and beat the mixture with electric whisks to obtain a cream.
At this point, add the whole egg and then the yolk , a pinch of salt, continue beating the mixture with the whisks and pour in the room temperature milk gradually , still with the whisks running.
When the ingredients are well mixed, turn off the whisks and flavor the batter with the lemon zest and the vanilla bean seeds . Now incorporate the egg mixture into the powders
stir with a spatula to obtain a smooth batter without lumps . Grease a 9-inch diameter pan and line it with parchment paper following the instructions of the Lining a cake tin with parchment paper guide. Pour the cake batter into the tin and bake the dessert in a static preheated oven at 338°F for 40 minutes (if using a convection oven, 302°F for 30 minutes). Once baked, remove the cake from the oven and let it cool before removing it from the mold. Enjoy your Bertolda cake for breakfast or a snack!
Store the Bertolda cake under a glass dome for 2-3 days. The cake can be frozen if you used all fresh non-defrosted ingredients.
To make the Bertolda cake even more delicious, you can fill it with Nutella or fruit jams.
