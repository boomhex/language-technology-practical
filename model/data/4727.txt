name:
Spaghetti with Leek Cream, Nduja, and Hazelnuts
ingredients:
Spaghetti: 11.3oz(320 g)
Leeks: 2lbs(900 g)
Nduja: 1.75oz(50 g)
Hazelnuts: ¼cup(25 g)
Extra virgin olive oil: to taste
Fine salt: to taste
Black pepper: to taste

info:
Difficulty: Easy
Prep time: 15 min
Cook time: 25 min
Serving: 4

instructions:
Prepare your taste buds for a first course that is pure joy: spaghetti with leek cream, nduja, and hazelnuts! These ingredients come together to create a mix of flavors that will make your mouth water. Let's start with the leek, which gives a fantastic creaminess and a sweet, very appealing taste. Then there's the nduja, that Calabrian sausage that adds a nice spicy note making everything even more delightful. And finally, the toasted hazelnuts: crunchy and fragrant, they add an unexpected touch of originality. This recipe is perfect for a dinner with friends when you want to impress without too much effort, or for a Sunday family lunch. In short, it's a dish to share, ideal for creating special moments and making your guests happy. Ready to serve spaghetti with leek cream, nduja, and hazelnuts? Enjoy your meal! To prepare the spaghetti with leek cream, nduja, and hazelnuts, first clean the leeks: trim them and remove the outer leaves , then slice the white part and set it aside.
Slice the green part as well and transfer it to another bowl. Now, in a pot, heat a drizzle of oil, add the white part of the leek and pour about 2.82 oz of water .
Season with salt , cover with a lid , and let it cook for about 20 minutes on a low flame. Meanwhile, place the hazelnuts in another pan and toast them , then transfer them to a cutting board to cool slightly.
Meanwhile, in the same pan, heat a drizzle of oil and add the green part of the leek . Sauté in the pan for about 7-8 minutes , then season with salt and pepper . Set aside in a small bowl.
Coarsely chop the hazelnuts . Bring a pot full of salted water to the boil, which will be used for cooking the pasta. As soon as the white part of the leek is soft, blend it with an immersion blender until you get a cream. Transfer it to the pan where you cooked the green part of the leek and add the nduja .
Then add a ladle of hot water and heat until the nduja is well melted . Meanwhile, cook the spaghetti and drain them al dente.
Transfer the pasta to the pan with the sauce , add a ladle of cooking water if necessary , and a grind of black pepper . Mix everything well.
Plate the spaghetti , then garnish with the green part of the leek and the chopped hazelnuts . The spaghetti with leek cream, nduja, and hazelnuts are ready to be served!
It is advisable to serve the spaghetti with leek cream, nduja, and hazelnuts immediately.
The leek cream can be stored in the refrigerator for 1-2 days.
For a vegetarian version, replace the nduja with a flavorful cheese such as pecorino, taleggio, or gorgonzola!
