name:
Bloody Eyes
ingredients:
Ricotta cheese: 1 ¼cup(300 g)
Cookies: 3.5oz(100 g)
White chocolate: 2 ⅓cups(400 g)
Colored candies: 30- (brown, yellow and blue)
Egg whites: 1
Powdered sugar: 1 ¼cup(150 g)
Food coloring: to taste- red (in paste or powder form)
Dark chocolate: 2.1oz(60 g)

info:
Difficulty: Average
Prep time: 30 min
Cook time: 5 min
Makes: 30 pieces
Cost: Average

instructions:

To prepare the bloody eyes, start by mixing the ricotta with the crushed cookies in a bowl , add 50 g of white chocolate that you have melted in a double boiler (or in the microwave) and cooled slightly. Stir vigorously with a wooden spoon to mix all the ingredients well. With this mixture, form balls with a diameter of about 1 1/4 inches, slightly flattened , and place them on a tray covered with parchment paper, put in the freezer for 15 minutes to set and harden. In the meantime, melt the remaining white chocolate in a double boiler (or microwave) until it is fluid enough to be used as a coating.
Let it cool slightly before dipping the eyeballs with a fork . Move the fork to remove excess chocolate and place the eyeballs on the parchment paper , immediately place a colored chocolate candy on top of each eyeball (as if it were the iris of the eye). Place in the refrigerator for about 10/15 minutes to harden the white chocolate. When the white chocolate is completely hardened, remove the drips at the base using a sharp knife.
With the melted dark chocolate, create the pupil on top of each chocolate candy using a piping bag or a toothpick . Finally, prepare the red royal icing to create the eye veins: whip the egg white in a stand mixer until it's white, then add the sifted powdered sugar, finally add enough red food coloring to achieve a nice intense red . Place the icing in a piping bag with a small tip opening and create the red veins around the eye, starting from the base of the eye and drawing irregular veins towards the pupil . Enjoy the bloody eyes immediately or store them in the refrigerator until ready to serve!
