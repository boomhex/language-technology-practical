name:
Classic puff pastries with quark and salmon
ingredients:
Smoked Salmon: 3.9oz(110 g)
Chives: 5tsp(5 g)
Cream cheese: 5.6oz(160 g)- (quark)
Fine salt: 1pinch
Cracker: 12
White pepper: 1pinch

info:
Difficulty: Easy
Prep time: 10 min
Makes: 12 pieces
Cost: Low

To prepare the classic puff pastries with quark and smoked salmon, start by preparing the salmon mousse. In a mixer, blend the quark cheese with 2.8 oz of salmon , a pinch of white pepper , and a pinch of salt , just enough to season.
You should achieve a creamy consistency . Finely chop the chives and incorporate them into the mousse , then place the mixture in a piping bag with a star nozzle.
At this point, julienne the remaining salmon and start assembling the snacks: pipe a dollop of salmon cream on each cracker and arrange some pieces of smoked salmon .
The classic puff pastries with quark and smoked salmon should be assembled at the moment: we suggest preparing the mousse in advance and storing it in the refrigerator until it's time to assemble the pastries. It is recommended to eat the classic puff pastries with quark and smoked salmon within the day.
