name:
Salmon en papillote with mixed vegetables
ingredients:
Salmon steaks: 1.9lbs(860 g)
Capers: 4tsp(20 g)
Black olives: 2.1oz(60 g)- pitted
Artichokes: 5
White zucchini: 1 ¾cup(200 g)
Round eggplants: 1- violet
Dill: 1bunch
Extra virgin olive oil: 2tbsp(30 g)
Fine salt: to taste
Black pepper: to taste

info:
Difficulty: Easy
Prep time: 10 min
Cook time: 45 min
Serving: 4
Cost: Low

To prepare the salmon en papillote with mixed vegetables, soak the capers in a small bowl of water for 5 minutes and meanwhile wash the vegetables under fresh running water. After the necessary time, drain the pitted black olives and the capers, washing them well with water, and set aside. Slice the zucchinis to a thickness of 0.4 inches or, if they are rather small, into cubes .
Cut the eggplant in half , then into slices, and then reduce it into 0.4 inch cubes . How to clean artichokes , remove the tips, cut them in half, and then into 4 wedges ;
as you cut the artichokes, put them to soak in a bowl with water and lemon and set aside . Take the salmon, run your fingertip along the flesh to check for any remaining bones and remove them with the help of kitchen tweezers if necessary ; then with a knife, divide it into 4 fillets weighing 7.6 oz each .
At this point, you can prepare the papillote: in a high-sided baking dish, lay down a piece of aluminum foil at least 12 inches long and on top of it a slightly shorter piece of parchment paper . Place a salmon fillet and 1/2 cup of zucchinis on it ; drain the artichokes cut into cubes from the acidulated water and add 1/4 .
Add 1 tsp of capers , 1 tbsp of black olives, dill , and 1/4 of the eggplants cut into chunks ; season with salt and pepper to your liking.
Finish by pouring a teaspoon of oil and then close the papillote , first joining the edges of the parchment paper and then sealing the aluminum well .
Close the two lateral ends as candy wrappers , then repeat the operation for the remaining 3 salmon fillets. Bake in a preheated static oven at 302°F for about 40 minutes (or in a fan oven at 266°F for 30 minutes). When the fillet is ready , remove from the oven and serve the salmon en papillote with mixed vegetables piping hot .
It is recommended to consume the salmon en papillote with mixed vegetables immediately while hot.
The lower cooking temperature ensures that the salmon is cooked more evenly and allows the vegetables time to cook gently, releasing all their flavors.
