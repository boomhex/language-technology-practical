name:
Puff pastries with cannellini cream and sautéed shrimp
ingredients:
Crispy baked puff pastry: 12
Precooked Cannellini beans: ¾cup(140 g)
Black sesame seeds: 1spoonful
Fine salt: to taste
Brandy: 1.7oz(50 ml)
Butter: 0.7tbsp(10 g)
Shrimps: 12
Sweet Paprika: 1pinch

info:
Difficulty: Easy
Prep time: 20 min
Cook time: 6 min
Makes: 12 pieces
Cost: Low

To prepare the puff pastries with cannellini cream and sautéed shrimp, start by cleaning the shrimp, removing the head, legs, and shell (but keeping the tail) . In a non-stick pan, melt the pat of butter, and when it's hot, sear the shrimp on both sides . Flambé with the brandy , allowing it to evaporate, and then add salt.
Proceed to make the cannellini cream: drain the beans well and put them in a mixer with a pinch of salt and a pinch of paprika and blend finely until creamy. Roll the shrimp in black sesame seeds on both sides , making sure they adhere well. Transfer the cannellini cream into a piping bag and assemble the snack by squeezing a bit of cream onto each pastry and placing a shrimp on top . Your puff pastries with cannellini cream and sautéed shrimp are ready!
Consume the puff pastries with cannellini cream and sautéed shrimp immediately!
If you can't find black sesame seeds, use white ones.
