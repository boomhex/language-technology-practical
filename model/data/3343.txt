name:
Flatbreads with Bresaola Rolls
ingredients:
Baked mash potatoes: 12
Bresaola: 12slices
Robiola cheese: 7.1oz(200 g)
Chives: 1bunch
Fine salt: to taste
Black pepper: to taste

info:
Difficulty: Very easy
Prep time: 20 min
Makes: 12 pieces
Cost: Low

To prepare the flatbreads with bresaola rolls, start by making the cream: place the robiola cheese in a bowl and mix it until creamy , add salt, pepper , and chopped chives using scissors . Mix well to blend everything together and put the cream in a piping bag.
Meanwhile, prepare the rolls: roll up the slices of bresaola to form small cones , then tie the cones with chive strands . Take the piping bag with the cream and fill the rolls . Place one roll on each flatbread and serve.
