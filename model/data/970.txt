Spiced and creamy chocolate, chili, and vanilla coffee
ingredients:
Instant coffee: 1spoonful- Nescafé Gold
Dark chocolate: 0.9oz(25 g)
Vanilla bean: ½
Green Peperoncino: to taste
Sugar: 2 ½tsp(10 g)
Water: 0.4cup(100 g)
Ice: 7oz(200 g)
Dark chocolate: to taste

To prepare the chocolate, chili, and vanilla coffee, pour the water into a small pot with the seeds from the vanilla bean and the sugar . Bring the water to just below a boil, then turn off the heat and let it cool slightly for a few minutes: you should reach a maximum temperature of 176°F .
Meanwhile, pour the instant coffee into the shaker and add 2 tablespoons of the hot vanilla water . Shake well, then add the chili .
When the water has reached the right temperature, add the coarsely chopped chocolate and stir well with a spoon to dissolve it , then add an ice cube .
Pour the remaining ice into the shaker and the chocolate water , then close and shake vigorously .
Open the shaker and, with the help of a strainer, pour the coffee into a clear cup , then remove the strainer and let 2-3 ice cubes fall in. Garnish with grated chocolate to taste and enjoy your chocolate, chili, and vanilla coffee !
It is recommended to prepare the chocolate, chili, and vanilla coffee fresh.
If you prefer, you can omit the chili.
