name:
Risotto with red lentils and shrimp
ingredients:
Carnaroli rice: 1 ½cup(320 g)- ideal for risottos
Salt: to taste
Black pepper: to taste
Butter: 2.5oz(70 g)
Water: 3 ½cups(800 g)- warm (or vegetable broth)
Shrimps: 5.6oz(160 g)
Extra virgin olive oil: to taste
Lemon juice: ½
Lemon peel: ½
Wild fennel: to taste
Peeled red lentils: 1cup(200 g)
White onions: 1
Tomato purée: 0.4cup(100 g)
Water: 2.1cups(500 g)
Stracciatella cheese: 5.6oz(160 g)

info:
Difficulty: Very easy
Prep time: 30 min
Cook time: 50 min
Serving: 4

instructions:
To prepare the risotto with red lentils and shrimp, first focus on the How to clean shrimp . Remove the head, shell , and intestine , then cut them into 3 pieces .
Place everything in a bowl and season with salt, pepper , oil , and fennel .
Flavor with grated zest and lemon juice . Mix well and cover the bowl with plastic wrap . Let marinate in the fridge until ready to use.
Rinse the lentils thoroughly under running water . Finely chop the onion . In a pot, pour the oil, add the onion , and stew over low heat for 6-7 minutes.
Add the lentils and mix. Pour in the tomato purée and cold water .
Cover with a lid and cook for 25 minutes. Put the rice in another pot and toast it dry for 3-4 minutes. Adjust with salt .
Add the lentils and cook the rice; gradually pouring in hot water (or broth). At the end of cooking, adjust with salt and pepper . Turn off the heat, add cold butter , and mix.
Plate the dish, placing the marinated shrimp and stracciatella on the surface of the rice. Finish with fennel . The risotto with red lentils and shrimp is ready to be enjoyed !
The risotto with red lentils and shrimp can be stored in the fridge, in a closed container, for up to 2 days. To reheat, pour a little water or broth in a pan and stir over low heat until well heated. The marinated shrimp and stracciatella should be added fresh at the time of serving.
You can enrich the risotto with spices such as turmeric, smoked paprika, or freshly grated ginger. Instead of lemon, you can use lime. You can replace the stracciatella with burrata or a goat cheese cream with fresh herbs. If you don't like lentils, you can use split peas.
