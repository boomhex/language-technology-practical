name:
Fresh Borlotti Bean Salad
ingredients:
Borlotti beans: 2 ⅛cups(500 g)
Cherry tomatoes: 1cup(150 g)
Basil: 9leaves
Shallot: 3.5oz(100 g)
Fine salt: to taste
Black pepper: to taste
Extra virgin olive oil: 2 ¼tbsp(30 g)
Thyme: 3sprigs

info:
Difficulty: Easy
Prep time: 15 min
Cook time: 60 min
Serving: 4
Cost: Low

instructions:

To make the fresh borlotti bean salad, start with the legumes: shell them and collect the beans in a bowl : you will get 270 g of cleaned beans. Place a large pot filled with water on the stove, and as soon as it reaches a boil, add the borlotti beans . Cook for about 35 minutes without a lid. Meanwhile, clean the shallots
and cut them into four parts . Wash the cherry tomatoes and cut them in half . In a baking tray, add the cherry tomatoes and shallot wedges .
Fragrance with fresh thyme leaves and then season with 1.5 tablespoons of olive oil . Mix to flavor and bake in the center of a preheated static oven for 20 minutes at 390°F. Once cooked, remove the tray from the oven and let them cool at room temperature .
Meanwhile, the 35 minutes of bean cooking time will have passed, add salt and continue cooking for another 5 minutes. Once done, turn off the heat, drain the beans and transfer them to a bowl, also add the cherry tomatoes and shallots cooked in the oven . Adjust salt, pepper
and season with 2 teaspoons of olive oil . Mix to flavor and serve the fresh borlotti bean salad on plates with fresh basil leaves .
Store the fresh borlotti bean salad in the refrigerator for no more than 2 days covered with plastic wrap. Freezing is not recommended.
Those who love sweet and sour notes can add a pinch of brown sugar to the cherry tomatoes before baking, to caramelize them slightly.
