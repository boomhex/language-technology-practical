name:
Meatballs with Meat and Apples
ingredients:
Beef: 0.9lb(400 g)- ground
Golden delicious apples: 1
Breadcrumbs: 1cup(90 g)
Whole milk: Â¼cup(60 g)
Eggs: 1
Garlic: 1clove
Parsley: 1tuft
Fine salt: 1pinch
Black pepper: to taste
Olive oil: to taste

info:
Difficulty: Easy
Prep time: 10 min
Cook time: 10 min
Makes: 20 pieces
Cost: Low

instructions:
To make the meat and apple meatballs, first peel the apple, cut it into quarters, remove the core , and then cut it into small cubes . In a bowl, pour the ground meat, add the apple ,
and then the rest of the ingredients: breadcrumbs , milk , the whole egg
season with chopped parsley , salt , and pepper to taste. Mix with a spoon to blend all the ingredients together
and finally add the minced garlic clove , which you can also omit, at your discretion. Now take small portions of the mixture with wet hands and form walnut-sized balls . With the given amounts, you will get about 20 meatballs. Now pour the oil into a pan, heat it, and fry the meatballs for about 3 minutes.
Once they are golden brown , drain them and place them on a tray lined with absorbent paper to dry the excess oil . Serve the meat and apple meatballs hot .
You can store the meat and apple meatballs in the refrigerator covered with plastic wrap for 3-4 days. You can freeze them if you used fresh ingredients.
You can season the mixture with grated cheese or with finely chopped fresh chili if you love spicy flavors.
