name:
Baked Zucchini with Breadcrumbs and Parmesan
ingredients:
Zucchini: 4.4cups(1000 g)- Romanesco
Breadcrumbs: 0.67cup(80 g)
Parmigiano Reggiano PDO cheese: 1.4oz(40 g)
Water: ¼cup(50 g)
Extra virgin olive oil: 3tbsp(40 g)
Fine salt: 1tsp(5 g)
White pepper: to taste
Garlic: 1clove

instructions:

To prepare baked zucchini with breadcrumbs and Parmesan, start by trimming the zucchini . Slice them into rounds , then place them in a bowl and pour in almost all the extra virgin olive oil .
Season them with a pinch of salt , a dash of white pepper , and mix well to combine the dressing .
Now for the breading. In a bowl with the breadcrumbs, crush the garlic clove . Add the grated Parmesan and mix with a spoon .
Pour almost all of the breading over the zucchini , mix well , and arrange them on a round baking tray lined with parchment paper .
Once arranged in a circular pattern, brush the zucchini with a mix of water and the remaining extra virgin olive oil . Before baking, add the reserved breading . Bake in a static oven at 392°F for 25 minutes, then continue for another 5 minutes in grill mode for a delicious gratin finish. The baked zucchini with breadcrumbs and Parmesan are ready !
The baked zucchini with breadcrumbs and Parmesan can be stored in the refrigerator for a couple of days.
Once cooked, they can be frozen.
For a richer version, you can add some cubes of sweet scamorza cheese among the zucchini slices. To add fragrance to your zucchini tray, you can sprinkle the surface with chopped aromatic herbs like sage, thyme, and rosemary.
