name:
Manhattan
ingredients:
Rye whiskey: 3.4oz(100 ml)
Sweet red vermouth: 1.4oz(40 ml)
Angostura bitters: 1drop
Ice: to taste
Maraschino cherries: 6

To prepare the Manhattan, pour the rye whiskey into a mixing glass already filled with ice . Now add the sweet red vermouth and a dash of Angostura .
With a bar spoon, that is with a long handle, stir the ingredients well . Pour the drink into two coupe glasses. Garnish the drinks with cherries, we used 3 for each, skewered with a toothpick . Your Manhattan cocktail is ready to serve !
The Manhattan cocktail should be consumed immediately while it's nice and chilled.
Besides the classic Manhattan, there is the "dry" version where dry vermouth is used and the "perfect" version where, in addition to whiskey, half a dose of sweet vermouth and half of dry is mixed.
