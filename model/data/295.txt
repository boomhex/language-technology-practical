name:
7 Jar Cake with Cocoa
ingredients:
Natural plain yogurt: 1vasetto
Sugar: 2vasetti
Type 00 flour: 3vasetti
Sunflower seed oil: 1vasetto- poor
Unsweetened cocoa powder: 0.4cup(40 g)- 1/3 of a jar
Eggs: 5.8oz(165 g)- (3 medium)
Baking powder: 4tsp(16 g)- 1 packet
Fine salt: 1pinch
Butter: to taste
Type 00 flour: to taste
Powdered sugar: to taste

To prepare the 7 jar cake with cocoa, begin by pouring the sugar into a bowl , add the oil , and then the eggs .
Add the yogurt as well and start the beaters until the mixture is light and fluffy (about 10 minutes) .
After the indicated time, sift the flour , cocoa , and baking powder directly into the bowl, then add the salt .
Mix for a few moments with the beaters to get a smooth and lump-free batter , transfer it to a greased and floured 9-inch mold . Bake in a preheated static oven at 350°F for about 40 minutes, and once the 7 jar cake with cocoa is cool, you can unmold it. Dust with powdered sugar as desired .
The 7 jar cake with cocoa keeps under a glass dome at room temperature for 5 days.
You can freeze it after baking, already portioned.
Add chocolate chunks to the 7 jar cake with cocoa batter for an unbeatable dessert.
