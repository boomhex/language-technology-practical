name:
Caprese puffs
ingredients:
Puff pastry: 8.1oz(230 g)- round
Datterino tomatoes: 1cup(140 g)
Mozzarella cheese: 1cup(150 g)
Oregano: to taste
Basil: to taste
Extra virgin olive oil: to taste
Fine salt: to taste

info:
Difficulty: Very easy
Prep time: 10 min
Cook time: 12 min
Makes: 8 pieces

instructions:
To prepare the Caprese puffs, first preheat your oven to 392°F in static mode. Then wash and quarter the cherry tomatoes and cut the mozzarella into cubes . Roll out the puff pastry without detaching it from the parchment paper and use a 3.5-inch round cutter to create 8 discs.
Remove the excess dough and prick the discs with a fork . Now add a tablespoon of cherry tomatoes on top of each disc ,
drizzle with a bit of olive oil , a pinch of salt , and some dried oregano , then bake in the preheated static oven at 392°F for 10 minutes.
When the puffs are golden, remove them from the oven and add a teaspoon of mozzarella cubes on each one , then bake again for a couple of minutes. Once out of the oven, add some fresh basil torn by hand and serve your Caprese puffs hot and fragrant !
Caprese puffs can be stored in the refrigerator for up to 2 days; it is recommended to reheat them before serving. It is not advisable to freeze them.
Add a tasty and unexpected touch to this simple appetizer by garnishing Caprese puffs with some grated lemon zest!
