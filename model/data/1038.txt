name:
Cream and Nutella Cake
ingredients:
Type 00 flour: 1.67cups(200 g)
Nutella: 0.8cup(200 g)
Eggs: 6.3oz(180 g)- about 3 medium
Sugar: 0.8cup(160 g)
Whole milk: 0.8cup(200 g)- at room temperature
Baking powder: 3.8tsp(16 g)
Fresh liquid cream: 1 ½cup(350 g)
Vanilla-flavored powdered sugar: ¾cup(100 g)

instructions:

You get that sweet, melt-in-your-mouth bite with every forkful, and the outside is usually piled high with more cream, sometimes piped into little clouds for extra fun. To prepare the cream and Nutella cake, start by cracking the room-temperature eggs into a bowl, add the sugar and whisk with beaters until you get a fluffy and frothy mixture. Add Nutella
and mix with a spatula . Then pour in the room-temperature milk and incorporate with the sifted flour and baking powder .
Continue mixing until you get a smooth batter without lumps . Transfer it into an 8.5-inch mold, buttered and floured , and bake in a static oven at 356°F, preheated, for about 45 minutes . Once the cake is ready, let it cool and then remove from the mold.
Pour the cream into a bowl and whisk with electric beaters , just before it becomes frothy, add the powdered sugar , and continue to whisk until it's firm .
Cut the cake in half and fill the base with two-thirds of the whipped cream . Transfer the remaining cream into a piping bag with a star nozzle and decorate the surface. Your cream and Nutella cake is ready .
The cream and Nutella cake can be stored in the refrigerator, covered under a cake dome, for 2-3 days.
The cake can be frozen, preferably without decoration; for convenience, you can cut it into slices already.
Missing a bit of chocolate? Add some chocolate flakes or chips either in the batter or on the whipped cream filling.
