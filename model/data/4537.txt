name:
Cold pasta with ricotta, avocado, and green beans
ingredients:
Pasta: 11.3oz(320 g)
Cow's milk ricotta cheese: 1cup(240 g)
Avocado: 1
Green beans: 3.5oz(100 g)- already cleaned
Yellow cherry tomatoes: ½cup(80 g)
Datterino tomatoes: ½cup(80 g)
Basil: to taste
Lemon juice: to taste
Extra virgin olive oil: to taste
Salt: to taste
Black pepper: to taste

info:
Difficulty: Very easy
Prep time: 10 min
Cook time: 20 min
Serving: 4
Cost: Low

instructions:
Cold pasta with ricotta, avocado, and green beans is a first course perfect for those seeking a tasty and nutritious alternative to the usual cold pasta . This recipe is designed for those who want a quick and easy dish without sacrificing flavor and quality ingredients. The ricotta and avocado cream, enriched with fresh basil and a touch of lemon juice, gently coats the pasta, giving it a warm and delicate flavor. The green beans, with their crunchy texture, add a pleasant contrast, while the cherry tomatoes, with their natural sweetness, complete the dish with a lively and colorful note. Hurry up to prepare the cold pasta with ricotta, avocado, and green beans... it will be on the table in just half an hour!

To prepare cold pasta with ricotta, avocado, and green beans, first cut the green beans into pieces and blanch them in salted water . Drain them and reserve the cooking water.
Throw the pasta into the green bean cooking water and cook it al dente . Meanwhile, in a blender, place the avocado and ricotta .
Add the basil , lemon juice , a pinch of salt, and blend until you get a smooth and homogeneous cream .
Cut the cherry tomatoes . Drain the pasta and transfer it to a large bowl. Add the ricotta and avocado cream .
Then add the green beans and cherry tomatoes . Mix well to combine the pasta with the dressing .
Pour a drizzle of olive oil, adjust the salt and pepper , and give it a final stir . Your cold pasta with ricotta, avocado, and green beans is ready to be enjoyed !
Cold pasta with ricotta, avocado, and green beans is a dish that can be prepared in advance and enjoyed even outside the house. It can be stored in the refrigerator in a closed container for 2-3 days.
This type of preparation lends itself to many customizations; you can enrich the dish by adding, for example, grilled chicken cubes or sautéed shrimp.
Instead of basil, you can use other aromatic herbs such as mint, parsley, or dill!
