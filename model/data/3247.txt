name:
Braised Brussels Sprouts with Beer
ingredients:
Brussels sprouts: 1.4lbs(650 g)
Blond beer: 4.4oz(130 ml)
Shallot: 1
Butter: 3 ½tbsp(50 g)
Sugar: 1tsp
Bay leaves: 1leaf
Fine salt: to taste
Black pepper: to taste

info:
Difficulty: Very easy
Prep time: 20 min
Cook time: 20 min
Serving: 4
Cost: Very low

instructions:
To prepare braised Brussels sprouts with beer, first remove the outer leaves from the sprouts , trim off the protruding bottom part , and cut a cross into them with a small knife .
Wash the sprouts thoroughly under running water and let them drain. Peel and chop the shallot , then sauté it in a non-stick pan with the butter .
Once the shallot is well browned, add the sugar and immediately after, add a bay leaf to the sprouts and sauté them over high heat for 5 minutes until they are well braised .
Deglaze the sprouts with beer and let it evaporate for 3 minutes. Salt and pepper, cover with a lid, lower the heat, and let cook for about ten minutes . Remove the lid, increase the heat, and cook for another 5 minutes, stirring occasionally to prevent the sprouts from sticking to the bottom . The braised Brussels sprouts with beer are ready!
Braised Brussels sprouts with beer can be stored in an airtight container in the refrigerator. They can be frozen and then reheated in a pan for a few minutes.
You can enhance the flavor of braised Brussels sprouts with beer by adding chili powder.
