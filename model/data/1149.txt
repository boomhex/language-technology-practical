name:
Sweet focaccia with apples, pine nuts, and chocolate
ingredients:
Manitoba flour: 2cups(250 g)
Brewer's yeast: 1tsp(5 g)
Sugar: 4tbsp(50 g)
Whole milk: ½cup(130 g)
Extra virgin olive oil: 1 ¼tbsp(20 g)
Peach jam: ⅔cup(150 g)
Apples: 2
Pine nuts: 2tbsp(20 g)
Dark chocolate chips: 0.75oz(20 g)

info:
Difficulty: Average
Prep time: 30 min
Cook time: 25 min
Serving: 8
Cost: Average
Note plus the rising times

instructions:
To prepare the sweet focaccia with apples, pine nuts, and chocolate, pour the yeast, milk , a teaspoon of sugar into a small bowl and mix . In another bowl, pour the flour and the remaining sugar ,
Combine the dissolved yeast in the milk and mix with a fork . Then also add the oil
and mix . Start kneading with your hands , then transfer the dough to a work surface and knead until smooth and homogeneous .
Shape the dough into a ball and place it in a bowl. Cover with plastic wrap and let it rise for 3 hours in the oven with only the light on. After this time, take the well-risen dough and transfer it into a 12x16 inch baking tray lined with parchment paper and greased with a little oil. Gently press with your hands
and spread it to cover the entire surface of the tray . Cut the apple in half, remove the core, and slice it thinly . Place the jam in the center
and spread it over the entire surface . Also arrange the apple slices on top and brush them with a bit of jam.
Add the pine nuts and chocolate chips . Bake your focaccia in a static oven at 375°F for 20-25 minutes . Serve warm!
The sweet focaccia can be stored for 1 day under a glass dome. Alternatively, it can be frozen after baking.
In the summer, you can substitute apples with peaches or apricots.
