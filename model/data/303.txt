name:
Stewed Cod with Potatoes
ingredients:
Desalted cod: 2.7lbs(1.2 kg)
Potatoes: 0.9lb(400 g)
Peeled tomatoes: 1.67cups(400 g)
Onions: 1
Garlic: 2
Fresh chili pepper: 1
White wine: 0.7cup(150 g)
Rosemary: 1sprig
Bay leaves: 1leaf
Parsley: to taste
Extra virgin olive oil: to taste
Fine salt: to taste
Black pepper: to taste

info:
Difficulty: Easy
Prep time: 20 min
Cook time: 35 min
Serving: 4
Cost: Average

instructions:
To prepare stewed cod with potatoes, first clean the onion and chop it finely , then slit the chili pepper lengthwise . In a saucepan, heat a splash of oil with the chili pepper and crushed garlic cloves, then add the chopped onion and saut√© for a few minutes.
When the onion becomes translucent, add the peeled tomatoes, crushing them with your hands , and cook over low heat for about ten minutes . Meanwhile, peel the potatoes and cut them into 0.4-inch slices .
When the sauce has thickened, add the potatoes and mix well, then deglaze with the white wine . Allow it to completely evaporate, season with salt , and continue cooking for another 10 minutes, still on low heat.
In the meantime, cut the cod fillet into pieces , after removing the skin and any bones. After 10 minutes, add the rosemary and bay leaf , then place the pieces of cod inside .
Cook with the lid on for another 10 minutes . At the end of cooking, adjust the salt and pepper and flavor with chopped parsley .
Plate the cod with potatoes , drizzle with the sauce , and garnish with more parsley. Your stewed cod with potatoes is ready to be served !
Stewed cod with potatoes can be stored in the refrigerator for up to 2 days, in an airtight container.
Freezing is not recommended.
If you like, you can add olives or capers for a more intense flavor!
