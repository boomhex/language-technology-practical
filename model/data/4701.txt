name:
Spelt and Lentil Soup
ingredients:
Dried lentils: 1 ¼cup(250 g)
Spelt: 1.7cups(250 g)
White onions: 1- small
Carrots: 1
Celery: 1- stem
Tomato paste: 1spoonful
Extra virgin olive oil: to taste
Salt: to taste
Black pepper: to taste

info:
Difficulty: Very easy
Prep time: 10 min
Cook time: 1 h 10 min
Serving: 4
Cost: Very low
Note + soaking time for the lentils (120 minutes)

instructions:
Spelt and lentil soup is a rustic and genuine first course, perfect for bringing the warmth and flavors of tradition to the table. The hearty texture of spelt perfectly complements the creaminess of lentils, creating a balance of flavors that captivates at the first taste. Preparing this soup is simple and requires only a few steps, but the result is a rich and substantial dish, ideal for those seeking a healthy and nutritious comfort food. Served hot with a drizzle of raw olive oil, this soup becomes a true embrace of flavors, perfect for colder days.

To prepare the spelt and lentil soup, first soak the lentils in cold water for 2 hours. Drain them, place in a pot with salted cold water , and cook for 30 minutes. Drain again and set aside both the lentils and the cooking water. Finely chop the onion .
Do the same for the celery and the carrot . In the same pot where you cooked the lentils, pour in the oil .
Add onion , carrot , and celery . Sauté for a few minutes.
Add the tomato paste and the lentils . Cover with the lentil cooking water .
Adjust the salt . Add the spelt and cook for 30 minutes . If needed, pour in more lentil cooking water.
Plate up and finish with a drizzle of oil and a sprinkle of pepper . The spelt and lentil soup is ready to be enjoyed .
The spelt and lentil soup can be stored in the refrigerator, in a closed container, for 2-3 days.
If you want a bolder touch, you can add spices like cumin or smoked paprika, which will enrich the dish with aromatic and slightly spicy notes. For an even heartier version, try enriching it with crispy bacon bits or crumbled sausage.
