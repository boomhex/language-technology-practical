name:
Cheese Crostoni
ingredients:
Homemade bread: 8slices
Emmentaler cheese: 1.75oz(50 g)
Edam cheese: 1.75oz(50 g)
Fresh liquid cream: 2tbsp(30 g)
Grana Padano PDO cheese: 2tsp(10 g)- to grate
Rosemary: 1sprig
Black pepper: 2pinches
Butter: to taste

info:
Difficulty: Very easy
Prep time: 15 min
Cook time: 15 min
Makes: 8 pieces
Cost: Low

instructions:
To prepare the cheese crostini, first cut the bread into slices about 0.6 inches thick . Place the slices of bread on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper and spread them with butter , then toast them in a preheated static oven at 392°F for about 4 minutes. Meanwhile, cut both the Edam and Emmental into cubes .
Pour the cheese into a food processor , then add the grated Grana Padano PDO and the pepper .
Also add the cream and blend to obtain a smooth mixture . Chop the rosemary needles with a knife .
Take the toasted bread slices and spread them with the cheese cream , then garnish with the chopped rosemary . Put them back in the hot oven, lower the temperature to 356°F, and cook for about 10 minutes. Your cheese crostini are ready to be enjoyed !
It is recommended to consume the cheese crostini immediately.
If you prefer, you can substitute the cream with milk.
