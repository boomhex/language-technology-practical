name:
Bulgur with cherries and goat cheese
ingredients:
Bulgur: 1 ¼cup(200 g)- pre-cooked
Cherries: 1cup(150 g)
Goat: 2.8oz(80 g)
Water: 2 ½cups(600 g)
Celery: 1cup(130 g)
Whole hazelnuts: ½cup(70 g)
Arugula: 0.7oz(20 g)- approximately
Lime peel: 1
Fresh ginger: 0.6tsp(3 g)
Mint: to taste
Extra virgin olive oil: to taste
Fine salt: to taste
Black pepper: to taste

info:
Difficulty: Very easy
Prep time: 15 min
Cook time: 5 min
Serving: 4
Cost: Average

instructions:
To prepare the bulgur with cherries and goat cheese, first put the water in a large pot, then add the bulgur and a pinch of salt and cook it for 4-5 minutes from boiling. Once cooked, drain the bulgur and transfer it to a bowl; season with a drizzle of oil , mix well, and let it cool.
In the meantime, wash the cherries, cut them in half, and remove the pits . Peel the ginger and cut it into thin slices . Coarsely chop the hazelnuts with a knife .
Finally, wash the celery stalks, remove the ends, peel the outer filaments with a peeler , and cut them into thin slanted slices . Now place the pitted cherries in a bowl and add the celery ,
the ginger , the torn mint leaves , the chopped hazelnuts ,
the grated zest of a lime , and the now-cooled bulgur . Season with a drizzle of oil , salt, and pepper,
then add the arugula that you have previously washed and dried , mix well, and finally add chunks of goat cheese on top : your bulgur with cherries and goat cheese is ready to be enjoyed !
It is recommended to consume the bulgur with cherries and goat cheese immediately or store it in the refrigerator for up to one day. It cannot be frozen.
If the cherry season has passed too quickly and you didn't have time to make this recipe, you can try replacing them with loquats or green apples. If you prefer a version without fruit, prepare the Bulgur with fresh herb and walnut pesto : a healthy, wholesome, and very tasty dish!
