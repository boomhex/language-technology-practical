name:
Nuggets in air fryer
ingredients:
Chicken breast: 1.1lbs(500 g)
Bread crumbs: 1.67cups(200 g)
Fine salt: 1tsp
Sweet Paprika: 1tsp
Onion powder: 1tsp
Garlic powder: 1tsp
Extra virgin olive oil: 0.3tsp(20 g)
Remilled durum wheat semolina: 0.8cup(100 g)
Eggs: 3
Breadcrumbs: 0.833cup(100 g)

To prepare the nuggets in an air fryer, start by cleaning the chicken breast from bones and fat 1 . Once cleaned, cut it into 1-inch pieces 2 and place it in a mixer equipped with blades 3 .
Add the bread crumbs 4 and season with salt 5 and sweet paprika 6 .
Also add the onion powder 7 and the garlic. Pour the extra virgin olive oil 8 and close the mixer with its lid 9 .
Blend everything until the mixture detaches from the sides 10 and is homogeneous. With wet hands, take 0.7 ounces of the mixture 12 , form balls and place them on a tray 12 . With these doses, you will get 16 nuggets.
Roll each ball first in the semolina 13 , making sure to remove the excess 14 . Then dip it in the beaten eggs 15 .
Roll it in the breadcrumbs 16 , then give it the classic shape by molding it with your hands 16 . Place the nuggets in the air fryer basket and pour the remaining extra virgin olive oil 19 .
Cook for 14 minutes at 356°F, turning the nuggets halfway through cooking 19 . After the cooking time, the air fryer nuggets will be golden and crispy 20 , ready to be enjoyed 21 !
We recommend consuming the air fryer nuggets immediately or freezing them after cooking. Alternatively, they can be stored for one day in the refrigerator, well covered.
If you don't have garlic and onion powder at home, you can replace them with a minced garlic clove and onion in the mixer. For a touch of extra freshness, serve your chicken nuggets with a sprinkle of fresh parsley and grated lemon zest.
