name:
Spaghetti with pea cream, asparagus and mussels
text:
Spaghetti: 11.3oz(320 g)
Mussels: 2.2lbs(1 kg)- (already cleaned)
Garlic: 1clove
Fresh chili pepper: 1
Extra virgin olive oil: to taste
Peas: 1.4cups(200 g)
Water: 0.4cup(100 g)
Fine salt: to taste
Asparagus: 7oz(200 g)- (to clean)

Each forkful is an encounter of textures and scents, with mussels taking center stage. But you know, the veggies make sure things don’t get too heavy.

To prepare spaghetti with pea cream, asparagus, and mussels, start with the pea cream: shell the peas 1 , then blanch them in salted boiling water for 2 minutes 2 3 .
Drain the peas and immediately transfer them into ice water to stop the cooking and keep the color vibrant 4 . Pour them into a jug 5 and add water 6 .
Salt 7 and blend 8 until smooth and velvety 9 . Set aside the pea cream.
In a pan, heat a drizzle of oil with a clove of garlic and a piece of fresh chili 10 . Add the cleaned mussels 11 , raise the heat, cover with a lid 12 , and cook for about 5 minutes.
When the mussels are all opened 13 , shell them and set aside 14 . Now cut the asparagus into thin rings 15 , after removing the toughest part of the stalk, and split the tips in half.
In a pan, heat another drizzle of oil with a clove of garlic 16 , then add the asparagus 17 and cook over medium heat for about 5 minutes. Meanwhile, cook the spaghetti in plenty of salted water 18 , draining them a couple of minutes before the end of cooking.
Pour a couple of ladles of pasta water into the pan 19 , then drain the spaghetti here 20 and bring to cooking. Off the heat, add the pea cream 21 .
Add the shelled mussels 22 and a drizzle of oil to mix. Stir well 23 and serve the spaghetti with pea cream, asparagus, and mussels immediately 24 !
We recommend consuming the spaghetti with pea cream, asparagus, and mussels immediately.
If you want to vary the recipe, you can try replacing the pea cream with a delicious fava bean cream and use artichokes instead of asparagus: a tasty and seasonal alternative!
