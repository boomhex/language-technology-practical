name:
Air Fryer Salt Cod
ingredients:
Desalted cod: 1.3lbs(600 g)
Eggs: 2
Breadcrumbs: to taste
Extra virgin olive oil: to taste
Fine salt: to taste
Black pepper: to taste
Salt: to taste

To prepare the air fryer salt cod, first remove the skin from the cod 1 , then cut the fillet into slices about 1.5 inches 2 . Finally, remove the bones with tweezers 3 .
Break the eggs into a bowl, add salt 4 , and beat with a fork 5 . Also add the pepper 6 .
Dip the cod fillets into the egg mixture 7 , then coat them with breadcrumbs 8 and place them on a tray 9 .
Drizzle the breaded cod fillets with a little oil on both sides 10 . Place them in the air fryer basket (we used a fryer with a 6-liter capacity) 11 and cook at 356°F for a total of 15 minutes 12 .
After 10 minutes, turn the fillets over 13 and continue cooking for another 5 minutes on the other side 14 . Once cooked, serve your air fryer salt cod with a sprinkle of pepper and sea salt flakes 15 !
It is recommended to consume the air fryer salt cod immediately. If necessary, you can store it in the refrigerator for 1-2 days.
