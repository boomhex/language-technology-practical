name:
Lime and Mint Chicken
ingredients:
Chicken: 4lbs(1.8 kg)- cut into pieces
Lime: 2
Garlic: 2cloves
Mint: to taste
Extra virgin olive oil: to taste
Fine salt: to taste

To prepare lime and mint chicken, first wash the lime well and slice it 1 . Then peel the garlic 2 and slice it 3 .
Place the chicken pieces in a baking dish 4 , add the lime slices on top 5 , and the mint leaves 6 .
Season with salt 7 , add the garlic 8 , and drizzle with some olive oil 9 .
Mix with your hands 10 and cover with plastic wrap 11 . Let it marinate for 30 minutes in the refrigerator. Then bake in a fan oven, preheated to 350°F, for about 1 hour. When it's nicely golden, take the chicken out of the oven 12 .
It's time to plate. Transfer the chicken pieces to a serving dish, add more mint leaves, tearing them by hand 13 , pour the cooking juices over 14 , and serve 15 .
We recommend consuming the chicken immediately. Alternatively, you can store it in an airtight container and reheat it before serving.
You can use lemon instead of lime.
You can increase the marinating time if you prefer a more intense flavor.
