name:
Smash burger
text:
Ground beef: 10.5oz(300 g)
Ground pork: 3.5oz(100 g)
Black pepper: to taste
Hamburger muffins: 2
Cheddar cheese: 4slices
Salad: 2leaves
Pickles: to taste
Mayonnaise: to taste
Ketchup: to taste

To prepare the smash burger, first combine the ground beef and ground pork in a bowl 1 , pepper 2 , and knead with your hands to obtain a homogeneous mixture 3 .
Cover with plastic wrap 4 and place in the refrigerator while you prepare the other ingredients for the bun; you should have everything ready so you can cook the burgers at the last minute. Cut the buns in half 5 , then spread the bottom part with mayonnaise 6 .
Add lettuce leaves 7 and cut the pickles into slices 8 . Set aside and place an iron, cast iron, or steel pan over the heat; it should be very hot. Meanwhile, take the meat mixture, divide it into 4 portions of about 3.5 oz each, and shape them into a patty with your hands 9 .
Place one patty at a time in the hot pan 10 , then immediately smash it with a meat press, taking care to press very hard to flatten the meat well 11 . After 60 seconds, flip the burger with a spatula 12 .
Let the other side cook for a maximum of a minute and a half 13 , then transfer the burger to a plate 14 and proceed in the same way to cook the others. Immediately cover the burgers with sliced cheese so that it melts with the heat 15 .
Now you are ready to complete the buns: stack 2 burgers with cheese on each bun 16 , then add sliced pickles. Finally, close with the top of the bun that you will have spread with ketchup 17 . Your fantastic smash burgers are ready to be enjoyed 18 !
It is recommended to consume the smash burgers immediately.
Non-stick pans are not recommended for cooking burgers as they do not retain heat well.
It is important to cook one burger at a time to ensure it forms a nice crust.
Follow our instructions and prepare all the ingredients in advance, then cook the burgers just before serving!
