name:
Buckwheat penne with wok mushrooms
ingredients:
Buckwheat penne: 11.3oz(320 g)
Champignon mushrooms: 40
Carrots: 1
Onions: 1
Garlic: 1clove
Gluten-free soy sauce: 4tsp
Extra virgin olive oil: 4spoonfuls
Parmigiano Reggiano PDO cheese: 4spoonfuls

To prepare the buckwheat penne with wok mushrooms, start by cleaning the vegetables. Slice the onion thinly 1 and carrot 2 . Preheat a wok pan and pour in half of the oil 3 .
Add to the wok the peeled and finely chopped garlic 4 , sliced onion 5 , and chopped carrots 6 .
Keep the temperature medium-high and stir frequently with a spoon 7 . How to Clean Porcini Mushrooms and cut them into chunks 8 , then transfer them into the pan with the rest of the ingredients 9 .
Continue to cook over medium-high heat, stirring often to prevent the ingredients at the bottom of the wok from burning 10 . Boil the buckwheat penne in salted boiling water 11 . Once ready, drain them and transfer them to the wok 12 .
To finish, pour in the other half of the oil 13 , the gluten-free soy sauce 14 , and the grated Parmesan cheese 15 .
Now all you have to do is stir well to mix all the ingredients 16 , scent with sage leaves 17 , and your dish is ready to be served! 18 .
Buckwheat penne with wok mushrooms can be stored for one day in an airtight container. Freezing is not recommended.
For those who love a stronger mushroom flavor, instead of champignons, you can rehydrate dried porcini and add them to the wok last, perhaps finely sliced. If you don't have a wok pan, you can use a non-stick pan with a thick base.
